FOOD: chocolate sundae

Dessert confection that typically contains ice cream, chocolate syrup, whipped cream and a cherry. 
Another of Lieutenant Commander Deanna Troi's pick-me-up chocolate treats - although real chocolate ice cream and whipped cream are beyond the replicator's programming bounds. 

Reference: The Price
