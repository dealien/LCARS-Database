FOOD: potatoes

Traditional Terran tuber root used as an ingredient in many dishes. Potatoes are often one of the side dishes to many Terran entrees, and can be served fried, scalloped, julliene (also known as French Fries), boiled, mashed, baked, and twice baked (which is a combination of previous methods). Potatoes are used in a variety of recipes including salads, entrees, and accompaniments.
