FOOD: icoberry juice

A fruit juice made from the icoberry.
Lieutenat Commander Jadzia Dax was very fond of the drink, despite the fact that she was allergic and it made her spots itch. Lieutenant Commander Worf had to remind her of this on more than one occasion.
