FOOD: tomato soup

Popular Earth-based soup that comes in several varieties. Some 14 varieties were programmed originally into the U.S.S. Voyager's Mess Hall replicators, including with added rice, with added vegetables, and Bolian-style. Tom Paris simply wanted it plain.
