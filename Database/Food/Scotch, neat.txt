FOOD: Scotch

An alcoholic Earth drink. Served "neat" is to serve is with nothing added. "On the rocks" includes ice.
Preferred drink of fictional detective and Captain Jean-Luc Picard's favorite Dixon Hill and by Chief Miles O'Brien's RAF pilot holo-character.
