FOOD: Kalavian biscuits

A snack food prepared by Neelix. During Torres' all-nighter to solve a shuttle pylon shear problem in Warp 10 simulations, Neelix had some Kalavian biscuits for her but became so intrigued in helping he made her get them herself.
