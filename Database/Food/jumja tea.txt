FOOD: jumja tea

A tea, apparently made from the same tree that provides the sweet yet Vitamin C-laden sap that's a major ingredient of jumja sticks. It is enjoyed at least in the mirror universe. 
Neelix- {This is a lot like] "blackberry currant tea, which goes well with Delvin Fluff Pastries, Petit Fours, or Tuwaly Pie. For a relaxing dessert tea or even as a late-night tea, add just a touch of honey per cup and a spritz of lemon juice.
