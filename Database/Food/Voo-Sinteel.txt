FOOD: Voo-Sinteel

Red, intoxicating beverage. 
Traditionally, the Voo-Sinteel is consumed as the Great Plume of Agosoria reaches its full brilliance. This event occurs once every 11 years. Sonsorra offered a decanter of Voo-Sinteel to Trip Tucker.
