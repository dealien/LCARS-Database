FOOD: red leaf tea

A Cardassian favorite of Gul Dukat's, ordered to interrupt the self-defense phaser lens in the Ops replicator. It was also enjoyed by Cardassian scientist Gilora, who asked Chief Miles O'Brien for a cup during her science team's mission to Deep Space Nine.
