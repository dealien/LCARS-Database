FOOD: jellybeans

Candy concoction, a staple for humans with a sweet tooth and a former U.S. President. 
Out of bored mischief, Nog once tossed this 500-year-old Terran candy from his second-level Promenade perch on passersby just befoe he spotted Varis in 2369.
