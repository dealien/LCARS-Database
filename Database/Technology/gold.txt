CHEMISTRY: gold

A semi-precious metal that is also an excellent conductor of electrical current. On Earth Terrans reveared the metal as a precious metal, but other worlds found this basic metal more common and used it fur numerous utilitarian purposes. A weak and plyable metal it remained in use as ornamental for plaques and combadges among other uses. Gold was also widely used in a casing to contain specific measurements of liquid latinum, the newer currency standard outside of the Federation.
