TECHNOLOGY: holodeck imaging processor


The technology in a holodeck system which produces the visual imagery. 
The imaging processors in a U.S.S. Enterprise-D holodeck malfunctioned following exposure to plasmonic energy in the atmosphere of planet Boraal II in 2370, and the cimulation of Boraal II suffered many resolution failures as a result.
