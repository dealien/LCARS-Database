MEDICAL: Bicuprodyanide

Neural toxin fatal to Andorians, Bolian, and any other species with a bicupric-based oxygen transport metabolism.  Races without will merely suffer from metal poisoning that lasts a few days.  To them it can only be fatal in a dose the size of a coffee mug. 

Reference: Fall of Terok Nor
