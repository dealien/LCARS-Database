TECHNOLOGY: Sensor security net

Forcefield used to keep people out/in. After the murderous shapeshifter, Meta, escaped through a hole in the wall in 2369, Ben Sisko claimed that from that point on, they would put forcefields in the walls. 

Reference: The Siege
