TECHNOLOGY: teraphasic coils

Component of Xyrillian technology. A Xyrillian ship secretly used plasma exhaust from Enterprise NX-01 to replenish their malfunctioning teraphasic coils. When they were discovered, Trip Tucker visited their vessel to restore the coils to working order.
