TECHNOLOGY: self-sealing stem bolts

An assembly tool used to connect and secure other parts together. Self-sealing units were able to deploy pins out the sides to lock themselves into place without having to use a tool like a screwdriver or similar to secure the fitting. 
Devices that Nog and Jake Sisko received in trade for yamok sauce in 2369. Nog and Jake traded the 100 gross bolts in turn for seven tessipates of land on planet Bajor.
