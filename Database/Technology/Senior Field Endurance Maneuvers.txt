TECHNOLOGY: Senior Field Endurance Maneuvers

Also called the Outlast.  Ten teams of two Starfleet Academy seniors (once command candidate, and one science specialist) are pitted against each other.  Participants must be nominated by Academy faculty to take part.   Teams are dropped into a wilderness and must seek out and eliminate each other.  The last team remaining, wins. 

Reference: Battlestations!
