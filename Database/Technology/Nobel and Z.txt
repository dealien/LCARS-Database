RECREATION: Nobel and Z. Magnees Prizes

Created shortly after Earth/Centaurus first contact by combining the extinct Nobel Prizes and Z Magnees Prizes. Enthusiastically adopted by the Federation Council in 2161. Prizes are awarded every four years. The winners are chosen from amongst a group of nominees by a secret voting of those nominees. Categories include peace, physics, medicine, and biogeology.
