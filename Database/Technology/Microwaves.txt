CHEMISTRY: Microwaves

Type of energy on the electromagnetic spectrum. Used by pre-subspace EM-based civilizations converging toward C-451-45018-3 on the Richter’s scale of culture for communications, welding, beamed-energy weapons, and cooking food. Dal Norton was murdered by a microwave burst. 

Reference: Fall of Terok Nor
