CHEMISTRY: rubindium

An element often found in crystalline form. A gemstone which provides one component of a subcutaneous transponder. When imprisoned on planet Ekos in 2268, Commander Spock used crystals from two transponders to create a primitive laser. 

Reference: Patterns of Force
