TECHNOLOGY: alien transporter

A unique form of transporter created by an unknown alien race and used by Supervisor 194, Agent 203 and Agent 204, and other operatives of their classified organization on Earth. The system was kept behind a large safe-like door, and created an active portal to convey personnel about the planet. The range, energy use, and other engineering facts are not known.
