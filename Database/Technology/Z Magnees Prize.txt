RECREATION: Z Magnees Prizes

Commissions in the warlord's personal corps of scientists given on Centaurus by warlord Zalar Mag'nees and her successors. Began in late seventeenth century; discontinued in the mid-nineteenth. It was later resurrected and combined with the Terran Nobel prize to create the Nobel & Z Magnees Prizes.
