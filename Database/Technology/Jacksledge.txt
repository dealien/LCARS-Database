TECHNOLOGY: Jacksledge

Engineering equipment. A large remote-controlled hammering device used to crush large metal objects.  Chief O’Brien's attempts to use a jacksledge to install the impulse energy buffers on Gul Tahgla's ship were not met with approval by the Cardassians.
