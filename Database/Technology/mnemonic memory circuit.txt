TECHNOLOGY: mnemonic memory circuit

Type of computer circuit used in modern databank access. A primitive circuit, composed of a variety of electrical and radio parts of the 1930s on Earth, was jury-rigged by Commander Spock to retrieve tricorder data while he and Captain Kirk were trapped there. 

Reference: The City on the Edge of Forever
