TECHNOLOGY: Pattern fitter

Tailoring equipment used by Garak.  The device was damaged by an explosion in 2370 and was fixed by the Cardassian engineer, Berat.  After the machine was fixed the calibration was more even and the cut more precise than before. 

Reference: Betrayal
