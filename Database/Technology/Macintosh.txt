TECHNOLOGY: Macintosh

Personal computer model used on Earth in the twentieth century. Chief Engineer Montgomery Scott had trouble determining what the mouse accessory was for and found using the keyboard "quaint." 

Reference: The Voyage Home 
The Encyclopedia and other quasi-canon books like the technical manuals were all written and laid out using Macintosh computers and software designed specifically for the Apple OS.
