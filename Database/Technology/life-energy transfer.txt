TECHNOLOGY: life-energy transfer

Technique invented by the now-extinct natives of planet Camus II to swap the minds and personalities of two subjects with each other. Dr. Janice Lester discovered the device used for this procedure in 2269 and used it to switch her mind with that of longtime rival James Kirk.
