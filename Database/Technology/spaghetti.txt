FOOD: Spaghetti

One of Jake Sisko's favorite foods. A pasta dish using string shaped noodles served with a tomato sauce. Variations include different kinds of noodles (angel hair, linguini, spinach, etc) and meat included in the sauce and/or as meatballs.

Reference: The Big Game
