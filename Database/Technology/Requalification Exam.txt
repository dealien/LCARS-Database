RECREATION: Requalification Exam

Every officer was required to take this exam in their area of specialization, but this regulation was usually not very strictly enforced. However, Commander Elizabeth Wu ordered Lieutenant Idun Asmund to take the exam immediately, even though she was only over one week late.
