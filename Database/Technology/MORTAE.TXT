TECHNOLOGY: mortae

Mining tools used by the Troglytes on planet Ardana, and adapted by their Disruptor freedom-fighters to be used as weapons in their striuggle for equality circa 2269. They frequently targeted the planet's suspended "cloud city," Stratos, for such attacks.
