PERSONNEL: Balok
Commander, starship commander, First Federation Flagship Fesarius

Balok conducted his people's first contact with the United Federation of Planets in 2266. In an effort to ascertain the sincerity of Federation offers of friendship, Balok staged an incident in which he first threatened the Enterprise, then later claimed his ship had suffered severe damage.

Reference: The Corbomite Maneuver
Played by Clint Howard who also played Grady in "Past Tense" nearly 30 years later, and a Ferengi thief in "Acquisition."
