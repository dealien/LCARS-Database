PERSONNEL: Jazik
Jal, Sect Leader, Kazon Ogla

Kazon leader of the Ogla sect. Razik dispatched young Kar to kill Chakotay for displaying Starfleet markings in Ogla space. Razik was later assassinated by the young Kar after he failed to kill Chakotay. 

Reference: Initiations
Portrayed by Patrick Kilpatrick.
