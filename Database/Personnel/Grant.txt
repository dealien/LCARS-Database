PERSONNEL: Robert Grant
Crewman, security officer, U.S.S. Enterprise

Security guard aboard the Starship Enterprise who was part of the landing party at planet Capella IV on stardate 3497. Grant was killed by a Capellan with a local weapon called a kligat. 

Reference: Friday's Child 
Played by Robert Bralver. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
