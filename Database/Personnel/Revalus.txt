PERSONNEL: Revalus

Famous author who penned "The Wait." Revalus was a proponent of "visceral writing," a retro method that uses pen and paper and is comparable to an artist using a brush on canvas. Onaya gave Jake Sisko a pen that was once used by Revalus.
