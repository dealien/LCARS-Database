PERSONNEL: Ralkana

Female member of the Alsaurian resistance movement on a Delta Quadrant planet governed by the heavy-handed Mokra Order. Ralkana was killed while trying to rescue her mother from prison. Ralkana's father, Caylem, never quite accepted her death and once mistakenly believed Captain Janeway to be his daughter.
