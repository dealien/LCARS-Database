PERSONNEL: Connor Rossa
 
Starfleet officer who was killed in the line of duty in 2356. At the time, Rossa was stationed at the Federation colony on Galen IV. His son, Jeremiah, survived the attack by Talerian forces. 

Reference: Suddenly Human
