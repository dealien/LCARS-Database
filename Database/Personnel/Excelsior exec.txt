PERSONNEL: Miguel Ferrer
Commander, navigator, U.S.S. Excelsior

This officer served as the executive officer under Captain Styles while the starship was still being tested for transwarp drive. When Admiral Kirk hijacked the U.S.S. Enterprise Ferrer and crew were assigned to pursue and stop the renegade officer. While he operated the controls, the starship failed to respond after Commander Montgomery Scott sabotaged the drive systems.

Reference: The Search for Spock
Portrayed by Miguel Ferrer. The character's name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
