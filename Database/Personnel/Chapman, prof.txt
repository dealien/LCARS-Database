PERSONNEL: Chapman
Professor, Starfleet Academy

Instructor at Starfleet Academy who was one of B'Elanna Torres' teachers. Prof. Chapman considered Torres to be one of the most promising cadets he had ever taught, despite the fact that she often challenged him and eventually quit the Academy.
