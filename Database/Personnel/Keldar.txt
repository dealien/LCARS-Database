PERSONNEL: Keldar

Ferengi male who married Ishka and established a home on Ferenginar. Keldar had little talent for generating profit,  but was very good at family matters. Before dying, Keldar fathered and raised two sons, Quark and Rom. He often advised his sons, including one bit he tried to give to Quark, to disuade him from leaving the homeworld. Keldar told Wuark that there were plenty of opportunities on Ferenginar, but Quark decided to live up to the 75th Rule of Acquisition, "the stars are lined with latnium."
