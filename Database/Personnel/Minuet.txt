PERSONNEL: Minuet

Holo-woman of Commander William T. Riker's dreams, created by the Bynars as a distraction from their ship-napping activities. Minuet was so real in Riker's memory that later a virtual reality based on his neural responses included Minuet when he was trapped on Alpha Onias III. 

Reference: 11001001, Future Imperfect
Portrayed by Carolyn McCormick.
