PERSONNEL: Quinn

Member of the Q Continuum who requested that he be able to commit suicide — a direct result of his disenchantment with the idea of eternity, among other things. His death later triggered a Continuum civil war that was settled with the birth of the first Q baby. 

Reference: Death Wish, They Q and the Grey
Portrayed by Graham Gerritt.
