PERSONNEL: Al Wellington

A casino money counter in the Vic Fontaine holoprogram. Every night, Al left his station at 11:45 for six minutes. This absence was exploited by thieves to pull a complex heist. 

Reference: Badda-Bing Badda-Bang 
Played by James Wellington. The character’s last name is conjectural based on the actor’s real name.
