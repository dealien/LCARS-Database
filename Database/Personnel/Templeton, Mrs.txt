PERSONNEL: Mrs Templeton

One of the dramatis personae in Janeway's gothic holonovel. Mrs. Templeton tended Lord Burleigh's mansion, guarded his first wife's secrets, and made life difficult for Janeway's character, the new governess. 

Reference: Cathexis, Persistence of Vision
Portrayed by Carolyn Seymour, whom also played Subcommander Taris in "Contagion" and Commander Toreth in "Face of the Enemy."
