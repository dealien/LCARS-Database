PERSONNEL: Edward Albert
Lieutenant Commander

Starfleet officer and father of Cadet Joshua Albert, who as a member of the elite Nova Squadron died in a flight accident in 2368. Lt. Commander Albert was present at the inquiry into his son's death, which initially blamed the cadet for pilot error. When it was revealed the Nova Squadron was attempting an illegal maneuver which was the actual cause of the mishap, Albert received some degree of exoneration by learning that his son did not die a coward. 

Reference: The First Duty 
Played by Ed Lauter. The first name of the character was not mentioned but is conjectural based on the real name of the actor.
