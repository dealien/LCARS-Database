PERSONNEL: Morla

Argelius II native, of Cantaba Street. Morla's jealousy triggered endless arguing with his betrothed, Kara. Mora was a suspect after Kara's murder in 2267. He testified on the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701. 

Reference: Wolf in the Fold 
Played by Charles Dierkop.
