PERSONNEL: Jon Daily
Captain, transport commander, Astral Queen

Captain of the passenger vessel Astral Queen. In 2267 Daily bypassed a scheduled stop at Planet Q at the request of Captain Kirk. This forced the Karidian Company of Players to request passage aboard the Enterprise, allowing Kirk to investigate murder allegations against the actor Anton Kiridian. 

Reference: The Conscience of the King
