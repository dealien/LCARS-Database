PERSONNEL: Yareena

Rich native of Ligon II and wife of reigning leader Lutan. To escape her loveless marriage, yet retain her riches, Yareena allowed herself to be killed by Natasha Yar, then revived. 

Reference: Code of Honor 
Portrayed by Karole Selmon.
