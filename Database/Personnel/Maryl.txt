PERSONNEL: Maryl

One of the assimilated beings that briefly took control of Seven of Nine's personality during U.S.S. Voyager's 2375 encounter with a Borg Vinculum. Maryl, a juvenile Terrelian female with twelve brothers, played Kadis-Kot with Naomi Wildman.
