PERSONNEL: Maxwell Burke
Commander, executive officer, U.S.S. Equinox

One of Captain Ransom's most loyal officers. He and B'Elana were intimate friends back at the Academy. Tom Paris became jealous of the officer when the U.S.S. Equinox and U.S.S. Voyager starships encountered each other in the distant Delta Quadrant.

Reference: Equinox
Portrayed by Titus Welliver. The Continuum did not mention the character's first name or position, but stated that he was Captain "Maxwell's" loyal officer- instead of Captain Ransom.
