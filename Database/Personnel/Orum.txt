PERSONNEL: Orum

Assimilated Romulan freed from the Borg in 2368. He became the medic for a Delta Quadrant colony of ex-Borgs. Orum suggested a neural collective field to save Chakotay in 2373. 

Reference: Unity
Portrayed by Ivar Brogger.
