PERSONNEL: Barbo

Quark's cousin who was imprisoned for selling defective warp drives to the Tarahong government. Quark was also involved in the scam, but he betrayed Barbo to the Tarahong authorities and kept all the profits. Barbo was released from the Tarahong detention center in 2369.
