PERSONNEL: Mur'Eq
Emporer, Planetary leader, Qo'noS

Klingon Emporer who devised the non-lethal challenge with blunted bat'leths so that his warriors would kill only their enemies, not each other. Captain Kohlar follows this precedent when T'Greth challenges Tom Paris. 

Reference: Prophecy
