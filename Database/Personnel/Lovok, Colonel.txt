PERSONNEL: Lovok
Colonel, fleet commander, Tal Shiar

A Romulan officer who was believed to be killed and replaced by a Founder. In 2371 the faux Lovok leaded a Tal Shiar and Obsidian Order attack force that was overwhelmed at the Founders' homeworld. 

Reference: The Die Is Cast 
Played by Leland Orser.
