PERSONNEL: Ms Malvin

Fourth grade teacher at Worley Elementary School in Kenmare, County Kerry, Ireland. Trip Tucker's nephew is in her class, and the students sent the Enterprise NX-01 drawings, letters and questions. Captain Archer and his crew recorded some answers for the class; Tucker was embarrassed when he had to answer a "poop question."
