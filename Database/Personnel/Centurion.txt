PERSONNEL: Centurion

Nameless officer aboard a Romulan bird-of-prey encountered by the U.S.S. Enterprise in 2266. The Centurion and his ship had crossed the Romulan Neutral Zone and were destroyed when the Commander of the vessel ordered a self destruct. 

Reference: Balance of Terror 
Played by John Warburton. Centurion was a rank in the Romulan guard, approximately equivalent to a captain in the Federation Starfleet.
