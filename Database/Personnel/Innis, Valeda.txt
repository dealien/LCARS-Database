PERSONNEL: Valeda Innis
First Electorine, planetary leader, Haven

When a Tarellian ship approached Haven in 2364 carrying survivors from the Tarellian biological war, Innis unsuccessfully attempted to convince U.S.S. Enterprise-D Captain Jean-Luc Picard to stop the vessel by force. 

Reference: Haven 
Played by Anna Katarina.
