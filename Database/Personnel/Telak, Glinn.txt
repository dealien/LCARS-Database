PERSONNEL: Telak
Glinn, Vetar

One of Gul Evek's officers on the Cardassian ship Vetar. Telak was forced to recall his men from Dorvan V after Evek and Jean-Luc Picard agreed to retreat. 

Reference: Journey's End
