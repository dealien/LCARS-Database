PERSONNEL: Reginold

Engineer and crew member on the Pakled vessel Mondor. Together with his fellow crew, Reginold coerced Geordi La Forge to help fix their vessel. In the eyes of the Pakleds, La Forge was considered to be "smart." 

Reference: Samaritan Snare 
Portrayed by Leslie Morris.
