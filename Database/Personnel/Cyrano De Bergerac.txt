PERSONNEL: Cyrano De Bergerac

Main character in a famous French play of the same name. This play, by Edmond Rostand, was performed for many years including by Dr. Beverly Crusher and her acting troop aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise-D. The main character, Cyrano, was played by Lieutenant Reginald Barclay.

Reference: The Nth Degree
Played by Dwight Schultz.
