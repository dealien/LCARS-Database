PERSONNEL: Janice Rand
Commander, Communications officer, U.S.S. Excelsior

Starfleet officer who started her career as a yeoman for Captain James T. Kirk of the U.S.S. Enterprise in 2266. Her Starfleet career covered many roles, including transporter chief for the refit Enterprise in 2271. She later served at Starfleet Command in San Francisco. 
Then she joined the crew of the U.S.S. Excelsior as communications officer under the command of Captain Sulu. Rand also served as team leader for the Excelsior's Gamma shift. 

Reference: The Corbomite Maneuver, Charlie X, Miri, The Motion Picture, The Search For Spock, The Voyage Home, Undiscovered Country, Flashback
Played by Grace Lee Whitney. The character was originally designed to be another regular on the show and appeared in numerous promotional flyers. She was contracted for 13 episodes during the first season, which she fulfilled. But during a controversial overture by a senior show producer she was no longer favored and her contract was not renewed. In fact, her last appearance, " " simply featured her appearance on the bridge for the briefest of moments. She has been a popular attraction at the  Conventions over the years.
