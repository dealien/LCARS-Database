PERSONNEL: Mowray
PhD, archaeologist, Landris II 

Archaeologist working on Landris II in 2369. Mowray's site schematics were accessible on the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701-D. Picard's hopes of communicating with Mowray were thwarted by a communications blackout.
