PERSONNEL: Dejaren

A Serosian model HD-25 isomorphic projection maintenance unit with extreme hazard clearance who, in 2373, killed the six biological crewman aboard his ship. Dejaren's pet holographic fish was named Spectrum. 

Reference: Revulsion
Portrayed by Leland Orser.
