PERSONNEL: Joe DiMaggio

Twentieth-century baseball player, also known as Joltin' Joe and the Yanjee Clipper. Arguably baseball's greatest center fielder. DiMaggio scored hits in 56 consecutive games, a record that stood until 2026, when it was broken by Buck Bokai, a shortstop from the London Kings.
