PERSONNEL: Rebecca Sullivan

Maquis fighter, resident of Altos IV, and wife of Michael Eddington. In 2373, Rebecca intercepted a message about cloaked missiles bound for Cardassia. This event led to the freeing of her husband before the Jem'Hadar razed Altos IV.
