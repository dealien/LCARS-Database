PERSONNEL: Kell

Gray-haired Klingon ambassador unmasked, in 2367, as a Romulan collaborator intent on dissolving the Klingon/Federation alliance by mind-controlling Geordi La Forge into killing a Klingon governor. Kell was arrested by the Klingons. 

Reference: The Mind's Eye 
Played by Larry Dobkin, who also directed "Charlie X."
