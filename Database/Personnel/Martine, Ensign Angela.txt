PERSONNEL: Angela Martine
Ensign, phaser control officer, U.S.S. Enterprise

Plans to marry her superior officer, Lieutenant Robert Tomlinson, ended with his death during a battle with Romulans. In 2267 she and Esteban Rodriguez surveyed the amusement park planet. 

Reference: Balance of Terror 
Played by Barbara Baldavin, the wife of original series casting director Joseph D'Agosta.
