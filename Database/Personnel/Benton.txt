PERSONNEL: Seamon Benton

A lithium miner on planet Rigel XII, working under Ben Childress, during Harry Mudd's sojourn there in 2266. 

Reference: Mudd's Women 
Played by Seamon Glass. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
