PERSONNEL: Hildebrandt
Crewman, science officer, U.S.S. Enterprise-D
 
A geomechanics and volcanology specialist and Starfleet officer aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise-D. She was a member of the team, led by Wesley Crusher, that surveyed the Selcundi Drema sector in 2365. 

Reference: Pen Pals 
Played by Anne H. Gillespie, who also played a nurse on many episodes of Deep Space 9.
