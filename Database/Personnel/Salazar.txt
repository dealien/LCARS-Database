PERSONNEL: Salazar

Crewmember and transporter chief aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise-D under Dr. Beverly Crusher's command in 2370 during an incident with the Lore-led self-aware Borg. 

Reference: Descent
Portrayed by Benito Martinez.
