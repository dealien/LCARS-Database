PERSONNEL: Carl Gustav Jung
Psychiatrist, Earth

Psychiatrist from the Earth nation of Switzerland (1875-1961). Jung broke with colleague Sigmund Freud to found a branch of psychology utilizing 'collective unconscious' and 'Active Imagination' — unaware that Chakotay's people had been practicing this for centuries.
