PERSONNEL: Koval
Chairman, Tal Shiar, Romulus

Male leader of the Romulan Tal'Shiar who was in line for a seat on the Continuing Committee. In 2375 Julian Bashir realized that Koval was providing information to Section 31. 

Reference: Inter Arma Enim Silent Leges
