PERSONNEL: Meribor

Kataan native. Eline and Kamin's daughter, sister to Batai. Her life's experiences in the community of Ressik, c.1368, were recorded in a space capsule found in 2368 by the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701-D. 

Reference: The Inner Light
