PERSONNEL: Rex

Fictional bartender from the Dixon Hill novels. Rex was the proprietor of Rex's Bar in the holographic re-creation fashioned by Captain Picard. Lwaxana Troi was attracted to the man while she was unaware that he was not a real person- she was attracted to the mystery he presented since she couldn't read his thoughts.

Reference: Manhunt 
Portrayed by Rod Arrants.
