PERSONNEL: Howard
Casino countman, Fontaine holodeck

Holographic character appearing in the Vic Fontaine-Frankie Eyes story. Howard was a countman who enjoyed a nightly martini. Bashir's casino heist scheme turned on this routine and was thrown into chaos because Howard was sick the night of the robbery. 

Reference: Badda-Bing Badda-Bang
