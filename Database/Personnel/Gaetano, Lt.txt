PERSONNEL: Peter Gaetano
Lieutenant, security officer, U.S.S. Enterprise

A Starfleet officer killed in 2267 by the inhabitants of Taurus II while on a mission aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise's shuttlecraft Galileo to study the Murasaki 312 formation. 

Reference: The Galileo Seven 
Played by Peter Marko. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
