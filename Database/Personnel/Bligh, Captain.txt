PERSONNEL: William Bligh
Captain, naval commander

(1754-1817) Controversial British Naval officer from 18th-century Earth whose crew mutinied against him in 1789. In an alternate timeline, Chakotay compared the time-controlling Annorax to Bligh when his temporal weapon ship crew were disillusioned with his tactics.
