PERSONNEL: Porter

In the alternate timeline of Henry Starling, Porter belonged to a paramilitary antigovernment group in 1996 Phoenix, Arizona. Porter and his cohorts captured Chakotay and Torres, who were later rescued by the U.S.S. Voyager's EMH. 

Reference: Future's End
Portrayed by Clayton Murray.
