PERSONNEL: Nilrem
Director, Doctor, Sikla Medical Center, Malcor III

Sikla Medical Facility physician on Malcor III in 2367 who attended Commander William T Riker while he posed as Rivas Jakara, undercover. Nilrem became the facility's acting director by complying with the security minister's medically dangerous order to revive Riker when the previous head, Dr Berel refused to follow the order since it would cause harm to the patient. 

Reference: First Contact 
Portrayed by Steven Anderson. His name is Merlin spelled backwards.
