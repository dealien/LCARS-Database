PERSONNEL: L'Naan

B'Elanna Torres' Klingon grandmother, Captain Kohlar invokes her name as an honored warrior who has fallen in the name of Kahless. Torres calls her "L'Naan, son of Krelik," implying that her great-gradfather was Krelik.
