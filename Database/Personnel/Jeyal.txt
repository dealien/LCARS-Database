PERSONNEL: Jeyal
Tavnian husband of Lwaxana Troi in 2372. In accordance with Tavnian law, Jeyal intended to appropriate the son Lwaxana was carrying after delivery to be raised solely among males. He eventually relented when Odo claimed Lwaxana as his beloved. 

Reference: The Muse 
Played by Michael Ansara, who also played Kang in "Day of the Dove" and "Blood Oath."
