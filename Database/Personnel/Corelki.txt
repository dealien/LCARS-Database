PERSONNEL: Corelki
security officer, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

Corelki was part of a away team investigating an attack on the Federation outpost at Ohniaka III. The team was ambushed by a group of strangely acting Borg, and Corelki was killed by a phaser shot from one of those drones.
