PERSONNEL: Mardah

Bajoran Dabo girl estranged from siblings due to employment at Quark's. Mardah and Jake Sisko dated in 2370. She left DS9 in 2371 for the science academy on Regulus III. 

Reference: Playing God, Sanctuary, The Abandoned, Fascination
Played by Jill Sayre.
