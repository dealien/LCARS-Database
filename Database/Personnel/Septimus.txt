PERSONNEL: Septimus

Native to planet 892-IV, Septimus was an elderly gentleman who gave up his comfortable lifestyle to lead a non-violent, religous one as a member of the Children of the Sun movement. 

Reference: Bread and Circuses 
Played by Ian Wolfe, who also played Mr. Atoz in "All Our Yesterdays."
