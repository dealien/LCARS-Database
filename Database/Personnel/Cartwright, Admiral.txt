PERSONNEL: Brock Cartwright
Admiral, senior operations officer, Starfleet Command

Starfleet officer, based at the San Francisco Headquarters of Starfleet. When a mysterious probe of unknown origin threatened planet Earth in 2286, Cartwright was in charge of emergency operations. 
The politically conservative Admiral was later found to part of a conspiracy to upset burgeoning relations between the Federation and the Klingon Empire. 

Reference: The Voyage Home, The Undiscovered Country
Played by Brock Peters who also played Joseph Sisko, father to Benjamin.
