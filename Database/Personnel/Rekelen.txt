PERSONNEL: Rekelen
resistance fighter, Cardassia Prime

One of three anti-military Cardassian dissidents who fled to DS9 in 2370 for asylum. With Rekelen were her partner, Hogue, and Professor Natima Lang, a former flame of Quark's. 

Reference: Profit and Loss 
Played by Heidi Swedberg.
