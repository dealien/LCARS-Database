PERSONNEL: Timothy

Pre-teen boy who survived the Black Cluster in 2368, but lost both parents. To avoid facing his pain, Timothy decided to be an emotionless android. Eventually Timothy learned to cope. 

Reference: Hero Worship 
Portrayed by Joshua Harris.
