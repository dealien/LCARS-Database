PERSONNEL: R. M. Merrick
Captain, survey commander, S.S. Beagle

Starfleet Academy washout who later captained a survey ship that vanished in 2261. Kirk found Merrick, renamed Merikus, in 2268 on an ancient Rome-like planet. Merrick died helping Kirk escape. 

Reference: Bread and Circuses 
Played by William Smithers.
