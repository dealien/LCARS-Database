PERSONNEL: D'Ghor

Head of a wealthy Klingon family. 
From 2366 to 2371 D'Ghor systematically attacked the financial assets of the House of Kozak, with the intentions of taking it over. His acts were found shameful by the High Council and he was disgraced for all Klingon society.

Reference: The House of Quark
