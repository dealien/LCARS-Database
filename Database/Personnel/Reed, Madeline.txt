PERSONNEL: Madeline Reed

Younger sister of Malcolm Reed. Madeline was contacted by Hoshi Sato during preparations for Malcolm's surprise birthday party. She revealed that her brother is a very difficult person to get to know, and that she often can't tell if he's joking or serious. 

Reference: Silent Enemy
Portrayed by Paula Malcomson.
