PERSONNEL: Steven Hayes
Major, MACO team leader, Enterprise NX-01

Leader of the Military Assault Command Operation (MACO) officers assigned to the Enterprise NX-01 mission to the Delphic Expanse. Hayes clashed with Lieutenant Reed when the Major suggested that Reed's men were too valuable to take on a risky rescue mission.

Reference: The Xindi
Portrayed by Steven Culp. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
