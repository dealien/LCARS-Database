PERSONNEL: Rollman
Admiral

In 2369, Major Kira Nerys contacted Admiral Rollman from Deep Space Nine to express her resentment over Commander Sisko's reluctance to grant political asylum to the Bajoran Kohn-Ma terrorist Tahna Los. 

Reference: Past Prologue, Whispers
Played by Susan Bay, who is married to Leonard Nimoy.
