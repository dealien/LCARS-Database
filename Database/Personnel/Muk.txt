PERSONNEL: Muk

Ferengi thief working with Ulis. Muk was one of the marauders who raided the Enterprise NX-01, and tried to keep much of what he found for himself. Muk was convinced that Captain Archer's dog, Porthos, was especially valuable. 

Reference: Acquisition
Portrayed by Clint Howard, whom played Balok 30 years earlier in "The Corbomite Maneuver."
