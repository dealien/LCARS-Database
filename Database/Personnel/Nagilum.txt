PERSONNEL: Nagilum

An indefinable life-form studying the crew of the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701-D in 2365 in the Morgana Quadrant. Nagilum felt justified in his terrifying approach to learning about life and death. 

Reference: Where Silence Has Lease 
Portrayed by Earl Boen.
