PERSONNEL: Rager
Ensign, helm officer, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

Conn officer aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise. In 2367, Rager was affected by the Enterprise's encounter with a Tyken's Rift. 

Reference: Galaxy's Child, Schisms, Relics, Night Terrors 
Portrayed by Lanei Chapman who later had a regular role in "Space, Above and Beyond."
