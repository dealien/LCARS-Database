PERSONNEL: Gia

Daughter of town magistrate Garvin in a village on planet Barkon IV. Gia and her father took Data into their home when the android stumbled into their village in 2370. Data had amnesia and could not remember his name, so Gia called him Jayden. 

Reference: Thine Own Self 
Played by Kimberly Cullum.
