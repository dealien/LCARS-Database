PERSONNEL: Tony Cicci
bodyguard, Fontaine Lounge

Holographic character in the Vic Fontaine holoprogram. Tony Cicci was a bodyguard for Frankie Eyes. Miles O'Brien could not delete Cicci because he was part of a jack-in-the-box subroutine. 

Reference: Badda-Bing Badda-Bang 
Played by Mike Starr. The Continuum features the wrong image of this character, instead it features Charlie the security guard.
