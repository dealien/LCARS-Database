PERSONNEL: McPherson

Genetically-enhanced human who escaped Earth on the S.S. Botany Bay in 1996 with Khan. After commandeering the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701 in 2267, McPherson controlled a key section of the ship. 

Reference: Space Seed
