PERSONNEL: Badar N`D`D

Served as the Antican Chief Delegate to the Parliament Conference in 2364. 

Reference: Lonely Among Us 
Played by Marc Alaimo, most known as Gul Dukat, but also played Commander Tebok in "The Neutral Zone," Gul Macet in "The Wounded," and the gambler Frederick La Rouque in "Time's Arrow.
