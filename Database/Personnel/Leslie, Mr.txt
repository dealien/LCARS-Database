PERSONNEL: Eddie Leslie
crewmember, U.S.S. Enterprise

This crewman served in many positions on board the starship.
 
Reference: Where No Man Has Gone Before, The Conscience of the King, Return of the Archons, This Side of Paradise, The Alternative Factor, The Omega Glory
Played by Eddie Paskey, who also played an Eminiar guard in "A Taste of Armegeddon." The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name. Pasky was one of the regular stand-ins on the original series. He had been working at the gasoline station where all Desilu studio trucks and cars were serviced when studio vice-president Herbert F. Solow got him the job on the show.
