PERSONNEL: Kargan
Captain, starship commander, I.K.S. Pagh

This was the ship Riker was assigned to during the Officer Exchange Program. Riker earned Kargan's respect. 

Reference: A Matter of Honor 
Played by Christopher Collins, who also played the Pakled captain in "Samaritan Snare."
