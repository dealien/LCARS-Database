PERSONNEL: Joseph Sisko
Merchant, New Orleans, Louisiana, Earth

Benjamin and Judith Sisko's father. Joseph, chef and owner of "Sisko's Creole Kitchen" restaurant in New Orleans. He was married twice; his first wife, Sarah, was Benjamin's mother. 

Reference: Explorers, Homefront
Played by Brock Peters, who also played Admiral Cartwright in "The Voyage Home," and "The Undiscovered Country," and the preacher in "Far Beyond the Stars."
