PERSONNEL: Carl Zeemo

A character who appeared in the Vic Fontaine holoprogram's preprogrammed plot twist. Zeemo was an eldery, vicious mob boss who sent Frankie Eyes to buy Vic Fontaine's establishment. 

Reference: Badda-Bing Badda-Bang
