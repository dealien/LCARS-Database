PERSONNEL: Sajen

One of the civilian Suliban held in the Tandaran internment camp, and friend of fellow prisoners Danik and Narra. Sajen distrusted Captain Archer and Ensign Mayweather, but eventually joined them and the rest of the prisoners in an escape. 

Reference: Detained
Portrayed by Christopher Shea.
