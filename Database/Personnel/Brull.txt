PERSONNEL: Brull

A leader of a camp of outlaws on Gamma Hromi II known as the Gatherers. Proud of his maurauding lifestyle, Brull nevertheless hoped for a better life for his two sons. He was part of a delegation, along with his chieftain Chorgan, that negotiated a truce between the Gatherers and Acamarian Sovereign Marouk in 2366. Brull, along with the other Gatherers, agreed to return to their original home planet, Acamar III. 

Reference: The Vengeance Factor
