PERSONNEL: Ptera

Vhnori woman who was accidentally transported to the U.S.S. Voyager in 2371 soon after her death. Upon her revival in Sickbay, she was frightened as it did not resemble her race's version of the afterlife. 

Reference: Emanations
Portrayed by Cecile Callan.
