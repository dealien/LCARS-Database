PERSONNEL: Alan Keogh
Captain, starship commander, U.S.S. Odyssey

l 2370 when the ship was demolished in the Gamma Quadrant by the Jem'Hadar. Keogh died while he was investigating reports that the Jem'Hadar were intimidating Federation citizens. The Jem'Hadar rammed their attack ship into the Odyssey, destroying it and the entire crew, including Keogh.

Reference: The Jem'Hadar 
Played by Alan Oppenheimer. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
