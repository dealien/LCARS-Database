PERSONNEL: Shelby Brossmer
Chief, transporter operator, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

Transporter engineer aboard the Enterprise-D in 2368. She was at the controls when Geordi La Forge and Ro Laren vanished into an interphase phenomenon in an apparent transporter accident, and were presumed dead. 

Reference: The Next Phase 
Played by Shelby Leverington. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
