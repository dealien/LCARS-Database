PERSONNEL: Rollins
Lieutenant, U.S.S. Voyager

Starfleet officer who was assigned to the U.S.S. Voyager in 2371, just prior to its being lost in the Delta Quadrant. Rollins was later promoted from ensign to lieutenant. 

Reference: Caretaker
Portrayed by Scott MacDonald, whom also played Subcommander N'Vek in "Face of the Enemy," and Tosk in "Captive Pursuit."
