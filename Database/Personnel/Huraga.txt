PERSONNEL: Huraga

A Klingon warrior and longtime friend to the House of Mogh. In 2372 Huraga told Worf about the Klingon Council's plan to invade Cardassia.

Reference: The Way of the Warrior
Played by William Dennis Hunt. The character's name was not mentioned in the episode but was written into the script.
