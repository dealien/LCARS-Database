PERSONNEL: Tava
Doctor, Sikla Medical Facility

Female crisis room physician on Malcor III. Tava was the first Malcorian to realize that the injured, and disguised, William Riker was an alien. 

Reference: First Contact 
Portrayed by Sachi Parker.
