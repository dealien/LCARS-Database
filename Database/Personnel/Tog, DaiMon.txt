PERSONNEL: Tog
DaiMon, starship commander, Krayton

Ferengi captain who went to Betazed for the 2366 Trade Agreements Conference. Tog and his associate, Farek, kidnapped Ambassador Lwaxana Troi, Commander William Riker, and Lieutenant Commander Deanna Troi to use the senior's mind-reading skills against business rivals. During the scandel he became enamoured with her and worked to court her, which Lwaxana used to manipulate Tog and secure their escape.

Reference: Ménage à Troi 
Portrayed by Frank Corsentino.
