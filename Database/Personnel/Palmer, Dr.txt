PERSONNEL: Palmer, Dr. 
Federation Anthropological Field Team member injured on Mintaka III in 2366. Mintakans captured Palmer to please their god, the Picard. Troi distracted the Mintakans so Riker could rescue Palmer. 
Reference: Who Watches The Watchers?
