PERSONNEL: Orfil Solis
Lieutenant j.g, helm officer, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

Male junior officer unexpectedly assigned to the conn when Geordi was commanding the U.S.S. Enterprise in 2364. Solis continued working the same position on the battle bridge. 

Reference: The Arsenal of Freedom 
Portrayed by George de la Pena.
