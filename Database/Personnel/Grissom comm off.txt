PERSONNEL: Mario Marcelino
Communications officer, U.S.S. Grissom

This man was on duty when the science vessel was studying the Genesis Planet. He maintained a subspace channel with the away team on the planet when a Klingon bird-of-prey decloaked and attacked their ship. He was killed when the ship was destroyed.

Reference: The Search for Spock
Portrayed by Mario Marcelino. The character's name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
