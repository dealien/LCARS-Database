PERSONNEL: Rashella

Aldean woman who, like the rest of her population, was rendered infertile by radiation. In 2364, several children from the U.S.S. Enterprise were abducted by the Aldeans and Rashella claimed one, Alexandra, as her own. The children were eventually rescued. 

Reference: When the Bough Breaks 
Portrayed by Brenda Strong.
