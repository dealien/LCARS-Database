PERSONNEL: Trefayne

A member of the tripartite Council of Elders on Organia in 2267. Trefayne reported escalating Klingon-Federation hostilities to Ayelborne. Trefayne, a noncorporeal lifeform, did not like being around violence. 

Reference: Errand of Mercy
Portrayed by David Hillary Hughes.
