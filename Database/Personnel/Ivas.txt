PERSONNEL: Ivas
Commander, executive officer, U.S.S. Cougar

This strong willed female was placed in command of coordinating fleet operations during the experiments being performed trying to create a stable wormhole. Ivas had to make sure that the researchers, headed by her good friend Lieutenant Jeff Corwin, had the facilities they needed, as well as the safety of the personnel and equipment involved.

Reference: Q Voyage
This character was based on the role played by Claudia Christian as Susan Ivanova on the sci-fi show Babylon 5.
