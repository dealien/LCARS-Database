PERSONNEL: Ssestar
Ambassador, diplomatic officer, Selay

Selay's chief representative. Ssestar and party were transported to Parliament on the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701-D in 2364 where they hoped to settle their differences with the Anticans. 

Reference: Lonely Among Us 
Portrayed by John Durbin.
