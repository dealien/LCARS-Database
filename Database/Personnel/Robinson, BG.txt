PERSONNEL: B.G. Robinson

Female crew member and transporter operator aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise-D. In 2365, Robinson met the dashing cargo vessel captain Thadiun Okona when he beamed aboard. 

Reference: The Outrageous Okona
