PERSONNEL: Stanley Kosinski
Specialist, engineering officer, Starfleet Command

Rude, self-important propulsion specialist working for Starfleet. His warp drive efficiency upgrade experiments brought him to the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701-D in 2364. Kosinki's ideas proved to be of minimal value. 

Reference: Where No One Has Gone Before 
Played by Stanley Kamel. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
