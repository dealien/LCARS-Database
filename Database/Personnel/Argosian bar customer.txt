PERSONNEL: Argosian bar patron

An unruly bar patron that attended the same bar as Benjamin Sisko and Curzon Dax. On one occasion the Argosian customer threw a drink is Sisko's face and Dax had to stop Sisko from retaliating. Sisko acquired a scar on his chin from a ring on Dax's finger.
Sisko and Jadzia Dax relived the story and he commented that he still had the scar.
