PERSONNEL: Miranda O`Brien
Planetary leader, Gaia

Elected leader of Gaia, a colony populated entirely by alternate-timeline descendants of the U.S.S. Defiant crew. Miles O'Brien was a direct ancestor of Miranda's, although not with Keiko, but Ensign Rita Tannebaum.
