PERSONNEL: Terence Epstein
PhD, cyberneticist, Earth

A leading 24th-century authority on cybernetics. Epstein lectured at Beverly Crusher's medical school, and she said she was looking forward to meeting him when the Enterprise-D docked at Starbase 74 in 2364.
