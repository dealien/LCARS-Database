PERSONNEL: Ravis

Male alien who encountered Hoshi Sato during her shore leave on Risa in 2152. Hoshi was immediately intrigued by Ravis' complex native language, and became even more interested when he asked her to join him in the exotic steam pools of Risa. 

Reference: Two Days and Two Nights
Portrayed by Rudolf Martin.
