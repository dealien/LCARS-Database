PERSONNEL: Judyann Ballard
Lieutenant, instructor, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

Crewmember aboard the Enterprise-D who taught the ship's primary school when Data enrolled his android "daughter" Lal into the class in 2366. 

Reference: The Offspring 
Played by Judyann Elder. The character's first name is conjectural based on the real name of the actor.
