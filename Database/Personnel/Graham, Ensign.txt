PERSONNEL: Mona Graham
Ensign, conn officer, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

Officer at the conn of the Enterprise-D during an attempt to save Lieutenant Hickman from the DNA he was infected with on Tarchannen III. 

Reference: Identity Crisis 
Played by Mona Grudt. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
