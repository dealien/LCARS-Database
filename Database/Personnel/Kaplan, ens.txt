PERSONNEL: Susan Kaplan
Ensign, helm officer, U.S.S. Voyager

Member of the U.S.S. Voyager crew. Kaplan piloted a shuttle returning her and Chakotay from the Nekrit Expanse when they encountered a ravaged planetoid. Kaplan was killed there by outlaw Ex-Borgs. 

Reference: Future's End
Portrayed by Susan Patterson. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
