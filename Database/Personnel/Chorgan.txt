PERSONNEL: Chorgan
sect leader, Gatherers, Acamar III

Acamarian leader of the Gatherers and member of the Lornak clan. In 2366, an attempt on his life was made by rival clan member Yuta. Following this event, a truce was later made between the Lornak and Trelesta clan members. 

Reference: The Vengeance Factor 
Played by Stephen Lee.
