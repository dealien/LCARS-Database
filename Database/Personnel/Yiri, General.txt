PERSONNEL: Yiri
General, planetary leader, Trelonia

Trelonian government leader who was persuaded by circumstantial evidence to execute his brother as a traitor. This historical incident was the subject of a lunchtime debate between Julian Bashir and Garak. 

Reference: Profit and Loss
