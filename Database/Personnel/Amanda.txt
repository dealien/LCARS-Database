PERSONNEL: Amanda Grayson

Wife to Ambassador Sarek and human wife to Spock. Amanda came aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise in 2267 with her husband who was attending the Babel Conference. Amanda helped Spock during his re-education process following his fal-tor-pan refusion in 2285 with his reborn self. She tried to help him get in touch with his human side and his feelings since his reeducation was being done in a Vulcan way.

Reference: Journey to Babel, The Voyage Home, The Final Frontier, Yesteryear
Played by Jane Wyatt with a special younger actor, Cynthia Blaise, used for "The Final Frontier." The character's last name is officially considered to be conjectural since it was not used in the live action series. But it was used in the animated series and many of the books.
