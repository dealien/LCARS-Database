PERSONNEL: Offenhouse, Ralph 
Dissatisfied businessman from late 20th Century Earth. Upon death, at 55, he was placed in a cryosatellite for later revival. In 2364 he was restored on the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701-D. 
Reference: The Neutral Zone
