PERSONNEL: David Bailey
Lieutenant, navigation officer, U.S.S. Enterprise

Junior navigator on the U.S.S. Enterprise during the early days of James Kirk's first mission. Although an inexperienced junior officer, Bailey was assigned by Kirk in 2266 to special duty as a cultural envoy to the flagship of the First Federation, the Fesarius.

Reference: The Corbomite Maneuver
Played by Anthony Hall. The Continuum spelled this character's name as Baily and Bailey, but the 2nd is supported by other sources.
