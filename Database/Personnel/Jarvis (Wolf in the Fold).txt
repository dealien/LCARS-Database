PERSONNEL: Jarvis
Prefect, planetary leader, Argelius II

A white-haired, slender gentleman serving as prefect of the peaceful planet of Argelius II in 2267. He oversaw Mr. Hengist's investigation into several murders including that of his own wife, Sybo. 

Reference: Wolf in the Fold 
Played by Charles Macauley, who also played the image of Landru in "Return of the Archons."
