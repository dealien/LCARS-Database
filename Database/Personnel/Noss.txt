PERSONNEL: Noss

Nomadic female of a previously unidentified species, encountered by Tuvok and Paris after their shuttle crashed in 2375. She helped them survive on the hostile planet, and she and Tuvok became romantically attached. Following their rescue, she was transported back to her homeworld by the U.S.S. Voyager. 

Reference: Gravity
Portrayed by Lori Petty.
