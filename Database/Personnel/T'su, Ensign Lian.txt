PERSONNEL: Lian T`su
Ensign, operations officer, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

Bridge officer serving aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise-D in 2364. T'su sat in the ops chair when Data joined an Away Team to Minos. The starship staff was investigating the disappearance of the U.S.S. Drake.

Reference: The Arsenal of Freedom 
Portrayed by Julia Nickson whom also played Cassandra in "Paradise" and Catherine Sakai on Babylon 5.
