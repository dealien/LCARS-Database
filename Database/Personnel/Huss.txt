PERSONNEL: Huss

A Klingon, the daughter of A'trom. Huss had the Order of the Bat'leth bestowed upon her in 2373, along with H'Ta, T'vis, and Jodomos (who was actually an undercover Ben Sisko). 

Reference: Apocalypse Rising
