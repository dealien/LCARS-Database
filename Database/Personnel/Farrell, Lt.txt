PERSONNEL: Dendrie Farrell
Lieutenant, engineering officer, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

She helped Geordi evacuate a group of children from the ship's drive section prior to an emergency saucer separation in 2371. 

Reference: Generations 
Played by Dendrie Taylor. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
