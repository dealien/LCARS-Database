PERSONNEL: Sarah Sisko

A woman possessed by the Bajoran Prophets to marry Joseph Sisko and give birth to Benjamin, the future Emissary. After Sarah was released by the Prophets, she left Joseph and later accidentally died. Sarah's image appeared to Benjamin Sisko as a Prophet, telling him not to wed Kasidy Yates.
