PERSONNEL: Korinas
Agent, Obsidian Order, Central Command

Female Cardassian from the Obsidian Order watchdog during Benjamin Sisko and Gul Dukat's joint search for the U.S.S. Defiant after it was stolen by Maquis operative Thomas Riker in 2371. 

Reference: Defiant 
Played by Tricia O'Neil, who also played Captain Rachel Garrett of the Enterprise-C in "Yesterday's Enterprise."
