PERSONNEL: Sutok

A shady character frequenting the Delta Quadrant's Nekrit Supply Depot. In 2373 Sutok opened fire after accepting a delivery of narcotics from Wixiban and Neelix, but was killed instead. 

Reference: Fair Trade
Portrayed by Steve Kehela.
