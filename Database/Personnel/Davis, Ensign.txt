PERSONNEL: Craig Davis
Ensign, engineering officer, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

Member of the Enterprise-D engineering staff. Davis was present when an antimatter containment failure occurred in Engineering. He was one of the last of the engineering crew to escape the matter/antimatter core area before the breach forced closure of the isolation doors, trapping fellow Ensign Keller and causing her death. 

Reference: Violations 
Played by Craig Benton. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
