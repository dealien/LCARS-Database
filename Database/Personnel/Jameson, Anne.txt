PERSONNEL: Anne Jameson

Wife to Admiral Mark Jameson from 2314 until his death in 2364, she watched her husband of 50 years regress in age due to the effects of a rejuvenation treatment he received on Cerebus II. 

TIMELINE:

2314: Married, Mark Jameson

Reference: Too Short a Season 
Played by Marsha Hunt.
