PERSONNEL: Broca
Legate, planetary leader, Cardassia Prime

A Cardassian promoted to Legate and the leader of the Cardassian Union after Damar vacated the post in 2375. Soon after, Broca was executed by the Jem'Hadar. 

Reference: The Dogs of War
