PERSONNEL: Nu`Daq
Captain, starship commander, I.K.S. Maht-H'a

In 2369, this Klingon captained the Maht-H'a attack cruiser. Nu'Daq vied against Gul Ocett and Picard for an ancient genetic encoded message. Nu'Daq destroyed life on Indri VIII during the quest. 

Reference: The Chase 
Portrayed by John Cotran. The Continuum credited this actor as John Cothran Jr, maybe by mistake.
