PERSONNEL: Jose Mendez
Commodore, station commander, Starbase 11

An illusion of Mendez acted as one of the three command officers who court-martialed Spock for kidnapping Captain Pike and communicating with Talos IV. 

Reference: The Menagerie
Played by Malachi Throne, who also provided the voice of the Talosian magistrate in "The Cage," and played Senator Pardek in "Unification."
