PERSONNEL: John Christopher
PhD, subspace theorist

Noted expert in Subspace theory. He and his wife, Dr T'Pan of the Vulcan Science Academy, attended Dr Reyga's metaphasic shield experiment aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise-D in 2369. 

Reference: Suspicions 
Played by John S. Ragin. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
