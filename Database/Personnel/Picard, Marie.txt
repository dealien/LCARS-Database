PERSONNEL: Marie Picard

Wife of Robert Picard and Jean-Luc Picard's sister-in-law. Although relations between Jean-Luc and his brother Robert were often strained, Marie corresponded with her brother-in-law to keep him abreast of family matters. Marie was widowed in 2371 when Robert was killed in a fire along with their young son René. 

Reference: Family, Generations
Portrayed by Samantha Eggar.
