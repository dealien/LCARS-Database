PERSONNEL: J.P. Hanson
Admiral, tactical officer, Starfleet Headquarters

Distinguished Starfleet officer killed in the line of duty at Wolf 359. Hanson led efforts to develop counter-Borg technology which wasn't ready when the Borg attacked for their first attempt. He was a friend of Jean-Luc Picard. 

Reference: The Best of Both Worlds
Played by George Murdock, who also played the false god image in "The Final Frontier."
