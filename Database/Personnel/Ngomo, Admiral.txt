PERSONNEL: Ngomo
Admiral, Starfleet Headquarters

San Franciso-based Starfleet officer who aided Kira Nerys in the search for Sisko, Jadzia and Bashir when a transporter malfunction rematerialized the trio in the year 2024, instead of 2371.
