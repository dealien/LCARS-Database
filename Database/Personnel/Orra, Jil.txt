PERSONNEL: Jil Orra

Gul Madred's little girl. In 2372 she visited Madred's torture chamber to discuss her pet wompat and ask about a bedtime story. Picard's presence made her wonder if humans had parents, too. 

Reference: Chain of Command
Portrayed by Heather Laren Olson.
