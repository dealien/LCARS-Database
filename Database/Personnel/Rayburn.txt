PERSONNEL: Budd Rayburn
Security officer, U.S.S. Enterprise

In 2266, Rayburn was killed by the android Ruk on Exo III. 

Reference: What Are Little Girls Made Of? 
Played by Budd Albright. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's name.
