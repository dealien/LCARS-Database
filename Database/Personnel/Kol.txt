PERSONNEL: Kol

Ferengi pilot who ended up stranded in the Delta Quadrant, but along with another Ferengi named Arridor managed to convince the Takarian people that they were the Great Sages, local mythological figures. When the U.S.S. Voyager arrived in 2373, they were forced to flee and disappeared into the Barzan Wormhole.
