PERSONNEL: Sara Carson
Ensign, medical officer, Deep Space 9

Starfleet officer assigned to Deep Space 9. In 2372, she was stationed on the bridge of the U.S.S. Defiant when the ship came under Jem'Hadar attack during a trade conference with the Karemma. 

Reference: Starship Down 
Played by Sara Mornell. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
