PERSONNEL: Hedril

Young Cairn female who accompanied her father Maques on a diplomatic visit to the U.S.S. Enterprise-D in 2370. Hedril's resemblance to Lwaxana Troi's deceased daughter Kestra caused Lwaxana to lapse into a trauma-induced coma. 

Reference: Dark Page 
Played by Kirsten Dunst.
