PERSONNEL: Tal
Subcommander, starship commander, R.S.E. Vindicator

Commanded a Romulan battle cruiser in 2268. Tal endeavored to follow his commanding officer's orders to destroy the U.S.S. Enterprise despite knowing she was captured and on its bridge. 

Reference: The Enterprise Incident 
Played by Jack Donner.
