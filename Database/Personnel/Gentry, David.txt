PERSONNEL: David Gentry
Ensign, security officer, Starfleet Headquarters

Human male identity adopted by one member of Species 8472 during their Starfleet simulation studies. When Gentry detained Tuvok and Chakotay for entering a restricted area, he was himself taken prisoner and then committed suicide.
