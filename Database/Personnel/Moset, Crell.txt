PERSONNEL: Crell Moset
Doctor, Cardassia

Cardassian surgeon, Exobiology Department chairman at the University of Chulat; considered the leading practitioner is his field. The U.S.S. Voyager's Doctor created a Medical Consultant hologram of Moset in 2375, then deleted the program after Moset's history of inhumane experiments on Bajoran prisoners was revealed.
