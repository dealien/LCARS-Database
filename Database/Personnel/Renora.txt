PERSONNEL: Renora
Judge, Deep Space Nine

Bajoran judge who presided over Jadzia Dax's extradition trial on Deep Space Nine in 2369 for the murder of General Ardelon Tandro of Klaestron IV, a crime allegedly committed by Curzon Dax. Renora was 100 years old and said that at her age, she didn't want to waste time with needless legal maneuvers. Renora dismissed the case after the victim's widow testified. 

Reference: Dax 
Played by Anne Haney.
