PERSONNEL: Vilix`pran
Lieutenant, Deep Space 9

Officer stationed on Deep Space 9 who was promoted from ensign in 2371 to lieutenant by 2373, during which time he had six offspring via budding, with two more buds due. Jake Sisko babysat on occasion. 

Reference: Apocalypse Rising
