PERSONNEL: Rugal

Young Cardassian boy who was abandoned and later raised by a Bajoran named Proka Migdal. In 2370, Rugal discovered that his biological father, Kotran Pa'Dar, mistakenly thought he was dead but now wished to reclaim him. 

Reference: Cardassians 
Played by Vidal Peterson whom also played D'Tan in "Unification."
