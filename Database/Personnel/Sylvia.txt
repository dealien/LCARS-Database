PERSONNEL: Sylvia Beecher

Melkotian illusion of a young woman living in 1881 Tombstone, Arizona. Sylvia loved gunslinger Billy Claiborne who, apparently, looked like Chekov. Chekov played along, not wanting to hurt Sylvia's feelings. 

Reference: Spectre of the Gun 
Played by Bonnie Beecher. The character's last name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
