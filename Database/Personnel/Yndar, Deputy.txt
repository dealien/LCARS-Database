PERSONNEL: Yndar
Deputy, security officer, Deep Space 9

A member of Odo's Deep Space Nine security force. Deputy Yndar was recommended by Odo for an undercover mission to Ty'Gokor in 2373. Sisko vetoed Yndar and took Odo instead. 

Reference: Apocalypse Rising
