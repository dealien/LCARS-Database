PERSONNEL: Whitney Alans
Science officer, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

Officer who assisted in a geological survey of planets in the Selcundi Drema sector in 2365. Alans was an expert in volcanology and geomechanics. 

Reference: Pen Pals 
Played by Whitney Rydbeck. The first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
