PERSONNEL: Jev

A handsome male Ullian telepathic historian whose career ended with arrest on the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701-D in 2368 for telepathic memory invasion rape. Troi and two other crewmembers were among his victims. 

Reference: Violations 
Played by Ben Lemon.
