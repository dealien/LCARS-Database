PERSONNEL: Avel Durken
Chancellor, planetary leader, Malcor III

Progressive leader of Malcorian society, forced to abandon plans for Federation contact by xenophobic political opponents in 2369. Durken was able to share a bottle of Chateau Picard with the Enterprise-D captain, Jean-Luc Picard when that starship made first contact with the planetary government.

Reference: First Contact 
Played by George Coe.
