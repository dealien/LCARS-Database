PERSONNEL: Ishan Chaye

Bajoran man, a Rakantha native. In 2367, Ishan and two others were convicted, falsely, of the attempted assassination of Dukat, and publicly executed. Six years later, morphogenic enzymes in Odo's brain caused Sisko to become Ishan in the Great Link.
