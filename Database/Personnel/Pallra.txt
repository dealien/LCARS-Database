PERSONNEL: Pallra

Wife of Cardassian collaborator Vaatrik who was murdered while they lived on Terok Nor. After the occupation, Pallra successfully blackmailed other Bajoran collaborators until Odo put her in jail. 

Reference: Necessary Evil 
Played by Katherine Moffat, who also played Etana Jol in "The Game."
