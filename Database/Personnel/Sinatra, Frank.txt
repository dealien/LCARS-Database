PERSONNEL: Frank Sinatra
Singer, Earth

20th-century Earth balladeer with a singing style similar to Vic Fontaine. Fontaine often talked about seeing Sinatra's shows in Las Vegas and then going gambling with him. 

TIMELINE:
1915: Born
1998: Died
