PERSONNEL: Theodore Sturgeon
alias, science fiction wirter, Earth

Pseudonym for Edward Hamilton Waldo, a science fiction writer who emphasized human relationships over hardware. Herbert Rossoff wanted to have his stories appear in the same magazines as Sturgeon. 

TIMELINE:
1918: Born
1985: Died
