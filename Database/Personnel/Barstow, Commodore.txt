PERSONNEL: Richard Barstow
Commodore, operations officer, Starfleet Command

Starfleet officer who in 2267 ordered the U.S.S. Enterprise to investigate a galaxy-wide time warp distortion and determine if the phenomenon was natural or a prelude to invasion by an unknown enemy. 

Reference: The Alternative Factor 
Played by Richard Derr. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
