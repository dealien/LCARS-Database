PERSONNEL: Augris

Third Magistrate in the Mokra Order, a repressive but advanced regime. Augris questioned members of the U.S.S. Voyager crew in 2372 and was later killed by Caylem. 

Reference: Resistance
Played by Alan Scarfe, whom aslo played Torath and others in Trek.
