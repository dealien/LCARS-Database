PERSONNEL: Narik

Chief engineer on Arctus Baran's ship in 2370. Narik was forced to serve because of a deadly neural servo device under Baran's control. Narik was later killed with a psionic resonator on Vulcan. 

Reference: Gambit
Portrayed by Cameron Thor.
