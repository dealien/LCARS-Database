PERSONNEL: Mirasta Yale
Minister, Chief of Science, Malcor III

Malcor III's Minister of Science who pushed her planet toward warp-drive capability in 2367. Yale left her homeworld on a Federation ship after fearful Malcorians retreated from first contact. 

Reference: First Contact 
Played by Carolyn Syemour who also played Subcommander Taris in "Contagion" and Commander Toreth in "Face of the Enemy," and Mrs. Templeton in Janeway's holonovels.
