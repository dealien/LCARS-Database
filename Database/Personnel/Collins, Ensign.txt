PERSONNEL: Harley Collins
Ensign, transporter operator, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

Technician serving aboard the Enterprise-D in 2368. Collins was on transporter duty when an away team beamed down to rendezvous with the Bajoran leader Orta. 

Reference: Ensign Ro 
Played by Harley Venton. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
