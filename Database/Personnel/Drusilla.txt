PERSONNEL: Drusilla

A female slace in the Roman Empire culture of planet 892-IV. In 2267, Captain James Kirk was granted a night of Drusilla's favors by Claudius Marcus, as a pre-execution courtesy. 

Reference: Bread and Circuses 
Played by Lois Jewell.
