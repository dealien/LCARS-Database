PERSONNEL: Overseer, The 
All-powerful supernatural being once central to spiritual life on Mintaka III. A 2366 accidental encounter between a Mintakan and Picard almost revived the ancient Overseer superstitions with Picard in the role. 
Reference: Who Watches The Watchers?
