PERSONNEL: B`tardat
Minister, Kaelon II

Minister of Science from planet Kaelon II. B'tardat instigated a hostile confrontation with the U.S.S. Enterprise-D in 2367 when the Federation ship granted asylum to Kaelonian scientist Dr. Timicin, who was to return to his planet for a ritual suicide madated by law for those who reach the age of 60. 

Reference: Half a Life
