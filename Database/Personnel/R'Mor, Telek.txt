PERSONNEL: Telek R'Mor

Romulan astrophysicist who, while on a deep-space mission in 2349, contacted the future Federation starship U.S.S. Voyager via a micro-wormhole. He received messages to convey to the crew's families when the year 2371arrived for him, but he died in 2367, four years prior to his rendezvous with the appropriate year. 

Reference: Eye of the Needle
Portrayed by Vaughn Armstrong, whom also played Captain Korris in "Heart of Glory," Gul Danar in "Past Prologue," a Klingon Captain in "SLeeping Dogs," and Admiral Forrestt throughout ENT.
