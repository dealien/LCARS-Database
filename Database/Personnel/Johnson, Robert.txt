PERSONNEL: Robert Johnson

Leader of a six-person scientific expedition to Gamma Hydra IV and husband to Elaine Johnson. He was the last member of the team to die of radiation-induced old age at 29. 

TIMELINE:
2238: Born
: Married, Elaine
: Assigned to Gamma Hydra IV
2267: Died
