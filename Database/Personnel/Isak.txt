PERSONNEL: Isak

Young man who met Kirk and Spock in prison on planet Ekos. A native of peaceful Zeon, he worked to overthrow Ekosian brutality by introduced Kirk and Spock to the underground. 

Reference: Patterns of Force 
Played by Richard Evans.
