PERSONNEL: Ornithar

Karemma male selling tulaberry wine to the Ferengi. In 2371 Quark threatened canceling their contract to force Ornithar into revealing the Founders' location. Ornithar also offered to buy Kira Nerys's earring. 

Reference: The Search
Played by John Fleck, who also played Taibak in "The Mind's Eye," and Abaddon in "Alice."
