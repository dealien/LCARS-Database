PERSONNEL: Joaquin
 
Eugenics Warrior who fled earth with Khan Noonien Singh and was rescued by James Kirk in 2267. Joaquin was exiled to Ceti Alpha V after trying to take Kirk's ship. 

Reference: Space Seed 
Played by Mark Tobin.
