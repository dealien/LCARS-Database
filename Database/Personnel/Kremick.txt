PERSONNEL: Kremick
Captain, starship commander, Klawhand

A K'Vin starship commander that was invited to the retirement party held on Kirlos and head of one of the fleets that engaged the Federation during the conflicts that existed between the two when they made first contact with each other.

Reference: The K'Vin Wars
