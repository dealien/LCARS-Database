PERSONNEL: Palor Toff

Friend of Kivas Fajo and fellow collector of rarities. Toff and Fajo had an ongoing competition to own the superior collection. Toff scoffed at Fajo's new motionless android. 

Reference: The Most Toys 
Portrayed by Nehemiah Persoff.
