PERSONNEL: Robert Morrow
Admiral, Commander in Chief, Starfleet Headquarters

After James Kirk defeated Khan, Morrow wanted to decommission the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701. Morrow also blocked Kirk's hopes of revisiting the Genesis Planet. 

Reference: The Search for Spock 
Played by Robert Hooks. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
