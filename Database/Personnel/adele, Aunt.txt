PERSONNEL: Adele Picard

Relative of Jean-Luc Picard, who taught him various folk treatments for minor ailments, such as steamed milk with nutmeg for insomnia, and ginger tea for the common cold. 

Reference: Cause and Effect, Ensign Ro, Schisms
Named for assistant director Adele Simmons. The last name is conjectural based on the family connection.
