PERSONNEL: Talak`talan
Third

The first Jem'Hadar to interact with an Alpha Quadrant species. Talak'talan was disappointed the encounter did not include a Klingon. Talak'talan led the soldiers who captured Sisko and Quark and was the soldier to personally deliver the news to station Deep Space Nine. He also reported the destruction of New Bajor and several Federation ships. 

Reference: The Jem'Hadar 
Portrayed by Cress Williams.
