PERSONNEL: Quarren

Curator and founder of the Museum of Kyrian Heritage. In 3074, Quarren discovered the backup module of the U.S.S. Voyager EMH. This discovery led to some historical revisions in the history between the Kyrians and the Vaskans. 

Reference: Living Witness
Portrayed by Henry Woronicz whom also played J'Dan in "The Drumhead."
