PERSONNEL: Borum

Bajoran man imprisoned at the Hutet Labor Camp who engineered the escape of fellow prisoner and Bajoran hero, Li Nalas. Borum sacrificed himself so that Li Nalas, Kira, and O'Brien could escape. 

Reference: The Homecoming 
Played by Michael Bell. This character's name was never mentioned on air but was taken from the script.
