PERSONNEL: Thadial Bokar
Agent, Orion Syndicate, Sakurro System

Representative of the Orion Syndicate masquerading as a commodities broker. 
In 2375 Bokar used threats and sabotage to increase the Syndicate's hold on the Tigan Family Mines.
