PERSONNEL: Velal
Sub-commander 

Romulan officer who replaced Senator Cretak as Romulan liaison at Deep Space 9. Velal agreed to the all-out assault on Dominion strongholds that ultimately ended the Federation-Dominion war. 

Reference: Inter Arma Enim Silent Leges
