PERSONNEL: Krax

Grand Nagus Zek's son. Zek tested Krax's character by appearing to die in 2369 after naming Quark the next Grand Nagus. Krax passed. 

Reference: The Nagus 
Played by Lou Wagner, who also played DaiMon Solok in "Chain of Command."
