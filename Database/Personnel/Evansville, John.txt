PERSONNEL: John Evansville

Twenty-fourth century inhabitant of the human settlement on the former Briori homeworld in the Delta Quadrant. Evansville's civilization numbered more than 100,000, the descendants of 300 persons abducted from Earth in 1937 by the Briori. Evansville invited the Voyager crew to stay and live with his people on their world, but they declined his offer. 

Reference: The 37's
