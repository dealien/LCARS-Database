PERSONNEL: Krim
General, fleet commander, Bajor

A Bajoran commander and member of the Circle. He commanded the capture of DS9 from Federation control. Krim stepped down once he learned the Cardassians were the Circle's main backers. 

Reference: The Circle 
Played by Stephen Macht, who was in the final running when auditioning for the part of Jean-Luc Picard.
