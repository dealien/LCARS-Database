PERSONNEL: Edward M. La Forge
PhD, exozoology

Father of Geordi La Forge and Ariana La Forge, and husband of Silva La Forge. Geordi talked to him via subspace after the disappearance of the U.S.S. Hera. 

FAMILY:
Wife: Silva La Forge
Son: Geordi La Forge
Daughter: Ariana La Forge

Reference: Interface, Cause and Effect
Played by Ben Vereen. This character's name was suggested by Rick Berman and seen on Geordi's personnel file display during "Cause and Effect."
