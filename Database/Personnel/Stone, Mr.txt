PERSONNEL: Stone

Publisher of Incredible Tales, a science fiction magazine, during the 1950's. Mr. Stone wanted editor Douglas Pabst to include photos of all their writers except K.C. Hunter and Benny Russell. 

Reference: Far Beyond the Stars
