PERSONNEL: Tess Allenby
Ensign, conn officer, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

Flight Control officer who piloted the Enterprise-D during the mission at Gamelan V in 2367. Ensign Allenby was also at the conn when the ship encountered a school of two-dimensional creatures while en route to planet T'lli Beta. 

Reference: Final Mission, The Loss
Played by Mary Kohnert.
