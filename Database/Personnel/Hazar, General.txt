PERSONNEL: Hazar
General, Bajoran Militia

In 2370, Hazar perceived a Skrreean colony ship to be a threat to Bajor, so he ordered it fired upon. When it was learned the craft carried only a child, he revoked his order, but the ship was accidentally destroyed anyway. 

Reference: Sanctuary 
Played by Robert Curtis-Brown.
