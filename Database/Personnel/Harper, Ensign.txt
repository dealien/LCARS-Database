PERSONNEL: Sean Harper
Ensign, engineering officer, U.S.S. Enterprise

A member of the U.S.S. Enterprise crew who participated in Dr. Daystrom's M5 computer field tests in 2268. When the multitronic unit needed more power, Harper was standing in the wrong place at the wrong time and was vaporized by an energy beam. 

Reference: The Ultimate Computer 
Played by Sean Morgan. The character's first name on conjectural based on the actor's real name.
