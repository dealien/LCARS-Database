PERSONNEL: Lal

A Vian scientist. One of the Vians who tested Gem to see if her people were worthy of being rescued from an impending nova in 2268. 

Reference: The Empath
Portrayed by Alan Bergmann. The name Lal is from the episode script only and was not actually spoken in the aired episode.
