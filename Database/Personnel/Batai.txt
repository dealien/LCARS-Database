PERSONNEL: Batai
Councillor, City leader, Ressik, Kataan

Native of planet Kataan and council leader of the Ressik community. His good friend Kamin named his son after Batai. Jean-Luc Picard "knew" Batai when the captain experienced Kamin's entire life as part of the Kataan attempt to leave a record of their doomed civilization. 

Reference: The Inner Light 
Played by Richard Riele.
