PERSONNEL: Tango

A horse that was a pet to young Christopher Pike when he lived in Mojave, California, Earth. He was fond of sugar cubes and enjoyed them after rides with Pike. 
The Talosians recreated the illusion of Tango during their conditioning of Captain Pike after he had been abducted from this team.

Reference: The Cage, The Menagerie
