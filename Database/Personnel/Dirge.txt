PERSONNEL: Dirge
Cadet 2nd class, security division, Starfleet Academy

A Selay citizen and one of the first of his kind to attend the Starfleet Academy. This cadet was camping with friends from the Presidio in Yosemite Park, California, Earth when he and his colleagues were taken hostage by a radical faction representing the Earth Alliance. The group demanded recognition and negotiating power toward efforts to cede Earth from the United Federation of Planets. Dirge and his associates were targeted since they were aliens.
