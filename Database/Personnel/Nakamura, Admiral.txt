PERSONNEL: Nakamura
Admiral, 

Starfleet senior officer stationed at Starbase 173 in 2365 and Starbase 219 in 2370. He agreed with plans to disassemble Data and was not happy that Picard missed the Admiral's Banquet. 

Reference: The Measure of a Man
