PERSONNEL: Rusot 
Gul, resistance leader, Cardassia Prime

Cardassian officer who allied himself with Legate Demar during his revolt against the Dominion's occupation of Cardassia in 2375. 

Reference: The Changing Face of Evil
Portrayed by John Vickery, whom also played Andrus Hagen in "Night Terror."
