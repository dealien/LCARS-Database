PERSONNEL: Rodriguez

Genetically enhanced human and loyal follower of Khan Noonien Singh who fought with him during Earth's Eugenics wars of 1996. He was also aboard the S.S. Botany Bay when it was recovered by the U.S.S. Enterprise in 2267. He had expereince with communications equipment.
