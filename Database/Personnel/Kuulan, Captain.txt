PERSONNEL: Kuulan

Captain of the Kantare vessel that crash-landed far from the Kantare home planet, thanks to an ion storm. Kuulan died in the crash, but survivor Ezral created holo versions of Kuulan and the rest of the crew in order to keep the only other survivor, Ezral's daughter, company. 

Reference: Oasis
Portrayed by Rudolph Willrich, whom also played the Academy Commandant in "Broken Link" and "Paradise Lost" as well as Reittan Grax in "Menage a Troi."
