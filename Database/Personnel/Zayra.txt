PERSONNEL: Zayra

Bajoran manager of the Tourist Aid center on Deep Space Nine. In 2369, Zayra fomented anti-Odo hysteria when it seemed as though Odo had murdered another Bajoran on the station. 

Reference: A Man Alone 
Played by Edward Laurance Albert.
