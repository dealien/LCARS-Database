PERSONNEL: Temo`Zuma

Jem'Hadar engineer hiding out on Bopak IIII with rebel Goran'Agar in 2372. Temo'Zuma installed a dampening field around their ship's engine after its tell-tale magnetron pulse signaled their location. 

Reference: Hippocratic Oath 
Played by Marshall Teague. The character's name was not mentioned on air, but is in the script.
