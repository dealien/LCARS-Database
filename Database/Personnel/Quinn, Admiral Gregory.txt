PERSONNEL: Gregory Quinn
Admiral, senior operations officer, Starfleet Headquarters

Starfleet officer who, in 2364, alerted Captain Jean-Luc Picard to what he felt was a hostile takeover of Starfleet Command by outside forces. Quinn was later taken over by these unknown aliens. 

Reference: Coming of Age, Conspiracy 
Portrayed by Ward Costello.
