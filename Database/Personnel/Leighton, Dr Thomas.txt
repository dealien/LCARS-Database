PERSONNEL: Thomas Leighton 
PhD.Science officer, Planet Q

A researcher living on Planet Q with his wife, Martha. Leighton's face was scarred during the massacre on Tarsus IV in 2246. In 2266 Leighton was killed by Lenore Karidian, fearing he might identify her father as the massacre's architect, Kodos the Executioner. 

Reference: The Conscience of the King 
Played by William Sargent.
