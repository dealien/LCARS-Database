PERSONNEL: Keevan

Vorta who betrayed his Jem'Hadar soldiers to Sisko and his crew, and then surrendered as a prisoner of war in order to be rescued from a desolate planet. Quark and his Ferengi cadre later traded a dead but artificially-animated Keevan for Ishka. 

Reference: Rocks & Shoals, The Magnificent Ferengi
