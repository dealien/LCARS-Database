PERSONNEL: Ocett
Gul, starship commander

Female Cardassian commander, in 2369, of a Galor-class warship. Ocett's need to solve a four-billion-year-old genetic puzzle forced a brief cooperation with Picard and Nu'Daq that ended when Ocett opened fire. 

Reference: The Chase 
Portrayed y Linda Thorson.
