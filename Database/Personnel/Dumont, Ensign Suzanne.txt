PERSONNEL: Suzanne Dumont
Ensign, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

Starfleet enlisted officer. She accompanied Ensign Wesley Crusher on a date that took them into the Enterprise arboretum instead of the Mozart concert in honor of Ambassador Sarek.
