PERSONNEL: Charlie
security guard, Fontaine Lounge casino holodeck

A holographic character in the Vic Fontaine holo-program. Charlie was a guard in Zeemo's casino. He intended to take Miles O'Brien into custody for stealing Kasidy Yates's money. 

Reference: Badda-Bing Badda-Bang 
The Continuum had no image under his own entry, but was pictured as "Tony Cicci" also from the episode- another of the mistakes on this website.
