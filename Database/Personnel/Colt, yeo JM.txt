PERSONNEL: J.M. Colt
Yeoman, captain's assistant, U.S.S. Enterprise

Officer aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise under the command of Captain Christopher Pike. After their mission to planet Talos IV, Colt wondered who Pike would have chosen to become his "Eve."

Reference: The Cage, The Menagerie
Played by Laurel Goodwin.
