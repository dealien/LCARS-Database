PERSONNEL: Miri

Several hundred year old girl entering puberty when the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701 discovered her plague world in 2266. Miri's crush on Kirk nearly doomed the landing party. 

Reference: Miri 
Played by Kim Darby.
