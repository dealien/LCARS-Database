PERSONNEL: Dara

Daughter of noted Kaelon scientist, Timicin, and the mother of his only grandson. When Dara's elder father was aboard the Enterprise-D in 2367, she visited and implored him to return to Kaelon II and carry out the Resolution ritual, which would end his life in the traditional way. 

Reference: Half a Life
Portrayed by Michelle Forbes whom also played Ro Laren and was groomed to the the executive officer of Deep Space Nine.
