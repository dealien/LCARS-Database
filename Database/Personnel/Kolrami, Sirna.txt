PERSONNEL: Sirna Kolrami

Zakdorn master strategist who served as tactical consultant aboard the Enterprise-D during a Starfleet battle simulation exercise in 2365. Kolrami was a third-level grand master at the game of Strategema and prided himself in never losing. The arrogant Zakdorn was humilated when the android Data forced him into a stalemate. 

Reference: Peak Performance 
Played by Roy Brocksmith whom also played Razka Karn in "Indiscretion."
