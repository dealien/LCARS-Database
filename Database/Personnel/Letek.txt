PERSONNEL: Letek

Ferengi with a whip. William Riker encountered Letek on planet Delphi Ardu. Letek commanded a team of Ferengi bandits investigating the planet's draining effect on their power sources. Letek's superior was DaiMon Taar. 

Reference: The Last Outpost 
Played by Armin Shimerman, who also played Bractor in "Peak Performance," and Quark throughout Deep Space 9, and the betazoid gift box in "Haven."
