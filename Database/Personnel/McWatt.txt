PERSONNEL: McWatt

Starfleet officer and Benjamin Sisko's contemporary. At one time both were assigned to the U.S.S. Okinawa. McWatt was repositioned within Starfleet in 2372 to defend Earth against the Dominion. 

Reference: Paradise Lost
