PERSONNEL: Keena

Female Bajoran living on Jeraddo with Baltrim and Mullibok. The Provisional Government ordered their evacuation, enforced by Kira Nerys, because Jerrado was to become a new energy source for Bajor. 

Reference: Progress 
Played by Annie O'Donnell.
