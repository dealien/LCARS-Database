PERSONNEL: Michael Williams

A fictional character in the play Henry V by William Shakespeare. He was one of the citizens that the King encountered while the King was in disguise. Lieutenant Commander Data participated in a holodeck recreation of the play- with him in the title role. In the recreation he looked like Captain Jean-Luc Picard even while the same captain witnessed Data's portrayal.

Reference: The Defector
Portrayed by Patrick Stewart, Jean-Luc Picard.
