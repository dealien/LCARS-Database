PERSONNEL: Hagan Hanson
Crewman, helmsman, U.S.S. Enterprise

Sandy-haired helmsman on the U.S.S. Enterprise in 2267. Mr. Hanson was part of a skeleton crew that remained aboard ship when Captain Kirk's court martial re-convened on the bridge. 

Reference: Court Martial 
Played by Hagan Beggs. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
