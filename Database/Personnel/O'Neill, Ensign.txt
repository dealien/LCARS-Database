PERSONNEL: O`Neill
Ensign, U.S.S. Enterprise

A member of Kelowitz's search team on Taurus II. O'Neill died from a spear through the chest during a surprise attack by the large furry humanoids living on the planet. 

Reference: The Galileo Seven
