PERSONNEL: Bronowsky
Ensign, science officer, U.S.S. Voyager

Crewmember working in U.S.S. Voyager's Airponics Bay. Bronowsky's unblemished work record and accordion skills put him on Seven's dating candidate list. Harry Kim thought Bronowsky cheerless and musically weak.
