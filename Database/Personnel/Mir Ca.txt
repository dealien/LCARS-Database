PERSONNEL: Mir Ca
Lieutenant, operations officer, U.S.S. Sutherland

The primary relief officer for the operations station when the craft is engaged in service and not being operated by cadets. This officer also serves other relief positions aboard the bridge of the ship as well as on a number of away missions. She is one of the ship's junior officers.
