PERSONNEL: Three of Nine

One of the former Borg drones who contacted Seven of Nine, seeking to become an individual. Prior to assimilation, she was Marika Wilkarah, an engineer aboard the starship Excalibur. 

Reference: Survival Instinct
Portrayed by Bertila Damas.
