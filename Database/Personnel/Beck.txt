PERSONNEL: Don Beck
Nurse, obstetrics specialist, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

He came aboard the Enterprise-D in 2369 from Starbase 218 to serve as a nurse under Dr. Beverly Crusher. 

Reference: Lessons 
Named for Don Beck, who produced promotional preview commercials for Next Generation and Deep Space Nine. The character's first name is conjectural based on the producer's real name.
