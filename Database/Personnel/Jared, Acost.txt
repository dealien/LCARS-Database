PERSONNEL: Acost Jared
Planetary leader, Ventax II

Male humanoid leader of Ventax II in 2367 who was willing to give his people and world to Ardra, their devil, as payment for one thousand years of peace and plenty. 

Reference: Devil's Due 
Played by Marcello Tubert.
