PERSONNEL: Worf
Colonel, attorney, Qo'noS

A Klingon warrior given the job of defending James Kirk and Leonard McCoy against charges of assassinating Chancellor Gorkon in 2293. 
He was the great grandfather to Commander Worf, grandfather to Mogh.

Reference: The Undiscovered Country 
Played by Michael Dorn, who also played Worf in Next Generation and the last four seasons of Deep Space 9.
