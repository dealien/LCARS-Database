PERSONNEL: Etana Jol

Attractive Ktarian female who tricked Commander William Riker into bringing an addictive, mind-controlling game aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise-D so she could commandeer the ship. She almost succeeded. She met the commander while he was vacationing on Risa.

Reference: The Game 
Played by Katherine Moffat.
