PERSONNEL: Kipp

A Delta Quadrant merchant from a planetoid called Velos who had dealings with a sleazy Dralian trader named Gar. Kipp let Gar convince him to provide 3000 induction units on consignment, and then he never heard from Gar again. The U.S.S. Voyager contacted Kipp in their own attempts to track down Gar and recover their stolen EMH program. 

Reference: Critical Care
Portrayed by John Franklin.
