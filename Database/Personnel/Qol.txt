PERSONNEL: Qol
 
One of two Ferengi who tricked their way on board the U.S.S. Enterprise in 2368 in an attempt to talk business with the Kriosian Ambassador Briam. Qol worked for Par Lanor at the time. 

Reference: The Perfect Mate 
Portrayed by Michael Synder whom also played Dax in "The Undiscovered Country" and Morta in "Rascals."
