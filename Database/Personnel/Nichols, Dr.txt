PERSONNEL: Alex Nichols
PhD.Plant manager, engineer, California, Earth

As Plexicorp's plant manager, Nichols gave Dr. McCoy and Montgomery Scott a tour and then fabricated transparent aluminum to Scott's specifications. Two duo from the future supplied the man with the molecular structure for the clear, strong metal accepting the fact that he was going to invent it anyway.

Reference: The Voyage Home 
Played by Alex Henteloff. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
