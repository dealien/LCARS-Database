PERSONNEL: Zora

Woman whose named became synonymous with evil when her monstrous experiments on the people of Tiburon became known. Zora was a member of the evil team created by Yarnek of Excalbia. 

Reference: The Savage Curtain 
Played by Carol Daniels Derment.
