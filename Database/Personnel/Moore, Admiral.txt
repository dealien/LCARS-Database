PERSONNEL: Moore
Admiral

In 2365 Starfleet's Admiral Moore met with Captain Picard to discuss a distress signal from the Ficus Sector that Starbase personnel had traced to the European Hegemony of Earth. 

Reference: Up The Long Ladder
