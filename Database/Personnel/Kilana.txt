PERSONNEL: Kilana

Vorta agent who led a squad of Jem'Hadar attempting to rescue a mortally injured Founder from a downed warship on Torga IV. She allowed Sisko to salvage the ship while she retrieved the Founder's remains. 

Reference: The Ship 
Portrayed by Kaitlin Hopkins.
