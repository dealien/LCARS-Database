PERSONNEL: Jackson Roykirk

Pioneering but erratic scientist who built a machine-intelligent space probe called Nomad in the 21st century. The probe was later discovered by the crew of the U.S.S. Enteprise, although it had altered considerably since its original programming. 

Reference: The Changeling
