PERSONNEL: Jaglom Shrek

Yridian male who thought Worf would pay him for information about a Romulan prison camp populated by Klingon survivors of the Khitomer massacre. Shrek believed Mogh to be among them. 

Reference: Birthright
Portrayed by James Cromwell, whom also played Hanok in "The Hunted" and Zefram Cochrane in "Star Trek: First Contact."
