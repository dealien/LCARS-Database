PERSONNEL: Lidell Ren

Banean woman who accused U.S.S. Voyager crew member Tom Paris of killing her husband, Tolen. Following an investigation by Tuvok, Lidell was found to be in collaboration with the Numiri. 

Reference: Ex Post Facto
Portrayed by Robin McKee.
