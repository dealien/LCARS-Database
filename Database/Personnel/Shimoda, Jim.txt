PERSONNEL: Jim Shimoda
Engineering officer, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

Assistant chief engineer aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701-D, reporting to Sarah MacDougal, in 2364. Influenced by the Psi 2000 virus, Shimoda pulled dozens of isolinear optical chips from their sockets, disabling the ship. 

Reference: The Naked Now 
Portrayed by Benjamin W.S. Lum.
