PERSONNEL: Korena Sisko
artist, Louisiana, Earth

A Bajoran artist and Jake Sisko's wife in an alternate time-line in which Benjamin Sisko was thought killed in 2372. Korena and Jake's marriage was destroyed by Jake's obsession with his father. 

Reference: The Visitor 
Played by Galyn Gorg whom also played Nori in "Warlord."
