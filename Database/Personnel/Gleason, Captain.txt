PERSONNEL: Gleason
Captain, starship commander, U.S.S. Zhukov

Commanding officer of the U.S.S. Zhukov. Captain Gleason recorded satisfactory reports for Lt. Reginald Barclay and spoke very highly of his performance on the Zhukov, just prior to Barclay's transfer to the Enterprise-D in 2366.
