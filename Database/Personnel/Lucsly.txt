PERSONNEL: James Lucsly
Agent, Temporal Investigations, Earth

One of the Department of Temporal Investigations investigators assigned to debrief Sisko after the U.S.S. Defiant's voyage to 2267. Lucsly took notes and had a basic knowledge of Bajorans. 

Reference: Trials and Tribble-ations 
Played by James W. Jansen. This character's name is an anagram of Scully, the name of the FBI agent from the popular show "The X Files." The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name. This actor also played Faren Kag in "The Storyteller."
