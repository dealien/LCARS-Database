PERSONNEL: Kubus Oak

Bajoran banished to Cardassia for facilitating relations with the Cardassians during the occupation. In 2370 Kubus tried to sneak back to Bajor, via DS9, but was quickly recognized on the station. 

Reference: The Collaborator 
Played by Bert Remsen.
