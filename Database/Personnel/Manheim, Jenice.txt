PERSONNEL: Jenice Manheim

Once involved in a relationship with Picard that ended abruptly. Married Dr. Paul Manheim and was living with him on Vandor IV when a disaster summoned Picard's aid in 2364. 

Reference: We'll Always Have Paris 
Portrayed by Michelle Phillips.
