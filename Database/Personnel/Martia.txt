PERSONNEL: Martia

A shape-shifting Chameloid, was an inmate at the Klingon asteroid prison mine Rura Penthe at the time Starfleet officers Captain James Kirk and Commander Dr. McCoy were imprisoned there for the murder of Klingon Chancellor Gorkon. Martia was killed during an unsuccessful ploy to eliminate Kirk.

Reference: The Undiscovered Country
