PERSONNEL: Reyga
Ph.D, scientist 

Ferengi who developed metaphasic shielding. In 2369, Dr. Reyga was murdered aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise by a jealous scientist who wanted to lay claim to the new shielding technique. 

Reference: Suspicions 
Portrayed by Peter Slutsker.
