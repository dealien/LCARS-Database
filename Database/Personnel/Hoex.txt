PERSONNEL: Hoex

Ferengi entrepreneur who attended a high-level Ferengi business conference on Deep Space 9 organized by Grand Nagus Zek in 2369. Turot could not be seated next to Hoex because Hoex had acquired Turot's controlling interest in a cargo port on Volchok Prime.
