PERSONNEL: T`Kar
mercenary

Klingon-for-hire providing the muscle for Verad's plot to kidnap and join with the Dax symbiont. T'Kar did not respect the contemporary Klingon government nor its alliance with the Federation. 

Reference: Invasive Procedures 
Played by Tim Russ, who also played Devor in "Starship Mine," a bridge officer in "Generations," and Tuvok throughout VOY.
