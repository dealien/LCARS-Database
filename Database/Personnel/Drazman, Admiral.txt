PERSONNEL: Drazman
Admiral, station commander, Proxima Maintenance Yards

Commander of the Proxima Maintenance Yards, nicknamed 'Droner' for his dull personality. Sisko, Dax and Bashir were heading to a formal dinner with Drazman when a transporter malfunction landed them in 2024 San Francisco.
