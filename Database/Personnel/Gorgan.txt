PERSONNEL: Gorgan

Also known as the Friendly Angel. Noncorporeal life form that forced the adults from the Starnes Expedition to planet Triacus to commit suicide and deceived their children into following him and doing his evil bidding. The children could summon the Gorgan by chanting, "Hail, hail, fire and snow. Call the angel, we will go. Far away, for to see, Friendly Angel come to me."

Reference: And the Children Shall Lead
Played by Melvin Belli, whose son was one of the children in the episode.
