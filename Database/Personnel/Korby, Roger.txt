PERSONNEL: Roger Korby, PhD.
Archaeologist, Exo III

Renowned medical archeologist, authority on ancient android civilizations, and fiancé of Christine Chapel, whose corporeal form expired in 2266. Korby's consciousness survived for a time in an android body. 

Reference: What Are Little Girls Made Of? 
Played by Michael Strong.
