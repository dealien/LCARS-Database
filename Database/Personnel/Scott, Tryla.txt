PERSONNEL: Tryla Scott
Captain, starship commander

Scott was in fact the youngest to become a Starfleet captain and was considered a promising officer. She fell victim to the parasitic lifeform that mysteriously attacked other officers and almost succeeded in taking control of Starfleet. 

Reference: Conspiracy 
Portrayed by Ursaline Bryant.
