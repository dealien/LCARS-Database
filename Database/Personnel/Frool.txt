PERSONNEL: Frool

Ferengi waiter in Quark's bar. Frool was persuaded to join a labor union in part by Rom's suggestion that he deserved a day off to rest his chronically sore back. 

Reference: Bar Association 
Played by Emilio Borelli. He is pictured near the far left, the one apparently speaking.
