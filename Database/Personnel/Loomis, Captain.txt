PERSONNEL: Loomis
Captain, starbase commander, AR-558

Initial ranking officer of the Starfleet contingent besieged at Starbase AR-558 by Dominion forces. Loomis was killed in battle, and command was assumed first by Parker, and then Nadia Larkin. 

Reference: The Siege of AR-558
Portrayed by Leland Orser, whom previouly played Colonel Lovok in "The Die Is Cast" and "Improbable Cause."
