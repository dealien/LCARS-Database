PERSONNEL: Tiron

One of Quark's favorite customers. Tiron paid Quark for a holosuite program of Kira. When Kira discovered Quark's scheme she made significant changes to the program that assured Tiron would never return. 

Reference: Meridian 
Played by Jeffrey Combs, who also played Weyoun and Brunt throughout DS9, Krem in "Acquisition," Shran throughout ENT, Penk in "Tsunkatse," and Kevin Mulkahey in "Far Beyond the Stars."
