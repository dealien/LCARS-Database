PERSONNEL: Taxco
Ambassador, diplomatic officer, Arbazan

Taxco came to Deep Space 9 to learn more about the wormhole in 2369. She did little more than complain and berate until Dr. Bashir saved her life. 

Reference: The Forsaken 
Played by Constance Towers.
