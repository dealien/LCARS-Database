PERSONNEL: Ishara Yar

Turkana IV native born in 2342. After being orphaned, Ishara was raised by older sister Natasha. As an adult Ishara became a Coalition cadre fighter. 

Reference: Legacy 
Played by Beth Toussaint. Beth also played Anna Sheridan during the first apparance of the character in the Babylon 5 series.
