PERSONNEL: Ty Kajada
Officer, security officer, Kobliad Police

Kobliad security official who pursued Rao Vantika for two decades before capturing him. A spacecraft accident landed them at Deep Space 9, where Vantika was pronounced dead. A series of strange events aroused suspicions within Kajada that Vantika was still alive and she learned that he had transferred his consciousness into Dr. Julian Bashir. Kajada eventually destroyed Vantika's consciousness after it was removed from Julian Bashir by breaking the containment cell that his consciousness was transferred into.

Reference: The Passenger 
Played by Caitlin Brown.
