PERSONNEL: Losira

The final living Kalandan on an outpost far from home. Dead for 10,000 years in 2268, Losira's image was the basis for a deadly guardian still protecting the ghostly station from invaders. 

Reference: That Which Survives 
Played by Lee Meriwether.
