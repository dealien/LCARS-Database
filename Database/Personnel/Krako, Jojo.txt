PERSONNEL: Jojo Krako
Sect leader, Sigma Iotia II

Sigma Iotia II's second most powerful boss. He controlled the south side territory. In 2268 his men kidnapped Kirk, Spock and McCoy from rival Bela Oxmyx's control. 

Reference: A Piece of the Action 
Played by Victor Tayback.
