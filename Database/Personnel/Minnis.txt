PERSONNEL: Minnis
First Maje, Sect Leader, Kazon-Pommar

Leader, or First Maje, of the Delta Quadrant humanoids known as the Kazon-Pommar. Minnis attended Janeway's peace meeting in 2372 that turned out to be a trap.
