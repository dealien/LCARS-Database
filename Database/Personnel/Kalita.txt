PERSONNEL: Kalita

Maquis member on planet Ronara. She was initially suspicious of Ro Laren's motives for joining their her cell, but became convinced of Ro's sincerity following a successful raid on the U.S.S. Enterprise-D. Kalita served at the U.S.S. Defiant's conn when Thomas Riker commandeered the starship in 2371 and attempted to attack some secret shipyards within the Orias System of the Cardassian Union.

Reference: Preemptive Strike, Defiant
Played by Shannon Cochran.
