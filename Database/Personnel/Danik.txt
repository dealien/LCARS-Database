PERSONNEL: Danik

Danik, a Suliban male imprisoned in Tandaran Dentention Complex 26 with his daughter, Narra. His grandfather settled on Tandar Prime, and Danik was the Director of Research for an engineering institute in Querella Province before being imprisoned. Captain Jonathan Archer convinced Danik and his fellow Suliban prisoners to escape from the Detention Complex.
