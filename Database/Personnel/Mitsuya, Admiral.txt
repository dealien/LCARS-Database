PERSONNEL: Mitsuya
Admiral, Tactical Officer, Starfleet Headquarters

Starfleet officer, policy-maker and poker player. In 2370 the U.S.S. Crocket ferried Admiral Mitsuya to DS9 for a Cardassian strategy conference with Benjamin Sisko.
