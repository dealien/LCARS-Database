PERSONNEL: Kovat
court conservator, Cardassia Prime

Nervous Cardassian servant of the court who's specialty was crafting guilty pleas. Kovat was horrified when his client, Miles O'Brien, was found innocent of arming the Maquis. 

Reference: Tribunal 
Played by Fritz Weaver.
