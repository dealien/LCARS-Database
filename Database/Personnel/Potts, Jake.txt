PERSONNEL: Jake Potts

Elder brother to Willie Potts. who were stationed aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise by special arrangement. In 2367, Jake nearly killed his younger brother in a prank. The two were playing a laser duel game on Ogus II when Jake pretended to be hurt by the game. Willie ran and hid from shame into a forest where he found and ate a deadly cove palm fruit. His brother was rushed back to the starship where his condition was stabilized until they could be taken to Starbase 416 for treatment.
