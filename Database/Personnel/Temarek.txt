PERSONNEL: Temarek

Male member of Acamar III's Gatherer faction attending negotiations to repatriate his people. Temarek was bullied into drinking wine his leader, Brull, suspected to be poisoned. The wine was okay. 

Reference: The Vengeance Factor 
Portrayed by Elkanah Burns.
