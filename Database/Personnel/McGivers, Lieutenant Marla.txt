PERSONNEL: Marla McGivers
Lieutenant, historian, U.S.S. Enterprise

She was assigned to the team that beamed over to the S.S. Botany Bay when it was discovered in 2267. She joined ex-dictator Khan Noonien Singh to commandeer the ship. McGivers was sentenced to Ceti Alpha V where a native parasite claimed her life. 

Reference: Space Seed 
Played by Madlyn Rhue.
