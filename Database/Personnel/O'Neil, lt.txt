PERSONNEL: Sean O`Neil
Lieutenant, U.S.S. Enterprise

O'Neil investigated a missing starship crew on Beta III and was captured and absorbed. Later he betrayed his shipmates to Landru's lawgivers. 

Reference: The Return of the Archons 
Played by Sean Morgan. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
