PERSONNEL: Treen
Ambassador, diplomatic officer, Kradin

Resident of the Delta Quadrant and representative for the Kradin government. Treen's aid was vital to the recovery of Chakotay from Vori imprisonment and brainwashing. 

Reference: Nemesis
Portrayed by Terrance Evans.
