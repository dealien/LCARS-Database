PERSONNEL: Jay Stevens
Crewman, engineering technician, Deep Space Nine

Starfleet engineer who was under Worf's command on the U.S.S. Defiant. Until Miles O'Brien gave Worf some hints about handling noncommissioned personnel like Stevens, Stevens' life was very difficult. 

Reference: Starship Down 
Played by Jay Baker. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
