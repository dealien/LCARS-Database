PERSONNEL: John Haden
Admiral, Lya III

Starfleet officer at Lya III. In 2367, Admiral Haden ordered Captain Picard to enter Cardassian space and find Benjamin Maxwell. The previous year, he shared Picard's skepticism regarding Romulan defector Alidar Jarok. 

Reference: The Defector 
Played by John Hancock. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
