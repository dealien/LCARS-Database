PERSONNEL: Klim Dokachin

Zakdorn quartermaster and administrator of Starfleet's off-planet surplus depot near Qaulor II in 2368. The diffident Dokachin assisted in the investigation of the mysterious Vulcan ship T'Pau. 

Reference: Unification
Played by Graham Jarvis.
