PERSONNEL: Masada
Science officer, U.S.S. Constellation

After being evacuated from the starship, Masada died on a planet in system L-374, a victim of the massive planet killer machine. 

Reference: The Doomsday Machine 
Named after the hilltop fortress where Jews defied Roman legions, evetually committing suicide en masse rather than surrender.
