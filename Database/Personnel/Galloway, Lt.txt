PERSONNEL: David Galloway
Lieutenant, security officer, U.S.S. Enterprise

He was part of the landing party to Eminiar VII in 2267. Galloway was killed on Omega IV by Captain Ronald Tracey in 2268. 

Reference: The Omega Glory 
Played by David L. Ross. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
