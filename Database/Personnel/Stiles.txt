PERSONNEL: Stiles

Resident of Athos IV and a member of Michael Eddington's Maquis cell. Stiles, Eddington, and others fought numerous Jem'Hadar to the death in order to save the rest of their cell in 2373. 

Reference: Blaze of Glory
