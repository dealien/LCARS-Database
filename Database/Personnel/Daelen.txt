PERSONNEL: Daelen

Inhabitant of the Delta Quadrant. Daelen's genome was stolen by Steth sometime prior to Stardate 51762. While in the body of a woman who was Steth's previous victim, Daelen caught up with his original body when it was occupied by Tom Paris. Paris and Daelen returned to Voyager and were able to compel Steth to return their bodies. 

Reference: Vis a Vis
Portrayed by Elizabeth McGlynn.
