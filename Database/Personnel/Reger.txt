PERSONNEL: Reger
 
Resident of Beta III. In 2267 Reger helped shelter Kirk during Festival and later realized that this visitor was not under the spell of Landru. Later, Reger helped Kirk end Landru's computer-assisted reign. Reger had one daughter, Tula. 

Reference: The Return of the Archons 
Played by Harry Townes.
