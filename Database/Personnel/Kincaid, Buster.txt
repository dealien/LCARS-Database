PERSONNEL: Buster Kincaid

Ace reporter and trusty sidekick to Captain Proton in Tom Paris's futuristic holodeck serial-adventure. Buster, usually portrayed by Harry Kim, was often rescued from the clutches of Malicia and Demonica. 

Reference: Thirty Days, Bride of Chaotica
Portrayed by Garrett Wang/ Harry Kim.
