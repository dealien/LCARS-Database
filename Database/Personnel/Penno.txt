PERSONNEL: Penno

Photometric character in the Vori Larhana Settlement simulation. Penno was Karya's kindly grandfather. In 2374 Chakotay, unaware Penno was not flesh and blood, was outraged by his mistreatment. 

Reference: Nemesis
Portrayed by Booth Colman.
