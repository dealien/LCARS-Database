PERSONNEL: Vyto Logan
Lieutenant, chief engineer, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

He was the department head when the starship was on a mission at Minos. He objected to the assignment of then Lieutenant jg Geordi La Forge to acting commander during that mission. Ironically he was succeeded by Geordi La Forge the next year. 

Reference: The Arsenal of Freedom 
Played by Vyto Ruginis. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
