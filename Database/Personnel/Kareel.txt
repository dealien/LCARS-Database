PERSONNEL: Kareel

The Trill who joined with the Odan symbiont after the previous host died on board the U.S.S. Enterprise-D. Kareel wanted to continue a romance with Beverly Crusher, but the doctor declined. 

Reference: The Host 
Played by Nicole Orth-Pallavicini.
