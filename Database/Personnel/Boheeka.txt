PERSONNEL: Boheeka

Cardassian who accepted a bribe from Quark in 2370. Boheeka was asked to order a cranial implant device, thus arousing the suspicions of the Obsidian Order. 

Reference: The Wire 
Played by Jimmie F. Skaggs.
