PERSONNEL: Peter Kirk

George and Aurelan Kirk's son and James Kirk's nephew. In 2267 the Denevan neural parasites orphaned Peter. He was taken to the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701 until cured and then transported to Starbase 10. 

Reference: Operation: Annihilate!
Played by Craig Hundley, who also played Tommy Starnes in "And the Children Shall Lead" and composed some background music for "The Search For Spock."
