PERSONNEL: Grant Kelowitz
Lieutenant Commander, security officer, U.S.S. Enterprise

He led the away team to Taurus II while searching for the Galileo shuttlecraft. He reported in to Captain James Kirk about the humanoid creatures that inhabit the planet. Kelowitz also served on the team that transported down to Cestus III, and fared better than his teammate officer Lang. Kelowitz was also one of the crewmembers that abandoned ship after being subjected to the happiness spores.

Reference: This Side of Paradise, Arena, The Galileo Seven
Played by Grant Woods. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
