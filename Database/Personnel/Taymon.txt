PERSONNEL: Taymon

Humanoid man infected with the Taresian retrovirus that convinced him he was from Taresia and needed to return home. Once on Taresia, the women killed him for his genetic material. 

Reference: Favorite Son
Portrayed by Patrick Fabian.
