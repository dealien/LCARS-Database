PERSONNEL: Isaac Newton
Sir, scientist, Earth

Theorist and mathematician whose work became the foundation of modern science, thanks to hints provided by Quinn of the Q Continuum. The story goes, Newton discovered gravity after an apple hit him in the head. Quinn claimed to be responsible for dropping the apple. Among his scientific and mathematical advances were the development of the laws of motion and universal gravitation. He invented calculus. He served as the Lucasian Chair at Cambridge University for a time. A holographic Newton played poker with Data in 2369.
