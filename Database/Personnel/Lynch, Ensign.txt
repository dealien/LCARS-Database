PERSONNEL: Lynch
Ensign, U.S.S. Enterprise-E

Starfleet crewman assimilated by the Borg during their invasion of the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701-E. Captain Picard killed Lynch in The Big Goodbye holonovel, and retrieved a Borg neuro-processor from the corpse. 

Reference: Star Trek: First Contact
