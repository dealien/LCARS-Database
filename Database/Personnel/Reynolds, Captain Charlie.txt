PERSONNEL: Charlie Reynolds
Captain, starship commander, U.S.S. Centaur

In 2374, Reynolds mistakenly attacked a Jem'Hadar ship, unaware that his old friend, Benjamin Sisko, was in command on a covert mission. Reynolds was also present during the retaking of Deep Space 9.
