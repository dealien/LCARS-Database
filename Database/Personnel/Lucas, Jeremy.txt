PERSONNEL: Jeremy Lucas
Doctor, Interspecies Medical Exchange, Denobula

Dr. Phlox's friend and colleague from the Interspecies Medical Exchange. Dr. Lucas was the first human to serve on Phlox's homeworld of Denobula, and he and Phlox exchanged personal correspondence in 2151. Phlox had many observations on human behavior, as well as tips for dealing with Denobulan mating rituals. 

Reference: Dear Doctor
