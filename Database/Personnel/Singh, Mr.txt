PERSONNEL: Blaisdell Singh
Engineering officer, U.S.S. Enterprise

Crewmember working in auxiliary control aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise in 2267 when Kirk was giving the Nomad probe a tour of the ship. 

Reference: The Changeling 
Played by Blaisdell Makee. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
