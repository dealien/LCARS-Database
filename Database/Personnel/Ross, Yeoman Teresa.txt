PERSONNEL: Teresa Ross
Yeoman, operations officer, U.S.S. Enterprise

Starfleet crew member aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise in 2267. Ross was one of several crew members abducted by Trelane, a powerful entity, and taken to the planet Gothos. 

Reference: The Squire of Gothos 
Played by Venita Wolf.
