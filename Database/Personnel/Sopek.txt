PERSONNEL: Sopek

Vulcan captain of the Ni'Var. Sopek and his ship were sent to take T'Pol off of the Enterprise NX-01 following the destruction of the sacred Vulcan monastery of P'Jem. 

Reference: Shadows of P'Jem.
Portrayed by Gregory Itzin whom also played Ilon Tandro in "Dax" Dysek in "Critical Care" Admiral Black in "In a Mirror Darkly," and Haim in "Who Mourns for Morn?" This actor's name has also been mispelled as Itzen on The Continuum by mistake.
