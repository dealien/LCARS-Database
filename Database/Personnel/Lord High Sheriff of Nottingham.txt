PERSONNEL: Lord High Sheriff of Nottingham

Mythical persona enacted by Q during a Robin Hood simulation he created to teach Picard a lesson about romantic love. The Sheriff was not a sympathetic character. 

Reference: QPid 
Played by John de Lancie/ Q.
