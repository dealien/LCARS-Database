PERSONNEL: Niles

Wearing a Starfleet uniform and phaser, Niles helped Sakonna and Amaros kidnap Gul Dukat from DS9, the Maquis' first official act in 2370. He also tried to destroy a Cardassian weapons facility on Bryma by piloting one of the Peregrine-class interceptors acquired and armed by the terrorist group. 

Reference: The Maquis
Played by Michael Rose.
