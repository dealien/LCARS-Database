PERSONNEL: Zolan

Man who whispered to Julian Bashir about Proka Migdal's mistreating his adopted Cardassian son, Rugal. Zolan's gossip launched an investigation that revealed Rugal's biological father. 

Reference: Cardassians 
Played by Dion Anderson.
