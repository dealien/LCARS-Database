PERSONNEL: Kenicki

A crew member serving on the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701-D in 2367. REM sleep deficiency caused Kenicki to hallucinate in a turbolift after the ship was trapped in a Tyken's Rift. 

Reference: Night Terrors
