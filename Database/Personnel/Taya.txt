PERSONNEL: Taya

Ten-year-old girl on Yadera II who was actually a holographic lifeform created by Rurigan, her "grandfather." Taya's mother, and other villagers, were disappearing until Rurigan's holographic projector was repaired. 

Reference: Shadowplay 
Played by Noley Thornton.
