PERSONNEL: Katik Shaw

Male from Rutia IV and believer in the Ansata cause. Shaw was questioned and released by Alexana Devos after William Riker gave him a message for the Ansata leader. 

Reference: The High Ground 
Portrayed by Marc Buckland.
