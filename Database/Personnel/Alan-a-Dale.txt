PERSONNEL: Alan-a-Dale

In Earth mythology, a minstrel who was a member of Robin Hood's band of outlaws in ancient England. Geordi La Forge was cast in his part by Q during a fantasy crafted for Captain Jean-Luc Picard in 2367. Unfortunately, Geordi's musical skills were not quite up to the part.

Reference: Qpid
Played by LeVar Burton.
