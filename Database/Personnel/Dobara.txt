PERSONNEL: Dobara

Resident of a village on Boraal II that was unknowingly transplanted on the Enterprise-D to the planet Vacca VI in 2370 thanks to Nikolai Rozhenko. Dobara had become pregnant with Dr. Rozhenko's child before the journey started, and when they arrived at their new home Rozhenko chose to remain with her people and live with her. When she met Rozhenko's brother Worf, she witnessed the differences between the brothers, and she urged Worf to stand by Nikolai. 

Reference: Homeward 
Played by Penny Johnson, who also played Kasidy Yates on Deep Space 9.
