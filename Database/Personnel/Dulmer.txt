PERSONNEL: Jack Dulmer
Agent, Federation Department of Temporal Investigations

In 2373 agent Dulmer and his partner, Lucsly, traveled to Deep Space 9 to investigate the U.S.S. Defiant's accidental trip back in time 105 years to stardate 4523.

Reference: Trials and Tribble-ations
Played by Jack Blessing. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name. The names Dulmer and Lucsly were anagrams for Mulder and Scully, a tip of the hat to  Fox's popular fantasy show, "The X Files."
