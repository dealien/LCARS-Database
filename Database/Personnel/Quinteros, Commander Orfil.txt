PERSONNEL: Orfil Quinteros
Commander, starbase commander, Starbase 74
 
Quinteros participated in the construction of the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701-D at the Utopia Planetia Fleet Yards. Quinteros was also at Starbase 74 in 2364 when the Enterprise arrived for computer upgrades. 

Reference: 11001001 
Portrayed by Gene Dynarski, whom also played Ben Childress in "Mudd's Women."
