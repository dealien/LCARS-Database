PERSONNEL: Wagnor

Angosian pilot of a law enforcement transport. Wagnor expected to take Roga Danar back to prison, but Danar overpowered Wagnor and used the ship to attack the prison instead. 

Reference: The Hunted 
Portrayed by Andrew Bicknell.
