PERSONNEL: Seltin Rakal
planetary leader, Meridian

A planet in the Gamma Quadrant that shifts between a corporeal and non-corporeal existence every 60 years. In 2371, Meridian was discovered by the crew of the U.S.S. Defiant. 

Reference: Meridian 
Played by Christine Healy.
