PERSONNEL: Juliana (O'Donnell-Soong) Tainer
PhD, sciencetist, Atrea IV

Data's mother. Born Juliana O'Donnell, she wed and worked beside Dr. Noonien Soong until she died in 2336. An duplicate android continued in her place, divorcing Soong and marrying Dr. Pran Tainer. 

Reference: Inheritance 
Portrayed by Fionnula Flanagan whom also played Enina Tandro in "Dax."
