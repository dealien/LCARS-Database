PERSONNEL: Elani

A female Drayan encountered by Tuvok when he was marooned on their sacred moon in 2372. Like all Drayans, Elani appeared to be a young child, but was actually at the end of her natural life. 

Reference: Innocence
Portrayed by Sarah Rayne.
