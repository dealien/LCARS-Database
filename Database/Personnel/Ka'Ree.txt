PERSONNEL: Ka'Ree

Beloved wife of Haakonian scientist Ma'Bor Jetrel. Following Jetrel's development of the Metreon Cascade weapon of mass destruction, Ka'Ree took the couple's three children and left her husband. Jetrel never saw them again.
