PERSONNEL: Tamar

Inhabitant of Beta III and elderly citizen of Landru's mind-controlled populace who resided with Reger, Tula, and Hacom. Tamar's participation in a weak resistance movement ended in 2267 with his execution for mocking the Lawgivers. 

Reference: The Return of the Archons
