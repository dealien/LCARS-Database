PERSONNEL: Vekor

Mercenary among the band who kidnapped Will Riker from Barradas III. She later rebelled against group leader Arctus Baran, despite his possessing the controller for her neural implant, and was killed on Vulcan by the activation of a psychic resonator. 

Reference: Gambit
Portaryed by Caitlin Brown whom also played Ty Kajada in "The Passenger" and Na'Toth in Babylon 5.
