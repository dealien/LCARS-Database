PERSONNEL: John Masefield

Famous English author of the book, "Sea Fever." His line, "All I ask is a tall ship and a star to steer her by," appears on the dedication plaque of the U.S.S. Defiant. 

TIMELINE:
1878: Born
: wrote "Sea Fever"
1967: Died
