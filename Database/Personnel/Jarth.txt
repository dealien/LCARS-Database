PERSONNEL: Jarth
Humanoid male who visited planet Rekag-Seronia in 2369 as an assistant to Ambassador Ves Alkar while he mediated a treaty between the Rekags and Seronians. 

Reference: Man of the People 
Played by Rick Scarry.
