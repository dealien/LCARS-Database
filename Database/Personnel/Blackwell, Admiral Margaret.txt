PERSONNEL: Margaret Blackwell
Admiral, operations officer

Blackwell ordered the U.S.S. Enterprise-D to rendezvous with the U.S.S. Crazy Horse in sector 1607 in 2370. The adjustment ended the mapping of the Merkoria Quasar, and authorized high warp speed.

Reference: The Pegasus
