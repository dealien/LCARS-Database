PERSONNEL: Sargon
Planetary leader

One of three beings who survived his planet's destruction by having his consciousness stored below ground in a special containment device. In 2268, Sargon and his wife, Thalassa, and long-time foe Henoch, had the temporary use of the bodies of Captain Kirk, Dr. Ann Mulhall and Spock. 

Reference: Return to Tomorrow
Played by William Shatner, and voiced by James Doohan.
