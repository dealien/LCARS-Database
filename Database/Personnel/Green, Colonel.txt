PERSONNEL: Phillip Green
Colonel, Earth

Despotic leader of a faction in a genocidal war on Earth in the 21st century. The image of this notorious historical figure was re-created by the Excalbians in 2269 as part of their study of the nature of the human concepts of "good" and "evil." 

Reference: The Savage Curtain 
Played by Phillip Pine. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
