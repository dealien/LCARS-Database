PERSONNEL: Ronald Tracey
Captain, starship commander, U.S.S. Essex

Starfleet captain who tragically assumed a virus on Omega IV was the Fountain of Youth. Tracey's crew, countless natives, and the Prime Directive were casualties of Tracey's desire for immortality. 

Reference: The Omega Glory 
Played by Morgan Woodward, who also played Dr. Simon van Gelder in "Dagger of the Mind."
