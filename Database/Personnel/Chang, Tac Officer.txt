PERSONNEL: Robert Chang
Lieutenant, tactical officer, Relva VII.

Starfleet officer who conducted the Starfleet Academy entrance exam on Relva VII in 2364. Chang administered a variety of academic tests and psychological evaluations to four finalists, including Wesley Crusher in his first attempt to join the academy. But Chang could pick only one candidate, and that honor went to Mordock, who became the first Benzite to enter Starfleet. 

Reference: Coming of Age 
Played by Robert Ito. The character's firt name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
