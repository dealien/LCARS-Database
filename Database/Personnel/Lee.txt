PERSONNEL: Lee Lifford

A female Sanctuary District A government worker in 2024 on Earth. Her job in San Francisco inadvertently forced her to be among the Bell Riots captives. 

Reference: Past Tense
Played by Tina Lifford. Her first name was not mentioned in the episode but is in the script. Her last name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
