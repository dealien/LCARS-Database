PERSONNEL: Victoria Haines
Ensign, navigator, U.S.S. Enterprise

Starfleet enlisted person, she served as a navigator aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise in 2268. 

Reference: The Gamesters of Triskelion
Played by Victoria George. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
