PERSONNEL: Jeff Corwin
Lieutenant, chief science officer, U.S.S. Cougar

A mission specialist in charge of observing the area where experiments were being conducted in trying to create a stable wormhole. Corwin had to coordinate activities with the civilian research team headed up by Dr. Lenara Kahn and with the other vessels assigned to monitoring the events.

Reference: Q Voyage
This character was inspired by Lt David Corwin on the sci-fi show Babylon 5.
