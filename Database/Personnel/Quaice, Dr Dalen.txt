PERSONNEL: Quaice, MD.
Doctor, Commander, medical officer, Starfleet Medical 

Dr. Beverly Crusher interned with Dr. Quaice in 2352 on Delos IV. Quaice was a passenger aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise in 2367 on his journey to Kenda II. 

Reference: Remember Me 
Portrayed by Bill Erwin.
