PERSONNEL: Howard Clark
Director, anthropologist, Ventax II

Anthropologist and director of the Federation research station on Ventax II. Dr. Clark sent a distress call to the Enterprise-D in 2367 when his staff was threatened by planet-wide civil unrest. 

Reference: Devil's Due 
The Continuum spelled this character's name with an "e" at the end, Clarke, but it was not that way in print. Played by Paul Lambert.
