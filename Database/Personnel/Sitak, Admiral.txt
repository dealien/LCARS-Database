PERSONNEL: Sitak
Admiral

Vulcan Starfleet official who met with Benjamin Sisko, Admiral Ross, and Admiral Coburn at Starbase 375. The Admirals listened to and approved Sisko's plan to regain control of Deep Space 9 in 2374 through Operation Retrieve.
