PERSONNEL: Gralik Durr

Xindi-Sloth in charge of a kemocite manufacturing plant. Unbeknownst to Gralik, his kemocite was being used by the Xindi council to build a massive weapon that would annihilate humanity. Gralik aided Captain Archer in sabotaging the last shipment of kemocite. 

Reference: The Xindi, The Council, Zero Hour
Portrayed by John Cothran Jr.
