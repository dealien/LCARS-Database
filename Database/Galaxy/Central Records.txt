STATION: Central Records
PLANET: Cardassia Prime
SYSTEM:
SECTOR:
QUADRANT: Alpha

This office complex contained the DNA profile of every Cardassian citizen.  During the Dominion War, the governments of the United Federation of Planets and the Cardassian Union established unofficial lines of communications in and out of this facility for the purpose of identifying war dead.
