Neil Armstrong Starbase is located in the Ordover system. Nurse Chief Broome transferred to Deep Space Nine in 2370 from the Ordover System.

Reference: Fallen Heroes
Presumably named after Pocket Books/ Star Trek novel editor John J Ordover.
