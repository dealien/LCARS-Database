PLANET: Danula II
SYSTEM: Danula
SECTOR:
QUADRANT:

Planet. In 2324, the Starfleet Academy marathon took place there.  First-year student Jean Luc Picard won the race, beating senior cadets like David Gold and received a trophy. 

Reference: Belly of the Beast, Progenitor
