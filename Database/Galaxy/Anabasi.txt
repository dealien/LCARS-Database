PLANET: Anabasi
SYSTEM:
SECTOR:
QUADRANT: Alpha

Xawe colony. Anabasi was Xawen's richest colony and was located on the Cardassian border. The Gift of Flight was transporting money and handicrafts from Anabasi in 2370 when the ship was attacked.
