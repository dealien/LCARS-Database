PLANET: Yrskatdon
SYSTEM:
SECTOR:
QUADRANT:

During her time at Starfleet Academy, Marta Batanides was exposed to a Berengarian Virus that put her in a coma, nearly killing her. Without authorization, Jean-Luc Picard and Corin Zweller secretly took her to Yrskatdon, where she underwent a gene resequencing therapy that saved her life.
