STATION: Plat

Kursican Orbital Incarceration Platform.  Prison facility located in Kursican space, nine hours by shuttle from Kursican Primus.  Augustus Bradford was among the prisoners being held at the facility which housed approximately 100 prisoners.  Both criminal and political prisoners were held on the Plat.  Staff of about 100.  Upon arriving on the station the SCE team found signs of major sabatoge.
