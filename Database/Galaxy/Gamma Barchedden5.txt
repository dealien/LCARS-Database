PLANET: Gamma Barchedden V
SYSTEM: Gamma Barchedden
SECTOR:
QUADRANT:

A gas giant in a distant star system. Ensign Jiterica was studying the file on it a few weeks after she came on board. She claimed she was doing it to gain a better understand of the planet, but Commander Elizabeth Wu suspected the real reason was homesickness.
