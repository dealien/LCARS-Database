CITY: Bennikar
PLANET: Bajor
SYSTEM: B’hava’el
SECTOR: Bajoran
QUADRANT: Alpha

Village in the Kaladrys Valley.  Bennikar was known for brewing kis and for breeding the sturdiest verdanis. Bennikar was destroyed in the Cardassian Withdrawal and much of its populace was forced to relocate to refugee camps.  Kai Opaka's prophecy claimed that the child who would unite Bajor came from Bennikar.
