STATION: Neil Armstrong Starbase

Starfleet facility located in the Ordover System.  Chief Broome transferred to Deep Space Nine in 2370 from Neil Armstrong Starbase. 

Reference: Fallen Heroes
The Ordover System may be named for Pocket Books/ Star Trek editor John J Ordover.
