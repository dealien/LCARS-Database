STATION: Starbase 32

Federation facility of the "hourglass" variety. It served as the location of a meeting attented by the captains and executive officers of the starships in the sector in 2333 because Admiral McAteer wanted to closely coordinate the activities of the ships in the sector he was appointed to supervise.  Captain Jean-Luc Picard and Commander Gilaad Ben Zoma attended the meeting.  Mollie Katz was a transporter officer on Starbase 32.
