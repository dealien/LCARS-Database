PLANET: B'hal Ta
SYSTEM:
SECTOR:
QUADRANT: Alpha

Bajoran colony world.  Home to Arla Rees and her family.  When Bajor was annexed by the Cardassian Union in 2328, the family relocated to New Sydney, away from Cardassian rule.
