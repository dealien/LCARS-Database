PLANET: Xenex
SYSTEM:
SECTOR: 221-H
QUADRANT: Alpha

A class-M world near the border of Thallonian space. It is the homeworld of the Xenexians and of Starfleet Captain Mackenzie Calhoun, a.k.a. M’k’n’zy of Calhoun, who in his youth led a revolt that ended the 300-year long occupation and oppression of Xenex by the Danteri.
Xenex features several unique locations like The Pit, a large arid desert surrounded by mountains that traps weather patterns, causing for quick developing storms.
