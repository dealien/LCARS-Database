ANOMALY: Sinkhole

Name given to a space-time rift the U.S.S. Stargazer encountered in the Oneo Madrin system. Part of the S.S. Belladonna was caught in the sinkhole, which was why the Stargazer had trouble detecting the science ship.  Progenitor
Sinqay—where the Markanians long to return, its a holy shrine that the  Thallions/Areon and the Marrkanaians claim as their own.
