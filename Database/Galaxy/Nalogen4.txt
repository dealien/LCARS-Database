PLANET: Nalogen IV
SYSTEM: Nalogen
SECTOR:
QUADRANT:

M-class planet on the outer edge of the Milky Way Galaxy. A Kelvan colony, it had been accepting Kelvan refugees since its founding at the beginning of the 23rd century.
