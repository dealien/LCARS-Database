REGION: Mount Tulleah
PLANET: Vulcan
SYSTEM: 40 Eridani
SECTOR: Vulcan
QUADRANT: Beta

A high elevation located in the Gondi desert on Vulcan.  Selar ascended Mount Tulleah many times during her youth, finding it a source of peace and contemplation. It exists in at least one very accurate holodeck simulation aboard the U.S.S. Excalibur NCC-26517.
