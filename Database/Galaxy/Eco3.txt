PLANET: Eco III
SYSTEM: Eco
SECTOR:
QUADRANT: Gamma

Eco III was uncharted by the Federation as of 2370 and was the farthest planet Benjamin Sisko had visited in the Gamma Quadrant at that time.  A class-M planet, Eco had a lime-green sphere with dense but not unpleasant looking clouds. The planet's surface was dark green with splotches of salmon colored sand dunes.
