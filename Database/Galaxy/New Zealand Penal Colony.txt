STATION: New Zealand Penal Colony
CITY: Auckland
STATE: New Zealand
PLANET: Earth
SYSTEM: Sol
SECTOR: 001 Sol
QUADRANT: Alpha

Federation penal facility on Earth.  Lieutenant Hans Werber was sentenced to serve a short time there instead of a longer time on a high-security asteroid because of Jean-Luc Picard’s intervention with the judge advocate general.
