REGION: Dahkur Province
PLANET: Bajor
SYSTEM: B’hava’el
SECTOR: Bajoran
QUADRANT: Alpha

An area where three tectonic plates collided to create a perfect “X” of intersecting mountain ranges. The symbol is so well known it’s almost the galactic symbol for Bajor. In 2369, the province was swept by lake flu. 

Reference: Fall of Terok Nor
