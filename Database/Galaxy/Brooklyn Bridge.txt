LOCATION: Brooklyn Bridge

Spectacular public works in New York City on Earth in the 1800s.  Immigrants were offered deeds to it and “to buy the Brooklyn Bridge” became a colloquialism for gullibility.  In the twenty-second century, someone really did buy the Brooklyn Bridge and moved it in sections to an amusement park on Luna.
