STATION: Pride of Mintak
PLANET: Bajor
SYSTEM: B’hava’el
SECTOR: Bajoran
QUADRANT: Alpha

Bar on Bajor where Goodman, an engineer on Deep Space Nine, went in a  test of the Runabout’s Transporter.  According to O’Brien, the bar isn't up to the standards of Quark's, but it is hospitable to Federation personnel.
