SHIPS: Portland, U.S.S.
CLASS: Chimera
REGISTRY: NCC-57418

A starship sent in addition to a Cardassian cruiser to look for Odo and Garak's missing Runabout along the United Federation of Planets-Cardassian border in the Algira Sector.
