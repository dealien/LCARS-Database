SHIPS: C.S.S. Delphinus

A multiuse submarine. A cross between a submarine and cruiser. Capable of traveling up to 20 knots both above and below the water.  The CSS Delphinus was under the command of Captain Jason Nyere and was given the assignment of intercepting the aliens that had crash landed on Earth.  Tatya Bilash and Yoshi Nakamura called the Delphinus the "whale".   

Reference: Strangers from the Sky
