SHIPS: Al-Batani, U.S.S.
CLASS: Excelsior
REGISTRY: NCC-42995

An explorer vessel that has had a few celebrity officers serve aboard her. Commanded by Captain Paris, whom later became Admiral; and science officer Ensign Kathryn Janeway who later captained the U.S.S. Voyager with the Admiral's son.
