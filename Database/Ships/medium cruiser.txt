SHIPS: Medium cruisers

Another multipurpose craft designed for long voyages, just not as long as a heavy duty unit. They are generally smaller in size and mass than a heavy machine and with a crew smaller than 500 persons.

SHIPS OF THIS TYPE:
Miranda-class
Nebula-class
Steamrunner-class
