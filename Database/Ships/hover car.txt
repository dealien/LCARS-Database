SHUTTLECRAFT: hover car 
MISSION: personal transport

This type of vehicle has a wide range of classes and describes models on several different worlds, warp-capable and pre-warp civilizations alike. They are civilian ground-based transports used for personal and commercial purposes. In some minor cases they are still used by military personnel. They were a popular transportation vehicle on Earth in the mid-21st century, and still popular on Grazeri to this day.
