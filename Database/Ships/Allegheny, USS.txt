SHIPS: Allegheny, U.S.S.
CLASS: Galaxy

The team leader for a fleet of ships sent into the Delta Quadrant looking for a Borg staging ground that was preparing a major offensive against the Federation and other Alpha Quadrant species. The fleet encountered Borg ships and most of the fleet did not survive. The Allegheny did, but suffered serious injury. It was rescued by the U.S.S. Enterprise-E and a fleet of repair ships, then sent back to Starfleet Command for mission debriefing.
