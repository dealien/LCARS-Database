PROBE: Viking I

On July 20, 1976 this vessel soft-landed on Chryse Planitia, Mars and began transmitting photographs, climatic measurements and soil composition data. Designed for 90 days, the lander remained functional two years. 
This mission was followed up by the Viking II with a robot lander.
