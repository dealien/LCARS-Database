SHIPS: Ticonderoga, U.S.S.

In 2375, this Federation starship was sent to the Ba'ku planet in an effort to make the planet a Federation protectorate. This was after Captain Picard exposed a conspiracy involving the Federation and the Son'a to destroy the Ba'ku way of life. 

Reference: Insurrection
