SHIPS: Kobheerian
TYPE: freighter

To make the trip to Cardassia Prime to rescue Major Kira, the U.S.S. Defiant's shields were modulated to resemble those of a Kobheerian freighter on long-range sensors, enabling the ship to pose as the Rak-Miunis.
