FLEET: Fifth Fleet

A collection of Starfleet ships that had been assigned to front-line duty along the Cardassian border during the Dominion War. Parts of this task force were included in Captain Sisko's bold mission to retake Deep Space Nine from the Dominion, Operation Retrieve.
