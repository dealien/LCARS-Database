SHIPS: U.S.S. Exeter
CLASS: 
TYPE:
REGISTRY:

Federation starship. Thomas Paris served aboard the Exeter prior to his incarceration at the New Zealand Penal Settlement. Mollie Katz had been assigned to the Exeter some time before 2333. Owen Paris served as executive officer on the Exeter in 2333.  The ship was considered one of the most prestigious vessels in the fleet. 

Reference: Non Sequitur, Progenitor, Gauntlet
(Not to be confused with the 23rd-Century Constitution-class starship of the same name.)
