SHIPS: Sreba

T'Kreng’s ship. A scientific research vessel. It had unpowered lifepods, 47 unmanned, self-contained probes for purposes of data collection and sample retrieval as well as seven different configurations of launchable sensor arrays. Half her crew held degrees in either singularity physics or quantum chronodynamics. 

Reference: Time’s Enemy
