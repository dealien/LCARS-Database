SHUTTLECRAFT: Ferengi cargo vessel

Transport vessel used by the Ferengi for hauling freight and merchandise. Remains of one such ship were discovered in the Hanolin asteroid belt in 2368. Included in the the cargo were remains of the Vulcan craft T'Pau's navigational deflector.
