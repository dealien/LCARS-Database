SHIPS: Cleponji
TYPE: battle cruiser

Promellian ship over a thousand years old (contemporary to Earth's Middle Ages, the AD 1300's) powered by Lang Cycle fusion engines.
In 2366 it was discovered intact at the final battle site of the Promellian-Menthar war at Orelious IX within an asteroid belt. The ship was maintained in near perfect conditions by the vacuum of space. The crew had died as a result of aceton assimilators located within the rocky field.
The ship was accidentally destroyed by the Enterprise-D in an escape attempt when it also was caught by the aceton assimilators. but later destroyed accidentally.

The Promellian battle cruiser was designed by Steve Burg and built by Ron Thornton for "The Night of the Creeps."
