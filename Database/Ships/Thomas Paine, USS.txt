SHIPS: Thomas Paine, U.S.S.
CLASS: New Orleans
REGISTRY: NCC-65530

A frigate under the command of Captain Rixx, who joined in the secret rendezvous on Dytallix B amid concerns of a feared conspiracy within Starfleet in 2364. Two years later, it was one of the ships in Picard's blockade fleet on the Klingon-Romulan border. 

Reference: Conspiracy 
This ship was named for the American patriot and writer.
