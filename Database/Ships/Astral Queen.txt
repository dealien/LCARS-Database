SHIPS: Astral Queen
TYPE: Transport

Captain Jon Daily commanded this Federation passenger craft in 2266. When the ship didn't make a scheduled stop at Planet Q, the Karidian Company of Players was forced to seek passage on the Enterprise. 

Reference: The Conscience of the King
