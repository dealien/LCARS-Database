SHIPS: Groumall
TYPE: freighter

A boxy Cardassian freighter (pronounced "gru-MAUL"), captained by the disgraced Dukat in 2372, sent to escort Kira to the Korma intelligence conference before being converted to a warship and overtaking a Klingon Bird-of-Prey and being detonated with the captured Klingons. It has at least five levels. 
It's weapons were upgraded using the disruptors from the facilities on the planet.
