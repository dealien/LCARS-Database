SHIPS: Constantinople, U.S.S.
CLASS: Istanbul
REGISTRY: NCC-34852

A Starfleet transport ship of 2,012 crew and settlers.
In 2365 the ship suffered a hull breach near Gravesworld. Its distress signal forced the Enterprise-D to leave only an away team to assist Dr. Ira Graves with his health emergency. 

Named for the Turkish city also known as Istanbul.

Reference: The Schizoid Man
