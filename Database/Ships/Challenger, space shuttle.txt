SHUTTLECRAFT: Challenger
TYPE: OR1 space shuttle
REGSITRY: OV-103

One of five reusable planetary launch vessels designed by the National Aeronatics and Space Administration of Earth. 
This 20th century Earth space ship was destroyed in a horrific explosion shortly after a launch in 1986. All seven lives were lost. 
The five astronauts included McNair, Onizuka, Resnik, Scobee, and Smith. The mission to space featured two civilians; a female teacher, McCauliffe, and Jarvis.

Reference: The Voyage Home, The Ensigns of Command 
The film, The Voyage Home, bore the dedication: "The cast and crew of Star Trek wish to dedicate this film to the men and women of the spaceship Challenger, whose couragous spirit shall live to the 23rd century and beyond..."
