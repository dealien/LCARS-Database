TYPE: Bird of prey

Designation for a scout-class or light cruiser ship used by Klingons and Romulans. Originally designed by Romulans during the 2200s, the technology was shared with the Klingons during their cooperative years. Nearly all birds of prey are able to cloak.

Some of the designations have included:
Romulan:
Abtru-class light cruiser
Brigatol-class light cruiser
Cevil-class scout
Deros-class light cruiser
Shrike-class light cruiser
Talon-class scout

Klingon:
B'Rel-class scout
D12-class light cruiser
K'Vort-class light cruiser
NuQ'Duj-class scout
Qui'Fly light cruiser
