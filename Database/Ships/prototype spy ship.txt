SHIPS: prototype spy ship
TYPE: scout

This small craft was built secretly in an effort to test stealth technology that proved to be unworkable to most sensor systems in use by many alien races. It was launched in 2117 and decommissioned in 2119 after the two years of testing.

DIMENSIONS:
Length:	35 meters
Weight:	80 tons

ACCOMODATIONS:
Crewmen:	3

PROPULSION:
Advanced second generation warp dive
