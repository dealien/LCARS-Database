SHIPS: Pagh, I.K.S.
CLASS: K'Vort

Klingon cruiser under the command of Captain Kargan where Riker served as First Officer as part of a new officer exchange program in 2365. He was reportedly the first human to serve aboard a Klingon vessel, and helped delay tensions when hull damage was found to be caused by an odd bacteria, not Starfleet sabotage. 

Reference: A Matter of Honor
