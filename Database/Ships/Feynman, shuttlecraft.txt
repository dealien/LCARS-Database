SHUTTLECRAFT: Feynman
TYPE: 6
REGISTRY: NCC-1701-D-

Enterprise shuttlecraft (pronounced "FINE-man") carrying Picard, Worf and Crusher on a secret mission to Torman V. 

Reference: Chain of Command
Named for Terran American physicist Richard Phillips Feynman (AD 1918-88), co-winner of the 1965 Nobel Prize. The name was misspelled as Feyman on the shuttle's exterior because of a mistake made in the art department.
