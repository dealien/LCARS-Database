SHIPS: Son'a flagship
TYPE: heavy cruiser

This unique craft was used by Ahdar Ru'afo and Admiral Dougherty to oversee the study and relocation efforts of the Ba'ku people from their world within the Briar Patch. This ship also controlled the Son'a Collector.
