SHIPS: Korinar, I.K.S.
CLASS: K'Vort
TYPE: light cruiser

Klingon bird-of-prey which initially challenged the runabout U.S.S. Yukon, tipping off Kira and O'Brien to a covert cloaked mining plot around the Bajor system in 2372.
