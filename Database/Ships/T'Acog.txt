SHIPS: T`Acog, I.K.S.
CLASS: K't'inga 

A Klingon cruiser assigned to return three renegade Klingons criminals, who opposed the current Federation-Klingon alliance, to Qo'no'S. Koris. Konmel, and Kunivas managed to escape and commandeered Talarian freighter Batris in 2364. They then used that ship to destroy the T'Acog and all its crew.

Reference: Heart of Glory 
The T'Acog was a re-use of footage from "The Motion Picture."
