SHIPS: Tecumseh, U.S.S.
CLASS: Excelsior
NCC-

A veteran of the Cardassian wars like the U.S.S. Rutledge, the Tecumseh helped evacuate Ajilon Prime against the Klingons during the Archanis sector crisis, after the U.S.S. Farragut was destroyed. Dr. Kalandra's husband was science officer.
