SHIPS: Ilon Tandro's vessel
TYPE: transport

Tandro took this ship to Deep Space Nine, in an effort to extradite Dax in order for Curzon Dax, a former host of the Dax symbiont, to face murder charges.  

Reference: Dax
This same vessel design was used by the Tamarians, T'Lani, and Drayans throughout Trek.
