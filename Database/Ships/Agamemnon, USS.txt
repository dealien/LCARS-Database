SHIPS: Agamemnon, U.S.S.
CLASS: Apollo
REGISTRY: NCC-11638

Part of Captain Picard's 2369 impromptu task force, #3, to fortify and brace for the expected Borg Invasion. 

Reference: Descent
The Agamemnon was named for the Greek mythological figure who was commander of the Greek forces during the Trojan War.
