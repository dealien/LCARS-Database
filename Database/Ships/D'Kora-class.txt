CLASS: D'Kora
TYPE: heavy cruiser

A type of starship used by the Ferengi Alliance. Sophisticated vessels, some Marauders were equipped with the ability to fire a powerful plasma energy burst, capable of disabling a Galaxy-class starship. The first encounter with the Federation was in 2364 near planet Gamma Tauri IV.

Crew: 450

Reference: The Last Outpost, The Battle, Peak Performance, The Price, Menage a Troi, Rascals
The Ferengi ship was designed by Andrew Probert and built by Greg Jein.
