SHIPS: Carolina, U.S.S.
CLASS: Daedalus
REGISTRY: NCC-235

This Federation starship supposedly sent a distress call to the Enterprise in 2267. It was actually a hoax transmission from the Klingons, who faked the distress call in order to keep the Enterprise from the planet Capella IV.
