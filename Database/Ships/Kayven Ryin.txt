SHIPS: Kayven Ryin

A science and research vessel named for a famous Thallonian writer and editor. The Kayven Ryin sent the U.S.S. Excalibur an S.O.OO. and a passenger manifest, which included the name of Si Cwan’s missing sister, Kalinda. The listing of Kalinda later proved to be ea ruse by Zoran to lure Si Cwan into a trap.
