SHIPS: Majestic, U.S.S.
CLASS: Miranda
REGISTRY: NCC-

In 2374, this Federation starship was destroyed during Captain Sisko's bold attack on the Dominion forces occupying Deep Space Nine. This and another Miranda-class vessel flanked the U.S.S. Defiant as the lead vessel tried to cut through a Dominion blockade attempting to prevent the Federation fleet from getting to the space station.
