SHIPS: Ekina
TYPE: transport

Supposedly a cargo ship crippled by the plasma storm in the Denorios belt on stardate 47182, it was actually the ship taken by Verad when he came to Deep Space 9 to steal the Dax symbiont in 2370. It was manually released in the airlock by Constable Odo to thwart Verad's main escape plan.
