SHIPS: Gandhi, U.S.S.
CLASS: Ambassador
REGISTRY: NCC-26632

Captain Picard helped Lieutenant Thomas Riker get assigned to this ship after Riker was rescued from Nervala IV in 2369. Riker eventually left the ship to join the Maquis. 

Reference: Second Chances, Defiant
Named for Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi (1869 - 1948), leader of the Indian nationalist movement, an influential philosopher who advocated nonviolent confrontation and civil disobedience as a means of fostering political change on Earth.
