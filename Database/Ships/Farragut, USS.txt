SHIPS: Farragut, U.S.S.
CLASS: Constitution
REGISTRY: NCC-1647

Timeline:
Commissioned and assigned to Captain John Garrovick.
Lieutenant James Kirk accepted his first starship posting here.
2257: 200 crew members were slaughtered by the dikironium cloud creature on Tycho IV. 

Reference: Obsession 
Named for American naval officer David Glascow Farragut, who commanded Union ships during the American Civil War, famous for his rallying cry, "Damn the torpedoes, full speed ahead.
