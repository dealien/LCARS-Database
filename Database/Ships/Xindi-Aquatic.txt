SHIPS: Xindi-Aquatic vessel

The Xindi-Aquatics have the largest and arguably the most formidable vessels in the Xindi fleet. 
Large, water-filled chambers house the Aquatics, as they command their ships in an effort to prevent their doomsday weapon from reaching Earth.
