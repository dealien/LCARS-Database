SHIPS: Batris
CLASS: Antares

This Talarian freighter was hijacked by Klingons under the command of Captain Korris on stardate 41503.7. The craft exploded, but the Klingon renegades were rescued by personnel from the U.S.S. Enterprise-D. 

Reference: Heart of Glory 
The Batris was a modification of a Visitors freighter from the miniseries V. The Batris itself was further modificed and seen as a variety of other freighters in later episodes, presumably suggesting that it is a design in use by many different planets. The Batris was built by Greg Jein.
