FLEET: Valtese vessels

A group of roughly blockish ships with a squarish fin of an unspecified function extending outward at one end both port and starboard at a 45-degree angle. 
The vessel that brought Valtese Chancellor Alrik to meet the U.S.S. Enterprise-D was a Valtese design.
