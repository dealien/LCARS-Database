CLASS: Century
TYPE: long range scout

A Starfleet vessel with a small engineering section attached at the rear of the main saucer and equipped with two nacelles; each longer than the entire ship. This vessel design was also unique because it housed dorsal entry “ceiling door” shuttle bay, where ships descended into the bay to land.
