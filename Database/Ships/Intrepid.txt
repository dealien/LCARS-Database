SHIPS: Intrepid

An Earth warship that was part of a group of three ships that stopped Duras's Bird of Prey from destroying the Enterprise NX01 upon its return to Earth's solar system on April 24, 2153. It was commanded by Carlos Ramirez.   

Reference: The Expanse
