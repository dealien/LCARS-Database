SHUTTLECRAFT: Columbus
TYPE: 2
REGISTRY: NCC-1701-2

A shuttlecraft on board the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701.  In 2267, this craft took part in a visual search for the crashed Galileo on the surface of Taurus II. 

Reference: The Galileo Seven
Named after the explorer, Christopher Columbus (1451-1506).
