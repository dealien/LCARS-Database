SHIPS: Gettysburg, U.S.S.
CLASS: Constellation
REGISTRY: NCC-3890

Cruiser under the command of Mark Jameson. In 2319 the ship was sent to Mordan IV to resolve a hostage crisis involving Federation citizens. The captain was successful in securing the release and restoring the government to power. It was later found that the captain secured the hostages with an offering of weapons from his ship's stores. It was his last starship command before his promotion to admiral.
