SHIPS: Kronos One, I.K.S.
CLASS: K't'inga

The flagship of the Klingon fleet, named after the homeworld and issued a number designation; similar in practice to American designations for Air Force One for their president. Chancellor Gorkon was murdered aboard this Klingon battle cruiser on his way to an Earth peace conference in 2293. 

Reference: The Undiscovered Country
Kronos One was a modification of the Klingon battle cruiser built for the The Motion Picture, which was in turn based on the ships used for the original series.
