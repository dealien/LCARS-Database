SHUTTLECRAFT: hopper
TYPE: transport

A light ground-to-orbit vehicle easily picked off by ground-based fire.
These craft were often incorporated into the hulls of Iwo Jima-class troop transport frigates. 
Starfleet marines used these vessels often to deploy forces and collect troops and rescue hostages/ civilians. Such units were used on Ajilon Prime in the skirmish against the Klingons.
