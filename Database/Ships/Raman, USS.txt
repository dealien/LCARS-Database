SHIPS: Raman, U.S.S.
CLASS: Oberth
REGISTRY: NCC-59983

Federation science vessel.
In 2370, the Raman's seven person crew was killed by subspace life-forms inhabiting the atmosphere of Marijne VII. The U.S.S. Enterprise-D responded to a distress call, but was too late to save them. 

Reference: Interface 
Named for the Nobel-prize winning physicist, Sir Chandrasekhara Venkata Raman.
