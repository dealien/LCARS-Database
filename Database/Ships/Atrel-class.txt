CLASS: Atrel
TYPE:  fighter

A Romulan attack craft capable of atmospheric flight. These ships are often seen in planetary orbits or in defense of an important system within the Star Empire. They do have warp engines and can travel between planets, even between systems but are raely used for the longer journey.
