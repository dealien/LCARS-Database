SHIPS: Heavy cruisers

A multipurpose craft designed for long voyages on missions of scientific, exploration, military, and diplomatic causes. These craft are often some of the most heavily armed within the fleet, largest mass, length, height, width, and have crews over 500 persons. These ships usually serve as fleet flagships, ships of the line.
