SHUTTLECRAFT: Jem'Hadar
TYPE: shuttlecraft

A utility craft in use by the Dominion for transporting supplies and other low security items to a planetary surface. These ships are not generally used to transport Founders, troops, or other tactical and important items. These models are capable of long voyages and atmospheric maneuverability.
