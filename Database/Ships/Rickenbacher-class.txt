CLASS: Rickenbacker
TYPE: armored transport

With one notorious exception, the Flying Fortresses have enjoyed a remarkably flawless security records for their precious cargoes. From multi-trillion currency shipments to classified military packages to the rarest materials in the galaxy (dilithium, anti-matter), these heavily armored and gunned vessels have provided safe passage throughout the Federation for decades.
