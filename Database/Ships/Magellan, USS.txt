SHIPS: U.S.S. Magellan
CLASS: Constellation
TYPE: Heavy cruiser
REGISTRY: NCC-3069

Commanded by Captain Flynn then Captain Conklin. This starship was docked at the Remmler Array over Arkaria Base on stardate 46682.4. Lieutenant Piper was originally ordered to report to as this ship as her first assignment before being redirected to U.S.S. Enterprise.
