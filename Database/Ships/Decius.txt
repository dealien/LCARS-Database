SHIPS: Decius
CLASS: D'deridex

A fantasy ship created by the entity "Barash" on stardate 44286.5 as part of an engineered reality intended to keep Commander Riker on the planet Alpha Onias III. The Decius appeared as a Romulan Warbird which rendezvoused with the Galaxy-class U.S.S. Enterprise, commanded in this reality by Captain Riker, in order to deliver Admiral Picard, Counselor Troi, and Ambassador Tomalak.
