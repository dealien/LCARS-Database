SHUTTLECRAFT: Magellan
TYPE: 6
REGISTRY: NCC-1701-D-15

One of the shuttlecraft attached to the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701-D, destroyed in 2368 during a rescue attemnpt for a lost J'naii shuttle, Taris Murn. It was earlier used by Worf in an abortive rescue of Captain Picard from the surface of the El-Adrel IV. 

Reference: Darmok, The Outcast 
This ship was named for Ferdinand Magellan, Spanish navigator, and the first explorer to circumnavigate planet Earth. This was the first appearance of the new full-sized shuttle set and model introduced during the fifth seasons. The ship was an extensive modification of the Galileo 5 shuttle built for The Final Frontier. Designers included Richard James, Nilo Rodis, Herman Zimmerman, Andy Neskoromny, and Rick Sternbach. The model was built by Greg Jein.
