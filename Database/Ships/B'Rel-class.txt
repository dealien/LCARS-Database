CLASS: B`rel
TYPE: Scout

A type of Klingon Bird-of-Prey that is mostly used for reconaissance or patrol. These ships, which are smaller than the K'Vort-class vessels, usually have a crew of about 12. 

Reference: The Search For Spock, The Voyage Home, The Final Frontier, The Undiscovered Country, Rascals 
The ship was first seen in the movie but was not named until the Next Generation episode, and at that time was given the distinction between itself and its larger brother, the K'Vort.
