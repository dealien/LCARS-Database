MEDICAL: benzocyatizine-24, 25, 39

A series of drugs used by Trill doctors to stimulate failing isoboramine levels in joined hosts to avoid late rejection of the symbiont. It can be administered in small doses, approximately 2 ccs for a crisis situation.
