MEDICAL: Blight, the

Disease unleashed on the Teplan civilization by the conquering Jem'Hada. Sufferers showed blue welts and lesions once they contracted the disease, but they began to die once the welts turn red in a phase known as "the quickening." In 2372, Julian Bashir fashioned a cure which could not help the current generation of sufferers but would take effect with their newborn.
