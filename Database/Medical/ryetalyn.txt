MEDICAL: ryetalyn

A vital mineral used in the cure of deadly Rigelian fever. In 2269 the fever -struck crew of the U.S.S. Enterprise was cured by a deposit of ryetalyn found on a small planetoid in the Omega system. 

Reference: Requiem for Methuselah
