MEDICAL: cardiac replacement

A surgical technique originally developed by Doctor Van Doren in which a patient's diseased or injured heart is replaced by an artificial device. In 2327 and again in 2365, Captain Jean-Luc Picard underwent this procedure. 

Reference: Samaritan Snare
