WEAPONS: Tholian Web

A Tholian energy field which is woven like a web to entrap an enemy spacecraft. Two Tholian ships are literally able to spin a web of energy in space in order to trap an enemy vessel, after which the web is drawn together, constricting and finally destroying the vessel inside.
