WEAPONS: nisroh

A knife-like weapon with a curved blade that is used by the native people of the planet Tilonus IV. A nisroh was given to Riker to further authenticate his disguise on a mission to rescue a Federation research team trapped on that planet. Before beaming down Worf briefed Riker on the use of the nisroh.
