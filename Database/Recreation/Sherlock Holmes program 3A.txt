RECREATION: Sherlock Holmes program 3A

A recreational holodeck program of Commander Data's. It contained a virtual reality world of Sherlock Holmes. In 2369, the program malfunctioned and a character from the story, James Moriarty, tried to escape the bonds of the holodeck. 

Reference: Ship in a Bottle
