XENOLOGY: Immunocytic Gel Worms

Corkscrew-shaped organisms that live in a viscous pink fluid.  Doctor Phlox kept a jar full of them in his sickbay on Enterprise NX-01 for medical purposes. 

Reference: Broken Bow (TV and novelization)
