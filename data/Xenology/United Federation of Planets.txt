GOVERNMENT: United Federation of Planets

A union of numerous planets which cooperate for their mutual economic and cultural benefit within the Alpha and Beta quadrants of the Milky Way Galaxy.  Established in 2087. Charter members of the UFP were Earth, Vulcan, Alpha Centauri, Tellar and Epsilon Indii. It comprised of over 150 planets and colonies. 

Reference: Stranger from the Sky, Bless the Beasts
