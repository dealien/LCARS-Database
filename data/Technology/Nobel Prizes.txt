RECREATION: Nobel Prizes

Prestigious annual prizes for achievement in the sciences and peace created by Alfred Bernhard Nobel using the money he had accumulate through his invention of dynamite. The awards were suspended three times, during World War I, World War II, and the Eugenics Wars. They were not resurrected after the Eugenics Wars. See: Nobel and Z. Magnees Prize
