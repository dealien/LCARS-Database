TECHNOLOGY: magnetospheric energy taps

A power-supply technology developed on the Alpha Moon of planet Pelar Zel. It enabled the inhabitants to use the magnetic field of Pelar Zel as an energy source. However, the field created by the taps intersected the orbit of and caused extensive damage to Beta Moon, which only heightened the already tense political animosity between the two moon's governments.
