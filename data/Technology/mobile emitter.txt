TECHNOLOGY: mobile emitter

Also known as an autonomous holo-emitter. A portable holographic imaging device used by Voyager's EMH to access areas without holographic projectors. 
Henry Starling constructed the mobile emitter in 1996 from 29th-century technology he appropriated from Braxton's timeship.
