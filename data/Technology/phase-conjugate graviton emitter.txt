TECHNOLOGY: phase-conjugate graviton emitter

This engineering device is occasionally employed by tractor beams, allowing them to lift more. It was used in 2373 to pull a nearly buried Jem'Hadar ship out of the ground of a Gamma Quadrant world and back to Deep Space Nine for study.
