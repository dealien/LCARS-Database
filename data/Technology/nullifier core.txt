TECHNOLOGY: nullifier core

The nullifier core is the main engineering element of the propulsion systems on Romulan Warbird's. The nullifier core must be kept within strict levels of performance in order to prevent a phenomena known as magnetic disruptions, that can be detected by other ships while the Romulan Warbird is cloaked.
