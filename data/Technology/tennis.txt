RECREATION: Tennis

Earth game that involves striking a ball with a racket over a net.  Tennis is played on a tennis court and is played by both men and women, singly and in pairs, which is known as doubles.  Melody Sawyer was an excellent tennis player.  There was a tennis court for recreational purposes aboard the CSS Delphinus. 

Reference: Strangers from the Sky
