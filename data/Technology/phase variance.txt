TECHNOLOGY: phase variance

Fluctations or oddities in natural and artificial emissions. Thomas Riker's attempt to fool the Cardassians with a fake U.S.S. Defiant warp signature was thwarted when Benjamin Sisko recognized that the phase variance and sub-space harmonics readings were incorrect.
