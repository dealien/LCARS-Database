TECHNOLOGY: Auxiliary computer memory clips room

Located on Level 7 of Deep Space Nine.  Large rooms originally used as ammunition storage dumps during the Cardassian operation of Deep Space Nine.  After Starfleet took over command of the station, the rooms were used for auxiliary computer memory clips in an attempt to bring the computer power up to Starfleet standards.
