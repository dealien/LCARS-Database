TECHNOLOGY: MK-12 scanner

A security device used for identify verification. The array office on the Promenade of Deep Space Nine uses such a scanner to verify the identify of individuals storing valuables there. While on Deep Space Nine in 2369, Vash questioned whether the array office's scanner, which was further equipped with an L-89 enhanced resolution filter, was a sufficient safeguard.
