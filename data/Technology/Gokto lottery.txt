RECREATION: Gokto lottery

Game of chance purchased on the Promenade at Deep Space Nine. In 2370, Constable Odo was concerned that he hadn't seen an application from the Bajorans for permission to run a game on Deep Space Nine and decided to speak to Captain Benjamin Sisko about it.
