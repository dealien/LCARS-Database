PROBES: Quadros-1

A 22nd century deep space scientific probe launched from the Alpha Quadrant into the Gamma Quadrant, without the benefit of the Bajoran wormhole's shortcut. Among the discoveries about the Gamma Quadrant it reported was the finding of a trinary star system called Idran.
