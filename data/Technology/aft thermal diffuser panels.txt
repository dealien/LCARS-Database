TECHNOLOGY: Aft Thermal Diffuser Panels

Location on the U.S.S. Defiant where the waste heat of the warp core is vented into space. The Unclean used the thermal trail coming from the diffusers to track the ship in 2371. 

Reference: Time’s Enemy
