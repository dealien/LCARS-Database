TECHNOLOGY: adaptive interface link

A connection between computer systems of different origins. An adaptive link is necessary when attempting to transfer data or functionality between to disparate types of equipment. In 2369, such a link was used to download data from an alien space probe nicknamed PUP to the computers on station Deep Space Nine.
