TECHNOLOGY: Magnatomic Adhesion Manacle

Short bar of blue gray metal that flows to connect its opposite end when its charge is lost. When the ends meet, they connect in a molecular bond. Used to bind the hands of prisoners. Commonly referred to as handcuffs despite the connotation to older technology. 

Reference: Memory Prime
