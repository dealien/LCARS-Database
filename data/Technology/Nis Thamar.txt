RECREATION: Nis Thamar

A Bajoran festival roughly a week prior to the Berajin Harvest Festival.  In the tradition of the Dessin-ka, the Berajin festival officially begins on the eve of Nis Thamar. In early 2370, the Dessin-ka demanded that the Nekor be at the Temple by the eve of Nis Thamar.
