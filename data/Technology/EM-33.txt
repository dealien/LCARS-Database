WEAPON: EM-33 pistol

Particle weapon that fires a pulse of plasma high of electromagnetic energy. It was phased out by Starfleet in favor of the more advanced phase pistol in 2151.   

Reference: Broken Bow, Fight or Flight, What Price Honor?
