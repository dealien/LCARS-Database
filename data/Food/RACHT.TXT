FOOD: racht

An edible, higher-order Klingon worm (pronounced "rahkt"), served live - at such spots as the Klingon restaurant on Deep Space Nine. Doctor Julian Bashir is familiar with it and ordered a serving for he and Ensign Melora Pazlar. 

Reference: Melora
