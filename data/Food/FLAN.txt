FOOD: flan

An Earth dessert of molded custard topped with caramel syrup, kind of like a tart. 
This dessert happens to be a favorite of Miles O'Brien, who's wife, Keiko, once served flan to a replicant of Miles who was unaware he was a replicant. The replicant didn't eat it.
