FOOD: Yridian brandy

The species known as information brokers are also adept at alcoholic distilling. Worf had kept a bottle of Yridian brandy for a special occasion - and opened it after meeting Kor. 

Reference: The Sword of Kahless 
Use a cola syrup or a supermarket cola, lightened with either ginger ale or plain water.
