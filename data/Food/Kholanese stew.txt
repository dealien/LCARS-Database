FOOD: Kholanese stew

A stew that Quark nominally served from his food replicator. A bad batch, ordered when DS9's malfunctioning replicators almost drove away business, so disgusted the Markalian customer Asoth that the alien man forced the soup down Quark's throat. 

Reference: Babel
