FOOD: Trellian crepes

Dish of thin, egg-based pancakes wrapped with mushrooms and spices. Much thinner than omelettes.
Kes' favorte meal, always prepared on Wednesdays by Neelix during her stay. They included mushrooms, and Tuvok prepared the dish less spicy to let the flavor through.
