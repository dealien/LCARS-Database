FOOD: butter

The yellowish product made from churning cream, a longtime Terran spread. Ensign Wildman introduced Kes to mashed potatoes and butter, which became one of the meals she pigged out on at the onset of her premature elogium.
Rhombolian butter is often used to saute jumbo Vulcan mollusks, as Quark did in his recipe for a meal for Fallit Kot.

Reference: Melora, Elogium
