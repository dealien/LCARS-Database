FOOD: Kohlanese barley

A Kohlanese grain. 
A foodstuff from the same origin as Kohlanese stew which the "de-evolved" Zek pledged to sell to a planet devastated by solar flares. In mid-2371 the market value of 2,000 tons was 189 bars of gold-press latinum.
