FOOD: Spring Wine

A red Bajoran beverage.

Minister Shakaar Edon offured some profusely to Major Kira Nerys on their date to persuade her to attend a Cardassian conference to share Bajoran intelligence on the Klingons. Gul Dukat later offered Kira his last bottle during their Ravinok mission; he misses having it easily available. 

Reference: Return to Grace
