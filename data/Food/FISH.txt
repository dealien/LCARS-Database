FOOD: fish

A waterbound animal of various types often served as an entree in many cultures.
Neelix serves bits of an otherwise unspecified Terran fish with rice in a replicated ginger seasoning to Japanese officer Nogami of the " '37s." 
Fish byproducts are also used in meals and as entree's, like caviar (fish eggs).
Some types of popular fish entree's are:
caviar
salmon
sushi
tuna
vermicula
