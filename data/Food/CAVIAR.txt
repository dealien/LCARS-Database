FOOD: caviar

Fish eggs. A Terran delicacy often served with cheese spreads. Typically from the Sturgeon fish, one of the most noted kinds are from the Beluga. A variety found in the Caspian Sea is among Captain Jean-Luc Picard's favorites. Disappointed by the replicator's version, he kept several cases aboard for special occasions, such as Kurn's welcome buffet in 2366.
