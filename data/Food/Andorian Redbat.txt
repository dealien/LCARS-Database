FOOD: Andorian Redbat

Apparently, a flying mammal of Andor considered a delicacy. In perhaps some kind of stunt, Ben Sisko and Curzon Dax once saw science officer Kustanovich of their U.S.S. Livingston eat eight helpings of Redbat.
