SHIPS: Sh'Raan
CLASS: Suurok

A Vulcan combat cruiser, the Sh'Raan came to the Enterprise NX-01's assistance when it was about to be attacked by pursuing Mazarites. 
The Sh'Raan then transported Vulcan Ambassador V'Lar to safety.
