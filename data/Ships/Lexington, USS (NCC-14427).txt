SHIPS: Lexington, U.S.S. 
CLASS: Excelsior
TYPE: heavy cruiser
REGISTRY: NCC-14427

The second starship to bear the name. This ship rendezvoused with the U.S.S. Enterprise-D to transfer medical supplies bound for the Taranko colony in 2370.
