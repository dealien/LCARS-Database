SHIPS: Nerada

Captain Alben commanded this Nasari vessel. In 2373, Harry Kim launched an unauthorized attack on the Nerada, saying he somehow sensed it was going to fire on Voyager. Later, it was learned that Alben did indeed plan an attack, as he believed Kim to be a Taresian.
