SHIPS: Ulysses, U.S.S.
CLASS: Nebula
REGISTRY: NCC-66808

Commanded by Captain Entebe. Starship studying protoplanetary masses in the Helaspont Nebula ca. SD 48962.5 - the closest ship to the Defiant and an apparent Tzenkethi attack on Barisa Prime. The attack was later found to be a Founder-engineered ruse. 

CREW MANIFEST:
Captain: Entebe

Reference: The Adversary 
Its name honors the hero of Greek mythology, the king of Ithaca, and a leader of the Greeks in the Trojan War.
