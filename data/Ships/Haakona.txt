SHIPS: Haakona, R.S.E.
CLASS: D'deridex

A Romulan warbird commanded by Subcommander Taris. The Haakona intervene when the Federation starships U.S.S. Yamato and U.S.S. Enterprise-D violated the Romulan neutral zone in 2365 while in search of the planet Iconia. The Haakona, along with the Enterprise-D, was nearly destroyed by the Iconian software weapon that did destroy the Yamato.
