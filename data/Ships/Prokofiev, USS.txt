SHIPS: Prokofiev, U.S.S.
CLASS: Andromeda
REGISTRY: NCC-68814

One of three starships ordered to the Demilitarized Zone border during the tensions surrounding O'Brien's arrest by the Cardassians. 

Reference: Tribunal
Its namesake is the 20th century Terran Russian composer Sergei Prokofiev (1891-1953), best known for the symphonic fairy tale "Peter and the Wolf."
