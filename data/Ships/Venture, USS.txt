SHIPS: Venture, U.S.S.
CLASS: Galaxy
TYPE: heavy cruiser
REGISTRY: NCC-71854

Leader of a six-ship relief force for Deep Space Nine. The Venture helped DS9 deal with Klingons wanting Cardassian leaders handed over in 2372. The vessel's captain was a friend of Captain Benjamin Sisko's, who offered Worf a posting on his ship if Worf decided not to stay with the station. 

Reference: The Way of the Warrior
