SHIPS: P`Rang, I.K.S.

Shortly after stardate 42901.3, this Klingon vessel was ordered to intercept the T'Ong, a pre-Alliance sleeper ship now awakened, and convince it not to prey upon defenseless and unsuspecting Federation colonies.
