SHIPS: Renegade, U.S.S.
CLASS: New Orleans
REGISTRY: NCC-63102

Captain Tryla Scott commanded this vessel in 2364, when it met up with the U.S.S. Enterprise-D at Dytallix B. It helped collect information about an alien intelligence attempting to take over Starfleet command.
