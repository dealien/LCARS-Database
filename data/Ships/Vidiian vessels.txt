FLEET: Vidiian vessels

Massive cruisers designed with a control room of open modular design and an emphasis on isolation of crew, befitting the phage danger. Only two man the control room, a commander and surgeon sitting opposite sides of a console. They leave an ion trail. 

Reference: The Phage, Deadlock
