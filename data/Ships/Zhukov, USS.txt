SHIPS: Zhukov, U.S.S.
CLASS: Ambassador
TYPE: heavy cruiser
REGISTRY: NCC-62136

In 2367 it ferried Vulcan Ambassador T'Pel to the Enterprise before she was revealed as a deep-cover, long-time Romulan spy. This was also Lieutenant Reginald Barclay's assignment just prior to the U.S.S. Enterprise-D.
2369: The starship dropped off at least five science teams to the U.S.S. Enterprise-D to study the uncharted Phoenix Cluster, including two exobiologists and three stellar cartographers. 

CREW MANIFEST:
Starship Commander: Captain Gleason

Reference: Hollow Pursuits 
The ship was named after Russian general Grigori Konstantinovich Zhukov (1896-1974).
