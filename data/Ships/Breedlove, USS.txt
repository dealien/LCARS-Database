SHIPS: U.S.S. Breedlove
CLASS: Galaxy
TYPE: heavy cruiser
REGISTRY:

Federation starship. In 2371, the Breedlove was directed to Starbase 1 after another U.S.S. Defiant was found frozen in an asteroid.
