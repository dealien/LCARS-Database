SHIPS: Aldara
CLASS: Galor

Under the command of Gul Danar, this warship patrolled the Bajoran border, placing it within the Second Order and under the direct command of Gul Dukat. This vessel was encountered when it pursued Bajoran terrorist Tahna Los into Bajoran space and to Deep Space Nine in 2369. Danar discovered Tahna after he stole an antimatter converter from a Cardassian depot, and knowing that Tahna was a member of the Kohn-ma terrorist organization he demanded the extradition of the accused criminal.
