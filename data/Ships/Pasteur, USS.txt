SHIPS: Pasteur, U.S.S.
CLASS: Olympic
TYPE: medical frigate
REGISTRY: NCC-58928

"I WILL GIVE NO DEADLY MEDICINE TO ANYONE IF ASKED, NOR SUGGEST ANY SUCH COUNSEL" ~ Hippocratic Oath
A hospital ship with a distinctive spheroid primary hull
In a future representation the ship was commanded by Captain Beverly Crusher. Circa stardate 47988.1 - which was destoyed by a core breach after being attacked by two Klingon attack cruisers inside their territory. 

Reference: All Good Things... 
Its name honors Louis Pasteur (1822-1895), the French chemist who founded the science of modern microbiology, invented the process knows as pasteurization, and developed several vaccines. The Pasteur miniature was deisgned and built by Industrial Light & Magic art director Bill George. An early version of the dedication plaque indicated the Pasteur as being a Hope-class vessel.
