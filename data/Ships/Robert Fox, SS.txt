SHIPS: Robert Fox, S.S.
CLASS: Whorfin
TYPE: transport

The Robert Fox and the S.S. Lakul carried El-Aurian refugees in 2294. Both ships were caught in and torn apart by the nexus energy ribbon. Some of the passengers from the Lakul were saved by the Enterprise-B. 

Reference: Generations 
The name of this ship was not mentioned in dialog but comes from a screen display on the science officer's console aboard the Enterprise-B bridge. The name was suggested by movie co-writer Ronald D. Moore.
