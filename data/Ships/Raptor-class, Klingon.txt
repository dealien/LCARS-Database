CLASS: Raptor
TYPE: scout

Type of Klingon scout ship in use during the 2100s. A Raptor is equipped to carry a crew of a dozen officers and men, and it also features a galley. The hull of a Raptor-class scout ship is comprised of a coherent, molecular alloy, that is reinforced and twice as thick as that of the Enterprise NX-01. 
In 2151, the Enterprise crew discovered the derelict Raptor, the Somraw.
