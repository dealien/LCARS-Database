SHIPS: Sticky Fingers

Ferengi trading vessel commanded by Daimon Idris.  The ship docked at Deep Space Nine in 2370 to deliver a shipment of holotapes to Quark.  "Sticky Fingers" was the Federation translation of the Ferengi markings on the ship, which also translated as "Pickpocket".
