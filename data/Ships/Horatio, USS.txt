SHIPS: Horatio, U.S.S.
CLASS: Ambassador
REGISTRY: NCC-10532

Commanded by Captain Walker Keel. Picard's old friend who arranged the secret meeting of four captains on Dytallix B. The ship imploded as it pulled away from that meeting, cementing Picard's fears that Keel's story of conspiracy within Starfleet was true.
