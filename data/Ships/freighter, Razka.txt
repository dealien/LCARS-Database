SHIPS: Razka Karn's vessel 
TYPE: freighter

The beat-up ship of the Bajoran junk dealer in 2367. He found a piece of wreckage from the Cardassian transport Ravinok that crash landed in Cardassian space while transporting Bajoran citizens to a new labor camp. Razka knew that Major Kira would be interested since one of the passengers was Li Nalas, a noted resistance fighter. Razka was helpful to the resistance during the occupation of Bajor.
