SHIPS: Rak-Minunis
TYPE: freighter

A Kobheerian ship that traveled a trade route along the Cardassian-Federation border. On one of its voyages it transported Marritza to Deep Space Nine. 
With the U.S.S. Defiant disguised through shield harmonics to a Cardassian patrol, Commander Sisko claimed the ship was this Kobheerian freighter.
 
Reference: Second Skin, Duet
