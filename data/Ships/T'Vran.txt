SHIPS: T`Vran

A Vulcan spacecraft that offered to assist the U.S.S. Ganges Runabout from Deep Space Nine following the destruction of a Miradorn vessel in the Chamra Nebula in 2369. It later quietly transported harassed Rakhari fugitive Croden and his daughter to their freedom on planet Vulcan.
