SHUTTLECRAFT: Curie
TYPE: 6
REGISTRY: NCC-1701-D-3

The replacement Enterprise Shuttlecraft 3 for the Justman, named for the Terran Maire Curie (AD 1867-1934) who discovered radium and radioactivity. Worf used the shuttle to attend a bat'leth tournament in 2370. The ship was lost when Joret Dal, a Cardassian double agent, was sent back across the border with Ensign Sito.
