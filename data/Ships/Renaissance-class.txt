CLASS: Renaissance
TYPE: medium cruiser

Type of Federation starship and variant of the Constellation-class. The U.S.S. Aries and U.S.S. Hornet were of this class. In 2365, William Riker was offered command of the Aries. 

SHIPS OF THE LINE:
U.S.S. Renaissance, NX-
U.S.S. Aries, NCC-
U.S.S. Hornet, NCC-

Reference: The Icarus Factor, Redemption
Named for te period in European history when civilization as we know it had a dramatic rebirth, or renaissance.
