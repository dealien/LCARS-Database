SHIPS: Valdemar, U.S.S.
CLASS: Ambassador
REGISTRY: NCC-26198

One of three starships ordered to the Demilitarized Zone border during the tensions surrounding O'Brien's arrest by the Cardassians on stardate 47944.2. 

Reference: Tribunal 
Named for the Terran Danish king Valdemar I (1131-82). Also named for Valdemar Poulsen, the inventor of the first tape recorder.
