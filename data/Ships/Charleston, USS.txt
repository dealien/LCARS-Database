SHIPS: Charleston, U.S.S.
CLASS: Excelsior
REGISTRY: NCC-42285

An Earth-bound ship that the Enterprise, out near the Neutral Zone, offers as transport home for its three 20th-century passengers. The trip to Earth will be of several months, though, on top of the Charleston's unexpected delay at Arloff IX.
