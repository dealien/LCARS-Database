SHIPS: M'Char, I.K.S.
CLASS: K'Vort
TYPE: light cruiser

Klingon bird-of-prey ("EMM-charr") assigned to patrol the Cardassian-Federation border. Commanded by Commander Kaybok until he was executed in 2372 after refusing to fire on the U.S.S. Defiant when it challenged him for tractoring the freighter Xhosa for a search.
