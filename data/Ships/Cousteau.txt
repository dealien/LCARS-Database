SHUTTLECRAFT: Cousteau
TYPE: Captain’s Yacht (custom)
REGISTRY: NCC-1701-E-

"THE FUTURE IS IN THE HANDS OF THOSE WHO EXPLORE... AND FROM ALL THE BEAUTY THEY DISCOVER WHILE CROSSING PERPETUALLY RECEDING FRONTIERS, THEY DEVELOP FOR NATURE AND FOR HUMANKIND AN INFINATE LOVE," ~ Jacques Eves Couteau.
The name of the Enterprise-E's captain's yacht. In 2375, the craft was destroyed above the Ba'ku homeworld. 

Reference: Insurrection
The captain's yacht of the previous Enterprise was named Calypso, the naval ship of Mr. Cousteau.
