SHIPS: Sepulo
TYPE: transport

Transport that was the third Ferengi ship within 18 hours to arrive with attendees for Grand Nagus Zek's conference on future business in the Gamma Quadrant; the first was likely Zek's ship itself. It was moored at Docking Bay 5. 

Reference: The Nagus
