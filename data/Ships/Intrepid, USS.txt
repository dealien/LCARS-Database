SHIPS: U.S.S. Intrepid 
CLASS: Constitution
TYPE: Heavy cruiser
REGISTRY: NCC-1631

This Federation starship was built over Vulcan and had an all-Vulcan crew. It served as a science vessel.

Timeline:
2267: the Intrepid had been given second priority after the U.S.S. Enterprise when both ships were docked in maintenance section 18 at Starbase 11. 
2268: The starship was destroyed by a gigantic spaceborne amoeba creature near the Gamma 7A System.

Reference: The Immunity Syndrome, Court Martial, Strangers from the Sky
