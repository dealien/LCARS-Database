SHIPS: Cairo, U.S.S.
CLASS: Excelsior

The Cairo was commanded by Captain Edward Jellico.
In 2369 it transported Vice-Admiral Alynna Nechayev to a rendezvous with the Enterprise-D and transferred command of that ship to Captain Jellico temporarily while Picard was sent on a mission on Cardassia Prime.
