SHIPS: Khazara, R.S.E.
CLASS: D'deridex

Commander Toreth captained this Romulan warbird. The Khazara was used by Subcommander N'Vek and Deanna Troi to transport Romulan defector Vice-Proconsul M'ret to the Federation. 

Reference: Face of the Enemy
