SHUTTLECRAFT: Drake
TYPE: 8
REGISTRY: NCC-74656-

A shuttlecraft attached to the U.S.S Voyager. It was destroyed when Ensign Harry Kim encountered a temporal anomaly in 2372, sending him to an alternate reality where he never served on the U.S.S. Voyager.
