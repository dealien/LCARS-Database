SHIPS: U.S.S. Idaho
CLASS: Excelsior
TYPE: medium cruiser
REGISTRY: NCC-

Federation Starship. The captain of the Idaho, Kiser, responded to Deep Space Nine's distress call concerning subspace distortions and a possible attack from Cardassians in 2370.
