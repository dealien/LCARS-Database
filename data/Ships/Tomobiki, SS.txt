SHIPS: Tomobiki, S.S.
REGISTRY: RT-2203

Under the command of Captain Peter Lauritson, This deep space explorer made an expedition to the Ficus Sector in 2119.

Reference: Up the Long Ladder
The ship was read from a list taken off the Enterprise-D's monitor. The captain actually is one of the Star Trek producers and his name was included as one of the many jokes around the set; the list was full of them.
