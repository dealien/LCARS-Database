SHUTTLECRAFT: Goddard
TYPE:
REGISTRY: NCC-1701-D-

A shuttle, its number unspecified but known not to be Shuttlecraft 4, that ferries Worf and Data to the disabled Romulan ship - along with the interphased Ro and LaForge, and later "loaned" to retired Starfleet engineer Montgomery Scott. 

Reference: The Next Phase
The Goddard was named for American rocket scientist Robert H. Goddard (1882-1945), inventor of the liquid-fueled rocket.
