PLANET: Daribund
SYSTEM:
SECTOR:
QUADRANT:

A planet discovered by Emil Carridine. It was enormously rich in dilithium, but any mining enterprise would have threatened Daribund’s pre-sentient species. The planet was also the home of a sentient species that had not yet achieved space-flight when Carridine discovered it.
