PLANET: Cestus III
SYSTEM: Cestus
SECTOR:
QUADRANT: Beta

Federation colony that was attacked by a Gorn starship in 2267. After intervention by the Metron Consortium a border was negotiated and a treaty was signed. In 2271, the colony was resettled by both humans and Gorn and admitted to the United Federation of Planets.

Reference: Arena, Requiem, Star Charts
Requiem established that the colony was ceded to the Gorn Hegemony, but several episodes of DS9 contradicted that.
