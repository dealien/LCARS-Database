SYSTEM: Eris Alpha
SECTOR:
QUADRANT:

A binary system.  The larger star in the system in Eris Alpha Prime, which is orbited by several planets, including Eris Alpha III.  First charted by Enterprise NX01 on January 13, 2152.  Whenever the two stars reach perihelion, there is a flare-up of radiation across the EM spectrum lasting two hours.
