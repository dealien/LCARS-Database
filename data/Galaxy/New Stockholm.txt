PLANET: New Stockholm
SYSTEM:
SECTOR:
QUADRANT:

Federation colony. Erik Hansen came from there and offered Ensign Jiterica to show her around should she ever decide to visit it. As mentioned in the timeline, it is assumed that New Stockholm is the birthplace of Magnus Hansen.
