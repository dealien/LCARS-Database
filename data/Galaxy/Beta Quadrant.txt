QUADRANT: Beta 

Designation given to a quarter of the galaxy which includes the Klingon and Romulan Empires, parts of the United Federation of Planets and the Gorn Hegemony. 
In 2345, the U.S.S. Stargazer was hailed by a Gorn ship in the Beta Quadrant.   

Reference: Requiem
