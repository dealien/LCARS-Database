MEDICAL: anti-hallucinogen

An unspecified drug prescribed as a thrice-daily dose of 30 mg for O'Brien to take the edge off the hallucinations and depression stemming from his virtual-world imprisonment on Argratha. Bashir ordered it for a month before realizing their emotional cause.
