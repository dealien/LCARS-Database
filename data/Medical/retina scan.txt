MEDICAL: retina scan

Also known as a retinal imaging scan. Medical examination enabling the physician to verify the presence or absence of visual cortex activity. It scanned an individuals blood vessel pattern within the retina. As a security procedure is proved to be a more reliable way of detecting a persons identity than voice or dermal scans. 
Kirk had to submit to a retina scan to gain access to the Project Genesis files. Bajoran security also used retinal imaging routines.

Reference: The Wrath of Khan, The Passenger, Collaborator
The various data sources created separate entries for the medical and security applications of this procedure but they were combined into a single entry.
