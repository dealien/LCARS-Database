MEDICAL: Delactovine

Used to stabilize a patient undergoing seizures or shock. Dr. Crusher used 2 cc in a hypospray on Captain Picard when he went into convulsions amid signs of massive body tissue failure when the Kataanan beam was cut off. The drug had no effect in this case.
