MEDICAL: parasitic infection

Otherwise undescribed disease which approximated death in a patient -- possibly a Bajoran if Doctor Bashir's off-station medical mission is short-ranged -- and even fooled a tricorder until Doctor Bashir intuitively found the infection near the Scapula Node.
