PERSONNEL: Kara
Planetary leader, Sigma Draconis VI

The chief of the Eymorg. She installed Spock's brain in a computer system controlling the underground complex on Sigma Draconis VI in 2268. Kara used the Teacher to accomplish this. 

Reference: Spock's Brain 
Played by Marj Dusay.
