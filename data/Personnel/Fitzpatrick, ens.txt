PERSONNEL: Fitzpatrick
Ensign, U.S.S. Voyager

Member of the U.S.S. Voyager crew. Fitzpatrick is occasionally assigned to assist Neelix in the ship's mess hall. On Stardate 51501.4, he received a letter from home in a datastream transmission sent by Starfleet through a Hirogen relay station.
