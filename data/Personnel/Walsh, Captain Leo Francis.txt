PERSONNEL: Walsh, Captain Leo Francis

Alias used by Harcourt Fenton Mudd. Officially the owner and pilot of a small spacecraft "borrowed" by Mudd. Under questioning, Mudd claimed to be Walsh. Mudd later testified that Walsh had become unexpectedly incapacitated. 

Reference: Mudd's Women
