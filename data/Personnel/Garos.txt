PERSONNEL: Garos

Posed as an Akaali in order to conduct a covert Malurian mining operation. 
He owned a curio shop on the Akaali planet that served as a front for the operation. Garos was eventually found out by Captain Archer and the crew of the Enterprise NX-01. 

Reference: Civilization
Portrayed by Wade Andrew Williams.
