PERSONNEL: John Hengist
City Administrator, Argelius II

An unassuming man hijacked on Rigel IV by an energy lifeforce that committed grisly murders there and on Argelius II to generate fear, which it fed on. Mr. Hengist was exposed and transported into space using wide dispersal, putting an end to the entity and centuries of terror. 

Reference: Wolf in the Fold 
Played by John Fiedler. The character's first name is conjectural and based on the actor's real name.
