PERSONNEL: Sam Lavelle
Lieutenant jg, operations officer, U.S.S. Enterprise-E

In 2370 he and Ensign Sito vied for a promotion and a posting to ops. Lavelle was advanced after Sito died. He was transferred to the U.S.S. Enterprise when that ship was launched.
