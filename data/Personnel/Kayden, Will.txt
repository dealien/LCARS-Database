PERSONNEL: Will Kayden
Crewmember, U.S.S. Rutledge

Nicknamed "Stompie" by his shipmates, which included Captain Benjamin Maxwell, and Chief Miles O'Brien. Kayden was killed in 2347 during the battle with the Cardassians at Setlik III. Kayden used to sing, 'The Minstrel Boy.'
