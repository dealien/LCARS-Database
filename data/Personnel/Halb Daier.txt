PERSONNEL: Halb Daier

Male Bajoran food server on Terok Nor. Halb recruited Kira Nerys into his resistance cell during her Orb of Time experience, arming her with an ultritum resin bomb to kill Gul Dukat. 

Reference: Wrongs Darker than Death or Night
