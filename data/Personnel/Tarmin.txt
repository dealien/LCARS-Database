PERSONNEL: Tarmin

White-haired Ullian gentleman leading a team of telepathic researchers collecting memories for a cultural database. While in transit aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise-D in 2368, Tarmin learned his son, Jev, was guilty of telepathic memory invasion rape. He personally had been implicated in three cases of the crime but was cleared of the charges when it was learned that he was not at all locations involved but his son was; and attempted to frame his father.

Reference: Violations 
Portrayed by David Sage.
