PERSONNEL: Arell Dickerson
Lieutenant, security officer, U.S.S. Enterprise

Security officer aboard the original U.S.S. Enterprise. On occasion Lt. Dickerson also sounded the bosun's pipe for the arrival of visiting dignitaries, such as the Excaliban re-creation of President Abraham Lincoln in 2269. 

Reference: The Savage Curtain 
Played by Arell Blanton. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
