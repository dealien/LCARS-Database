PERSONNEL: Latha Mabrin

A Shakaar resistance cell operative who became a vedek after the occupation. In 2373 a hunter probe, sent by Silaran Prin, killed him at the Calash Retreat. 

Reference: The Darkness and the Light 
Played by Matt Roe.
