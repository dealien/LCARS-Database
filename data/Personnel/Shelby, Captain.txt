PERSONNEL: Julia Shelby
Captain, starship commander, U.S.S. Sutherland

The U.S.S. Sutherland's commanding officer and Jadzia Dax's friend. Jadzia called in one of the several favors owed her by Shelby to get Lieutenant Atoa the free time needed to be at Jadzia's pre-wedding party.
