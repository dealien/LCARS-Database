PERSONNEL: Rishon Uxbridge

Human female botanist, married to Kevin Uxbridge, who also composed tao-classical music. She died, aged 82, on Delta Rana IV during a Husnock attack on her colony. Her husband Kevin retained an image of her after death. 

Reference: The Survivors 
Portrayed by Anne Haney whom also played Renora, the Bajoran judge in "Dax."
