PERSONNEL: Lyris

Bureaucratic officer on Taresia. She persuaded Harry Kim that he was a Taresian indigene as part of a scheme to reap genetic matter for their propagation program. 

Reference: Favorite Son
Portrayed by Deborah May. The Continuum featured a different set of characters in their entry by mistake.
