PERSONNEL: Rumplestiltskin

This fictional dwarf, created by Earth's Brothers Grimm, turned straw into gold. An image of Rumplestiltskin appeared on Deep Space Nine in 2369, but he was actually a Gamma Quadrant alien. 

Reference: If Wishes Were Horses 
Played by Michael John Anderson.
