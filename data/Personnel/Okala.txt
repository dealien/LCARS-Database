PERSONNEL: Okala
Bajoran officer serving on Deep Space 9. Okala was working under Worf in Ops in 2373 when she detected unusual EM signatures on the hull of Captain Sisko's runabout. 

Reference: Things Past 
Played by Louahn Lowe.
