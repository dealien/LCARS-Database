PERSONNEL: Mauna Apgar

Widow to Tanugan scientist Dr. Nel Apgar. Mauna accused Commander William Riker of being responsible for the explosion that killed her husband in 2366.

Reference: A Matter of Perspective
Played by Gina Hecht.
