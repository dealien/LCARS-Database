PERSONNEL: Ginger

Holographic blonde showgirl in the Vic Fontaine holographic program. Ginger, and friend Melissa, double-dated with Vic and Odo. Ginger likened Odo's piano playing to Liberace - with whom she had performed. 

Reference: His Way
Portrayed by Cyndi Pass.
