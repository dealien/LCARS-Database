PERSONNEL: Kleg

Officious Ledosian flying instructor in charge of meting out piloting lessons and tests to those who break the laws of his planet. Kleg takes Tom Paris in his charge soon after the Voyager pilot transgresses the Ledosian airspace laws. 

Reference: Natural Law
Portrayed by Neil C Vipond.
