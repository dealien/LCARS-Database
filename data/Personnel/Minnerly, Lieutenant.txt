PERSONNEL: Minnerly
Lieutenant, security officer, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

Martial-arts and kick-boxing expert serving on the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701-D in 2364. Minnerly was to have competed against Tasha Yar in a martial-arts competition on the ship. While Yar was favored in the ship's pool, Yar admits that his kick-boxing skills would be a challenge. Yar was killed before the match.
