PERSONNEL: Tahna Los

Bajoran freedom fighter; a contemporary of Kira Nerys. Tahna's faction, the Kohn-Ma, despised Federation aid as much as the Cardassian occupation. Tahna hoped destroying the wormhole would eliminate Bajor's strategic value. 

Reference: Past Prologue 
Played by Jeffrey Nordling.
