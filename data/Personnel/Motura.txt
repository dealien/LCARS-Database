PERSONNEL: Motura

Vidiian individual, a noted sculptor on his homeworld, who received a lung of Neelix's in 2371 to allay the organ-depleting phage. Motura also recounted the devastating effect of the affliction on his civilization to Janeway. 

Reference: Phage
Portrayed by Stephen Rappaport.
