PERSONNEL: Mabus

One of the Trabe prisoners on the Kazon planet Sobras. Claiming to want peace, Mabus gained protection from Janeway and the U.S.S. Voyager, but instead used a treaty conference to attempt a massacre of all the Kazon Majes. 

Reference: Alliances
Portrayed by Charles O Lucia.
