PERSONNEL: Kinis
Lieutenant, executive officer, Starfleet Headquarters

Vulcan assistant to Admiral Bullock at Starfleet Headquarters in Species 8472's invasion training re-creation. Kinis continued this role on Terrasphere Eight following a rapprochement negotiated by Captain Janeway.
