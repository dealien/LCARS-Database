PERSONNEL: Hagath

Aristocratic but merciless arms merchant, working for the Regent of Palamar. Hagath wanted Quark to supply a biogenic weapon capable of killing 28 million people. Quark duped both Hagath and his enemy, General Nassuc, averting mass destruction. 

Reference: Business as Usual
