PERSONNEL: Kyle Riker

Father of Starfleet officer William T. Riker. Kyle served as a civilian advisor to Starfleet during the 2353 Tholian conflict. The elder Riker was also once romantically linked to Dr. Katherine Pulaski. 

Reference: The Icarus Factor 
Portrayed by Mitchell Ryan.
