PERSONNEL: Devil

A mythic figure in several Earth cultures. The Devil, or Satan, was an angel who fell from grace with God and came to rule the underworld, where the sinful would be punished for all eternity. In 2367, Ardra appeared on planet Ventax II as the Devil, while trying to impress the Ventaxians with her allegedly supernatural powers.
