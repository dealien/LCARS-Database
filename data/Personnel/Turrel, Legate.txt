PERSONNEL: Turrel
Legate, diplomatic officer, Deep Space Nine

Cardassian Central Command official. Turrel and Vedek Bareil spent five months outlining a peace treaty between Bajor and Cardassia that Winn and Turrel finalized and signed on Deep Space 9 in 2371. 

Reference: Life Support
