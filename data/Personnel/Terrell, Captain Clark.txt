PERSONNEL: Clark Terrell
Captain, starship commander, U.S.S. Reliant

In 2285, Terrell was subjugated by Khan Noonien Singh's brain-eating Ceti eel. The information was used to help steal Project Genesis. Terrell later killed himself rather than obey orders to execute Kirk. 

Reference: The Wrath of Khan 
Played by Paul Winfield, who also played Captain Dathon in "Darmok."
