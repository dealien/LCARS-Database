PERSONNEL: Sylvia

Name adopted by a lifeform from Pyris VII who was overtaken by sensations after the transmuter turned her into a human female. Sylvia attempted to woo James Kirk in 2267. 

Reference: Catspaw 
Played by Antoinette Bower. The Continuum also credits Bonnie Beacher- but she played a different Sylvia in a different episode., and the website is mistaken. They also placed images of this character with the other Sylvia's entry, also by mistake.
