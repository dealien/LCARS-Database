PERSONNEL: Eneg

A Zeon rebel opposed to the dictatorship on planet Ekos. Eneg's work as an undercover operative within the planet's Nazi regime was instrumental in its eventual overthrow in 2268. 

Reference: Patterns of Force 
Played by Patrick Horgan. Eneg is Gene spelled backwards.
