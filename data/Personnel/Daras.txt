PERSONNEL: Daras

Member of the underground resistance on planet Ekos. Posing as a government sympathizer, Daras pretended to betray her father to the Nazi party in 2268, and was presented the Iron Cross award by Deputy Fuhrer Melakon. Her actions were actually part of the Ekosians' efforts to discredit Melakon and the Nazis. 

Reference: Patterns of Force 
Played by Valora Noland.
