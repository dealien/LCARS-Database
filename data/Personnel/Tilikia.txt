PERSONNEL: Tilikia
Crewman, freighter crew

Young Boslic crew member on a freighter docked at Deep Space 9. Tilikia was unlucky enough to have his cargo inspected by Kurn during the Klingon's brief stint as a Bajoran Security officer. 

Reference: Sons of Mogh
Portrayed by Dell Yount. The character's name was not mentioned on air, but was written into the script.
