PERSONNEL: Ramirez
Captain, starship commander, U.S.S. Valiant
Captain of the U.S.S. Valiant in 2374 when Ramirez and his Red Squad cadets engaged the Cardassians. The crew suffered heavy losses and Ramirez was fatally wounded. His command passed on to Tim Watters.
