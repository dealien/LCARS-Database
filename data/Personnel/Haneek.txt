PERSONNEL: Haneek

Red-haired female Skrreean refugee leader who, after discovering the Bajoran wormhole, came to Deep Space 9 in 2370 believing Bajor to be the long-promised Skrreean homeworld. Her people ultimately settled on Draylon II instead. 

Reference: Sanctuary 
Played by Deborah May.
