PERSONNEL: Morta

Member of an outlaw Ferengi band commanded by DaiMon Lurin in 2369. Morta helped seize the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701-D, but was defeated by officers who had been transformed into children. 

Reference: Rascals 
Portayed by Michael Snyder whom also played Dax in "The Undiscovered Country" and Qol in "The Perfect Mate."
