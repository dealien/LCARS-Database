PERSONNEL: Pirak
Gul, station commander, Bajor

Pirak killed 15 farmers for not properly displaying a Cardassian banner. Pirak's family was killed and his servant injured when Kira Nerys sent a deadly plasma charge. 

Reference: The Darkness and the Light
