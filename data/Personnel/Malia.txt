PERSONNEL: Malia

One of the Taresian females who welcomed Harry Kim to their society in 2373, convincing him that he was a long-lost Taresian. After observing the effects of Malia's honeymoon on her mate Taymon, Harry made a hasty escape to Voyager. 

Reference: Favorite Son
Portrayed by Kristanna Loken.
