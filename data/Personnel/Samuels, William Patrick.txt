PERSONNEL: William Patrick Samuels
farmer, resistance fighter, Volon II

His planet was ceded to the Cardassians, prompting him to become a member of the Maquis rebel group. In 2370, Samuels killed himself while in the custody of Gul Evek. 

Reference: The Maquis
Played by Michael A. Krawic.
