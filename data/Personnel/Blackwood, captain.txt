PERSONNEL: Blackwood
Captain, starship commander, U.S.S. Tombaugh

Commander of Starfleet's U.S.S. Tombaugh until being assimilated by the Borg in circa 2362. Blackwood was known for running a tight ship and making life nightmarish for new recruits.
