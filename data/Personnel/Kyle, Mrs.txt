PERSONNEL: Jennifer Kyle

Alexander Rozhenko's teacher on the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701-D. She led a father-son field trip to Biolab 4. Kyle reported Alexander to be a liar, thief, and bully and recommended counseling. 

Reference: New Ground
Played by Jennifer Edwards. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
