PERSONNEL: Golin Shel-la
sect leader, 

Hapless head of the Ennis, a people confined to a prison moon in the Gamma Quadrant with their enemies, the Nol-Ennis. Neither side could die, so the fight never ended. 

Reference: Battle Lines 
Played by Jonathan Banks.
