PERSONNEL: David Johnson
Lieutenant, security officer, U.S.S. Enterprise

A crewman who was injured while fighting with the Klingons in 2268 while under the control of the Beta XII-A entity that fed on anger and hate. Johnson's critical wounds healed quickly so that he could fight again.

Reference: Day of the Dove
Played by David L. Ross, who also played Lieutenant Galloway in "A taste of Armegeddon" and "The Omega Glory." The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
