PERSONNEL: Dianna Giddings
Crewmember, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

Beautiful crewmember who attracted the attentions of Thadiun Okona. She was also a good friend of Keiko O'Brien.

Reference: The Outrageous Okona, Power Play
Played by Lorine Mendell. Although the character's name was never mentioned, it comes from the name on the plague on her door to her quarters. This character was a regular stand-in.
