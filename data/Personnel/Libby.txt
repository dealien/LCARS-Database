PERSONNEL: Libby

In 2371, Libby was engaged to marry Harry Kim. The marriage never took place because Kim's ship, the U.S.S. Voyager was presumed lost with all hands. Libby likes Ktarian music. 

Reference: Non Sequitur
Portrayed by Jennifer Gatti whom also played Ba'el in "Birthright."
