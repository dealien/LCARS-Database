PERSONNEL: Calvin Hutchinson
Commander, executive officer, Arkaria Base

"Hutch" was a short, balding man, and undisputed master of "small talk." Murdered in 2369 by mercenaries during a reception for U.S.S. Enterprise-D senior officers while their ship was being serviced by the Remmler Array.

Reference: Starship Mine 
Played by David Spielberg.
