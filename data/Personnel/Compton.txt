PERSONNEL: Geoffrey Compton

Enterprise crew member who died in 2268 when he was subjected to biochemical hyperacceleration on planet Scalos. The Scalosians exposed him to their water in an attempt to use him as a surrogate for their own sterile males, but he quickly sustained fatal cellular damage. 

Reference: Wink of an Eye 
Played by Geoffrey Binney. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
