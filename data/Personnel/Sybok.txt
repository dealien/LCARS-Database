PERSONNEL: Sybok

Brilliant, charismatic, and misguided Vulcan who used Nimbus III in 2287 as a springboard for a mission to locate Sha Ka Ree and God. Sybok was Spock's older, estranged half-brother. 

Reference: The Final Frontier 
Played by Lawrence Luckinbill.
