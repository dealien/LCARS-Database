PERSONNEL: William Telfer
Crewman, science officer, U.S.S. Voyager

Hypochondriac on U.S.S. Voyager. Seven claimed Telfer squandered resources with weekly sickbay visits that always proved negative. Telfer used medical exemptions to avoid away missions. 

Reference: Good Shepherd
Portrayed by Michael Reisz.
