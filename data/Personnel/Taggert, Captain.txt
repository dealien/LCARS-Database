PERSONNEL: Taggert
Captain, starship commander, U.S.S. Repulse

Commander of the Starfleet vessel U.S.S. Repulse NCC-2544. Taggert was not happy to lose his chief medical officer, Dr. Katherine Pulaski, when she opted to join the U.S.S. Enterprise. Taggert spoke highly of Pulaski, and once noted he would have given her a shuttlecraft if it would have kept her aboard his ship.

Reference: Unnatural Selection
