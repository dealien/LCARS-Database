PERSONNEL: Ihat

Character in D'Arsay mythology. An image of Ihat was stored in the D'Arsay archive. Ihat was one of the characters portrayed when the archive programmed him to reenact the Masaka legend in 2370. Ihat was the first personality to appear and acted as a "spokesman." He was the first to allude to the nature of Masaka and her subjects.

Reference: Masks
