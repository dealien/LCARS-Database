PERSONNEL: Groppler Zorn
Planetary leader, Deneb IV

Deneb IV native and the high official of the Bandi people. Zorn forced a massive shapeshifting life form to become a starbase and then offered those facilities to Federation representatives. 

Reference: Encounter at Farpoint
Played by Michael Bell
