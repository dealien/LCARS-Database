PERSONNEL: Tamal
resistance fighter (Maquis)

Maquis terrorist. Tamal, Kalita, and Thomas Riker appropriated the U.S.S. Defiant NX-74205 from Deep Space Nine in 2371 and headed for the Orias system. Tamal controlled tactical systems, including the cloaking device. 

Reference: Defiant 
Played by Michael Canavan.
