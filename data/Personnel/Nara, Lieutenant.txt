PERSONNEL: Nara 
Lieutenant, engineering officer, U.S.S. Enterprise 

Humanoid officer aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701-D in 2370. She was supervisor of the nacelle control room, putting her in charge of Daniel Kwan. She was interviewed after his suicide. 

Reference: Eye of the Beholder
