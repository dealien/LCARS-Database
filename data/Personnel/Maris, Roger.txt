PERSONNEL: Roger Maris

Baseball player during America's twentieth century. Maris eclipsed Babe Ruth's single-season record for hitting home runs in 1961, an achievement that was not surpassed until 1998. An extremely rare trading card of Maris was in Kivas Fajo's possession in 2366.
