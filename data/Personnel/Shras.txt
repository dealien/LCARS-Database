PERSONNEL: Shras
Ambassador, diplomatic liaison, Babel

Andorian representative traveling to the Babel Conference aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701 in 2267. He was surprised that his associate, Thelev, was actually an Orion killer. 

Reference: Journey to Babel 
Played by Reggie Nalder.
