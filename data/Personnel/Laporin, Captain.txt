PERSONNEL: Laporin
Captain

A Benzite captain who attended Starfleet Academy simultaneous with Benjamin Sisko. Laporin died in 2372 at the hands of Klingons ruled by the Gowron Changeling. 

Reference: Apocalypse Rising
