PERSONNEL: Boylen
Lieutenant, U.S.S. Pegasus

Starfleet officer and crew member aboard the ill-fated U.S.S. Pegasus in 2358. He was remembered by Commander Riker as the one who dubbed him the nickname "Ensign Babyface."
