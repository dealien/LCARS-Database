PERSONNEL: Otrin

Otrin was a member of the ravaged Delta Quadrant race, who suffered radiation sickness due to an antimatter containment failure blamed on Friendship One, a probe from Earth. Otrin was treated by the Doctor and blamed the Voyager crew for his people's plight. He eventually became the leader of his people, taking over from Verin. 

Reference: Friendship One
Portrayed by John Prosky.
