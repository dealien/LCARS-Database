PERSONNEL: Timor Landi

Bajoran bookkeeper from Rakantha Province. A father of two, Timor was charged with attempting to assassinate Gul Dukat on Terok Nor in 2367. Odo's perfunctory detective work did not hinder Timor's wrongful execution. Odo's guilt over that incident would later haunt him
