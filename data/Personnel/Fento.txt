PERSONNEL: Fento

An elderly male on planet Mintaka III. Fento related many of his people's old legends about an "overseer," their equivalent of a God, when his people struggled to understand otherwise inexplicable experiences with advanced technology. Fento was left to guard the injured Dr. Palmer and Commander William Riker was forced to overpower him to escape with Palmer.

Reference: Who Watches the Watchers
Played by John McLiam.
