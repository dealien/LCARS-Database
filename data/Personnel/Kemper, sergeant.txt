PERSONNEL: Nathan Kemper
Sergeant, MACO, Enterprise NX-01

Kemper is stationed outside of Atlanta, but is originally from Duluth, Minnesota. He was surprised that Hoshi Sato was able to recognize his Duluth inflections, as he hasn't lived there since junior high.
