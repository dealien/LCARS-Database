PERSONNEL: Krite

A member of the unisex J'naii race. Krite was a pilot and instructor who labored with Soren to recover a lost J'naii shuttle. Soren's overtly female activities were reported by Krite. 

Reference: The Outcast 
Played by Callan White. Heck, they even picked an actor/tress with a gender neutral name.
