PERSONNEL: Taris
Subcommander, starship commander, R.S.E. Haakona

Female Romulan commanding the Haakona in 2365. Taris intended to challenge the U.S.S. Enterprise-D for possession of Iconian secrets, but Iconian virus computer glitches crippled her ship. 

Reference: Contagion 
Portrayed by Carolyn Seymour, whom also played Mrs. Templeton in "Persistence of Vision" and Cathexis, and Commander Toreth in "Face of the Enemy."
