PERSONNEL: Annorax
Starship commander, scientist, Krenim Temporal Warship

Annorax, a time-tinkering Krenim scientist, developed a sophisticated temporal device that could change history. Annorax's temporal-weapon ship was a highly dangerous device capable of changing history for millions throughout the galaxy.

Reference: Year of Hell
Played by Kurtwood Smith who also played the Federation President in "The Undiscovered Conutry," and the Cardassian Security Chief of Terok Nor. He previously played the villain in "Robocop" and is most noted as Red Forman in "That 70s Show".
