PERSONNEL: Dekon Elig

Bajoran geneticist, Higa Metar resistance fighter, and inventor, along with assistant Sumak Ren, of the aphasia virus. Dekon died escaping the Velos VII internment camp on Bajor in 2360. 

Reference: Babel 
Played by Dan Curry who is the visual effects producer for Next Generation, Deep Space 9, and Voyager. The face of Dekon Elig was seen as a mug shot in a computer screen.
