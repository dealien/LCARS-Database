PERSONNEL: Robin Hood

Role played by Captain Jean-Luc Picard during an encounter with Q in 2367. In order to find out more about the human emotion of love, Q set Picard the task of rescuing Vash, who was playing Maid Marian to his Robin Hood. 

Reference: QPid 
Portrayed by Patrick Stewart/ Jean-Luc Picard.
