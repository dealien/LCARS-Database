PERSONNEL: Chantal Zaheva
Captain, starship commander, U.S.S. Brattain

Starfleet commander of the science vessel U.S.S. Brattain driven to insanity in 2367 while trapped in a Tyken's Rift. Zaheva killed her first officer before being murdered herself.
