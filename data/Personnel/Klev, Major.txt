PERSONNEL: Klev
Major, Chief security officer,  Tandaran internment camp

Tandaran male serving under the command of the militant Colonel Grat at the Tandaran internment camp for Suliban civilians. Klev was knocked out by an explosion during the Enterprise-aided escape of the prisoners. 

Reference: Detained
Portrayed by David Kagen.
