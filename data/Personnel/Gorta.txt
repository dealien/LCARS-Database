PERSONNEL: Gorta

A Dopterian who in 2370 made the mistake of going into a mining deal with Lursa and B'Etor, the Duras sisters. He traded a ride home aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise-D for information on the sisters. 

Reference: Firstborn 
Played by Joel Swetow who also played Gul Jasad in "Emissary." The Continuum credits Colin Mitchell as the actor but it was done by mistake.
