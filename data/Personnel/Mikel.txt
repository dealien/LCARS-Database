PERSONNEL: Mikel

Thirteen-year-old human boy from Gault fatally injured by a thirteen-year-old Worf during a soccer game accident. Worf was haunted by the incident, making him hesitant about expressing himself with Jadzia. 

Reference: Let He Who Is Without Sin...
