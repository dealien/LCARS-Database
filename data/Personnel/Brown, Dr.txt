PERSONNEL: Harry Brown, PhD.
Archaeologist, research assistant, Exo III

Android assistant to archaeologist Roger Korby on planet Exo III. Brown had originally been human, but Korby used alien technology to imprint his personality into a manufactured body. 

Reference: What Are Little Girls Made Of? 
Played by Harry Basch. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
