PERSONNEL: Faren Kag
Magistrate, Bajor

Bajoran magistrate. He sent a distress call to Deep Space 9 when the village storyteller-leader, the Sirah, was near death. During the relief mission, it was ordained that Chief O'Brien become the new Sirah. 

Reference: The Storyteller 
Played by Jim Jansen.
