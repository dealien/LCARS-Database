PERSONNEL: Pran Tainer
PhD, geologist, Atrea IV

Native of Atrea IV who wed an android version of Dr. Noonien Soong's ex-wife. In 2370, geologist Tainer and wife Juliana used the power of the U.S.S. Enterprise to reliquefy his planet's core. 

Reference: Inheritance 
Portrayed by William Lithgow.
