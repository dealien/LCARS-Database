PERSONNEL: Unferth

Character from the epic 8th-century Earth heroic poem. Unferth was a courtier to King Hrothgar, and during a holographic reconstruction of the epic objected to the intrusion of crew members from the U.S.S. Voyager. 

Reference: Heroes and Demons
Portrayed by Christopher Neame.
