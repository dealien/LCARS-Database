PERSONNEL: Brok'Tan

Klingon man who did not believe in Gre'thor, Klingon Hell, until he died and learned his dishonored soul was on its way there. Brok'Tan befriended a similarly dismayed B'Elanna Torres. 

Reference: Barge of the Dead
Portrayed by John Jenton Shull.
