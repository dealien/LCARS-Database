PERSONNEL: Michael Jonas
Maquis, engineering officer, U.S.S. Voyager

Member of the Maquis contingent that joined the U.S.S. Voyager crew in 2371. Jonas betrayed Starfleet secrets, and allied himself with Seska and the Nistrim Kazon, before dying during a sabotage attempt the following year. 

Reference: Threshold
Portrayed by Raphael Sbarge
