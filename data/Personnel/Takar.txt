PERSONNEL: Takar

Humanoid scientist from the Delta Quadrant's Srivani civilization. Takar covertly visited the U.S.S. Voyager as a member of Alzen's 50-person investigatory team. Takar tortured crewmembers with risky medical experiments. 

Reference: Scientific Method
Portrayed by Annette Helde.
