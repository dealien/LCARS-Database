PERSONNEL: Kirayoshi O`Brien

Son of Keiko and Miles O'Brien. Although conceived in his mother's womb, he was carried to term and delivered by Kira Nerys, following an injury to Keiko. Worf is an occasional babysitter for Yoshi.
