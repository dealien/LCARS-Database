PERSONNEL: Koral

Taciturn Klingon smuggler of Romulan artifacts who was intercepted and held in custody by the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701-D in 2370. 

Reference: Gambit
Played by James Worthy, the tallest actor to appear in Star Trek. The professional basketball player of the LA Lakers is 6'9".
