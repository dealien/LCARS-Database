PERSONNEL: Sulan

Highest-ranking Vidiian surgeon in 2371. Sulan's dedication to ending the phage eclipsed regard for other lifeforms. His experiments on sentient beings included B'Elanna Torres, from whom he collected Klingon DNA. 

Reference: Faces
Portrayed by Brian Markinson.
