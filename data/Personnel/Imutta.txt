PERSONNEL: Imutta
Brother, Deep Space Nine

Family name of the Bajoran monk whom Vedek Porta killed, by pushing him over a railing on DS9's Promenade, because Porta considered his D'jarra unclean. Members of this d'jarra were undertakers or morgue technicians during Bajoran history.
