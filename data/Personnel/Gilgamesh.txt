PERSONNEL: Gilgamesh

Mythic Earth figure of Earth's ancient Mesopotamia; a warrior king whose adventures were related on 12 incomplete stone tablets. Captain Jean-Luc Picard shared one of the stories of Gilgamesh with Tamarian Captain Dathon, while the two of them were together on El-Adrel IV.
