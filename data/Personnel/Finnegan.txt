PERSONNEL: Bruce Finnegan

Starfleet officer. Finnegan was an upperclassman and arch rival of James Kirk during his academy days in 2252. Finnegan delighted in playing practical jokes on Kirk. A replica of Finnegan was created on the amusement park planet in 2267, giving Kirk the chance to finally best his nemesis. 

Reference: Shore Leave 
Played by Bruce Mars. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
