PERSONNEL: James Barron, Ph.D.

Dr. Barron, a Federation anthropologist residing on Mintaka III in 2366. His study focused on the evolution, behavior and social development of the proto-Vulcan agrarian culture on Mintaka III. 

Reference: Who Watches the Watchers?
