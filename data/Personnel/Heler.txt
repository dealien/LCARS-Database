PERSONNEL: Heler

A Bajoran prisoner aboard the Ravinok, a Cardassian ship attacked by the Breen in 2366 and forced to land on planet Dozaria. Heler survived the crash and was forced to labor in the Breen dilithium mines on Dozaria. He and several other crash survivors were rescued by Kira Nerys and Gul Dukat in 2372. 

Reference: Indiscretion 
Played by Thomas Prisco.
