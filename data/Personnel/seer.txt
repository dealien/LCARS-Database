PERSONNEL: seer

A spiritual leader and secular guide of a Boraalan village. The seer studied by Nikolai Rozhenko was killed during the sotrms generated by atmospheric dissipation. Rozhenko told the villagers that Worf, who had been altered to pass as a Boraalan, was a seer, who had come to lead them to safety.
