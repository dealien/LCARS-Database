PERSONNEL: Rurigan
colonial founder, Yadera II

Biological person who existed in a holographic village on planet Yadera II. Rurigan created this world with a holographic generator after his village was wiped out by Dominion forces in 2340. 

Reference: Shadowplay 
Played by Kenneth Tobey.
