PERSONNEL: Elaine Johnson

A scientist stationed on Gamma Hydra IV with her husband, Robert Johnson. Elaine predeceased her husband by several hours, dying of radiation-induced old age at 27 in 2267. 

TIMELINE:
2240: Born
: Married, Robert Johnson
: Assigned to Gamma Hydra IV
2267: Died

Reference: The Deadly Years 
Played by Laura Wood.
