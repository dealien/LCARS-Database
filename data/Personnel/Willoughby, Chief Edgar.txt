PERSONNEL: Edgar Willoughby
Chief, Bajoran Sector

Sector quartermaster in 2375 for Deep Space 9. Willoughby and his wife Cynthia had two children: Edgar Jr., born in 2370, and Melissa, born in 2367. Willoughby let Nog know where to find a graviton stabilizer after the Ferengi sent him a bottle of Gamzian wine. 

Reference: Treachery, Faith, and the Great River
