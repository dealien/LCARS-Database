PERSONNEL: Fred Noonan

Twentieth-century Earth aviator. He was Amelia Earhart's navigator when they were transferred to the Briori homeworld, becoming primogenitors of a new human race. In 2371, Noonan chose to remain on Briori rather than join the U.S.S. Voyager crew. 

Reference: The 37s
Portrayed by David Graf, whom also played Leskit in "Sons and Daughters."
