PERSONNEL: Hayne

Human male, charismatic leader of the Coalition gang on Tasha Yar's home planet, Turkana IV. Hayne aided Picard's search for kidnapped Federation engineers in 2367, expecting to dupe the Starfleet personnel into incapacitating a rival faction, the Alliance. 

Reference: Legacy 
Played by Donald Mirault.
