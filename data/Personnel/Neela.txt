PERSONNEL: Neela
Crewman, engineering officer, Deep Space Nine

Young Bajoran engineer on DS9 secretly waiting for the opportunity to assassinate Vedek Bariel in 2369. She believed the Prophets wanted her to prevent him from becoming Kai Opaka's successor. 

Reference: In the Hands of the Prophets, Duet
Played by Robin Christopher.
