PERSONNEL: Rita Tannenbaum
Ensign, engineering officer, U.S.S. Defiant

Starfleet engineer aboard the U.S.S. Defiant in 2373 when, in an alternate timeline, the ship was marooned on Gaia in the past. In this timeline, Tannenbaum wed Miles O'Brien and began a family.
