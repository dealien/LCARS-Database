PERSONNEL: Miramanee
Tribal priestess

Woman living on a beautiful Class-M planet inhabited by a culture similar to that of Native American Indians. Miramanee and James Kirk were married after he lost his memory in 2268, but she died later the same year. Miramanee's planet was protected from an asteroid by a deflecting Obelisk installed by the Preservers. 

Reference: The Paradise Syndrome 
Played by Sabrina Scharf.
