PERSONNEL: Maques
Diplomatic officer, Cairn

A male Cairn being taught verbal communication skills by Lwaxana Troi in 2370. Lwaxana hoped her daughter and Maques would link romantically. Maques later help Lwaxana face a painful memory. 

Reference: Dark Page 
Portrayed by Norman Large. The Continuum spelled his name two different ways, including Marques by mistake.
