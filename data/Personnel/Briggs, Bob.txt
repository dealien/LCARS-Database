PERSONNEL: Bob Briggs
Director, Cetacean Institute, Earth

Director of the Cetacean Institute in Sausalito, California, on planet Earth in 1986. Briggs oversaw the release of two humpback whales, George and Gracie, to the open sea because the institute could no longer afford to feed them. 

Reference: The Voyage Home 
Played by Scott DeVenney. The images on The Continuum were incorrect.
