PERSONNEL: Wheeler
Lieutenant, U.S.S. Bellerophon

Crew member on the U.S.S. Bellerophon. Wheeler's conversation with Ensign Hickam on the way to a Romulan conference alerted Julian Bashir to Admiral Ross' sudden illness.
