PERSONNEL: B`iJik

Klingon High Council bureaucrat. In 2368, B'iJik who was asked by Captain Jean-Luc Picard to convey his request to Gowron to borrow a bird-of-prey. 

Reference: Unification
Played by Erick Avari who also appeared as Vedek Yarka in "Destiny."
