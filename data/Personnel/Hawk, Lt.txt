PERSONNEL: Neal Hawk
Lieutenant, helmsman, U.S.S. Enterprise-E

Conn officer aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise-E, who fought alongside Picard and Worf in a zero-G battle against Borg drones on the exterior the Enterprise. He lost a struggle to a drone and got assimilated, and was subsequently killed by Worf. 

Reference: Star Trek: First Contact 
Played by Neal McDonough. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
