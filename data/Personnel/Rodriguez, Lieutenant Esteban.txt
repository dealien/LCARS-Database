PERSONNEL: Esteban Rodriguez
Lieutenant, U.S.S. Enterprise

In 2267, Rodriguez and other survey team members visited the amusement park planet where mere thoughts got reproduced into reality. Rodriguez's thoughts centered on ancient war planes, which were then manifested in dangerous strafing runs. 

Reference: Shore Leave 
Played by Perry Lopez.
