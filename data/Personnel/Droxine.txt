PERSONNEL: Droxine

Daughter of Plasus, an official in the cloud-based metropolis of Stratos. Droxine, who also resided in the privileged city above the harsh planet Ardana, apparently became infatuated with Spock in 2369. 

Reference: The Cloud Minders 
Played by Diana Ewing.
