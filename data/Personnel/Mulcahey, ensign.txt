PERSONNEL: Mulcahey
Ensign, science officer, U.S.S. Voyager

U.S.S. Voyager crewperson qualified to serve in both the Transporter Room and the Science Lab. In 2375, a sample of Mulchaey's tissue was extracted by a Borg tubule, leading to the creation of the first-known Borg fetus.
