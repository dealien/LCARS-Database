PERSONNEL: Garland Wilson
Transporter Technician, U.S.S. Enterprise

In 2266, Wilson was duped by James Kirk's evil twin, giving the ersatz Kirk his phaser. 

Reference: The Enemy Within 
Played by Garland Thompson. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
