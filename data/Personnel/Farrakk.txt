PERSONNEL: Farrakk
arms merchant

A subordinate to arms merchant Hagath. When Farrakk failed to secure a deal with the Verillians because he was vacationing on Risa, Hagath exterminated him. 

Reference: Business as Usual 
Played by Tim Halligan.
