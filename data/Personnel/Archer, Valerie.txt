PERSONNEL: Valerie Archer
Commander, operations officer, Starfleet Headquarters

Commander Valerie Archer served at Starfleet Headquarters in 2375. She was duplicated by Species 8472 and used in a sophisticated replica of Starfleet in the Delta Quadrant as part of their preparation for an intelligence gathering operation.
