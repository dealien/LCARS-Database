PERSONNEL: Grathon Tolar

Miscreant and skilled holographic counterfeiter. Sisko rescued Tolar from a Klingon executioner to craft official Cardassian holographic images in an attempt to turn the Romulans against the Dominion. Tolar was later murdered by Garak. 

Reference: In the Pale Moonlight
