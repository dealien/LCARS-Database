PERSONNEL: Satler

One of the mercenaries involved in stealing trilithium resin from the U.S.S. Enterprise-D while docked at the Remmler Array in 2369. The ship was undergoing a baryon sweep at the time. Satler was killed by the effects of the deadly rays. 

Reference: Starship Mine 
Portrayed by Tim DeZarn.
