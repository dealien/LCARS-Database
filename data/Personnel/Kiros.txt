PERSONNEL: Kiros
Member of a mercenary group who stole trilithium resin in 2369 from the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701-D during a baryon sweep. Kiros died by triggering a booby trap set by Picard. 

Reference: Starship Mine 
Played by Patricia Tallman, who also played several stunt performers throught Next Generation and other SciFi shows. She was a featured character, Leeta Alexander in Babylon 5.
