PERSONNEL: John B. Watkins
Engineering officer, U.S.S. Enterprise

Engineer grade 4 aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise. In 2268, Watkins was checking the emergency overload bypass valve on the matter/antimatter reaction chamber when Losira, from the Kalandan outpost, killed him. 

Reference: That Which Survives 
Played by Kenneth Washington.
