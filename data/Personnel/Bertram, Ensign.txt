PERSONNEL: Bertram
Ensign, Deep Space 9

Starfleet officer on Deep Space 9 in 2375 who collected Cardassian, Klingon, and Federation weapons as a hobby. Bertram was caught accessing the replication pattern of the TR-116 weapon without permission.
