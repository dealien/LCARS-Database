PERSONNEL: Martin
Lieutenant, medical doctor, U.S.S. Enterprise

Physician on the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701-D. In 2368, after chief medical officer Dr. Crusher was felled by a coma, Dr. Martin was briefly in command of sickbay.
