PERSONNEL: Karina

Romulan diplomat. Together with Ruwon, Karina travelled to Deep Space 9 in 2371 for an intelligence briefing about the Dominion with Captain Benjamin Sisko and Major Kira Nerys. The two were actually intending to destroy the station and collapse the Bajoran Wormhole in order to prevent enemy forces from coming through, but were thwarted. 

Reference: Visionary 
Played by Annette Helde.
