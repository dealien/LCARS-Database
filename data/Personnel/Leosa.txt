PERSONNEL: Leosa

Dabo girl who worked for a Ferengi named Nunk. Leosa broke Reginald Barclay's heart by feigning interest in him and his work in a greedy plot to acquire Seven's nanoprobes. 

Reference: Inside Man 
Portrayed by Sharise Baker-Bernard.
