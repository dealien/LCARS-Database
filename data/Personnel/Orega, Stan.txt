PERSONNEL: Orega, Stan 
Humorist from the 23rd century specializing in jokes about quantum mathematics. When Data investigated humor in 2365, the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701-D computer told him the funniest person in the data banks was Stan Orega. 
Reference: The Outrageous Okona
