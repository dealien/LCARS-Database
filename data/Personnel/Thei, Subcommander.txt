PERSONNEL: Thei
Subcommander

Romulan first officer under Commander Tebok in 2364. Thei's investigation into destroyed Romulan outposts near the Neutral Zone brought him in contact with Captain Jean-Luc Picard and the U.S.S. Enterprise-D. 

Reference: The Neutral Zone 
Portrayed by Anthony James.
