PERSONNEL: Obrist

Krenim first officer to Annorax aboard the Temporal Weapon-Ship operating in the Delta Quadrant in 2374. Obrist eventually turned against his superior and aided the U.S.S. Voyager crew in defeating Annorax. 

Reference: Year of Hell
Portrayed by John Loprieno.
