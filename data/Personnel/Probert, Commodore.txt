PERSONNEL: Andrew Probert
Commodore

High ranking Starfleet officer who, in 2271, sent instructions for the U.S.S. Columbia to rendezvous with the U.S.S. Revere. This message was transmitted through the Epsilon IX monitoring station prior to its encounter with V'Ger. 

Reference: The Motion Picture 
This character was mentioned in the during a communication received and relayed by the Epsilon IX monitoring station. It was a tongue-in-cheek reference to Andrew Probert, one of the illustrators on that film and the man who designed the Galaxy-class starship.
