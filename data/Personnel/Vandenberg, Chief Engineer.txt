PERSONNEL: Ken Vanderberg
Chief Engineer, Janus VI

Head of the pergium mining operations on Janus VI in 2267. The mining disturbed the subterranean Horta, who responded by killing 50 of Vandenberg's men. Vandenberg in turn summoned the U.S.S. Enterprise for aid. 

Reference: The Devil in the Dark 
Played by Ken Lynch. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
