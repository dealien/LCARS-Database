PERSONNEL: Kenneth McDowell
Ensign, tactical officer, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

Wavy-haired young man at the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701-D's Tactical station in 2368. He was given bridge command while the senior officers attended a memorial service for Geordi La Forge and Ro Laren.
