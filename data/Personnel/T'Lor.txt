PERSONNEL: T`Lor

A Tiburon native serving in Starfleet stationed on Deep Space Nine. His survey of Torga IV helped reveal rich cormaline deposits. T'Lor later died in the Gamma Quadrant capturing a downed Jem'Hadar ship. 

Reference: The Ship 
Portrayed by Ken Lesco.
