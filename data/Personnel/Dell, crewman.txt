PERSONNEL: Dell
Crewman, engineering officer, U.S.S. Voyager

Male serving on U.S.S. Voyager in 2374. Dell was Three of Ten on Seven's Omega particle team and calibrated the ionic pressure seals on the observation points of the harmonic resonance chamber.
