PERSONNEL: Klingon gunner

This officer served under Commander Kruge and replaced the original gunner after he was executed for disobeying an order and proving to be careless with his firing. This officer knew he would have to be far more careful.

Reference: The Search for Spock
Portrayed by Branscombe Richmond.
