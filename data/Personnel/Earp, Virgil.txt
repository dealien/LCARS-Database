PERSONNEL: Virgil Earp

Town marshal of Tombstone, Arizona, during famous 1881 gunfight between the Earps and the Clanton gang. In 2268, the Melkotians created a virtual Virgil to repel Kirk and his crew. 

Reference: Spectre of the Gun 
Played by Charles Maxwell.
