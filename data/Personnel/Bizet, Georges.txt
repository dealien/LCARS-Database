PERSONNEL: Georges Bizet

French composer of Earth music. 
When Captain Jean-Luc Picard listened to Berlioz in his ready room on the U.S.S. Enterprise-E in 2373 Commander Riker mistook the music for the work of Bizet.

TIMELINE:
1838: Born
1875: Died
