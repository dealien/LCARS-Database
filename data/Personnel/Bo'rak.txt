PERSONNEL: Bo'rak
Agent, Klingon Intelligence, Qo'noS

One of three agents sent to Deep Space 9 in 2371 to observe and to take appropriate action against a Romulan delegation visiting the station. Bo'rak was under direct orders from the Klingon High Council.

Reference: Visionary
Played by Bob Minor.
