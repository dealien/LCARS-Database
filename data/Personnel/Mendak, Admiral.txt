PERSONNEL: Mendak
Admiral, starship commander, R.S.E. Devoras

Under the pretext of a peace mission, his warbird, the Devoras, successfully retrieved an undercover Romulan spy from the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701-D in 2367.
