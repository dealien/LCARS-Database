PERSONNEL: Nanclus
Ambassador, diplomatic officer, Earth

In 2293, Nanclus was the Romulan ambassador to the United Federation of Planets. Secretly, Nanclus worked with Starfleet and Klingon operatives to defeat overtures tendered by Chancellor Gorkon. 

Reference: The Undiscovered Country 
Played by Darryl Henriques, who also played Portal in "The Last Outpost."
