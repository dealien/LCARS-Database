PERSONNEL: Daniel Sutter
Ensign, engineering officer, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

Starfleet officer who served on a number of ships before joining the U.S.S. Enterprise-D's Engineering department in 2368. Daniel had one daughter, Clara Sutter, a youngster whose active imagination attracted a noncorporeal lifeform named Isabella, who became Clara's jealous protector. 

Reference: Imaginary Friend 
Portrayed by Jeff Allin.
