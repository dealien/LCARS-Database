PERSONNEL: Carolyn Palamas
Lieutenant, archaeologist, U.S.S. Enterprise

Crewmember on the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701 in 2267 as the archeology and anthropology officer. On Pollux IV she met Apollo but was forced to reject him to save her ship. 

Reference: Who Mourns for Adonais? 
An early draft of the script ended with McCoy learning that she was pregnant with Apollo's baby.
Played by Leslie Parrish.
