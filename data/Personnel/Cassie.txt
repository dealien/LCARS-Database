PERSONNEL: Cassie

Name of the woman in love with science-fiction writer Benny Russell during Benjamin Sisko's Orb-like vision of 1950s Earth. Cassie, who resembled Kasidy Yates, earned her living as a waitress. 

Reference: Far Beyond the Stars 
Played by Penny Johnson, who also played Kasidy Yates throughout Deep Space Nine and Dobara in "Homeward." The Continuum featured images of Penny Johnson as Kasidy, not as Cassie in their online entry. The Continuum also spelled the name as Kassie instead of with a "C".
