PERSONNEL: Erib

Friend of Dr. Julian Bashir from his Starfleet Medical School days. Julian and Erib, an Andorian, went to Bruce Lucier's 2368 New Year's eve Party. At the party Erib was mistakenly pointed out to Elizabeth Lense as Julian Bashir. As a result, Lense thought Bashir was Andorian until she met him on Deep Space 9 in 2371.

Reference: Explorers
