PERSONNEL: Donald Kaplan
Commander, chief engineer, U.S.S. Intrepid

Commander Kaplan was an Academy classmate of Geordi La Forge and chief engineer on the U.S.S. Intrepid NCC-38907. The two of them liked to have friendly competition with each other. Both engineers wanted to have the ship with the least wasteful power-conversion ratio.
