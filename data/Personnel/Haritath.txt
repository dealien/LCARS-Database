PERSONNEL: Mark Haritath

Member of illegal colony on Tau Cygna V. Haritath was unaffected by lethal hyperonic radiation in the atmosphere. He was the first to meet Data and agree to re-settle in the face of impending destruction by the Sheliak. 

Reference: The Ensigns of Command 
Played by Mark L. Taylor. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
