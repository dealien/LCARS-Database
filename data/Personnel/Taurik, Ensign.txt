PERSONNEL: Taurik
Ensign, engineering officer, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

Vulcan engineering ensign on the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701-D. In 2370 Taurik shared quarters with Sam Lavelle. Taurik participated in preparations for the mission that resulted in the death of classmate Sito Jaxa. 

Reference: Lower Decks 
Portrayed by Alexander Enberg.
