PERSONNEL: breathing boy

A Lumerian child being weined off his air tanks and on to an M-class environment. This boy and his mother were sitting on the edge of a market place while she pulled away his breathing mask in intervals to help him get used to the new environment.
While Subcommander T'Pol and Commander Charles Tucker were sitting in visual range Trip misunderstood the situation and assumed that she was torturing the child.

Reference: Broken Bow
Portrayed by Ethan Dampf.
