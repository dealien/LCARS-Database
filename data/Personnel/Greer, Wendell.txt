PERSONNEL: Wendell Greer
alias, assistant director UFP Cartography

Agent Luther Sloan's cover during the 2375 conference on Romulus. After 15 years with the UFP Department of Cartography, Greer had earned the title of Assistant Director. He also claimed an interest in etymology.
