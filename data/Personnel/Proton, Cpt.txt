PERSONNEL: Captain Proton

Fictional hero of a holodeck program created by Tom Paris. "The Adventures of Captain Proton" is based on old Earth film serials from the 1930s. Captain Proton is normally accompanied by his trusty sidekick, Buster. 

Reference: Night
Portrayed by Robert Duncan McNeil/ Tom Paris.
