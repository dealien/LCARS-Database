PERSONNEL: Janklow
Crewman, engineering officer, Deep Space Nine

Starfleet engineer who helped Chief O'Brien with the task of arming the U.S.S. Defiant's atmospheric probes with warheads to combat Jem'Hadar forces in 2372. 

Reference: Starship Down
