PERSONNEL: bar alien
Captain, transport commander

This alien captain heard the katra-confused Leonard McCoy was looking to book passage and atempted to offer services on his merchant spaceship. While attempting to barter over price he asked McCoy where he was looking to travel to, since that would affect the price. When McCoy revealed that his intended destination was the new Genesis Planet the alien was shocked enough that he spoke very loudly after that- attracting the attention of Federation Security. The alien captain refused to take the charter recognizing that the planet was off-limits.

Reference: The Search for Spock
Portrayed by Paul Sorensen.
