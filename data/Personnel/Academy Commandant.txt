PERSONNEL: Academy Commandant

Head of Starfleet Academy who, in 2372 conspired with Admiral Leyton to overthrow the government of Earth. Admiral Leyton's Red Squad of elite cadets were created with the Commandant's blessing. 

Reference: Paradise Lost, Broken Link 
Played by Rudolph Willrich whom also played Reittan Grax in "Menage a Troi" and Kuulan in "Oasis."
