PERSONNEL: Eddie Bau

Fictitious character, a bartender in a 1930s- 1940s nightclub featured in the Dixon Hill stories and holodeck representations.

Reference: First Contact
Played by C.J. Bau. The chracter's last name is from the script. The character's first name is also conjectural based on the actor's real name.
