PERSONNEL: Durango

Name for Counsellor Deanna Troi's holodeck character in Alexander Rozhenko's holodeck program, Ancient West. It was Troi's first chance to play the part of a "mysterious stranger."

Reference: A Fistful of Datas
Played by Mirina Sirtis.
