PERSONNEL: Rynar

Kor's father. Rynar was a decendant of the Klingon Imperial Court, able to trace his ancestry to the blood of Kahless. By the time of Kor's death, Rynar and that entire house was no more. 

Reference: Once More Unto the Breach
