PERSONNEL: Weldon
Ensign, Starfleet Medical

Young officer working at Starfleet Medical in 2375. Weldon reported to Lieutenant Douglas who reported to Lieutenant Commander Hilliard. Weldon told Julian Bashir that Odo's medical records were classified. 

Reference: When It Rains...
