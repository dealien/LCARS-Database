PERSONNEL: Kassa
Admiral, station commander, Spacedock

The senior officer in charge of all operations at the facility orbiting the Earth and starships parked in orbit. This man assumed control of all Starfleet operations when Q erected a shield around the planet that blocked all transporters, transport vessels and shuttles, and communications.
