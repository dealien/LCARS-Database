PERSONNEL: Melian
 
Aldea's foremost musician. In 2364, Radue abducted a little girl named Katie from the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701-D and assigned her to Melian's Unit so he could encourage her music abilities. 

Reference: When the Bough Breaks 
Portrayed by Paul Lambert.
