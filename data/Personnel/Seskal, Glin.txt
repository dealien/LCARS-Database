PERSONNEL: Seskal
Glin

Cardassian soldier who rose to the top of Damar's liberation movment. Seskal was killed in 2375 as he was orbitting Cardassia Prime in a stolen Jem'Hadar ship. He had been betrayed by Gul Revok. 

Reference: The Dogs of War
