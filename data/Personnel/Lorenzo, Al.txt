PERSONNEL: Al Lorenzo
Chief of operations, Decos Prime

Starfleet officer on a Federation facility. 
In 2375, Nog loaned Captain Sisko's desk to Lorenzo as part of an elaborate barter scheme to procure gravimetric stabilizers for the U.S.S. Defiant.
