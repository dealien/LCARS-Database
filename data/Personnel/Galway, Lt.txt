PERSONNEL: Beverly Galway
Lieutenant, U.S.S. Enterprise

Enterprise crew member who was part of the landing party to planet Gamma Hydra IV in 2267. Galway contracted a radiation illness that sped the aging process. Though she was ten years younger than Kirk, she succumbed to the disease, dying of old age. 

Reference: The Deadly Years 
Played by Beverly Washburn. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
