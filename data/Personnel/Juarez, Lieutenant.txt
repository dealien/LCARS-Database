PERSONNEL: Juarez
Lieutenant, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

U.S.S. Enterprise-D officer who gave birth to a boy aboard the vessel in 2367, on the same day that Miles O'Brien and Keiko Ishihara were married and while the starship was in a stand-off with a Romulan warbird.
