PERSONNEL: Torigan
Ensign, tactical officer, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

Tactical officer on the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701-D during the Gamma (night) shift. Worf got into trouble, in 2369, for failing to relieve Torigan in a timely fashion. 

Reference: Rightful Heir
