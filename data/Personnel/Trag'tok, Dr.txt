PERSONNEL: Trag`tok
Doctor, medical officer, Deep Space 9

A male stationed on Deep Space 9 whom Jadzia Dax thought an appropriate and attractive dinner companion for Kira Nerys. Kira vetoed the date because of the doctor's three eyes.
