PERSONNEL: Thot Gor
Thot, liaison officer, Cardassian Prime

General from the Breen Confederacy who turned the Dominion-Federation war to the Dominion's favor during a series of wildly successful campaigns. Gor's victories reduced the Cardassian's standing in the Dominion.
