PERSONNEL: Mulligan
Father, Saint Mary's church (holographic)

Name of the priest at Saint Mary's church in U.S.S. Voyager's holographic village of Fair Haven. The Doctor, who played the Mulligan role, was suspected of being an evil spirit. 

Reference: Fair Haven
Portrayed by Robert Picardo/ EMH Doctor.
