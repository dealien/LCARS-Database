PERSONNEL: Dee'Ahn

The name of a lovely female who flirted with Trip Tucker and Malcolm Reed during their shore leave to Risa. Dee'Ahn and her companion, Latia, turned out to be male alien thieves in disguise — they robbed Trip and Reed, leaving the officers tied up in the basement of a Risan night club. 

Reference: Two Days and Two Nights
Portrayed by Donna Marie Recco and Geoff Meed.
