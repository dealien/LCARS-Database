PERSONNEL: Amanin
 
Betazed psychiatrist who developed a means of extracting unconscious memories from humanoids. However, these memory extractions were not always reliable. Voyager's EMH incorporated some of his theories into his subroutines. 

Reference: Retrospect
This character was not seen, merely mentioned in the script.
