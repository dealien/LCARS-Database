PERSONNEL: Natira

Spiritual and political leader of Yonada. Natira learned the truth about her asteroid world after encountering Kirk, Spock, and McCoy in 2268. She proposed marriage to McCoy, who accepted. 

Reference: For the World Is Hollow and I Have Touched the Sky 
Played by Kate Woodville.
