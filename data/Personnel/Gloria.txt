PERSONNEL: Gloria

Guinan's personna when she particiapted in one of Captain Jean-Luc Picard's Dixon Hill holodeck programs. Picard, as Hill, identified Gloria as a cousin of his from Cleveland. Guinan, in preparing to be Gloria, had a great deal of trouble with the female accoutrements (i.e., stocking) of old Earth culture.

Reference: Clues
Played by Whoopi Goldberg.
