PERSONNEL: Pelk

Malon serving on a toxic waste freighter under Fesek in 2375. While trying to repair theta radiation leakage, preventing a calamitous explosion, Pelk was killed by the Vihaar 'creature.' 

Reference: Juggernaut
Portrayed by Lee Arenberg whom also played Gral in "The Nagus," and DaiMon Bok in "Bloodlines."
