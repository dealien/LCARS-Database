PERSONNEL: Yareth

Rakhari girl sentenced to death for her father's crimes. Yareth escaped execution by hiding in stasis on a Chamra Vortex asteroid. Yareth and her father, Croden, found passage with Vulcans in 2369. 

Reference: Vortex 
Played by Leslie Engelberg.
