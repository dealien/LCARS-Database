PERSONNEL: T'Jon

Ornaran freighter captain barely competent to shuttle goods between Brekka to Ornara. T'Jon's felicium addiction led to his ship's destruction - though the cargo was saved - and the death of two crewmembers. 

Reference: Symbiosis 
Portrayed by Merritt Butrick, whom also played Dr. David Marcus in "The Wrath of Khan" and "The Search For Spock." Butrick played opposite his life partner, Judson Scott whom also played in "The Wrath of Khan."
