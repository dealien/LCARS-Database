PERSONNEL: Steve O`Connel
Starnes Expedition lad. The Triacus Gorgon killed Steve's family in 2268 and used him to control the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701. Steve preferred chocolate, marble, pistachio, and peach ice cream. 

Reference: And the Children Shall Lead 
Played by Caesar Belli, and son of Melvin Belli, the actor that played the Gorgan in the same episode.
