PERSONNEL: St. John Talbot
Diplomatic officer, Nimbus III

Human male and the Federation's diplomatic presence in Paradise City on Nimbus III. Sybok captured Talbot in 2287 and the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701-A was dispatched to rescue him. 

Reference: The Final Frontier 
Played by David Warner, who also played Gorkon in "The Undiscovered Country," and Gul Madred in "Chain of Command."
