PERSONNEL: Wu

Omega IV native who worked for Captain Ronald Tracey. Wu, a Kohm, was 462 years old. He owed his longevity to ancestors capable of surviving a devastating biological war. 

Reference: The Omega Glory 
Played by Lloyd Kino.
