PERSONNEL: Sakonna

Vulcan female who wanted the Ferengi Quark to broker Maquis arms purchases. Sakonna also participated in the kidnapping of Gul Dukat in 2370 and was later apprehended by authorities. 

Reference: The Maquis
Played by Bertila Damas.
