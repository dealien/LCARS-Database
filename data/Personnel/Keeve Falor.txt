PERSONNEL: Keeve Falor

Respected male Bajoran who resettled on Velo II after the Cardassian occupation. In 2368 Ensign Ro and Jean-Luc Picard visited Keeve hoping he could help them locate Orta, a Bajoran terrorist. 

Reference: Ensign Ro 
Played by Scot Marlow.
