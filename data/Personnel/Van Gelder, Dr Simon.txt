PERSONNEL: Simon Van Gelder
PhD, Administrator, Tantalus V

Administrator of the Tantalus V penal colony until 2266, when his sanity collapsed after testing an experimental neural nuetralizer. When he recovered, Dr. Van Gelder replaced his deputy and successor, Dr. Tristan Adams, whose exposure to the neural neutralizer was fatal. 

Reference: Dagger of the Mind 
Played by Morgan Woodward, who also played Captain Ronald Tracey.
