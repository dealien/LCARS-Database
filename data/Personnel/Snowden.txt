PERSONNEL: Snowden
U.S.S. Okinawa

Starfleet officer aboard the U.S.S. Okinawa contemporaneous with Benjamin Sisko's term as the ship's executive officer. Snowden was among hundreds transferred by Admiral Leyton to aid his 2372 power grab.
