PERSONNEL: Ranora
PhD, scientist, theologist, Vhnori

Vhnori scientist and thanatologist who presides over his culture's death, or transference, rituals. Ranora performed the Vhnori transference ritual for Ptera, when Harry Kim appeared. 

Reference: Emanations
Portrayed by John Cirigliano.
