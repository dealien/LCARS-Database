PERSONNEL: Mobara

With Kira Nerys, a member of Shakaar Edon's resistance cell. In 2373 he was attending the Bajor University Engineering school in Musilla Province. Like some fellow underground members, he was assassinated using a remotely-controlled micro-explosive. 

Reference: Shakaar, the Darkness and the Light
