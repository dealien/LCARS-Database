PERSONNEL: Jack Crusher
Lieutenant commander, executive officer, U.S.S. Stargazer

Starfleet officer who was killed in the line of duty while serving under Captain Jean-Luc Picard of the U.S.S. Stargazer in 2354. Jack married Beverly Howard in 2348 and together they had one son, Wesley, in 2349. He made the unique marriage proposal of purchasing a book for his intended entitled "How to Advance Your Career Through Marriage."

Reference: Encounter at Farpoint, Family, Attached, Violations, Journey's End
Played by Doug Wert. Even though the character died several years before the series started, he was seen three times in the show. The first was in Wesley's holographic message in "Family," then briefly in Beverly's telepathic memory invasion flashback in "Violations," and lastly in Wesley's vision in "Journey's End."
