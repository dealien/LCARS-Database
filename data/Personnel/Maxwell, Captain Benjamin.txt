PERSONNEL: Benjamin Maxwell
Captain, starship commander, U.S.S. Prometheus

Highly decorated Starfleet officer and starship commander whose wife and children were killed in a Cardassian attack on Setlik III. Following the Federation-Cardassian truce, Maxwell launched an illegal offensive on the former enemies - until Miles O'Brien persuaded him to stand down.
