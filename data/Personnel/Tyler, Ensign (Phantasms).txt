PERSONNEL: Tyler
Ensign, engineering officer, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

Fresh-faced young woman in the U.S.S. Enterprise-D's Engineering department. In 2370, Tyler's crush on Geordi La Forge made her practically giddy over recalibrating EPS modules and checking deuterium cartridges. 

Reference: Phantasms 
Portrayed by Gina Ravarra.
