PERSONNEL: Koss

Vulcan male and T'Pol's fiancee. Koss was an architect, and was betrothed to T'Pol during childhood. Before T'Pol was assigned to the Enterprise NX-01, she and Koss had met only four times. Because marrying Koss would have forced T'Pol to leave her post, she broke the engagement. 

Reference: Breaking the Ice
Portrayed by Michael Reilly Burke whom also played Goval in "Descent" and Hogue in "Profit and Loss."
