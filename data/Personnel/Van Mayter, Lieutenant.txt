PERSONNEL: Van Mayter
Lieutenant, engineering officer, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

Part of the engineering crew aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise. Lieutenant Van Mayter died in 2367 when a section of the ship was phased out of existence in the Mar Obscura nebula.
