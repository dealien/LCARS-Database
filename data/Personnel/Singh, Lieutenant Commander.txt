PERSONNEL: Singh
Lieutenant Commander, engineering officer, U.S.S. Enterprise-D


Assistant chief engineer, under chief engineer Argyle, on the U.S.S. Enterprise-D in 2364. Singh was accidentally killed by the entity from the Beta Renner cloud. 

Reference: Lonely Among Us 
Portrayed by Kavi Raz. This was the first crewmember killed in TNG.
