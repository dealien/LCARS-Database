PERSONNEL: Strickler
Admiral, Advanced Starship & Design Bureau, Starfleet Headquarters

In an alternate reality, Admiral Strickler was a Starfleet official sent to meet Harry Kim and Lt. Lasca at Starfleet Headquarters, with reference to the future of their new runabout design. 

Reference: Non Sequitur
Portrayed by Jack Shearer, whom also played Ruwon in "Visionary," and Admiral Hayes in "Star Trek: First Contact."
