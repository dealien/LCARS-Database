PERSONNEL: Davis Ozaba
Scientist, Minara II

In 2268 he was assigned to observe a sun in the Minaran star system. Ozaba, and his associate Dr. Linke, were on Minara II when the Vians abducted and killed them. 

Reference: The Empath 
Played by Davis Roberts. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
