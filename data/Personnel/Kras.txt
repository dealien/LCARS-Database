PERSONNEL: Kras

Klingon representative vying for topaline mining rights on Capella IV in 2267. Kras inspired a coup to put his friend, Maab, in power. Kras was killed after his treachery was revealed. 

Reference: Friday's Child 
Played by Tige Andrews. The character's anme was never mentioned in the aired episode, but is in the script.
