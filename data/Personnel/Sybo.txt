PERSONNEL: Sybo

Gentle native of Argelius II and wife of Prefect Jarvis in 2267. Sybo, a medium, was murdered during a seance to reveal the identity of a serial killer she called Redjac. 

Reference: Wolf in the Fold 
Played by Pilar Seurat.
