PERSONNEL: Ian Andrew Troi II

Name Deanna Troi gave to the boy she delivered hours after becoming impregnated by a noncorporeal life-form. The boy departed upon realizing his lifeforce was endangering the ship. 

Reference: The Child 
Portrayed by R.J. Williams and Zachary Benjamin.
