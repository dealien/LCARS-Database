PERSONNEL: Nadine

Young human girl onboard the Earth cargo ship Fortunate. T'Pol discovered Nadine playing hide-and-seek behind a corridor access panel. When Nadine's "seeker" asked T'Pol if she knew where Nadine was, T'Pol truthfully answered, "I don't know which child is named Nadine." Nadine thanked her for her trouble.
