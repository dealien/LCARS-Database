PERSONNEL: Amelia Earhart

20th-century American pilot, famous for numerous aviation 'firsts,' including several solo flights. Reported missing during her 1937 attempt to fly around the Earth, the U.S.S. Voyager later discovered her in cryo-stasis on a Delta Quadrant planet. 

Reference: The 37's
