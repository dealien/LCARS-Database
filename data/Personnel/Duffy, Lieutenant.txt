PERSONNEL: Charley Duffy
Lieutenant, engineering officer, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

Officer on the Enterprise-D, serving in the engineering department. Duffy was unintentionally responsible for dispersing a contagion of invidium which caused various systems to malfunction. 

Reference: Hollow Pursuits 
Played by Charley Lang. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
