PERSONNEL: Warrior/Adonis

A humanoid male who represented mediator Riva's passion, honor, courage, and libido in the deaf diplomat's interpretive Chorus. Generally Warrior/Adonis was the least verbal of Riva's Chorus. The entire Chorus was killed on Solais V during a failed mediation. 

Reference: Loud as a Whisper 
Portrayed by Leo Damian.
