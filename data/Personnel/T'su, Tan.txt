PERSONNEL: Tan T`su

Federation engineer on the Arcos, a freighter that exploded in 2367. T'su's escape pod landed on Turkana IV in Alliance territory. Coalitionist Ishara Yar guided rescuers to T'su's underground cell. 

Reference: Legacy 
Portrayed by Vladimir Velasco.
