PERSONNEL: Gegis

Ferengi who reprogrammed Reginald Barclay's holographic program to convince U.S.S. Voyager to travel through a fatal geodesic fold. Gegis hoped to profit from selling Seven of Nine's nanoprobes. 

Reference: Inside Man
Portrayed by Frank Corsentino whom also played DaiMon Bok.
