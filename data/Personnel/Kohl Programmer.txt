PERSONNEL: Kohl Programmer

Female humanoid from the Kohl settlement. In 2372, the crew of the U.S.S. Voyager freed her from the Kohl hibernation system, a system she helped create. Her knowledge of the system helped save both her life and the lives of the Voyager crew. 

Reference: The Thaw
Portrayed by Shannon O'Hurley.
