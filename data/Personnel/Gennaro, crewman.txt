PERSONNEL: Gennaro
Crewman, U.S.S. Voyager

Member of the U.S.S. Voyager crew. Gennaro was the next-to-last crewman abducted by the Nyrians in their attempt to seize the ship by covertly supplanting the crew (Chakotay being the last).
