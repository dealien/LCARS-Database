PERSONNEL: Dax
Son of Kang

Firstborn son of Klingon warrior Kang and Godson of Curzon Dax. The son of Kang was murdered as a child by The Albino as revenge against the warrior. The Albino also killed the first born sons of Koloth and Kor. The group pledged a blood oath to avenge the murder.

Reference: Blood Oath
