PERSONNEL: Pel

Female Ferengi posing as male to realize her business ambitions. In 2370 Pel worked for Quark and assisted him during trade meetings. Revealing her gender prompted Pel's departure from DS9. 

Reference: Rules of Acquisition 
Played by Helen Udy.
