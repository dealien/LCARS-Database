PERSONNEL: Liko

A humanoid native of Mintaka III who was mourning the loss of his wife. He was nearby when a hologram generator malfunction revealed the presence of Federation scientists observing his planet. 

Reference: Who Watches The Watchers? 
Played by Ray Wise.
