PERSONNEL: Falcon

A character in Julian Bashir's secret agent holosuite program. Falcon was a deadly assassin that had been trying to kill Julian's secret agent character for nine years. A holosuite malfunction in 2372 caused the character to look exactly like Chief Miles O'Brien.
Falcon was also a character in "The Queen's Gambit" holosuite program.

Reference: Our Man Bashir, A Simple Investigation
Played by Colm Meaney.
