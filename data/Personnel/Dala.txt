PERSONNEL: Dala

A Delta Quadrant con artist who impersonated Captain Janeway, Dala used the Starfleet officer's good name to steal goods and services, before being stopped by the real Janeway in 2376. 

Reference: Live Fast and Prosper
Portrayed by Kaitlin Hopkins.
