PERSONNEL: Roga Danar

Native of Angosia III who served in his planet's Tarsian War. His government programmed him to be the perfect warrior by putting him through extensive psychological manipulation and biochemical modifications that made him extremely aggressive in combat. Danar fought in many campaigns during that conflict and received two promotions to the rank of subhadar. In 2366 Danar led an uprising of veterans to force the Angosian government to reintegrate their ex-soldiers into society. 

Reference: The Hunted 
Played by Jeff McCarthy.
