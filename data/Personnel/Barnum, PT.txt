PERSONNEL: Phineas Taylor Barnum

Famous Earth-based circus impressario from the 19th century whose sayings are still quoted well into the 24th century, like "The Greatest Show on Earth." As Captain Picard noted at Ventax II in 2367, "There's a sucker born every minute." He was referring to Ardra and her failed attempt to dupe a planet into submission.
