PERSONNEL: Jahn

A boy several centuries old, nearing puberty in 2266 on a planet where a so-called Life Prolongation Project kills adults. Jahn and the other children - called "onlies" - endangered Captain James Kirk and the landing party from the U.S.S. Enterprise. 

Reference: Miri 
Played by Michael J. Pollard.
