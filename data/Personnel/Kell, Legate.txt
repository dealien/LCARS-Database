PERSONNEL: Kell
Legate

Key Cardassian during the subjugation of Bajor. His counterinsurgency program booby trap on Deep Space Nine was accidentally triggered by Jake Sisko several years after Cardassian withdrawal. 

Reference: Civil Defense 
Played by Danny Goldring. The character's name and title were not mentioned on the air but were included in the script.
