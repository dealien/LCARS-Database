PERSONNEL: Tamoon

Female gladiatorial fighting instructor owned by Provider 1. Though she had trouble pronouncing Chekov's name, Tamoon thought he was cute and wanted to be more than just his drill thrall. 

Reference: The Gamesters of Triskelion 
Played by Jane Ross.
