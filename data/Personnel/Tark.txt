PERSONNEL: Tark

Argelian musician whose daughter, Kara, danced in the clubs. Tark was heartbroken when his daughter was murdered in 2267 and indicated that her jealous fiance might have been the culprit. 

Reference: Wolf in the Fold 
Played by Joseph Bernard.
