PERSONNEL: Wakasa

Humanoid male of Native-American descent. Wakasa was living on Dorvan V when a Federation-Cardassian treaty ceded that world to Cardassia. Wakasa, a tribal leader, resisted evacuation commands. 

Reference: Journey's End 
Portrayed by George Aguilar.
