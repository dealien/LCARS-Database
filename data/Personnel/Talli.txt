PERSONNEL: Talli

Grocery vendor on the Mari homeworld who befriended Neelix when the U.S.S. Voyager visited in 2374. Talli was stabbed to death by Miss Tembit, a fellow Mari who ended up with an errant hostile image stolen from B'Elanna Torres by Guill, a dealer in illegal violent thoughts. 

Reference: Random Thoughts
Portrayed by Rebecca McFarland.
