PERSONNEL: Darod

Member of the Alsaurian resistance movement on a planet of the Mokra Order in the Delta Quadrant. In 2372, Darod helped the crew of the U.S.S. Voyager obtain tellerium. When Tuvok and Torres were captured by the Mokra Order, Darod helped Captain Janeway and Caylem rescue them from prison. Darod had thought Caylem to be a coward, but later, after witnessing his bravery, Darod swore that he would spread the word of Caylem's heroism. 

Reference: Resistance
