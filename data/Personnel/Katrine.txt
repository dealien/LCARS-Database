PERSONNEL: Katrine

Holodeck character. Owner of Le Coeur de Lion nightclub in the French town of Sainte Claire, occupied by Nazi troops during World War II. Katrine, portrayed by Kathryn Janeway, was also a member of the Resistance. 

Reference: The Killing Game
Portrayed by Kate Mulgrew.
