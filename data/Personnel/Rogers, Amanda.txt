BIOGRAPHICAL DETAILS: Amanda Rogers was the child of two Q who had assumed human form, and was unaware of her origin. While she was assigned to the Enterprise-D as student intern, Q came to test her abilities, allowing Amanda to use her Q powers for the first time. Before she left with Q to the Q Continuum, Amanda Rogers cleaned the air pollution of planet Tagra IV.
ACHIEVEMENTS, AWARDS: not on file
FAMILY: Parents, names not on file - Actually Q in human form (killed by freak tornado caused by Q Continuum)
PERSONNEL: Rogers, Amanda
 Young member of the Q Continuum who was raised as a human female on Earth. In 2369, Rogers served briefly as a student intern aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise until an intervention by Q. Later in life she began to come to terms with her powers.
