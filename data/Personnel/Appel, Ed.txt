PERSONNEL: Ed Appel

Chief processing engineer at the underground mining colony on planet Janus VI in 2267. Appel helped to defend the colony against an unknown adversary later learned to be a life-form known as a Horta.

Reference: The Devil in the Dark
Played by Brad Weston.
