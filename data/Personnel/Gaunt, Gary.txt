PERSONNEL: Gary Gaunt

Notorious pool hustler at Ames Pool Hall in Earth's New York City in 1953, who was simulated in Tom Paris' Chez Sandrine holodeck program aboard the U.S.S. Voyager. Gary's chauvinistic manner offended B'Elanna Torres. 

Reference: The Cloud
Portrayed by Larry Hanklin, more known as Mr Heckles on "FRIENDS."
