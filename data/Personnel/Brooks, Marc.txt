PERSONNEL: Marc Brooks
Crewman, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

Died 2366 in an accident five months before his 38th birthday. He was survived by his wife, Ensign Janet Brooks, also an Enterprise-D officer, who had trouble coping with his death and sought therapy with Counselor Troi.
