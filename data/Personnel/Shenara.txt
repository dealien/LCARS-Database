PERSONNEL: Shenara

Klingon princess, daughter of Emperor Reclaw, killed during K'Trelan's coup. Ten years later ex-concubine Karana secretly became Shenara and took the throne. Martok's wife claimed kinship with Shenara, but was really descended of Karana.
