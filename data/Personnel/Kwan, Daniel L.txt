PERSONNEL: Daniel L Kwan
Ensign, engineering officer, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

He was part of the team at Utopia Planitia Fleet Yards that helped build teh starship. Kwan's father was human, but his mother was Napean, which gane him some empathic abilities. In 2370, Kwan experiences empathic hallucinations that caused him to commit suicide by jumping into the plasma stream of one of the ship's warp drive nacelles. Prior to his death, Kwan had been romantically involved with Maddy Calloway.
