PERSONNEL: McConnell
Lieutenant, U.S.S. Honshu

Starfleet lieutenant assigned to the U.S.S. Honshu in 2374. 
According to Gul Dukat, McConnell was killed by shrapnel during a Cardassian attack, while attempting to abandon ship with an injured Captain Sisko.
