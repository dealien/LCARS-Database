PERSONNEL: Ramirez
Captain

Ramirez was a three-time champion tongo player who challenged a highly confident Jadzia Dax to a "no-limit" match. Dax, unaware of his past glories, lost the match and ended up owing two bars of latinum. 

Reference: The Darkness and the Light
