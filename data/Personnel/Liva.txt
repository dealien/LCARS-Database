PERSONNEL: Liva
diplomatic aide

In 2369 she served Ambassador Ves Alkar during the peace negotiations on planet Rekag-Seronia. She almost became another victim of Alkar's negative emotions. 

Reference: Man of the People 
Played by Stephanie Erb.
