PERSONNEL: Kurak

Female Klingon warp-field scientist attending a metaphasic shield demonstration on the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701-D in 2369. After the shield's Ferengi inventor was killed, Kurak seemed guilty. The perpetrator was Jo'Bril.
