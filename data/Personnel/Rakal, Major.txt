PERSONNEL: Rakal, 
Major, intelligence officer, Tal-Shiar

This Romulan Tal Shiar officer was secretly killed in 2369. Deanna Troi briefly assumed her identity in an effort to get Romulan defectors to the U.S.S. Enterprise. 

Reference: Face of the Enemy 
Portrayed by Mirina Sirtis/ Deanna Troi.
