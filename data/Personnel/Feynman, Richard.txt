PERSONNEL: Richard Feynman
PhD, physicist, Earth

Noted Nobel prize winner and bongo player. He was honored years later when a shuttlecraft aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise-D was named after him.

TIMELINE: 
1918: Born
1988: Died

Reference: Chain of Command
The name on shuttle's exterior was accidentally misspelled as Feyman as the result of an accident from the show's art department.
