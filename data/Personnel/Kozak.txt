PERSONNEL: Kozak
Head, House of Kozak, Qo'noS

Noted Klingon failure despite heading an influential House and marrying the formidable Grilka. He died in 2371 after falling on a weapon during a drunken outburst in Quark's bar on DS9. 

Reference: The House of Quark 
Played by John Lendale Bennett, who also played Gabriel Bell in "Past Tense."
