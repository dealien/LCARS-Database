PERSONNEL: Birta
DaiMon, government official, Ferenginar

Birta responded to an inquiry made by the U.S.S. Enterprise-D captain Jean-Luc Picard in 2370 regarding DaiMon Bok. Birta confirmed that Bok had in fact been stripped of his DaiMon title, but added that Bok had bought his way out of Rog Prison in 2368 and that he was last seen somewhere in the Dorias Cluster.

Reference: Bloodlines
Played by Peter Slutsker who also played  Nibor in "Menage a Troi," and Dr. Reyga in "Suspicions." The Continuum released incorrect images of this character- using images from the original episode "The Battle" instead of the intended show, "Bloodlines."
