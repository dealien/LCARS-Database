PERSONNEL: Bud Da Vinci

This crewman assisted Commander Montgomery Scott when he had to cut through the wall outside main engineering in order to override the locking mechanism and to open the doors. Inside Kevin Riley had taken control of the engines while he was under the influence of the Psi 2000 virus.

Reference: The Naked Time
Portrayed by Bud Da Vinci. The character's name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
