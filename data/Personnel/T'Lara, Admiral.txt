PERSONNEL: T`Lara
Admiral, judicial officer, Bajoran sector

Vulcan female Starfleet official who convened a hearing on DS9 in 2372 to decide if Worf should be extradited to the Klingon homeworld and face charges of mass murder. 

Reference: Rules of Acquisition 
Played by Deborah Strang.
