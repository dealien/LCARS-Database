PERSONNEL: Tula

Young woman from Beta III, controlled by Landru. She resided in a rooming house with Hacom, Tamar and her father Reger. Tula returned home from Festival in hysterics. 

Reference: The Return of the Archons 
Played by Brioni Farrell.
