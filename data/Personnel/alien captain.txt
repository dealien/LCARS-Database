PERSONNEL: alien captain

Nameless spacefarer who died in Chaotic Space after a year adrift. The Doctor's autopsy of the alien captain helped him understand the effects of Chaotic Space. 

Reference: The Fight
Played by Danny Goldring, Dennis Cockrun, Jeff Rickets, Ken Feinberg, Kevin McCorkle, Nicholas Worth
