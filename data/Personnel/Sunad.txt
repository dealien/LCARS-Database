PERSONNEL: Sunad

Zalkonian starship commander who enforced a genocidal program that almost wiped out all Zalkonians exhibiting isoelectrical bursts. Sunad confronted the U.S.S. Enterprise for custody of John Doe in 2366. 

Reference: Transfigurations 
Portrayed by Chalres Dennis.
