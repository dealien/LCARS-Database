PERSONNEL: Sovak

Shady Ferengi who aided archaeologist Dr. Samuel Estragon. Sovak was cheated by Estragon's assistant after he paid for information about a certain artifact. Sovak tracked the assistant to Risa in 2366. 

Reference: Captain's Holiday 
Portrayed by Max Grodenchik, whom also played Rom throughout DS9. The Continuum website credits this actor as Michael by mistake.
