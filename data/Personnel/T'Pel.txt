PERSONNEL: T'Pel

Vulcan woman who married Tuvok in 2304 and bore him three sons and a daughter. Tuvok assumed T'Pel would remarry after his disappearance, but her letter in 2374 indicated otherwise. 

Reference: Persistence of Vision, Flashback
Portrayed by Marva Hicks, Sierra Pecheur.
