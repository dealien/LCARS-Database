PERSONNEL: Sev Maylor 
Diplomatic aide, Lumeria

Female companion to Ves Alkar, one of a series Alkar used as empathic repositories for his unpleasant feelings. The maltreatment caused Maylor to age rapidly. Dr. Beverly Crusher's investigation into her premature death uncovered Alkar's abusive conduct.
