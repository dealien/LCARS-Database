PERSONNEL: news vender

A young man selling newspapers on the street corner of New York City, Earth, in the 1960s- or at least in this Prophet's induced vision experienced by Captain Benjamin Sisko. The boy resembled Ensign Nog.

Reference: Far Beyond the Stars
