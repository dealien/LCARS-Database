PERSONNEL: Ben Prieto
Lieutenant, helm officer, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

Officer stationed aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701-D. In 2364, Prieto was aboard a shuttle with Counselor Troi when the ship was forced by an evil entity named Armus to crash-land on Vagra II. Prieto suffered injury, but made a full recovery. 

Reference: Skin of Evil 
Portrayed by Raymond Forchion.
