PERSONNEL: Jacqueline Susann

1921-1974. Human novelist whose wildly successful novels, including "Valley of the Dolls," became James Kirk's vocabulary reference for 1986 Earth, ensuring his conversations contained the requisite colorful metaphors and profanity.

1974: Died
