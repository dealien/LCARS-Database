PERSONNEL: Marnie Eiger
Engineering officer, U.S.S. Enterprise-E

An engineer on the U.S.S. Enterprise-E. La Forge assigned her and Paul Porter to investigate the ship's overheating environmental controls, a condition ultimately traced to a Borg incursion. 

Reference: Star Trek: First Contact 
Played by Marnie McPhail. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
