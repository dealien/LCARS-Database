PERSONNEL: Midro

Educated Troglyte and terrorist, or Disrupter, on Ardana. Midro and Vanna abducted U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701 crew members in 2269, fearful they were summoned to put down the Troglyte quest for equality. 

Reference: The Cloud Minders 
Played by Ed Long.
