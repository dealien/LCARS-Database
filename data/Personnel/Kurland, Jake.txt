PERSONNEL: Jake Kurland

Young man living aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701-D whose Starfleet Academy aspirations were dashed in 2364. Upset, he ran away in Shuttlecraft 13. He, and the slightly damaged craft, returned later. 

Reference: Coming of Age
Played by Stephen Gregory.
