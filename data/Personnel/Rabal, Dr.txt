PERSONNEL: Rabal
Ph.D, scientist, Hekara II

Hekaran physicist who concurred with his sister, Serova, that warp drive travel was damaging to the fabric of space. In 2370, Serova's theories were presented to Federation representatives. 

Reference: Force of Nature 
Portrayed by Michael Corbett.
