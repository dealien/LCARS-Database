PERSONNEL: Klag
Warrior, I.K.S. Pagh

Klingon warrior serving aboard the Pagh in 2365 as second officer. William Riker was Klag's commander during the officer exchange program. Klag respected humans more after seeing Riker in action. 

Reference: A Matter of Honor 
Played by Brian Thompson, who also played Inglatu in "Rules of Acquisition" and the Klingon helm officer in "Generations."
