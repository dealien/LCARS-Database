CHEMISTRY: thalmerite

Explosive used in 2369 to destroy the Klingon bird-of-prey Toh'Kaht after its crew fell under the control of Saltah'na energy spheres.  The captain of a Numeri patrol ship released a Federation shutle when U.S.S. Voyager catain Kathryn Janeway told him it contained 40 tons of thalmerite.
