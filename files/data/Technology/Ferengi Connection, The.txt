RECREATION: The Ferengi Connection

Crime novel by Jake Sisko.  Written in late 2374.  Concerned two crime lords named Higgs and Fermion on a fictional Cardassian mining station in orbit of Bajor. Companion to The Cardassian Connection. 

Reference: Fall of Terok Nor
