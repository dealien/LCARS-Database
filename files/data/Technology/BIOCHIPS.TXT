TECHNOLOGY: biochips

Cybernetic inserts found in members of the Borg. Placed into the body via surgery, the chip enhance the physical capabilities of Borg individuals as well as generating organic material needed for survival by the organic components of a Borg individual's body. Death would result if the chips were removed from the individual.
