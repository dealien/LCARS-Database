TECHNOLOGY: access terminal

Used within Borg vessels, this systems connector port allows individuals a direct link to the collective. It is also possibly used for transmission of energy to and from individuals. These terminals can be found in work stations and regeneration alcoves throughout a Borg ship.

Reference: The Best of Both Wrolds, I Borg
