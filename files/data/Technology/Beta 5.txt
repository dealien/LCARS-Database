TECHNOLOGY: Beta 5 computer

Powerful computer system built by unknown extraterrestrials, used by Gary Seven on Earth in 1968 and secluded into his New York apartment. The artificial intelligence within the computer had, what is user said, was a snobbish personality.

Reference: Assignment: Earth 
Voice portrayed by Majel Barrett.
