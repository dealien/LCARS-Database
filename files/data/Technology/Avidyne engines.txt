TECHNOLOGY: Avidyne engines

Type of impulse engines used on Constellation-class starships, obsolete by 2365. The U.S.S. Hathaway, equipped with this engine, was used in a Starfleet battle exercise with the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701-D in 2365. 

Reference: Peak Performance 
The term "avidyne" was devised by writer Melinda Snodgradd as a variation of "Yoyodyne," a reference to the sult s-f movie Buckaroo Banzai, whose writer borrowed it from Thomas Pynchon's novel The Crying of Lot 49.
