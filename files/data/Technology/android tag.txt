TECHNOLOGY: android tags

A visual identification worn by the androids on Mudd's planet where he created numerous robots to serve him. The tags blinked when the android was awaiting instructions from the central processor. The tags were numbered to indicate which robot of a particular series was being addressed, there were hundreds of each model.

Reference: I, Mudd
