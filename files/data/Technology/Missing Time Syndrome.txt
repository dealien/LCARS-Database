MEDICAL: Missing Time Syndrome

Goes back to pre-contact Earth when the Reticulii abducted humans for genetic profiling and erased their memories to prevent them from knowing of their orbital medical ships. The First Contact Office performs the same procedures if its duck blinds are compromised.
