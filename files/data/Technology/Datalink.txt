TECHNOLOGY: Datalink

Lifelike robot designed by Pathfinder Six and Pathfinder Eight. They used them for a variety of purposes in carrying out the assassination of Pathfinder Twelve. Races mimicked included Klingon, Vulcan, and human. 

Reference: Memory Prime
