PROBES: EGG

An instrumented sensor probe, designed by Dr. Paul Stubbs to record the decay of neutronium expelled during a stellar explosion. Dr. Stubbs, who nicknamed his brainchild "the Egg," worked on it for twenty years before successfully launching it from the U.S.S. Enterprise-D at the Kavis Alpha Sector in 2366. 

Reference: Evolution
The full-scale egg prop was a modification of the virus containment device originally built for "The Child."
