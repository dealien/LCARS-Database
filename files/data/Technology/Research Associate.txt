TECHNOLOGY: Research Associate

Blue robotic device shaped like a box. Two meters by one byz one. They carry supplies in their manipulator appendages, haul equipment in carts, carry out maintenance work and research assignments, and carry messages to free people for more creative work. Hundreds prowl the corridors of Memory Prime.
