FOOD: Droli

A pale green-flecked biscuit sold by the bakery vendor on Deep Space Nine. It has a sweet, savory, herbal scent. Nog stole a droli from the vendor shortly after the Federation took over Deep Space Nine in 2369.   

Reference: Emissary
