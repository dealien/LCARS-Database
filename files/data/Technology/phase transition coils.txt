TECHNOLOGY: phase transition coils

Transporter component that converts a person's from matter to energy and back again. Failure of the transition cells was discounted as the cause of Ambassador's T'Pel's apparent death in 2367 when Transporter Chief O'Brien noted that the cells had been replaced just the week before.
