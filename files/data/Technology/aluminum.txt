CHEMISTRY: aluminum

Atomic element # , abbreivated Al on the Periodic table of elements. A light weight and plyable metal. Usually opaque. By manipulating the composition of an aluminum compound it was found that a transparent version could be manufactured It became a material commonly used in the twenty-third century as a common construction component. It was invented by Dr. Nichols of Plexicorp, a company located in San Francisco on twentieth century Earth. Dr. Nichols apparently had help from time-traveling members of the crew of the U.S.S. Enterprise.
