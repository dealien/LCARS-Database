CHEMISTRY: Crystal Ball

An amorphous uncut alien crystal/ meteor fragment found by Parneb in the desert.  Parneb wore a smaller piece at all times on a chain around his neck.  The stone worked with Parneb's natural psychic abilities but he had never fully succeeded in understanding how it worked. 

Reference: Strangers from the Sky
