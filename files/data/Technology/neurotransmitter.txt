TECHNOLOGY: neurotransmitter

A chemical in the brain that aids in the transfer of electrical stimulus between the synaptic end of a axon to the dentrite of another. 
On stardate 45944 while under the influence of the Kataan probe, Captain Jean-Luc Picard had increased activity neurotransmitter output.
