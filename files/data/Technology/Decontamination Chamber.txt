TECHNOLOGY: Decontamination Chamber

Facility on Enterprise-Class starships such as NX-01 for decontaminating crewmembers after exposure to dangerous life forms on alien worlds. It used a combination of ultraviolet radiation and various gels to achieve its effect. 

Reference: Broken Bow
