TECHNOLOGY: Frosty the Vulcan

A snowman built by Ensign Travis Mayweather and Lieutenant Malcolm Reed while the two conducted mining operations on a distant comet. Captain Jonathan Archer was not impressed especially since a Vulcan starship watched their every move as well.
