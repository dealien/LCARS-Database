TECHNOLOGY: particle fountain

Mining device invented by Dr. Farallon in 2369. The device was mounted aboard a space station orbiting planet Tyrua VII-A, where it would project a forcefield down to the planet's surface. The fountain, however, malfunctioned when tested.
