FOOD: Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Earth food. In 2370, Quark served Jadzia Dax a grilled cheese sandwich at her request.  Both were repulsed by the look of it, though Quark was certain that it was cooked thoroughly since he put it through the replicator twice.
