TECHNOLOGY: duotronics

Breakthrough computer system invented in 2243 by Dr. Richard Daystrom, used  for over 80 years as the basis of computer processing on Federation starships -- such as the main computer system and sensor arrays of the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701. It was replacement by isolinear technology in 2329.
