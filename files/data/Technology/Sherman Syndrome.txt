MEDICAL: Sherman Syndrome

A complex pattern of crop failure, political mismanagement, and faulty economic planning on colony planets. First charted on Sherman's Planet. Instigated by Pathfinder Twelve. An entire analytical division at Memory Gamma was set up to investigate it, though no useful conclusions emerged.
