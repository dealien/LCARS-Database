TECHNOLOGY: EJ7 interlock

Device used to open critical system access panels by Engineering personnel aboard Deep Space Nine. Neela stole Chief O'Brien's interlock from his tool kit to prepare an escape for after her planned assassination of Vedek Bareil. 

Reference: In the Hands of the Prophets
