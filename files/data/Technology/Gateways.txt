TECHNOLOGY: Gateways

Iconian transporters which allowed instantaneous travel to anywhere in the galaxy, at least. Rebel Jem'Hadar found one on Vandros IV; another was located on their homeworld within the Neutral Zone near Romulan space. The Dominion knew of them as well as the Federation. They generate an energy dampening field.
