TECHNOLOGY: ultraviolet satellite

Artificial satellites designed to emit ultraviolet radiation. In 2267, the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701 used 210 satellites to kill the neural parasite infection on planet Deneva. 

Reference: Operation: Annihilate!
