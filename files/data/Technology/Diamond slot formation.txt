TECHNOLOGY: diamond slot formation

An aerobatic maneuver requiring five single-pilot spacecraft. The outer four craft form up in a diamond shape, with the fifth craft inserting itself into the center of slot of the diamond. The maneuver was used as ademonstration of piloting prowess by cadets at Starfleet Academy.
