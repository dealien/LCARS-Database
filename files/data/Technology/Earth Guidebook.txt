TECHNOLOGY: A Cadet's Guide to Sector 001: Earth

PADD containing an interactive program detailing Earth's customs, culture, history and geography. In 2372, Dr. Julian Bashir gave an Earth guidebook padd to Nog to use while he was at Starfleet Academy.

Reference: Little Green Men
