MEDICAL: Interspecies Medical Exchange

Vulcan-initiated program that facilitates the exchange of knowledge among physicians of many different species. 
Dr. Phlox served in the program prior to joining the crew of Enterprise. Phlox also made a human friend through the program, Dr. Jeremy Lucas who worked on Phlox’s homeworld of Denobula. Admiral Forrest attended a briefing with the Interspecies Medical Exchange (IME) in March 2152. 

Reference: Broken Bow, Shockwave (novelization)
