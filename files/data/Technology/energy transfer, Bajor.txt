TECHNOLOGY: Bajoran energy transfer

With Federation aid, Bajor planned to make its first-ever large-scale energy transfer to the planet by tapping the molten core of its one-time inhabited fifth moon, Jeraddo, to heat a few hundred thousand of its homes in the winter.
