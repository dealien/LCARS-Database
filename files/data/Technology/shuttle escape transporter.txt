TECHNOLOGY: shuttle escape transporter

A short range transporter built into some Federation shuttlecraft. Due to its limited range it is used mostly in case of an emergency. 
Lieutenant Worf and Commander Data used the shuttle escape transporter on their way mission to rescue Captain Picard from Borg captivity in 2367. 

Reference: The Best of Both Worlds
