TECHNOLOGY: holographic information module

Device which can store and transmit holographic information, like a floppy disk or ioslinear chip but more advanced to handle the more complicated type of information. Data discovered such a module in the cranium of his "mother" which transmitted a message from his creator, Dr. Noonien Soong. Soong asked that his wife not be told the reality of her situation -- that her consciousness had been transferred into the body of an artificial lifeform. 

Reference: Inheritance
