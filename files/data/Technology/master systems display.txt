TECHNOLOGY: master systems display

A display/control console located in Main Engineering aboard starships.

Reference: Encounter at Farpoint, Where No One Has Gone Before, Remember Me
The console was built from the video display table from Starfleet Command seen in The Voyage Home. For obvious reasons the crew nicknamed it the pol table.
