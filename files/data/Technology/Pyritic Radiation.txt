TECHNOLOGY: Pyritic Radiation

Type of radiation the Vulcans claimed emanated from the Xindi destroyer/probe.  In reality, there had been no radiation exposure, and its existence was part of a ruse concocted by Soval and Fer'at to determine Jonathan Archer's sanity.

Reference: The Expanse
