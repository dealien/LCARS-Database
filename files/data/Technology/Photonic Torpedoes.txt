WEAPON: Photonic Torpedoes

New weapon technology developed while Enterprise NX-01 was on its mission of exploration.  When the ship returned to Earth in April 2153, these new weapons were installed.  They had a ranged fifty times greater than Enterprise's old spatial torpedoes and a variable yield.
