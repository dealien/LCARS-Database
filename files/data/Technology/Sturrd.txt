FOOD: Sturrd

Pandrilite dish. According to Lieutenant Vigo, whose favorite it was, it is the signature dish of his homeworld. One of the humans aboard the U.S.S. Stargazer had described it as “a mound of sand and ground glass smothered in maple syrup”. 

Reference: The Valiant
