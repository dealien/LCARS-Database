TECHNOLOGY: subcutaneous transponder

Starfleet-issue device inserted into the skin to enable the wearer of such a device to be tracked. In 2268, Dr. McCoy placed two such devices in Captain Kirk and Commander Spock before they beamed down to planet Ekos.
