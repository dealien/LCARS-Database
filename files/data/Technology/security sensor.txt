TECHNOLOGY: security sensor

A variety of automated detection sensors on starships and stations of many different species. Typical security sensors are often programmed to scan for weapons and contraband passing through checkpoints like airlocks. 
Sensors on Deep Space Nine detected the Hunter's weapons and activated the alert.
