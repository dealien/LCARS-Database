TECHNOLOGY: Living Universe Theory

Theory created by the Pathfinders. Postulates the Universe is a living organism. According to the theory, the Universe is about to bud.  The Pathfinders designed a device to contact the Living Universe and then discovered that eighteen civilizations in other galaxies had already done so.
