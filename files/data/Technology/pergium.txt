CHEMISTRY: pergium

A rare element found on Janus VI. The Federation miners encountered problems with the extraction process when the disturbed The Horta and her eggs. After assistance from the U.S.S. Enterprise, and a mind meld between Commander Spock and the Horta, an amicable relationship was developed. The Horta did agree to use its poisonous extretions to dig through the planet to assist the miners in finding pergium deposits.
