WEAPONS: Gin'tak

Wavy-bladed ceremonial Klingon spear used in battle. 
Also a Klingon term for an advisor so trusted as to become part of the family. Alexander Rozhenko from 2410 posed a K'mtar, gin'tak to the house of Mogh when he visited Worf in 2370.

Reference: Birthright, Firstborn
The website and the book had different definitions for this word; the website listed the weapon, while the book indicated the family connection.
