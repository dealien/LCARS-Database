WEAPONS: laser weapons

Defensive directed-energy technology -- an acronym for Light Amplification by Stimulated Emissions of Radiation -- used by the Federation before the advent of the phaser. By the 2250s, hand-held sidearms as well as a large laser cannon for planetary use were employed. 

Reference: The Cage, The Menagerie
