WEAPONS: Tantulus field

Weapon secretly used by Captain Kirk of the I.S.S. Enterprise in the mirror universe. The device tracked targets with a small viewscreen, enabling the operator to vaporize the target at the touch of the activating button. 

Reference: Mirror, Mirror
