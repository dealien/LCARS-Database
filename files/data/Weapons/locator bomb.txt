WEAPONS: locator bomb

Advanced weapon mounted with a sorium argine explosive that could seek out and track a target using sensors capable of detecting the pheromones of the targeted individual. In 2369, Rom and Krax used one of these weapons in an attempted assassination of then-Grand Nagus Quark. 

Reference: The Nagus
