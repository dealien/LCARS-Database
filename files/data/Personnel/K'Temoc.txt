PERSONNEL: K`Temoc
Captain, starship commander, I.K.S. T'Ong

Klingon captain of the I.K.S. T'Ong who spent 75 years in stasis, awakening in 2365. He and his crew were part of a sleeper program designed to place warriors into the future in case the battle against the Federation cost the Klingon Empire too dearly. K'Temoc had orders to attack the nearest Federation settlement upon his awakening. He attacked the U.S.S. Enterprise-D until Worf convinced him that Klingon-Federation hostilities had ceased. 

Reference: The Emissary 
Played by Lance le Gault.
