PERSONNEL: Michael Burleigh
Lord, head of household

A holonovel character in the holodeck program Janeway Lambda-1. Lord Burleigh was a widower who hired Mrs. Lucille Davenport, played by Kathryn Janeway, as a governess to care for his children, Henry and Beatrice. He and "Mrs. Davenport" developed an improper romance that the children did not approve of. 

Reference: Cathexis
Portrayed by Michael Cumpsty. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
