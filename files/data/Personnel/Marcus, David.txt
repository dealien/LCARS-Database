PERSONNEL: David Marcus
PhD, biologist, Regula

A key scientist on Project Genesis who introduced protomatter in the project's matrix. Marcus was murdered by Klingons in 2285. The only son of James T. Kirk and Dr. Carol Marcus. 
His death was considered part of a motive by James Kirk during his murder trial on Qo'noS. An excerpt from his personal log quoted him for saying, "I have never trusted Klingons, and I never will. I can never forgive them for the death of my boy."

Reference: The Wrath of Khan, The Search for Spock, The Undiscovered Country
Played by Merrick Butrick who also played in "Symbiosis."
