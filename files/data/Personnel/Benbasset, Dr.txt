PERSONNEL: Benbasset
Doctor, medical officer, Deep Space Nine

One of Nog's doctors in 2375. 
Benbasset assured Nog and Ezri Dax that Nog's new biosynthetic leg worked perfectly and the young Ferengi's need for a cane was not biological.
