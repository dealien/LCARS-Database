PERSONNEL: Pierson
Lieutenant, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

Crew member aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701-D in 2369. Pierson helped work on the particle fountain, pioneered by Dr. Farallon, that was in orbit around planet Tyrus VII-A.
