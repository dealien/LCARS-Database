PERSONNEL: Gaul

Male Vaadwaur, who survived 900 years of bio-stasis. Gaul worked with Tuvok to develop a tactical plan against the Turei, but secretly plotted to seize U.S.S. Voyager. 

Reference: Dragon's Teeth
Portrayed by Robert Knepper.
