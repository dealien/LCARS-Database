PERSONNEL: Goris
Legate, batallion commander, Cardassia Prime

Cardassian soldier who joined Damar against the Dominion. Goris and Gul Revok commanded 500,000 men between them. Before Goris could fight, he was betrayed by Revok and killed by the Jem'Hadar. 

Reference: The Dogs of War
