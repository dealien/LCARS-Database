PERSONNEL: Sarin

Suliban female and former Cabal member. Sarin left the Cabal when she became disenchanted with its methods and direction. She provided Klaang with proof that the Cabal was responsible for the hostilities within the Klingon Empire, and later gave Captain Archer the same information. She was killed shortly thereafter by Silik. 

Reference: Broken Bow
Portrayed by Melinda Clarke.
