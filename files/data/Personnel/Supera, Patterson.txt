PERSONNEL: Patterson Supera

A child residing aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise in 2368. Supera distinguished himself in a shipboard science fair. Supera was later trapped with Picard when a quantum filament disabled the ship. 

Reference: Disaster 
Portrayed by Max Supera.
