PERSONNEL: Viorsa

Hailing from the Delta Quadrant Kohl settlement, where severe glaciation struck, Viorsa was one of five settlers to enter a hibernation system. A malfunction in the system created an evil clown from their collective subconscious fears. The clown soon killed Viorsa. 

Reference: The Thaw
Portrayed by Thomas Kopache, whom also played Mirok in "The Next Phase."
