PERSONNEL: John Farrell
Lieutenant, navigator, U.S.S. Enterprise

Starfleet officer serving on the U.S.S. Enterprise in 2266. 
In the mirror universe Farrell thwarted an assassination attempt on his Captain Kirk. By betraying Chekov in the attempt he hoped to earn favor with the captain. He looked much different in the mirror universe than in the regular universe.

Reference: Mudd's Women, Mirror, Mirror, The Enemy Within, Miri
Played by Jim Goodwin and Pete Kellett for the mirror universe scenes.
