PERSONNEL: Tristan Adams, MD
Assistant director, Tantalus V

In 2266, Adams took control of the colony after director Simon Van Gelder went insane from exposure to a neural neutralizer device he had been experimenting with. Adams himself died from exposure to the unit. 

Reference: Dagger of the Mind 
Played by James Gregory.
