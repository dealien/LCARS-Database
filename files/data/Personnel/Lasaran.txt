PERSONNEL: Lasaran
Soukara outpost

Cardassian undercover operative who was providing military intelligence for Starfleet in 2374. Lasaran was killed when Worf disregarded a rescue rendezvous in order to save Jadzia instead. 

Reference: Change of Heart 
Portrayed by Todd Waring.
