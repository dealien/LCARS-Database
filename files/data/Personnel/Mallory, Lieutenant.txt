PERSONNEL: Jay Mallory
Lieutenant, U.S.S. Enterprise

Young U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701 crew member who died in 2267 after stepping on a landmine-like rock on Gamma Trianguli VI. Mallory's father facilitated James T. Kirk's admittance into Starfleet Academy. 

Reference: The Apple 
Played by Jay Jones. The character's last name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
