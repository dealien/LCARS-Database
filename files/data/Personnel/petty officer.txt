PERSONNEL: petty officer

A rank of non-commissioned standing and of varying levels. Petty officer 1st-class, 2nd-class, 3rd-class, Chief petty officer, Master chief petty officer, etc. Miles Edward O'Brien earned the rank of Master Chief Petty officer, the highest rank a non-officer can get.

Reference: Elaan of Troyius 
Played by Jay Robinson.
