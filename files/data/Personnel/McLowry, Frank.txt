PERSONNEL: Frank McLowry

He and his brother Tom were outlaw brothers from Earth's 19th Century Wild West. 
Spock was cast as Frank and McCoy as Tom during the Melkotians' stylized recreation of the gunfight at the OK Corral in 2268. 

Reference: Spectre of the Gun
Played by Leonard Nimoy.
