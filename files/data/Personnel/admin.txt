PERSONNEL: Piet van Stralen
Fleet Admiral, software engineer, Starfleet Intelligence

This senior software developer works in the Okuda Labs working on the Library Computer Access & Retrieval System (LCARS). Building on the work started by his predecessor, Michael Okuda, Piet is responsible for making changes to the software to keep it up to date and in tune with various network terminals and their unique configurations.
