PERSONNEL: Noor
Planetary leader, J'naii

In 2368, Noor was the leader of the androgynous J'naii. After a hearing, Noor ordered Soren receive psychotectic therapy to lose her female orientation and mesh with normal J'naii society. 

Reference: The Outcast 
Portrayed by Megan Cole.
