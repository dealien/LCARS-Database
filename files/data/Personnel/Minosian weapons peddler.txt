PERSONNEL: Minosian weapons peddler

A holographic salesperson included in a computer program used to sell weapons like the Echo Papa series drones. The adaptive program included demonstration capabilities, question and answer algorithyms, and a presentation script. The program was able to learn from its previous encounters. Crew from the U.S.S. Enterprise-D activated the program, which then launched armed drones at them. They were able to stop the drones by agreeing to purchase the weapons at which time the program ended the demonstation and recalled the drones.
