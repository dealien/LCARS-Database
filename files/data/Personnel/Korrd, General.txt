PERSONNEL: Korrd
General, diplomatic officer, Nimbus III

Brilliant Klingon military strategist who spent his emeritus years on Nimbus III as a diplomat. In 2287 Korrd fell under Sybok's influence. 

Reference: The Final Frontier 
Played by Charles Cooper, who also played K'mpec in "Sins of the Father" and "Reunion."
