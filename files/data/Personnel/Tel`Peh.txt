PERSONNEL: Tel`Peh
Captain, starship commander, I.K.S. Tel'Peh

In 2369, Tel'Peh's senior officers thought him insane for executing two loyal crewmen. Tel'Peh was influenced by Gamma Quadrant artifacts that caused the ship's destruction. 

Reference: Dramatis Personae
