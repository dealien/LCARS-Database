PERSONNEL: Edward Fisher
Crewman, Geological Technician, U.S.S. Enterprise

Member of the landing party on Alfa 117 in 2266. He unknowingly contaminated the transporter of the U.S.S. Enterprise with magnetic ore, which resulted in the accidental creation of a duplicate James T. Kirk. 

Reference: The Enemy Within 
Played by Edward Madden. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
