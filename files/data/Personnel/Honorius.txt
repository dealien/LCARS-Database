PERSONNEL: Flavius Honorius

The last Western Roman emporer who was in power when the Visigoths sacked Rome.
Captain Jean -Luc Picard, on the eve of the battle of Wolf 359, compared the fate of the Federation at the hands of the Borg to the defeat of the Roman Empire under Honorius.

TIMELINE:
384: Born
423: Died
