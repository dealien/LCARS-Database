PERSONNEL: T`Vis

An outstanding Klingon warrior rewarded, in 2373, with the medallion of the Order of the Bat'leth from Chancellor Gowron in a ceremony on Ty'Gokor. T'Vis was the son of Barot. 

Reference: Apocalypse Rising
