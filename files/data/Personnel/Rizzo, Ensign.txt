PERSONNEL: Jerry Rizzo
Ensign, security officer, U.S.S. Enterprise

Rizzo perished in the line of duty when a dikironium cloud creature consumed 60 percent of his red blood cells. 

Reference: Obsession 
Played by Jerry Ayres. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
