PERSONNEL: James Kopf
Ensign, engineering officer, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

Kopf tried to take the ship out of warp when Picard transferred ship's controls to the engineering department. He didn't succeed. 

Reference: Brothers 
Played by James Lashly, who also appeared as a security officer on several episodes of Deep Space 9. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actors real name.
