PERSONNEL: William Barret Travis

Texas commander who died during the Battle of the Alamo. In the Alamo holoprogram on DS9, Bashir played the role of Travis. O'Brien was Crockett, and Odo was Santa Anna. 

TIMELINE:
1809: Born
1836: Died
