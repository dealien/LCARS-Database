PERSONNEL: Carlos Dern
Ensign, engineering officer, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

Systems engineer that was suggested by Lieutenant Reginald Barlcay to serve on an away mission to the U.S.S. Yosemite, but Geordi wanted Barclay to come along. He also served at the conn in 2370. He was killed by another member of the crew who was under the influence of Barclay's Protomorphosis Syndrome.

Reference: Realm of Fear, Genesis
Played by Carlos Ferro. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name. Dern's name was never mentioned on air and is from the script.
