PERSONNEL: Yassim
Vedek, Bajor

Senior Bajoran holy woman who believed the 2374 alliance with the Dominion was wrong and should be opposed. Yassim hung herself from Deep Space 9's promenade to dramatically urge Bajorans to resist. 

Reference: Rocks and Shoals 
Portrayed by Lilyan Chauvin.
