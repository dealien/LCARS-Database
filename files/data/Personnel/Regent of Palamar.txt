PERSONNEL: Regent of Palamar
Regent, planetary leader, Palamar

In 2373, the Regent arrived at DS9 attempting to buy biological weapons to use against his enemies, General Nassuc that led the rebellious faction in his planetary civil war. The Regent was later killed returning to his homeworld after Quark stopped the weapons deal from being completed. 

Reference: Business as Usual 
Played by Lawrence Tierney, wno also played Cyrus Redblock in "The Goodbye."
