PERSONNEL: Sharat
Ambassador, diplomatic officer, Kellerun

The Kellerun negotiator who framed a T'Lani peace accord in 2370. Sharat ordered the deaths of Bashir and O'Brien because they knew too much about Harvesters, the Kellerun/T'Lani biomechanical weapons. 

Reference: Armageddon Game 
Played by Peter White. The Continuum displayed an image of E'Tyshra, the T'Lani counterpart by mistake.
