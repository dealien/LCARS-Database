PERSONNEL: Lee Kelso
Lieutenant, chief helm officer, U.S.S. Enterprise

One of the original starship's senior officers that served under Captain Robert April, Captain Christopher Pike, and Captain James Kirk. He was killed by Commander Gary Mitchell after that officer was not working with his right mind and Kelso was helping to implement a plan to quarantine Mitchell on planet Delta Vega.

Reference: Where No One Has Gone Before, Q Neighbor
Played by Paul Carr.
