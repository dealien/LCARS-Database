PERSONNEL: Kruge
Commander, starship commander

Klingon bird-of-prey captain in 2285 tracking the Project Genesis weapon. Kruge's quest destroyed the U.S.S. Grissom and executed David Marcus. Kirk killed Kruge by dropping him into a lava pit. 

Reference: The Search for Spock 
Played by Christopher Lloyd.
