PERSONNEL: Tash

Individual encountered by the U.S.S. Voyager in the Delta Quadrant in 2377. Tash had constructed a massive tetrion-powered catapult in his attempt to return home, leading Seven of Nine to falsely accuse him of being a Caretaker. 

Reference: The Voyager Conspiracy
Portrayed by Albie Selznick.
