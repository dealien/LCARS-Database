PERSONNEL: Sarda
Dabo operator, Deep Space Nine

Dabo girl whose contract at Quark's contained a clause regarding sexual favors with the bar's proprietor. Sisko later dismissed this clause and said it could be ignored since such clauses are not enforceable in Federation jurisdictions. 

Reference: Captive Pursuit 
Played by Kelly Curtis.
