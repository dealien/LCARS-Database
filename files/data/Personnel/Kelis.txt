PERSONNEL: Kelis

Male poet on a Delta Quadrant planet were B'Elanna Torres crashed the Delta Flyer. Kelis saved Torres and traded dilithium for information he used as source material to write plays about the "Voyager Eternals." 

Reference: Muse 
Portrayed by Joseph Will.
