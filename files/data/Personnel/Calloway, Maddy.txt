PERSONNEL: Calloway, Maddy
Crewman, medical technician, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

Starfleet medical technician serving on the Enterprise-D. Calloway was romantically involved with Engineering officer Lt. Daniel Kwan when he committed suicide in 2370 due to empathic hallucinations. 

Reference: Eye of the Beholder 
Played by Johanna McCloy.
