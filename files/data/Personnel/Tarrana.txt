PERSONNEL: Tarrana

Young woman from Boraal II presented to Worf as a potential mate by her father, Kateras. Worf had other concerns, namely transplanting Tarrana and her people to Vacca VI. 

Reference: Homeward 
Portrayed by Susan Christy.
