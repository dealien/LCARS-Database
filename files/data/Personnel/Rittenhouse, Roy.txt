PERSONNEL: Roy Rittenhouse

Illustrator who worked for the science fiction magazine Incredible Tales on 20th-century Earth. Rittenhouse's lurid style influenced some of the magazine's writers, like Benny Russell. 

Reference: Far Beyond the Stars 
Played by J.G. Hertzler, who also played Martok in the latter seasons of DS9. The Continuum featured images of Benny Russel with this entry by mistake, not the proper character.
