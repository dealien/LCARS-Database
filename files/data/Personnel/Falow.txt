PERSONNEL: Falow

Head of the Wadi delegation that arrived at Deep Space 9 in 2369, the first formal contact between the Alpha and Gamma Quadrants. Bearing the title Master Surchid, Falow led the patrons of Quark's bar in the Wadi game of Chula. 

Reference: Move Along Home 
Played by Joel Brooks.
