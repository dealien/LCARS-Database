PERSONNEL: Vina

The only surviving crew member when the S.S. Columbia crashed on the Alpha Quadrant planet of Talos IV. Her care and recuperation were handled by the Talosians, but because they were unaware how a healthy human female should look, Vina remained severely disfigured. 

Reference: The Cage 
Played by Susan Oliver.
