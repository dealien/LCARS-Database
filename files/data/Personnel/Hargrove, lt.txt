PERSONNEL: Hargrove
Lieutenant, U.S.S. Voyager

Crewman aboard the U.S.S. Voyager. The ship's EMH believed Lt. Hargrove had Arethian flu in 2371. Neelex was jealous of him because he suspected Kes visited Hargrove in his quarters on Deck 7.
