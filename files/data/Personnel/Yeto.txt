PERSONNEL: Yeto

Impulsive young Klingon mercenary hired by Verad, a Trill, to rob Jadzia of her Dax symbiont in 2370. Yeto had to be kept in close check by his mentor T'Kar. 

Reference: Invasive Procedures 
Played by Steve Rankin, who also played Patahk in "The Enemy."
