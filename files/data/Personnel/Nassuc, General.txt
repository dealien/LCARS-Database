PERSONNEL: Nassuc
General, military leader, Palamar

Palamarian military officer, a favorite of the Regent, until she betrayed him by declaring her homeworld's independence. Quark and his business partners Gaila and Hagath attempted to sell lethal Varaxian LM-VII to Nassuc for use in her conflict with the Regent.
