PERSONNEL: Yeln

Bajoran man who confirmed sharing a Cardassian prison cell, at Elemspur Detention Center, with Kira Nerys during the occupation. Yeln was later exposed as an Obsidian Order operative. 

Reference: Second Skin 
Played by Tony Papenfus.
