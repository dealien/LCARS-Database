PERSONNEL: Ramsey, Dr. 
Archaeologist. In 2365, Ramsey was stationed aboard the U.S.S. Yamato during an expedition to Denius III. Some of the artifacts uncovered at Denius III led to the discovery by Captain Donald Varley of the lost planet of Iconia. 
Reference: Contagion
