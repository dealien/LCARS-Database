PERSONNEL: Vanna

Vanna led the working-class Troglyte agitators on the Alpha Quadrant planet Ardana. Her group, known as the Disrupters, were instrumental in the Troglytes winning greater parity with the cloud dwellers in 2269. 

Reference: The Cloud Minders 
Played by Charlene Polite.
