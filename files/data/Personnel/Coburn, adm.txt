PERSONNEL: Admiral Coburn

Starfleet officer who in 2374 was one of the admirals who approved Captain Sisko's plan to retake Deep Space 9 from Dominion forces. Cobum was concerned that the action would leave Earth vulnerable, but he supported the plan nevertheless.
