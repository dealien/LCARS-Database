PERSONNEL: Vince Matthews
Crewman, security officer, U.S.S. Enterprise

Security officer assigned, with Rayburn, to the caverns of Exo III in 2266. Matthews was shoved into an abyss by the ancient android, Ruk. 

Reference: What Are Little Girls Made Of? 
Played by Vince Deadrick. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
