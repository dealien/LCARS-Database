PERSONNEL: Trentin Fala

Bajoran woman who washed floors in a Cardassian records center to cover her spying activities for the Shakaar resistance cell. Trentin was later murdered by Silaran Prin using a remat detonator. 

Reference: The Darkness and the Light 
Played by Jennifer Savidge.
