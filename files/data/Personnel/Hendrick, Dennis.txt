PERSONNEL: Dennis Hendrick
Chief, transporter operator, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

Officer aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise-D assigned to transporter duty in 2367. Hendrick was on duty when the transformed Commander La Forge beamed down to the surface of Tarchannen III. La Forge overpowered Hendrick, and he was unable to stop the transport. 

Reference: Identity Crisis 
Played by Dennis Madalone, who also played many other parts while performing stunt acting. He has been seen as a variety of security guards and other victims of mayhem.
