PERSONNEL: Mika

Bajoran woman and follower of the Dukat-led Pah-wraith cult of 2375. Mika gave birth to a half-Cardassian child, which Dukat told his retinue was a miracle. When it became clear that Mika would reveal that Dukat was the father, he tried to kill her. Her husband Benyan figured out the deception but stayed quiet in support of their religeon. When Mika did reveal the news he supported her.

Reference: Covenant
Portrayed by Maureen Flannigan
