PERSONNEL: Cloud William

Leader of the Yangs on planet Omega IV whom a landing party from the U.S.S. Enterprise encountered in 2268. Cloud William was known among his people variously as the Guardian of the Holies, the Son of Chiefs and the Speaker of Holy Words. 

Reference: The Omega Glory 
Played by Roy Jenson.
