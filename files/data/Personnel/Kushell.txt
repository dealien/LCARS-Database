PERSONNEL: Kushell
Secretary, Legation of Unity, Straleb

Kushell intercepted the U.S.S. Enterprise-D demanding custody of Okona, accusing him of crimes later confessed to by Kushell's son and future daughter-in-law. 

Reference: The Outrageous Okona 
Played by Albert Stratton.
