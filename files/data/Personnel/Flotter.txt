PERSONNEL: Flotter

A fictional holodeck character that appeals to children. An aquatic being, he and his various "friends" are the featured participants in children's holodeck stories that are designed to teach basic skills and lessons. He is often accompanied by Trevis, a tree-like character. His stories include: "The Adventures of Flotter" and the story "Flotter and the Fire Ogre"

Reference: Bliss, Once Upon a Time
The stuffed doll featured in the series of this character was one of the items auctioned off by Christie's 40 Years of Star Trek Auction, lot 298, in October 2006.
