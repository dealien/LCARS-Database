PERSONNEL: Amat'igan

A Jem'Hadar soldier who accompanied the Founder leader on stardate 49962.4 aboard the U.S.S. Defiant when they were used to escort Odo to the Founders' homeworld. Amat'igan took the conn during the trip.

Reference: Broken Link
Played by Andrew Hawkes.
