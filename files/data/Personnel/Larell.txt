PERSONNEL: Larell

Humanoid female, once married to Morn for two years. Larell used her cobra-like seductiveness on Quark to try to find Morn's fortune before Krit and Nahsk. She failed, however, and was arrested. 

Reference: Who Mourns for Morn? 
Portrayed by Bridget Ann White.
