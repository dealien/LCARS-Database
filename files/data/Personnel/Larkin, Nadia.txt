PERSONNEL: Nadia Larkin
Lieutenant, starbase commander, AR-558

Starfleet Lieutenant stationed at the Federation base on AR-558. Following the deaths of her superior officers, Larkin was in command of the besieged troops resupplied by the U.S.S. Defiant in 2375. 

Reference: The Siege of AR-558
