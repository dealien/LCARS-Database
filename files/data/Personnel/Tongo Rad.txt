PERSONNEL: Tongo Rad

Son of the Catullan ambassador and one of the followers of Dr. Sevrin that was searching for the mythical place of Eden. He was part of the group that stole the Aurora, was seized by the U.S.S. Enterprise, and tried to commandeer that starship to continue their quest. He along with the other searchers, died after eating poisonous fruit on a planet they misidentified as Eden.

Reference: The Way to Eden
Played by Victor Brandt.
