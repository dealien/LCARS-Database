PERSONNEL: Martin Benbeck

Inhabitant of the isolated Genome colony on planet Moab IV. Benbeck was genetically engineered for his function as the interpreter of the society's laws. Benbeck was a strict isolationist, who even opposed letting Enterprise-D crew members visit the colony to help avert a natural disaster. 

Reference: The Masterpiece Society 
Played by Ron Canada who also played Ch'Pok iand Fesek. He is pictured on the far left.
