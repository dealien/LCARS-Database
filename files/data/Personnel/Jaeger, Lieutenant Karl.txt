PERSONNEL: Karl Jaeger
Lieutenant, geologist, U.S.S. Enterprise

After Captain James Kirk was kidnapped to Gothos by Trelane, Jaeger was one of three sent to ascertain Kirk's status. 

Reference: The Squire of Gothos 
Played by Richard Carlyle.
