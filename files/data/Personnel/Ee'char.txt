PERSONNEL: Ee`char

Miles O'Brien's "cellmate" during his Argrathi "artificial memory incarceration." During this experience, O'Brien killed Ee'char for some scraps of food, and was haunted by the virtual reality character after returning to DS9. 

Reference: Hard Time 
Played by Craig Wasson.
