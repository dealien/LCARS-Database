PERSONNEL: Loskene
Commander, starship commander

A Tholian starship captain. Loskene built an energy net around the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701 in 2268 when the Federation ship drifted into Tholian territory while rescuing Kirk. 

Reference: The Tholian Web 
Voice played by Barbara Babcock.
