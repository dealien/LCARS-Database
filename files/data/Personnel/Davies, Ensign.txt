PERSONNEL: Nicholas Davies
Ensign, geologist, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

Davies participated in the geological survey of planets in the Selcundi Drema sector in 2365 which was being supervised by Acting Ensign Wesley Crusher. Davies attempted to assist Crusher in the project, but his offers of help only served to undermine young Crusher's confidence. 

Reference: Pen Pals 
Played by Nicholas Cascone. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
