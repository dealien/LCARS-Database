PERSONNEL: Mariah Henley
Maquis, operations officer, U.S.S. Voyager

Served under Chakotay in 2371 on a Maquis ship chased by Cardassians into the Badlands and lost in the Delta Quadrant . After joining the Voyager crew she was, at first, a discipline problem. Accordingly, Henley was among several Maquis crew members assigned to Lieutenant Tuvok for field training.
