PERSONNEL: chronicle

The title given to the individual who kept the pictoral hisotry of a Boraalan village.
Vorin kept the chronicle of his village. Following his death in 2370 he was succeeded by Nikolai Rozhenko, an offworlder who had saved Vorin's village.
