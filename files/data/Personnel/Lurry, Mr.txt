PERSONNEL: Whit Lurry
Station manager, Deep Space Station K-7

In 2267 Lurry tried to keep things quiet despite the best efforts of Nilz Baris, Arne Darvin, annoying Klingons, Tribbles, and a shore leave party. 

Reference: The Trouble With Tribbles, More Tribbles, More Troubles, Trials and Tribble-ations 
Played by Whit Bissell. The character's first name is conjetural based on the actor's real name.
