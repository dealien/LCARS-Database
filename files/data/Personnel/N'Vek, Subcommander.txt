PERSONNEL: N`Vek
Subcommander, executive officer, R.S.E. Khazara
 
Romulan officer on the Khazara under Commander Toreth in 2369. N'Vek kidnapped Deanna Troi, embroiling her in his underground plot to deliver M'ret to the Federation. N'Vek died protecting Troi. 

Reference: Face of the Enemy 
Portrayed by Scott MacDonald, whom also played Tosk in "Captive Pursuit", Goran'Agar in "Hippocratic Oath" and Rollins in "Caretaker" and "Dreadnought."
