PERSONNEL: Yranac

Shady Yridian entrepreneur who was patronizing a watering hole on Dessica II at the time Picard was "vaporized" with phaser-fire. Yranac's eyewitness testimony seemed to prove Picard was dead. 

Reference: Gambit
Portrayed by Alan Altshuld.
