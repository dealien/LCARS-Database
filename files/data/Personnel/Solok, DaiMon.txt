PERSONNEL: Solok
DaiMon, trading commander, Torman V

Disreputable Ferengi trader based on Torman V. Beverly Crusher's promise of gratitude induced Solok to sneak her, Picard and Worf onto Celtris III in Cardassian space in 2369. 

Reference: Chain of Command
Portrayed by Lou Wagner.
