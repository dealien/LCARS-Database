PERSONNEL: Holliday, Doc

Gambler, gunfighter, and dentist in the American West. Deputized by Marshall Earp, he fought at the OK Corral against the Clanton family in 1881. The Melkots re-staged the legendary showdown to gauge human intentions. 

Reference: Spectre of the Gun 
Played by Sam Gilman.
