PERSONNEL: Pomet

Male humanoid, among several others who attempted to steal trilithium resin from the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701-D as it was undergoing a baryon sweep at the Remmler Array in 2369. 
Pomet died from in the sweep's deadly rays after being knocked out by Captain Picard. 

Reference: Starship Mine 
Portrayed by Alan Altshuld. The website The Continuum featured images of a different character by mistake.
