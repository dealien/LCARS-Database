PERSONNEL: Dan Dean
Lieutenant, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

Starfleet officer serving aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise-D. Lt. Dean was an accomplished swordsman and he was a regular fencing partner for Captain Picard in the ship's gymnasium. 

Reference: We'll Always Have Paris 
Played by Dan Kern. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's first name. The character was named for episode co-writer Deborah Dean Davis.
