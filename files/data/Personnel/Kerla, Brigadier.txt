PERSONNEL: Kerla
Brigadier, Military advisor, Qo'noS

High-ranking Klingon military consultant to Chancellor Gorkon and Chancellor Azetbur. In 2293, Kerla was among those against peace with the Federation. He attended a state diner aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise-A along with the other members of the Chancellor's senior staff.

Reference: The Undiscovered Country 
Played by Paul Rosilli.
