PERSONNEL: Sirol
Commander, starship commander, R.S.E. Terix

Romulan male commanding the Terix in 2370. Sirol and Picard were both looking for the U.S.S. Pegasus in the Devlin system. Sirol sealed the U.S.S. Enterprise-D inside an asteroid. 

Reference: The Pegasus 
Portrayed by Michael Mack.
