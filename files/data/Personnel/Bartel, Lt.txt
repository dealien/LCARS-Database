PERSONNEL: Stacie Bartel
Lieutenant, engineering officer, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

Engineer serving aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise-D when the ship encountered the Dyson Sphere near Norpin V. 

Reference: Relics 
Played by Stacie Foster. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
