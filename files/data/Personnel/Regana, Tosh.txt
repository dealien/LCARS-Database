PERSONNEL: Regana Tosh

Smuggler who arrived at Quark's bar on DS9 in 2372 attempting to sell Tallonian crystals. Security chief Odo wanted to use Regana Tosh to help infiltrate the Markalian smuggling ring, but Worf's interference quashed his plans. 

Reference: Hippocratic Oath 
Played by Roderick Garr. The Continuum transposed the names of this character by mistake- referring to him as Tosh Regana.
