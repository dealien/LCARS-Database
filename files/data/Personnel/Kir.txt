PERSONNEL: Kir

Kir was leader of the Brenari, a telepathic race of refugees. In 2375, the U.S.S. Voyager encountered Kir and his people when the Brenari used the ship to hide from Devore inspection teams. Kir and his group eventually escaped through a wormhole. 

Reference: Counterpoint
Portrayed by Randy Oglesby whom also played Ah-Kel in "Vortex," Silaren Prin in "The Darkness and the Light," Trena'l in "Unexpected," and Degra in various Xindi episodes of Enterprise.
