PERSONNEL: Talmadge
Captain, transport commander, Dorian

In 2369, on the way to planet Rekog-Seronia, Talmadge's vessel, the Dorian, was engaged by fighters desperate to prevent Talmadge from delivering his passenger, Ambassador Ves Alkar.
