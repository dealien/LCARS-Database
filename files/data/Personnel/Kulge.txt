PERSONNEL: Kulge
Councillor, government leader, Qo'noS

A Klingon warrior who, while in the Klingon High Council chambers, doubted Gowron's ability to hold his chancellorship during the Klingon civil war. Kulge was immediately felled by Gowron. 

Reference: Redemption
Played by Jordan Lund.
