PERSONNEL: Richard Kaplan
Lieutenant, security officer, U.S.S. Enterprise

During a trip to Gamma Trianguli VI in 2267, Kaplan was instantly vaporized by a bolt of lighting from Vaal. 

Reference: The Apple 
Played by Dick Dial. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
