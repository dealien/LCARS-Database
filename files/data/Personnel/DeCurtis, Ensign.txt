PERSONNEL: Todd DeCurtis
Ensign, Deep Space 9

Enthusiastic young Starfleet engineer serving on Deep Space 9. In 2370, he helped to protect sensitive areas of the station from the Paradan replicant of Miles O'Brien . 

Reference: Whispers 
Played by Todd Waring. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
