PERSONNEL: Coutu

Leader of a rebel faction on the Gamma Quadrant planet of Parada II. Coutu discovered that the Paradan government was trying to undermine peace talks with the rebels using an exact double of Miles O'Brien. The real O'Brien was being held captive by the Paradan government, and Coutu was instrumental in his rescue. 

Reference: Whispers 
Played by Philip LeStrange.
