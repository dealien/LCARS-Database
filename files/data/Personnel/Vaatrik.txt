PERSONNEL: Vaatrik

Bajoran national who was suspected of being a Cardassian collaborator on Terok Nor during the occupation of Bajor. He was married to Pallra, and ran the pharmacy on Terek Nor before being killed by Kira Nerys in 2365. 

Reference: Necessary Evil
