PERSONNEL: Mary Janowski

Human child who was part of the ill-fated Starnes Expedition to the planet Triacus in 2268. After her parents committed suicide, she was under the control of the Gorgon, but was ultimately rescued by Captain Kirk and the crew of the U.S.S. Enterprise. 

Reference: And the Children Shall Lead 
Played by Pamelyn Ferdin
