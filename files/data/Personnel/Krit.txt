PERSONNEL: Krit

Pernicious business associate of Morn's. Krit and his brother Nahsk, along with Quark and several other claimants, sought to retrieve an elusive thousand bricks of gold-pressed latinum after Morn 'died' in 2374. 

Reference: Who Mourns for Morn?
