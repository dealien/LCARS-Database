PERSONNEL: Charles Jimenez
Ensign, security officer, Deep Space 9

Odo relieved Jimenez of guard duty outside Major Kira's quarters when she was entertaining First Minister Shakaar in 2372. 

Reference: Crossfire 
Played by Charles Tentindo. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's first name.
