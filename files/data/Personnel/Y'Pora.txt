PERSONNEL: Y'Pora
midwife, Deep Space Nine

Bajoran woman selected by Kira Nerys to guide her through baby Kirayoshi O'Brien's traditional Bajoran delivery in 2373. Y'Pora chastised Prime Minister Shakaar for interrupting Kira's labor. 

Reference: The Begotten 
Played by Peggy Roeder.
