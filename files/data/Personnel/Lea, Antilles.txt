PERSONNEL: Antilles Lea
Commander, science officer, Io Base

A project team leader on the Io Development Base and senior officer of the Chariot-class project. In 2375 he oversaw the training of staff on the classified vehicle that had been modified for use by Starfleet in a massive research project.
Antilles was born on Grazeri during harvest season.
