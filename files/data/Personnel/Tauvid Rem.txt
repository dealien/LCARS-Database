PERSONNEL: Tauvid Rem
Agent, Idanian Intelligence officer, Deep Space Nine

A name implanted in Idanian undercover agent Arissa's trace memory as a person to be trusted. When someone named Tauvid Rem contacted her, Arissa agreed to meet him on Deep Space Nine. He was assigned to restore Arrissa's memories and use her new ones to bring the Syndicate to trial. Before he could revive Arissa's original memories and free her from the Syndicate's influence he was murdered by Orion agents Traidy and Sorm

Reference: A Simple Investigation 
Played by Brant Cotton. The Continuum featured an image of his killers, but not the man himself, apparently by mistake.
