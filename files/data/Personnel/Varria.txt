PERSONNEL: Varria

Assistant to Zibalian trader Kivas Fajo for over 14 years. In 2366, Varria was murdered by Fajo when she assisted the escape of the kidnapped Data. 

Reference: The Most Toys 
Portrayed by Jane Daly.
