PERSONNEL: Thaddius Riker

Ancestor of William T. Riker, Thaddius was known as "Old Iron Boots" during his days in the U.S. Army during the Civil War of the 1860's. It was later revealed to William Riker that a Q entity known as Quinn had once saved the life of his ancestor.
