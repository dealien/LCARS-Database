PERSONNEL: Syvar
Commander, science officer, U.S.S. Garuda

Confident Vulcan dart player and Science Officer on the Garuda. Quark arranged a dart match between Syvar and Miles O'Brien in 2371. Syvar won the match, but only because O'Brien's shoulder gave out.
