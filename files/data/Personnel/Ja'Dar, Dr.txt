PERSONNEL: Ja'Dar
PhD, scientist, Bilana III

Designer of the soliton wave, an experimental faster-than-light means of transportation. His theories were tested aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise-D in 2367, where the test almost destroyed a nearby colony. 

Reference: New Ground 
Played by Richard McGonagle.
