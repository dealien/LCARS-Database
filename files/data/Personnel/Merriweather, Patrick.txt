PERSONNEL: Patrick Merriweather

A persona adopted by Dr. Julian Bashir while undercover in his secret agent program in Quark's holosuite. Merriweather was a leading geologist, astonishing baccarat player and husband to Anastasia. 

Reference: Our Man Bashir 
Played by Alexander Siddig/ Bashir.
