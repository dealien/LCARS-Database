PERSONNEL: Jay Gordon

One of three winners in a science fair aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise-D. Captain Picard made the youngster an honorary science officer during a shipwide crisis in 2368. 

Reference: Disaster 
Played by John Christian Graas.
