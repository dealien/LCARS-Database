PERSONNEL: Viterian
Captain, transport commander, Rak-Minunis

In 2371, his image was programmed into a holo-filter on the U.S.S. Defiant during a stopover en route to Cardassia. 

Reference: Duet, Second Skin 
Played by Lesley Kahn.
