PERSONNEL: Preacher

When Ben Sisko experienced the the life of Benny Russell, Earth, circa 1953, Preacher was portrayed by his father, Joseph. Preacher's advice to Benny was to follow the prophets and have courage. 

Reference: Far Beyond the Stars 
Played by Brock Peters, who also played Jospeh Sisko throught Deep Space 9 and Admiral Cartwright in "The Voyage Home" and "The Undiscovered Country."
