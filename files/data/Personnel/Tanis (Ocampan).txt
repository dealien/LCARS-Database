PERSONNEL: Tanis

Ocampa male whose ancestors elected to live with the Caretaker's mate, Suspiria. Tanis believed his longevity, he was 14, was owed to Suspiria's nurturing. When she returned home to Exosia, Tanis followed. 

Reference: Cold Fire
Portrayed by Gary Graham, whom also played Sikes in the Alien Nation TV series & TV movies. The Continuum website also credited an actress to this role by mistake.
