PERSONNEL: Andrew Powell
Lieutenant, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

Powell began dating Nurse Alyssa Ogawa in 2370, and proposed marriage later that year. The two conceived a child, but he had difficulty adjusting to her pregnancy. Lieutenant Commander Data felt he was ready to advise Powell regarding the child birth process after he spent the past nine weeks dealing with Spot and her pregnancy.
