PERSONNEL: Harold Gentry
Captain, station commander, Atlantis

The former project coordinator turned city administrator of the underwater colony project known as Atlantis in the Atlantic Ocean of Earth. He was a frequent customer of the "Captain's Table" bar, and even had made sure that construction personnel built in a room near his office where he knew the bar create a portal for him at.
