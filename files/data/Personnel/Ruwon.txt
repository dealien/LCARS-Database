PERSONNEL: Ruwon
Tal Shiar

Leader of a Romulan delegation sent to station Deep Space 9 in 2371 to receive Starfleet's latest intelligence on the Dominion. The delegation had secret orders to destroy the Bajoran wormhole in order to prevent a feared Dominion invasion of the Alpha Quadrant, and to destroy Deep Space 9 as well.

Reference: Visionary
Played by Jack Shearer, who also played Admiral Strikler in "Non Sequitor," and Admiral Hayes in "First Contact" the movie.
