PERSONNEL: Jessel

In Q's anti-time future, Jessel was an elderly, cantankerous housekeeper working for Data while he taught at Cambridge University. She chided Data for adding a streak of white to his hair. 

Reference: All Good Things...
Played by Pamela Kosh, who also played Mrs. Carmichael in "Time's Arrow."
