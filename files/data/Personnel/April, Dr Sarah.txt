PERSONNEL: Sarah April, MD
Doctor, chief medical officer, U.S.S. Enterprise

The first medical officer of the starship Enterprise. She served on the vessel while her husband commanded the first five year mission on that vessel. She is credited with the creation of many of the innovative medical tools used by Starfleet even centuries later.

Reference: The Counter-Clock Incident
Portrayed by the voice of Nichelle Nichols.
