PERSONNEL: Parem

Romulan officer rendered undetectable, and able to pass through matter, after an interphase generator accident. He used his condition to enter the U.S.S. Enterprise-D. While there he noticed that Commander Geordi La Forge and Ensign Ro Laren were also phased and attacked them. Parem was last seen floating out into space after being thrown through an exterior bulkhead. 

Reference: The Next Phase 
Portrayed by Brian Cousins.
