PERSONNEL: Mendon
Ensign, science officer, U.S.S. Enterprise-D 

Benzite officer visiting the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701-D in 2365 as part of the officer exchange program. Both overly confident and eager to please, Mendon eventually adapted to Enterprise protocol. 

Reference: A Matter of Honor
