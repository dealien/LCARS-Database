PERSONNEL: Sonya Gomez
Ensign, engineering officer, Starbase 173

Young engineering officer assigned to Enterprise-D at Starbase 173. An attractive young woman, Gomez specialized in antimatter operations. Gomez helped devise a means of using the ship's Bussard collectors to create a harmless pyrotechnic display when Geordi La Forge was being held captive aboard the Mondor in 2365. Gomez was then transferred to that starship during that ship's layover.

Reference: Q Who?, Measure of a Man
Played by Lycia Naff.
