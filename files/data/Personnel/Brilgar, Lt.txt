PERSONNEL: Brilgar
Lieutenant, security officer, Deep Space 9

On Stardate 50416.2, Brilgar was assigned to protect Major Kira after other former members of the Shakaar resistance cell were murdered. 

Reference: The Darkness and the Light 
Played by Christian Conrad.
