PERSONNEL: Helena Rozhenko

Worf's adoptive mother and wife to Sergey Rozhenko. Helena and Sergey lived for a time on the farm world of Gault and later settled back on Earth. The two later accepted custody of young Alexander Rozhenko, Worf's son. 

Reference: Family, New Ground, Reunion
Portrayed by Georgia Brown.
