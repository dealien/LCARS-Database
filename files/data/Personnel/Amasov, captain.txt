PERSONNEL: Amasov
Captain, starship commander, U.S.S. Endeavor
 
Commander of the U.S.S. Endeavour, the only other Starfleet ship to survive the Borg incursion at Wolf 359. In Amasov's log that was read by Janeway when she encountered the Borg border, “It is my opinion that the Borg are as close to pure evil as any race we’ve ever encountered."
