PERSONNEL: Achilles
Gladiator, Eight Ninety Two-IV

A celebrated gladiator on planet 892-IV who battled Commander Spock and Doctor Leonard McCoy in the planet's televised arena in 2267. 

Reference: Bread and Circuses 
Played by Max Kelvin.
