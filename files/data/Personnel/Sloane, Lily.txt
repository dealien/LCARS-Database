PERSONNEL: Lily Sloane

A member of the Zefram Cochrane engineering team that constructed the first warp drive in 2063. Sloane also helped Picard defeat the Borg aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701-E. 

Reference: Star Trek: First Contact 
Portrayed by Alfre Woodard.
