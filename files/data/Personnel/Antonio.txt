PERSONNEL: Antonio Palmas
Guide, Central America, Earth

Twenty-fourth century safari guide on Earth, based in the Central American rain forest. Antonio was once host to Chakotay and his father, Kolopak, who were searching for descendants of the Rubber Tree People. 

Reference: Tattoo
Played by Joseph Palmas. The character's last name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
