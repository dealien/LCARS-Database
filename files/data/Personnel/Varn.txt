PERSONNEL: Varn

Delta Quadrant ship captain who paid to join Dala's Federation, hoping to receive weapons capable of defeating the Polonians in 2376. Varn later realized Dala defrauded him and sought reparation. 

Reference: Live Fast and Prosper
Portrayed by Ted Rooney.
