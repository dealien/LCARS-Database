PERSONNEL: Tavana
engineering officer, I.K.S. Rotarran

Female Klingon engineering officer on the I.K.S. Rotarran in 2373. Tavana's morale was at an all-time low when General Martok assumed command and inspired the crew to a glorious victory. 

Reference: Soldiers of the Empire 
Portrayed by Sandra Nelson.
