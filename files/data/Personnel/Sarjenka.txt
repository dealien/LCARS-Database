PERSONNEL: Sarjenka

Young female from the planet Drema IV. In 2365, she sent out radio signals to see if anyone was listening. Lieutenant Commander Data picked up the signal and began a correspondence, in violation of the Prime Directive. However, Data's intervention eventually led to the saving of Drema IV. Because of their conversation her memory was erased by Doctor Katherne Pulaski.

Reference: Pen Pals 
Portrayed by Nikki Cox.
