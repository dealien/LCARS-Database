PERSONNEL: Neema

Daughter of Audrid Dax. Trying to reassure Sisko, who was worrying about Jake, Jadzia told him about reading the entire Down the River Light to Neema when she was hospitalized at age six-only to have Neema, at twenty-one, not speak to her for years.
