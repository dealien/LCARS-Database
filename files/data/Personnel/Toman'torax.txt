PERSONNEL: Toman'torax
Second

A Jem'Hadar second who served under Omet'iklan for three years. In 2372 Toman'torax was executed by Omet'iklan for starting a fight with Worf after he had been ordered not to. 

Reference: To the Death 
Played by Brian Thompson, who also played the second officer in "A Matter of Honor."
