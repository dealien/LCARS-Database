PERSONNEL: The Gigolo

Holo-character in Sandrine's Bar, a holodeck program created by Tom Paris on the U.S.S. Voyager. Easily identifiable by his strange accent and his relentless pursuit of women, the Gigolo attempted to romance Captain Janeway when she visited the holodeck program in 2371.
