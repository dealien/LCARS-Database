PERSONNEL: Trevean
Healer, Teplan

A humanoid male, native to the Teplan system. Trevean's clinic provided quick-acting poison so those in the final stages of the Teplan Blight could die in pain-free dignity. 

Reference: The Quickening 
Played by Michael Sarrazin.
