PERSONNEL: Melora Pazlar
Ensign, science officer, Deep Space 9

Stellar cartographer and Starfleet's first Elaysian officer. Coming from a low-gravity world made mobility around Starfleet installations difficult. Pazlar was abducted by Fallit Kot, whom she helped defeat. 

Reference: Melora 
Played by Daphne Ashbrook.
