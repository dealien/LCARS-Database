PERSONNEL: Nori

Ilari native whose husband was Tieran, her nation's leader. Nori remained loyal to Tieran, even when his consciousness invaded Kes's body and took another spouse. In 2373, Nori was killed by Tuvok when she fired at Neelix and Chakotay while fleeing with Kes-Tieran. 

Reference: Warlord
Portrayed by Galyn Gorg whom also played Korena Sisko in "The Visitor."
