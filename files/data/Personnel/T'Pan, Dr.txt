PERSONNEL: T'Pan
Ph.D, Director, Vulcan Science Academy

Vulcan subspace morphology expert who was Director of the Vulcan Science Academy for 15 years ending in 2369. She attended Dr. Reyga's metaphasic shield demonstration on the U.S.S. Enterprise-D. 

Reference: Suspicions
Portrayed by Joan Stuart-Morris.
