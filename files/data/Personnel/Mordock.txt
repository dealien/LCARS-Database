PERSONNEL: Mordock

Male from planet Benzar who became his planet's first Starfleet cadet in 2364. Mordock was known to cadet candidate Wesley Crusher for conceiving the dazzling Mordock Strategy. 

Reference: Coming of Age 
Portrayed by John Putch, whom also played Mendon in "A Matter of Honor." This established that all Benzites from the same geostructure look identical, at least to non-Benzites. He also protrayed a journalist aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise-B in "Generations."
