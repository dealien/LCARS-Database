PERSONNEL: Wrightwell
Commander, administrative aide, Starfleet Command

Starfleet officer; Admiral Alynna Necheyev's assistant in 2370. Wrightwell cooperated with Picard's request for details on Necheyev's culinary favorites, like Bularian canapes. 

Reference: Journey's End
