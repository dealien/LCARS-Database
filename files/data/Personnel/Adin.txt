PERSONNEL: Adin
Ilari nation doctor

He was the personal physician to Tieran when the tyrant took over Kes's body after his death in 2373. Adin tried to convince Tieran to relinquish control over Kes, but Tieran disagreed, killing Adin. 

Reference: Warlord
Played by Anthony Crivello.
