PERSONNEL: Toran
Minister, project leader, Jeraddo

Provisional government official in charge of Bajor's first major works project after the occupation. Toran worried over every detail of the plan to transfer power from Jeraddo to Bajor. 

Reference: Progress 
Played by Michael Bofshever.
