PERSONNEL: Valkris

Female Klingon who copied top-secret data on Project Genesis from the Federation and provided it to the Klingons. She read the material before passing it over, and was killed by Klingon Commander Kruge for doing so. 

Reference: The Search for Spock 
Played by Cathie Shirriff.
