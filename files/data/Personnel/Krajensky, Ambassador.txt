PERSONNEL: Krajensky
Ambassador

Starfleet ambassador concerned with Tzenkethi relations. In 2371 he disappeared after heading for an extended vacation on Risa. Dominion forces are suspect because a Changeling appeared in his stead. 

Reference: The Adversary 
Played by Lawrence Pressman.
