PERSONNEL: Ranek
Captain, starship commander

Lokirrim starship captain dedicated to finding and deactivating photonic insurgents. Ranek was unaware that his tactical officer, Jaryn, was romantically interested in him and so he pursued Seven, who secretly harbored the Doctor. 

Reference: Body and Soul
Portrayed by Fritz Sperberg.
