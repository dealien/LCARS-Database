PERSONNEL: Suspira

The Caretaker's mate. Suspiria and the Caretaker both cared for the Ocampa planet until Suspiria started her own array in 2071, populating it with 2,000 Ocampa. After an encounter with U.S.S. Voyager in 2372, Suspiria returned to her subspace home, Exosia, with an Ocampan named Tanis. 

Reference: Cold Fire
Portrayed by Lindsay Ridgeway.
