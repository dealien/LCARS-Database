PERSONNEL: Ki Mendrossen

Human male serving as Ambassador Sarek's chief-of-staff in 2366. At the time of Sarek's visit to the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701-D, Mendrossen knew Sarek's health was failing and tried to conceal it.
