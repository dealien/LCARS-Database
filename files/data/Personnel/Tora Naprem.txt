PERSONNEL: Tora Naprem

Female Bajoran whose daughter, Tora Ziyal, was fathered by Gul Dukat. In 2366 Dukat sent mother and child away in a ship, the Ravinok, to Lissepia where they could live without causing fear to Dukat that their secret love child would be discovered. The ship was attacked and then crashed on Dozaria, killing Naprem.
