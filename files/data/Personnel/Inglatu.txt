PERSONNEL: Inglatu
Dosi negotiatiator who wouldn't sell Quark tulaberry wine, pointing out that the 100,000 vats discussed was more than existed on his entire planet. 

Reference: Rules of Acquisition 
Played by Brian Thompson, who also played Klag in "A Matter of Honor," and a Klingon helm officer in Generations.
