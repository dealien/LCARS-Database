PERSONNEL: Harrison
Technician 1st class, engineering officer, U.S.S. Enterprise

A technician first-class on the U.S.S. Enterprise in 2267. Harrison nearly suffocated on the bridge when Khan Noonien Singh cut the air supply. Captain Kirk's log reflected a commendation for him.
