PERSONNEL: Toscat

An Ocampa man who refused to admit that his people had devolved because of the Caretaker's actions. Toscat urged Kes to be grateful for the lifestyle the Caretaker provided. 

Reference: Caretaker
Portrayed by David Selburg, whom also played Whalen in "The Big Goodbye," and Dr. Syrus in "Frame of Mind." The Continuum spelled this actor's name as Selsburg by mistake.
