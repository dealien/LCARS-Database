PERSONNEL: Sorval
Commander, I.K.S. Drovna

Commander Sorval, son of M'tokra, was the name Worf supplied a curious Klingon guard questioning Worf and Kurn's presence on the I.K.S. Drovna. 

Reference: Sons of Mogh 
Played by Michael Dorn.
