PERSONNEL: Kivas Fajo
trader, Jovis

A Zibalian collector of antiquities and curios. Notably lacking in principles, Fajo constructed a ruse to add Data to his cache, but was ultimately foiled by Enterprise-D personnel, along with his own assistant, and was arrested. 

Reference: The Most Toys 
Played by Saul Rubinek.
