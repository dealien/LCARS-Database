PERSONNEL: Richard Washburn
Engineering officer, U.S.S. Enterprise

Washburn beamed over to the U.S.S. Constellation as a member of Scotty's repair team. 

Reference: The Doomsday Machine 
Played by Richard Compton, who also directed "Haven," and worked with the assistant director by the name of Charles Washburn. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
