PERSONNEL: Rata

Ferengi officer and second to DaiMon Bok. In 2364, Rata accompanied Bok and Kazago on a mission to the U.S.S. Enterprise where they offered sell the logs from the U.S.S. Stargazer. 

Reference: The Battle 
Portrayed by Robert Towers.
