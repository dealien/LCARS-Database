PERSONNEL: Mendez

Starfleet officer who studied Tarchannen III in 2362, becoming infected with an alien life-form. The infection exerted itself in 2367, forcing Mendez back to Tarchannen III where she metamorphosed into an alien. 

Reference: Identity Crisis
