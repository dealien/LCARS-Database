PERSONNEL: Leila Kalomi
Crewman, botanist, Omicron Ceti III

Beautiful colonist infected with happiness spores. She exposed Spock to the spores in 2267 hoping he would love her. Eventually, she lost the spores and Spock's love. 

Reference: This Side of Paradise 
Played by Jill Ireland.
