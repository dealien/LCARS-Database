PERSONNEL: Michael Eddington
Lieutenant commander, security officer, Deep Space 9
Maquis leader, Demilitarized Zone

Starfleet security officer and Maquis leader. Sisko vowed to capture his traitorous former officer, although the two eventually formed a deep mutual respect. A romantic, Eddington died saving his wife and Sisko from Jem'Hadar forces in 2373. He was fond of the book, "Les Miserables," and associated himself as Valjean and Captain Benjamin Sisko as Inspector Javert.

Reference: The Search, For the Uniform, The Die Is Cast, For the Cause
Played by Kenneth Marshall.
