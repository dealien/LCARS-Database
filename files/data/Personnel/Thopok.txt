PERSONNEL: Thopok
Commander, security guard, House of Kozak

Thopok, outraged by Quark's friendship with Grilka, challenged the Ferengi to combat. Quark won, with Worf's help, and Thopok was discharged from Grilka's service. 

Reference: Looking for par'Mach in All the Wrong Places 
Played by Phil Morris, who also played a cadet in "The Search For Spock."
