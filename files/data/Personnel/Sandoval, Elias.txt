PERSONNEL: Elias Sandoval
Planetary leader, Omicron Ceti III

Colony leader of agricultural-based group on the planet Omicron Seti III. In 2267, the U.S.S. Enterprise made contact with Sandoval and discovered that the residents of the colony were shielded from deadly radiation by indigenous spores. 

Reference: This Side of Paradise 
Played by Frank Overton.
