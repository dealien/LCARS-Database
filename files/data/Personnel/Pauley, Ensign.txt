PERSONNEL: Pauley
Ensign, Deep Space 9

Starfleet worker on DS9 in 2369. Returning from the Gamma Quadrant drained his runabout, the U.S.S. Ganges, of power and oxygen. Dr. Bashir escorted Pauley to the infirmary. 

Reference: Q-Less
