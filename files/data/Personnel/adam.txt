PERSONNEL: Adam

Musically minded wanderer who joined Dr. Sevrin's quest for the mythical planet Eden. In 2269, Adam helped to hijack both the Aurora and the Enterprise to find the elusive paradise, and once Sevrin's group thought it had done so, he died after eating an acid-saturated fruit on the planet. 

Reference: The Way to Eden 
Played by Charles Napier who also played General Rex Denning in "Little Green Men."
