PERSONNEL: Koroth
Director, Boreth Monestary

Klingon holy man and Boreth monastery director in 2369. He conspired to influence Klingon society by cloning Kahless the Unforgettable. Koroth used confidences from Worf to authenticate the ersatz icon. 

Reference: Rightful Heir 
Played by Alan Oppenheimer, who also played Captain Keogh in "The Jem'Hadar," and the Nezu Ambassador in "Rise."
