PERSONNEL: Rixx
Captain, starship commander, U.S.S. Thomas Paine

Bolian commander of the U.S.S. Thomas Paine in 2364. Together with fellow Starfleet officers Walker Keel and Tryla Scott, Rixx met with Captain Picard on Dytallix B to discuss a possible infiltration of Starfleet by unknown entities. 

Reference: Conspiracy 
Portrayed by Michael Berryman.
