PERSONNEL: Orr
Officer in Starfleet working on the U.S.S. Okinawa contemporaneous with Benjamin Sisko and Captain Leyton. In 2372, during the Changeling scare, Leyton transferred Orr as part of his takeover scheme. 

Reference: Paradise Lost
