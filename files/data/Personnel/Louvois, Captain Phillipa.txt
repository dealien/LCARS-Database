PERSONNEL: Phillipa Louvois
Captain, judge advocate general, Starbase

Straightforward attorney in Starfleet who, in 2365, made history by ruling that Data had the same rights as any living sentient life-form. Louvois court-martialed Jean-Luc Picard in 2355, but lost. 

Reference: The Measure of a Man
