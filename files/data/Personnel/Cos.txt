PERSONNEL: Cos

Humanoid who died in detention on Deep Space 9 in 2370 after a string of bad luck caused by a corrupting, probability-altering gambling device. His cellmate, Martus Mazur, took the device after Cos died. 

Reference: Rivals 
Played by Albert Henderson.
