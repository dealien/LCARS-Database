PERSONNEL: Willie Potts

Younger brother to Jake Potts, Willie nearly died after a shore leave mishap on Ogus II involving his elder sibling. The two were playing a laser duel game and Jake pretended to be hurt during the game- causing Willie to run and hide in shame within a forest. While he hid he ate a cove palm fruit, infected with deadly parasites. He was taken to sickbay where Dr. Crusher tried to keep him stable until they reached Starbase 416 where the superior medical facilites would be able to save his life.

Reference: Brothers 
Portrayed by Adam Ryen.
