PERSONNEL: God

A non corporeal being that impersonnated the All Mighty in an attempt to be freed from the Great Barrier. He telepathically contacted Sybok who staged elaborate hostage crimes in an effort to acquire a starship to travel through the Great Barrier to what he believed was Sha-ka-Ree. When Spock and Kirk doubted the authenticity of his claims and asked questions he attacked them.

Reference: The Final Frontier
