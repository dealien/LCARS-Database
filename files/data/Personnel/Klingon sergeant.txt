PERSONNEL: Klingon sergeant

One of the officers working under Commander Kruge that was killed when the U.S.S. Enterprise was destroyed by a self destruction sequence after being boarded by the Klingons.

Reference: The Search for Spock
Portrayed by Dave Cadiente.
