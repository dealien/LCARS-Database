PERSONNEL: Nador
Gul, starship commander

In 2370 he was commander of a Cardassian Galor-class warship. Nador's circuit included the Argus Array. In another quantum reality Nador suspected the array was actually spying on Cardassian activities. 

Reference: Parallels 
Portrayed by Mark Bramhall.
