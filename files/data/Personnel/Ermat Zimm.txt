PERSONNEL: Ermat Zimm
artist, Bajor

The latinum-plated Renewal Scroll inscription pens that Quark was selling in 2371 featured a portrait of Deep Space 9 by Ermat Zimm. 

Reference: Fascination 
This character was named after DS9 production designer Herman Zimmerman.
