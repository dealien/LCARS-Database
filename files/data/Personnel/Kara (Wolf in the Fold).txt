PERSONNEL: Kara

Attractive, friendly female dancer on Argelius II . Last seen walking with Montgomery Scott before being savagely killed in 2267. Scott was a suspect, but Kara's murderer was Mr. Hengist, an alien entity that fed on the emotion of fear. He was also known as Redjac on other worlds. 

Reference: Wolf in the Fold 
Played by Tania Lemani.
