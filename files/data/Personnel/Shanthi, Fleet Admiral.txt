PERSONNEL: Shanthi
Fleet Admiral

One of Captain Picard's superior officers. Shanthi approved Picard's plan to expose Romulan involvement in the Klingon civil war. She also investigated Admiral Erik Pressman's illegal cloaking technology research. 

Reference: Redemption
Portrayed by Fran Bennett.
