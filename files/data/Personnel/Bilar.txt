PERSONNEL: Bilar

Citizen of the society on planet Beta III who was under the control on the machine entity Landru. When an landing party from the U.S.S. Enterprise arrived on the planet in 2267, Bilar asked if they had found accommodations to sleep after the Red Hour.

Reference: Return of the Archons
Played by Ralph Maurer.
