PERSONNEL: Marla Aster
Lieutenant, archaeologist, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

This officer was killed during an away mission in the Koinonian ruins in 2366, orphaning her son Jeremy. Aster was killed by a bomb left over from an ancient Koinonian war. Koinonian energy-based life-forms later created a replica of Aster in an offer to care for her son.

Reference: The Bonding
Played by Susan Powell.
