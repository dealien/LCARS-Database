PERSONNEL: Mea 3

A government official on Eminiar VII. Mea 3 greeted the Enterprise landing party in 2267. She was declared a casualty of war during an attack by planet Vendikar. Mea 3 was expected to report to a disintegration chamber under the terms of her planet's agreement with Vendikar, but was prevented from doing so by Enterprise personnel.

Reference: A Taste of Armageddon
Played by Barbara Babcock, who also played Philana in "Plato's Stepchildren, and provided the voice of Trelane's mother in "The Squire of Gothos," the voice of Isis in "Assignment: Earth," and the voice of Commander Loskene in "The Tholian Web."
