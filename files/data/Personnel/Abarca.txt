PERSONNEL: Abarca
 
Qomarian male. Abarca, together with Tincoo, disdainfully agreed to be examined by Voyager's EMH following his ship's rescue. However, he later became enraptured by the Doctor's singing. 

Reference: Virtuoso
Played by Ray Xifo.
