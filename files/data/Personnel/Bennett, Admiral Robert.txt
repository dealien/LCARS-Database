PERSONNEL: Robert Bennett
Admiral, chief of staff, Starfleet Headquarters

Starfleet chief-of-staff who ordered the U.S.S. Enterprise-A to Nimbus III to rescue the Federation, Romulan and Klingon consuls taken hostage there in 2287. Even though the new Enterprise was not yet up to spec, Bennett needed the experience of Jim Kirk to handle the situation. Kirk was on a first-name basis with the admiral, calling him "Bob." 

Reference: The Final Frontier 
Played by Harve Bennett who was the movie's writer-producer and making a cameo appearance. The character's last name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
