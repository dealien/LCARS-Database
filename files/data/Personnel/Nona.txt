PERSONNEL: Nona
Kahn-ut-tu, hill people, Tyree's Planet

Bewitching, ambitious Kahn-ut-tu woman and wife of Tyree, of the hill people. Nona saved Kirk from the mugato's poison in 2268. She betrayed Tyree and was killed by his enemy, Apella. 

Reference: A Private Little War 
Played by Nancy Kovack-Mehta.
