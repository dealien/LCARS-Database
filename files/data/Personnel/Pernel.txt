PERSONNEL: Pernel
Centurion, batalion commander, Tal Shiar

A senior Romulan officer, traveling on a Talon-class scout around the Federation/Cardassian border, who made several stops at Deep Space Nine and Starbase 375 for fleet planning sessions.
