PERSONNEL: Dierdre Watley
Lieutenant, U.S.S. Enterprise

Woman who joined the U.S.S. Enterprise's crew roster in 2267 after an assignment on the U.S.S. Lexington NCC-1709. Watley may have been Julian Bashir's great-grandmother. 

Reference: Trials and Tribble-ations 
Dierdre L Imershein, who also played Joval in "Captain's Holiday." The character's first name is based on the actor's real name.
