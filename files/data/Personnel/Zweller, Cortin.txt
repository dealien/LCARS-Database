PERSONNEL: Cortwin Zweller
Ensign, Starbase Earhart

Nicknamed "Corey," Zweller was a contemporary of Jean-Luc Picard at Starfleet Academy who graduated in 2327 and was involved in the Nausicaan altercation at Starbase Earhart that resulted in Picard having to get an artificial heart. Zweller's first posting was the U.S.S. Ajax.
