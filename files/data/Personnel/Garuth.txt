PERSONNEL: Garuth

A figure in ancient Kriosian and Valtese history. Garuth was an empathic metamorph, who was loved by two brothers, Krios and Valt. Garuth was kidnapped by Krios and taken away to a neighboring system, triggering a war that lasted for centuries.
