PERSONNEL: Devor

Member of a gang of interstellar outlaws who attempted to steal trilithium resin from the warp core of the Enterprise-D during a baryon sweep in 2369. Devor intercepted Captain Picard as he was returning to the ship to fetch his saddle. Picard overpowered Devor and left him in the ship's sickbay. Devor was later killed by the baryon sweep. 

Reference: Starship Mine 
Played by Tim Russ who also played Tuvok on Voyager.
