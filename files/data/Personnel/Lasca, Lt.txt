PERSONNEL: Mark Lasca
Lieutenant, designer, Starfleet Engineering Corps

In an alternate reality, Lasca was a promising designer for the Starfleet Engineering Corps. He and Harry Kim created the U.S.S. Yellowstone, an experimental runabout. 

Reference: Non Sequitor
Portrayed by Mark Kiely. The character's first name is conjectural, based on the actor's real name.
