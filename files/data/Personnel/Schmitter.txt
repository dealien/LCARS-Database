PERSONNEL: Biff Schmitter
Miner, Janus VI

Pergium miner stationed on Janus VI. In 2267 Schmitter was killed by a native rock-eating creature called the Horta. 

Reference: The Devil in the Dark 
Played by Biff Elliott. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
