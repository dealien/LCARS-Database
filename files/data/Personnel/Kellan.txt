PERSONNEL: Kellan
First Minister, planetary leader, Rakosa V

Leader of a Class-M planet in the Delta Quadrant threatened by the Dreadnought missile. First Minister Kellan sent small Rakosan fighters to disable Dreadnought, but to no avail.
