PERSONNEL: Parmen
Planetary leader, Platonius

Long-lived male humanoid. By 2268 Parmen dominated a small colony of philosophers emulating the ancient Greeks. Parmen used telekinetic powers to amuse himself and torture others, including James Kirk. 

Reference: Plato's Stepchildren 
Played by Liam Sullivan.
