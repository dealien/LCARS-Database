PERSONNEL: Duke Gillespie 
Chief, engineering officer, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

Friend of Miles O'Brien while the two served aboard the Enterprise-D. Gillespie nearly started a riot in Ten-Forward while in the grip of paranoia caused by a lack of REM sleep, when the ship was trapped in a Tyken's Rift in 2367. 

Reference: Night Terrors 
Played by Duke Moosekian. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
