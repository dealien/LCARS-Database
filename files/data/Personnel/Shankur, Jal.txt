PERSONNEL: Sankur
Jal, Sect Leader, Kazon

Revered Kazon leader who inspired the Kazon factions to cooperate and defeat their Trabe slavemasters in 2346. Culluh hoped his capture of U.S.S. Voyager NCC-74656 would give him Shankur-level status.
