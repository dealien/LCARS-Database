PERSONNEL: Registrar

Ferengi equivalent of St. Peter, the Registrar is the one who presides over the entrance to the Divine Treasury. The Registrar must be bribed before entrance to the Ferengi afterlife is granted. 
This is also the title given to the head records officer of an organization. Colleges/ Universities and Conventions use this title often.
