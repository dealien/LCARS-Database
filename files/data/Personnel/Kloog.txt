PERSONNEL: Kloog

Humanoid male among the drill thralls kidnapped to Triskelion. Kirk battled him in 2268. Though Kloog had size, strength, and experience, Kirk won with agility and by concentrating Kloog's weaker side. 

Reference: The Gamesters of Triskelion 
Played by Mickey Morton.
