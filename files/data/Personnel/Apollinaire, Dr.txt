PERSONNEL: James Apollinaire
Doctor, San Francisco, Earth

Doctor at the Sisters of Hope Infirmary in 19th-century San Francisco on Earth, who was notorious for being tactless around female nurses. 

Reference: Time's Arrow 
Played by James Gleason. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
