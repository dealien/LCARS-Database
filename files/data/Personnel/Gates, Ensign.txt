PERSONNEL: Joyce Gates
Ensign, bridge officer, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

Enterprise-D crewmember in 2370, generally stationed on the bridge. Gates was on duty when the ship was en route to Starbase 219, and operated conn during the search for the U.S.S. Pegasus in the Devolin system. She was relieved at the conn by Lt. Ro Laren when the Bajoran visited the ship on Stardate 47941. 

Reference: Descent, Preemptive Strike, The Pegasus, Phantasms
Played by Joyce Robinson. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
