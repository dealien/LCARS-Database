PERSONNEL: Kova Tholl

Humanoid from Mizar II. Tholl, a self-described public servant, was abducted from private meditation and awoke as a captive by unknown forces. Eventually he was joined by Picard and others. 

Reference: Allegiance 
Portrayed by Stephen Markie.
