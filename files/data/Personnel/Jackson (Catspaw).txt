PERSONNEL: Jimmy Jackson
Crewman, security officer, U.S.S. Enterprise

He beamed down to Pyris VII in 2267. He was killed by Sylvia's black magic and sent back to the ship as a warning. 

Reference: Catspaw 
Played by Jimmy Jones, who also performed stunts for many other episodes of the original series.
