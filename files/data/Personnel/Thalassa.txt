PERSONNEL: Thalassa

Female from Sargon's planet whose consciousness survived 500,000 years in a receptacle. To prevent harming others with her god-like powers, Thalassa joined her husband, Sargon, in the great void. 

Reference: Return to Tomorrow 
Played by Diana Muldaur, who also played Dr. Ann Mulhall in the same episode, Dr. Miranda Jones in "Is There In Truth No Beauty," and Dr. Katherine Pulaski in the 2nd season of TNG.
