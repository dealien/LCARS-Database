PERSONNEL: Nuria

Leader of a pre-industrialized village of proto-Vulcans on Mintaka III in 2366. Nuria's logical thinking was shaken when an accident exposed her to Federation technology. Picard set her back on course. 

Reference: Who Watches The Watchers? 
Portrayed by Kathryn Leigh Scott.
