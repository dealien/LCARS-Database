PERSONNEL: Wayne Keffer
Lieutenant, security officer, U.S.S. Stephaleh

A fighter pilot assigned to escort and protect the scientific research team performing experimental tests to create a stable wormhole. A suspicious man by nature, he was very xenophobic to non-Federation races.
