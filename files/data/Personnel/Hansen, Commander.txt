PERSONNEL: Garry Hansen
Commander, Earth outpost 4

Starfleet officer stationed on Earth Outpost 4, an asteroid in Sector Z-6, monitoring the Romulan Neutral Zone in 2266. Hansen detailed Romulan weaponry capabilities to Captain Kirk before being killed by them. 

Reference: Balance of Terror 
Played by Garry Walberg. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
