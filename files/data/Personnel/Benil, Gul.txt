PERSONNEL: Benil
Gul, starship commander

In 2371, Benil's Galor-class warship intercepted the Federation starship Defiant on its way to Cardassia to rescue Major Kira from the Obsidian Order. 

Reference: Second Skin 
Played by Christopher Carroll.
