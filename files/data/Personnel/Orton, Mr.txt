PERSONNEL: Orton, Mr. 
Arkaria Base station administrator. Orton's family name was once Pel Orton, considered a more noble designation. In 2369 Orton helped mercenaries steal trilithium resin from the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701-D. 
Reference: Starship Mine
