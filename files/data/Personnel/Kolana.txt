PERSONNEL: Kolana
starship commander

Klingon commander of a bird-of-prey that was part of Martok's 'cavalry' outfit that attacked behind Cardassian lines in 2375. Martok, Worf and Kor were aboard her ship. 

Reference: Once More Unto the Breach
