PERSONNEL: Taar
DaiMon, starship commander

Ferengi captain whose theft in 2364 of a T-9 energy converter from a Federation outpost triggered first contact between Ferengi and Federation representatives on Delphi Ardu. 

Reference: The Last Outpost 
Portrayed by Mike Gomez.
