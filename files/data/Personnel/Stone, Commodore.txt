PERSONNEL: Percy Stone
Commodore, station commander, Starbase 11

Starfleet captain who was later promoted and put in charge of Starbase 11. In 2267 Stone convened a court-martial of James Kirk. Soon after, Stocker left Starbase 11. 

Reference: Court Martial 
Played by Percy Rodriguez. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
