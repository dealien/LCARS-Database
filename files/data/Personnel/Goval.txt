PERSONNEL: Goval

A self-aware Borg who disconnected himself from the Collective so he could keep his thoughts to himself, but then reconnected himself at the urging of Lore. 

Reference: Descent
Played by Michael Reilly Burke who also played Hogue in "Profit and Loss" and Koss in Enterprise.
