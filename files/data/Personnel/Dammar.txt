PERSONNEL: Dammar

An operative from the Nyria III colony, in the Delta Quadrant. During the Nyrian attempt to take over Voyager on Stardate 50912.4 (2373), Dammar was the first of his people to appear on the ship. Once aboard Voyager, Dammar exchanged himself with Kes by using a "translocator" and pretending to think he was abducted. 

Reference: Displaced
Portrayed by Kenneth Tigar.
