PERSONNEL: Sandra Rhodes
Lieutenant, security officer, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

Security officer aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise. In 2370, Rhodes was assigned by Worf to help prevent DaiMon Bok from killing Jason Vigo, a young man who was briefly thought to be Picard's son. 

Reference: Bloodlines 
Portrayed by Amy Pietz.
