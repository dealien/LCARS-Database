PERSONNEL: Nahsk

Nefarious business associate of Morn, along with his considerably smarter brother Krit. Nahsk provided 'muscle' when the brothers tried to collect their share of the Lissepian Mother's Day Heist from Quark, who inherited it from Morn. They brothers had to try to get the money before their other partners, Larell and , did.

Reference: Who Mourns for Morn?
