PERSONNEL: Cliff Kennelly
Admiral, Starfleet Headquarters

Secret collaborator with the Cardassians, in 2368, to kill Bajorans he believed attacked a Federation colony. Ro Laren was, temporarily, his co-conspirator. The plan was uncovered and Kennelly jailed. 

Reference: Ensign Ro 
Played by Cliff Potts. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
