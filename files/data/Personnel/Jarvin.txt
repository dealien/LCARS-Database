PERSONNEL: Jarvin
Maquis, engineering officer, U.S.S. Voyager

Former Maquis member deeply uneasy serving aboard a Starfleet ship. Together with Seska, he offered to support Chakotay if he decided to lead a mutiny against Captain Janeway, a suggestion Chakotay firmly rejected. Later, Jarvin was involved with a woman from Quantum Mechanics. 

Reference: Parallax
Portrayed by Justin Williams.
