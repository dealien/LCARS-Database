PERSONNEL: Silaran Prin
domestic, Cardassia Prime

Cardassian who was injured during an assault by a Shakaar resistance cell on Gul Pirak's residence. Prin later carried out a series of revenge attacks against the Bajoran attackers. 

Reference: The Darkness and the Light 
Played by Randy Oglesby whom also played Ah-Kel in "Vortex," Silaran Prin in "The Darkness and the Light," Degra in various Xindi episodes of Enterprise, Trena'l in "Unexpected," and Kir in "Counterpoint."
