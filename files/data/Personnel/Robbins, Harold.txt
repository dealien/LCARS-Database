PERSONNEL: Harold Robbins

North American male novelist from Earth, famous for writing "The Carpetbaggers." James Kirk peppered his own vocabulary with colorful metaphors and profanity based on Harold Robbins's documentation of 20th-century communications styles. 

TIMELINE:
1916: Born
1997: Died
