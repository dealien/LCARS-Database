PERSONNEL: Torak
Governor, station administrator

Elder Klingon administrator posted close to Starfleet's Relay Station 47. Torak reluctantly helped Picard investigate Lieutenant Aquiel Uhnari's murder. Torak complained loudly after locating Uhnari, alive, in Klingon space. 

Reference: Aquiel 
Portrayed by Wayne Grace.
