PERSONNEL: Mary Warren
Ph.D, science officer, Mintaka III

One of the anthropologists studying a humanoid culture on Mintaka III. Warren was injured by an exploding generator and died in 2366, despite best medical efforts, aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise-D. 

Reference: Who Watches The Watchers? 
Portrayed by Lois Hall.
