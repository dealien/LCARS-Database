PERSONNEL: Syrus
Ph.D, psychiatrist, Tilonus IV

The psychiatrist character in a fiendish, brain-washing scenario prepared by interrogators on Tilonus IV trying to harvest tactical data from William Riker. Syrus worked at the Tilonus Institute for Mental Disorders. 

Reference: Frame of Mind 
Portrayed by David Selburg, whom also played Whalen in "The Big Goodbye."
