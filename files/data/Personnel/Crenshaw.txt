PERSONNEL: Crenshaw

A Maquis fighter. During Michael Eddington's final moments in 2373 on Athos IV, Eddington shouted orders to Crenshaw, Yuen, Dimitris, and Stiles, though they were long gone, to confuse the Jem'Hadar.
