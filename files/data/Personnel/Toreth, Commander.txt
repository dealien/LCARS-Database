PERSONNEL: Toreth
Commander, starship commander, R.S.E. Khazara

Female captain of the Imperial Romulan warbird Khazara. Toreth was a political moderate and hated the Tal Shiar. Toreth's military credentials included a Sotrarek Citation. 

Reference: Face of the Enemy 
Portrayed by Carolyn Seymour whom also played Mrs Templeton in the VOY holodeck programs.
