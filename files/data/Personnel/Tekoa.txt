PERSONNEL: Tekoa
Crewman, engineering technician, Deep Space 9

Female Bajoran engineer assigned to the swing shift on DS9 with Rom. In 2373, Tekoa drank raktajino while waiting for Chief O'Brien's duty assignment for the day. 

Reference: The Assignment 
Played by Rosie Malek-Yonan.
