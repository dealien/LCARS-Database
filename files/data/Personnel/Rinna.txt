PERSONNEL: Rinna

Tareisan female. Rinna was one of several women on her planet who, on the pretense of trying to become the wife of Voyager crew member Harry Kim actually wanted to harvest his genetic material. 

Reference: Favorite Son
Portrayed by Kelli Kirkland.
