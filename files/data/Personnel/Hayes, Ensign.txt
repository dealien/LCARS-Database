PERSONNEL: Michael Hayes
Ensign, engineering officer, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

Crewmember aboard the Enterprise-D who served on the engineering staff in 2370 and the following year served as a tactical officer on the bridge. In an alternate quantum reality experienced by Worf in 2370, Hayes took charge of engineering when Commander La Forge was injured. 

Reference: Parallels 
Played by Michael Mack, who also played the Romulan commander Sirol in "The Pegasus."
