PERSONNEL: Lemec
Gul, starship commander, Reklar

Cardassian who, with Glinns Tajor and Corak, met with Captain Jellico on the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701-D in 2369. Lemec's ship, the Reklar, was forced into retreat by Jellico. 

Reference: Chain of Command
Played by John Durbin.
