PERSONNEL: Talpet
Deputy, security officer, Deep Space 9

Member of Odo's security team on Deep Space 9. 
In 2375, Talpet called Dr. Bashir for guidance after preventing Garak's suicide leap from Airlock 7 during a panic attack.
