PERSONNEL: Biff Argyle
Lieutenant Commander, chief engineering officer, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

Argyle worked with the Traveler and Mr. Kosinski during warp drive upgrade tests. He also supervised the assembly and activation of Data's brother, Lore, in the Enterprise's sickbay.

Reference: Where No One Has Gone Before, Datalore
Played by Biff Yaeger. The character's name is conjectural based on the real name of the actor.
