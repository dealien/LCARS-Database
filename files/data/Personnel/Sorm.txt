PERSONNEL: Sorm
Agent, Orion Syndicate

One of Draim's hired thugs. Sorm and partner Traidy murdered the Idanian Tauvid Rem in his quarters on DS9 and almost killed Arissa after securing her data crystal for the Orion Syndicate. 

Reference: A Simple Investigation 
Played by Nicholas Worth.
