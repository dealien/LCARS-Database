PERSONNEL: Haskins, Theodore
PhD, scientist, S.S. Columbia

Scientist with the American Continent Institute who died in 2236 when his craft, the S.S. Columbia, crashed on Talos IV. An illusion of Dr. Haskins was created by the Talosians in 2254 to make Captain Pike believe there had been survivors. 

Reference: The Cage, The Menagerie
Played by Jon Lorner. The character was apparently named for famed science-fiction film director and optical effects creator Byron Haskins, who was the associate producer of "The Cage."
