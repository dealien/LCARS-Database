PERSONNEL: Salia

Teenage female allasomorph who was raised to lead planet Daled IV. Educated on Klavdia III, she left in 2365 and boarded the U.S.S. Enterprise-D to journey home. Much of her journey was spent in the company of Wesley Crusher. 

Reference: The Dauphin 
Portrayed by Jaime Hubbard.
