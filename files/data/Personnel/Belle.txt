PERSONNEL: Belle

Holographic daughter character from the Doctor's family holoprogram Beta-Rho. In his family based holoprogram, the Doctor experienced the trials and tribulations of the nuclear family. Belle died following an injury obtained during a parrises squares match. 

Reference: Real Life
Portrayed by Lindsay Haun.
