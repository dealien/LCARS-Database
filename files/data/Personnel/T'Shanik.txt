PERSONNEL: T`Shanik

Young Vulcan woman seeking entrance into Starfleet Academy in 2364. Several candidates, including Wesley Crusher, came to Relva VII for testing. T'Shanik did not win the one open slot. 

Reference: Coming of Age 
Portrayed by Tasia Valenza.
