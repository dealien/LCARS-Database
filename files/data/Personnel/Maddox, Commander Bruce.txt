PERSONNEL: Bruce Maddox
Commander, chief of robotics, Daystrom Institute 

Starfleet officer and Daystrom Institute of Technology's Chair of Robotics. His desire to dismantle Data prompted the 2365 adjudication of Data as a sentient life-form. Maddox exchanged correspondence with Data afterwards. 

Reference: The Measure of a Man
