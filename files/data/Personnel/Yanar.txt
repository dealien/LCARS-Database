PERSONNEL: Yanar

Native of the planet Altec and young daughter of its ruler, Debin. Yanar's pregnancy by Benzen of Straleb, her secret lover and betrothed, nearly toppled the Coalition of Madena. 

Reference: The Outrageous Okona 
Played by Rosalind Ingledew.
