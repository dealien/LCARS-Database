PERSONNEL: Roberta Lincoln
Executive assistant to Gary Seven, Earth

Resident of Earth's New York City in 1968. Lincoln was hired by associates of Gary Seven under the pretext of developing a new encyclopedia. Though ditzy, Lincoln helped save Earth. She learned of the extra-terrestrial nature of her employers and continued to work for them. The office was located at 8111 East 68th Street, Suite 12B, New York, New York, Earth.

Reference: Assignment: Earth
Played by Teri Garr.
