PERSONNEL: Emi

Alluring humanoid female who skillfully performed oomox on Quark's ears while negotiating for self-sealing stem bolts needed by her family's production plant. The transaction went unconsummated due to Grand Nagus Zek's interruption. 

Reference: Prophet Motive 
Played by Juliana Donald.
