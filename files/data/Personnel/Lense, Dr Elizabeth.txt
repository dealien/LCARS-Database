PERSONNEL: Elizabeth Lense, M.D.
Lieutenant, chief medical officer, U.S.S. Lexington

Former class-mate of Julian Bashir's. She was valedictorian. Bashir and Lense didn't meet until 2371. 

Reference: Explorers
