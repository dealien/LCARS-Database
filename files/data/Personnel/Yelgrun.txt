PERSONNEL: Yelgrun

Vorta representative who expected to conduct a hostage exchange at Empok Nor. Yelgrun did not realize, until too late, that the Vorta he expected to collect was no longer alive. 

Reference: The Magnificent Ferengi 
Portrayed by Iggy Pop.
