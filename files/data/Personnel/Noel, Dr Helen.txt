PERSONNEL: Helen Noel
Doctor, psychiatrist, U.S.S. Enterprise

Noel was a psychiatrist, with a background in rehabilitative therapy, and was Kirk's technical expert while examining facilities at Tantalus V. 

Reference: Dagger of the Mind 
Played by Marianne Hill.
