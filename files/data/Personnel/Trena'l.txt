PERSONNEL: Trena'l

Xyrillian commander of the ship encountered by Enterprise NX-01 in 2151. Trena'l later provided a Klingon ship with Xyrillian holographic stimulators in order to spare the lives of his crew. 

Reference: Unexpected
Portrayed by Randy Oglesby whom also played Ah-Kel in "Vortex," Silaren Prin in "The Darkness and the Light," Kir in "Counterpoint" and Degra in various Xindi episodes of Enterprise..
