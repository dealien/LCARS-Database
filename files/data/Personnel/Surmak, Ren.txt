PERSONNEL: Ren Surmak
scientist, Bajor

Bajoran resistance fighter. As geneticist Dekon Elig's assistant on DS9, he helped create the aphasia virus booby trap. In 2369 Surmak was chief administrator of the Ilvian Medical Complex. 

Reference: Babel 
Played by Matthew Faison. This character was NOT named after the Nickelodeon animated TV series Ren & Stimpy.
