PERSONNEL: Kassa Kurati
Diplomatic attache, Klingon Embassy, Earth

Assistant to the Klingon office in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, Earth after the retirement of the former Ambassador, Ursula Manetti, and the decision not to maintain a full ambassador at that office. Kurati was assigned to assist Klingon officials in finding necessary resources and information within the Federation offices on Earth as well as cultural example and guidance.
Kurati was the father of Ursula LaMoore, co-chief medical officer of the U.S.S. Sutherland, and Joli Kassa; and married to Twebesta "Toby" Shifaraw. He and his family resided in Addis Ababa and observed traditional Umharac customs
