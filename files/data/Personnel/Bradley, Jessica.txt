PERSONNEL: Jessica Bradley

Holodeck character in Captain Picard's Dixon Hill program. A wealthy, beautiful socialite, the Jessica Bradley character was murdered as part of "The Big Goodbye" mystery. Picard, as Dixon Hill, suspected the work of Cyrus Redblock. 

Reference: The Big Goodbye 
Played by Carolyn Allport.
