PERSONNEL: Par Lenor

Ferengi Trade Mission representative who faked a shuttle accident to be brought aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701-D to negotiate with visiting Ambassador Briam. Lenor prematurely released an empathic metamorph and injured Briam. 

Reference: The Perfect Mate 
Played by Max Gredenchik, who also played Rom throughout Deep Space 9.
