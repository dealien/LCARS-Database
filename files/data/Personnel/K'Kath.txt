PERSONNEL: K'Kath

A holographic teenage Klingon boy whos friend was trying to date the daughter of the EMH on the U.S.S. Voyager. The Doctor created the holographic family to better understand the dynaimcs of family life and to give himself a better bedside manner. Lieutenant B'Elanna Torres found the program to be too predictable and conservative so she spiced it up to make it more realistic. The boys that came a knockin' were not the type of boys he would have expected his daughter to associate with. K'Kath arrived with his friend Larg.
