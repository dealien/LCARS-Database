PERSONNEL: Marta

Delusional, seductive green Orion Lady Bluebeard imprisoned in Elba II's penal institution, where she met Garth. Marta was Garth's queen until he blew her to bits demonstrating a new explosive. 

Reference: Whom Gods Destroy 
Played by Yvonne Craig.
