PERSONNEL: Tumak

Young boy who arrived at Deep Space 9 with his mother, Haneek, and Skrreean refugees from the Gamma Quadrant. Tumak died in a Skreean craft when leaking radiation was ignited by phaser fire. 

Reference: Sanctuary 
Played by Andrew Koenig. It is still unknown if this is the son of the actor for Chekov.
