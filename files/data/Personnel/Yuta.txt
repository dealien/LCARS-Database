PERSONNEL: Yuta

Attractive Acamarian woman from the Tralesta clan who was Sovereign Marouk's chef in 2286. Yuta's century of murdering rival clansmen was halted only because she forced Riker to kill her. 

Reference: The Vengeance Factor 
Portrayed by Lisa Wilcox.
