PERSONNEL: John Doe

Name given to a Zalkonian refugee treated aboard the Enterprise-D. Doe's breed was transmutating into energy-based beings while the Zalkonian junta attempted to annihilate them. With Dr Crusher's help, Doe was successful. 

Reference: Transfigurations 
Played by Mark LaMura.
