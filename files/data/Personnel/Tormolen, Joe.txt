PERSONNEL: Joe Tormolen
Lieutenant, U.S.S. Enterprise

Crew member aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise. Tormolen accidentally brought the Psi 2000 virus back to the ship. Sulu and Kevin Riley tried to prevent Tormolen's virus-induced suicide and became infected themselves. 

Reference: The Naked Time
Played by Stewart Moss, who also played Hanar in "By Any Other Name."
