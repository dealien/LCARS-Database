PERSONNEL: Linnis

The half-Ocampan daughter of Kes, and mother of her grandson Andrew, in a possible future. Linnis was opposed to a radical experiment to retard Morilogium and unnaturally extend Kes's lifespan. 

Reference: Before and After
Portrayed by Jessica Collins.
