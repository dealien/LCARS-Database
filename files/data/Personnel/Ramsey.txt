PERSONNEL: Ramsey

Crew member aboard the U.S.S. Defiant. In 2372, Ramsey was killed during a battle with the U.S.S. Lakota, which had been dispatched by Admiral Leyton to stop the Defiant. 

Reference: Paradise Lost 
This actor was credited as Sam Hennings on The Continuum by mistake. That actor played a different Ramsey character.
