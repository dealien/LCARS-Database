PERSONNEL: Divok

Young Klingon, disciple of Kahless the Unforgettable. Divok was at a monastery on Boreth during Worf's visit there in 2369. Divok received a vision of Kahless, and was present when a clone of Kahless appeared. 

Reference: Rightful Heir 
Played by Charles Esten.
