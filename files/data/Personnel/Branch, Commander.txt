PERSONNEL: David Branch
Commander, station commander, Epsilon IX

Commander of the Epsilon IX monitoring station that was consumed by the V'Ger machine-cloud. Branch's final transmission revealed that V'Ger was interpreting normal scans as a hostile act. 

Reference: The Motion Picture 
Played by David Gautreaux. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
