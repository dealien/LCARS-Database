PERSONNEL: Jean Hajar
Cadet 2nd class, navigator, Starfleet Academy Nova Squadron

Hajar was team navigator for Nova Squadron when it attempted the Kolvoord Starburst maneuver, with fatal consequences in 2368. She followed the instructions of her squad commander, Nicholas Locarno, and was responsible for the maneuvers of fellow cadets Jean Hajar, Wesley Crusher, Jixa Sato, and the deceased Joshua Albert.

Reference: The First Duty
