PERSONNEL: Kaval

In 2369, this provisional government official from Bajor tried to make Commander Benjamin Sisko understand the importance of releasing Cardassian Aamin Marritza to Bajor to answer for his war crimes. 

Reference: Duet 
Played by Ted Sorel.
