PERSONNEL: Karyn Berlin
Security officer, Briori

Colonist on the former Briori homeworld in the Delta Quadrant. Karyn was a defense soldier who fought with John Evansville against the Voyager crew when they thought the Starfleet visitors were working for the Briori.
