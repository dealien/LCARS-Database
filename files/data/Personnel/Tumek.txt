PERSONNEL: Tumek

Elder Klingon advisor to the Lady Grilka of the House of Kozak. Tumak officiated Grilka and Quark's brek'tal ritual. Tumek's wisdom quickly deflated Worf's hopes of courting Grilka. 

Reference: The House of Quark 
Played by Joseph Ruskin, who also played Galt in "The Gamesters of Triskelion," and Gul Rusol in "Improbable Cause."
