PERSONNEL: Martus Mazur
merchant, Deep Space Nine

El-Aurian con artist who opened a gaming establishment, Club Martus, to compete with Quark's on Deep Space 9 in 2370, utilizing a unique gambling device that actually altered mathematical probability. 

Reference: Rivals 
Played by Chris Sarandon.
