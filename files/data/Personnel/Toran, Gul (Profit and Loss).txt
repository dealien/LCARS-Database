PERSONNEL: Toran
Gul, military leader, Cardassia Prime

Cardassian officer chasing radical professor Natima Lang and students Rekelen and Hogue in 2370. Toran arrived on Deep Space 9 and discovered, seconds before being killed, that Garak was a Lang supporter. 

Reference: Profit and Loss 
Played by Edward Wiley.
