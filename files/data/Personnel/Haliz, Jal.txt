PERSONNEL: Jal Haliz

A Kazon-Ogla. Born "Hali," he earned his full warrior name after killing a warrior from the Kazon-Relora faction with his bare hands. A scrap of his dead opponent's tunic decorated the cell where he held Chakotay prisoner in 2372.
