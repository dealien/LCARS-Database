PERSONNEL: Trajok

Jadzia Dax's personal Galeo-Manada wrestling coach. Trajok and Jadzia had early morning sessions. Trajok skills were in demand; an appointment on Calondia IV kept him away from Deep Space 9 for a week. 

Reference: Playing God 
Played by Chris Nelson Norris.
