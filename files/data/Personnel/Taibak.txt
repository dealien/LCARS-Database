PERSONNEL: Taibak

Romulan officer and scientist who brainwashed Geordi La Forge, via his VISOR's interface, to assassinate a Klingon in a 2367 plot to wreck the Klingon-Federation alliance. 

Reference: The Mind's Eye 
Portrayed by John Fleck whom also played Abaddon in "Alice" and Ornithar in "The Search."
