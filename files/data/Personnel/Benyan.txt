PERSONNEL: Benyan
religious member, Empok Nor

Bajoran man who, with wife Mika, joined Dukat's Pah-Wraith cult. Benyan painted Dukat's portrait, but turned on him after realizing the Cardassian had fathered Mika's child and then tried to murder her. 

Reference: Covenant 
Portrayed by Jason Leland Adams.
