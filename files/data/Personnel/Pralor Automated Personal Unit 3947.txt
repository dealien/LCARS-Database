PERSONNEL: Pralor Automated Personal Unit 3947

Sentient, robotic android built by the Pralor race in 2222. The purpose of Unit 3947 and other numerical Automated Personal Units was purely to fight similar intelligent robots built by the Cravic race. 

Reference: Prototype
Portrayed by Rick Worthy.
