PERSONNEL: Ma'Bor Jetrel

Haakonian scientist who constructed the Metreon Cascade weapon used to conquer the Talaxians. Shunned by family and others, he devoted his later years to finding a cure for metremia, a fatal disease caused by exposure to the Cascade's isotopes. He died aboard the U.S.S. Voyager in 2371.
