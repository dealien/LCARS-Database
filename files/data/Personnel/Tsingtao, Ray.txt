PERSONNEL: Ray Tsingtao

Young boy whose parents committed suicide on Triacus in 2268. Tsingtao and the other Starnes Expedition children were discovered to have been controlled by an evil entity named Gorgan until Captain Kirk helped set them free. 

Reference: And the Children Shall Lead 
Played by Brian Tochi, who also played many years later as Ensign Peter Lin in "Night Terrors."
