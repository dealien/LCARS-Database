PERSONNEL: Lance Herbert
Chief, transporter operator, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

Crewman aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise-D who was a transporter operator in 2364 and promoted to Transporter Chief by 2365. 

Reference: We'll Always Have Paris 
Played by Lance Spellerberg. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
