PERSONNEL: Michael Cleary
Technician, engineering officer, U.S.S. Enterprise

Technician serving in the Engineering section of the refitted U.S.S. Enterprise in 2271 when the ship intercepted V'Ger. 

Reference: The Motion Picture 
Played by Michael Rougas. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
