PERSONNEL: Alexana Devos
Director, security officer, Rutia IV

She aided Enterprise operatives in a mission that rescued Captain Picard and Dr Crusher after they had been abducted by violent Ansata separatists. 

Reference: The High Ground 
Played by Kerrie Keane.
