PERSONNEL: Kryton
Body guard to Dohlman Elaan

Elasian bodyguard from a good family. Kryton's hopeless desire for Elaan, in 2268, lead to his conspiring with Klingons to thwart her upcoming nuptials. Kryton committed suicide after being caught. 

Reference: Elaan of Troyius 
Played by Tony Young.
