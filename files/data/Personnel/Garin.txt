PERSONNEL: Garin
Scientist, Bre'el IV

Garin was on duty in the planetary emergency center during the mission by the U.S.S. Enterprise-D to restore the Bre'el moon to its normal orbit. 

Reference: Deja Q
Played by Richard Cansino. The character's name was never mentioned on the air but is from the script.
