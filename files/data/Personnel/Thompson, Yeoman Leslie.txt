PERSONNEL: Leslie Thompson
Yeoman, U.S.S. Enterprise

Thompson was reduced to her chemical elements by the Kelvans and then crushed by Rojan. The Kelvan could have restored Thompson, but his actions resulted in her death. 

Reference: By Any Other Name 
Played by Julie Cobb.
