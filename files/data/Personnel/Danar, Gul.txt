PERSONNEL: Danar
Gul, starship commander, Aldara

In 2369 Gul Danar contacted Commander Sisko of Deep Space 9 to demand the return of Bajoran terrorist Tahna Los, who had been granted asylum aboard the station. 

Reference: Past Prologue 
Played by Vaughn Armstrong who also played a Klingon captain in "Sleeping Dogs," and Admiral Forrestt throughout ENT.
