PERSONNEL: Laurence Marvick 
PhD, starship designer, U.S.S. Enterprise

Gifted engineer and Constitution-class starship designer. In 2268 his desperate love for Dr. Miranda Jones led to attempted murder, insanity, and the near-loss of the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701. 

Reference: Is There In Truth No Beauty? Q Neighbor
Played by David Frankham.
