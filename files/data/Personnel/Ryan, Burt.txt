PERSONNEL: Burt Ryan
Sergeant, New York City Police Department/ Harlem Precinct

New York police officer from Earth's 1950's. Ryan and partner Kevin Mulkahey were racists who harassed Benny Russell and killed young Jimmy. Ryan's appearance was oddly similar to Gul Dukat. 

Reference: Far Beyond the Stars
