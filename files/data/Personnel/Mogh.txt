PERSONNEL: Mogh
Head, House of Mogh, Khitomer 

Illustrious Klingon warrior and head of a once-disgraced House in the Klingon Empire. Worf and Kurn, Mogh's sons, carried on the bitter rivalry with the House of Duras long after Mogh and their mother perished in the Khitomer massacre, and eventually regained the family honor. 

Reference: Soldiers of the Empire
