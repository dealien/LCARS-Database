PERSONNEL: Boq`ta
engineering officer, Deep Space 9

Boq'ta was killed in the line of duty when, on a salvage mission to Empok Nor in 2373, he was killed by a deranged Cardassian soldier who was under the influence of a psychotropic drug. 

Reference: Empok Nor 
Portrayed by Andy Milder.
