PERSONNEL: Jose Tyler
Navigator, U.S.S. Enterprise

In 2254, Tyler served as navigator when the Enterprise was diverted to Talos IV, and Captain Pike was abducted by the Talosians.

Reference: The Cage, The Menagerie
Played by Peter Duryea.
