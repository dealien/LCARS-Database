PERSONNEL: Elizabeth Palmer
Lieutenant, communications officer, U.S.S. Enterprise
She was on the bridge during the encounter with Commodore Decker's planet-killer in 2267. She was able to pierce local subspace interference to communicate with Kirk on the U.S.S. Constellation. 

Reference: The Doomsday Machine 
Played by Elizabeth Rogers. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
