PERSONNEL: Arctus Baran
Captain, mercenary starship

Baran was noted for his ruthless tactics and lethal punishments. In 2370, Baran was hired by members of the Vulcan isolationist movement to retrieve fragments of the fabled Stone of Gol. He was killed before the various fragments could be assembled and the true nature of the Stone could be revealed. 

Reference: Gambit
Played by Richard Lynch.
