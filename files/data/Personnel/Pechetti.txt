PERSONNEL: Pechetti
Technician, engineering officer, Deep Space Nine

Human engineer who volunteered for the O'Brien-led salvage mission to Empok Nor in 2373. Known as a practical joker and amateur collector, he was killed by a Cardassian Third Battalion soldier. 

Reference: Empok Nor 
Portrayed by Tom Hodges.
