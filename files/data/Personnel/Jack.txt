PERSONNEL: Jack

A genetically-engineered human being. Intellectual brilliant yet given to agitated ranting, Jack and his fellow mutants-Patrick, Sarina and Lauren-twice left the Daystrom Research Institute for encounters with Julian Bashir. When at the Institute they were under the care and legal responsibility of Dr. Karen Loews.

Reference: Statistical Probabilities
Portrayed by Hank Harris, Tim Ransom.
