PERSONNEL: Homn

Towering and taciturn individual of undetermined lineage who served as Lwaxana Troi's attendant. Mr. Homn was noted for his appetites, including intoxicating beverages and Betazoid uttaberries. 

Reference: Haven 
Played by Carel Struycken.
