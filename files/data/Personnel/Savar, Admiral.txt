PERSONNEL: Savar
Admiral, Starfleet Headquarters

Savar fell victim to the parasitic lifeforms that nearly seized control of Starfleet Command. He was a Vulcan officer.

Reference: Conspiracy 
Portrayed by Henry Darrow.
