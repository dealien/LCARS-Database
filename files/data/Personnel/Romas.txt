PERSONNEL: Romas

Ornaran crew member aboard the Sanction, a freighter. Romas was at one time addicted to felicium, a drug that the people of Ornara were addicted to courtesy of their planetary neighbors, the Brekkians. 

Reference: Symbiosis 
Portrayed by Richard Lineback, whom also played Selin Peers in "Dax."
