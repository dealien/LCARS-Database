PERSONNEL: Leslie Wong
Captain, starship commander, U.S.S. Cairo

Wong and her ship, the U.S.S. Cairo, were lost on patrol near the Romulan Neutral Zone in 2374. Wong taught at Starfleet Academy during Jadzia Dax's student years and the two became friends. 

Reference: In the Pale Moonlight
