PERSONNEL: Jomat Luson

Unpaid Bajoran social worker at the Tozhat Resettlement Center in 2362. She recalled that Rugal, the son of Kotan Pa'Dar, was brought to the center by a female Cardassian officer. 

Reference: Cardassians 
Played by Sharon Conley.
