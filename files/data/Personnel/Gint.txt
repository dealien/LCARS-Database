PERSONNEL: Gint

The first Grand Nagus, who wrote the Rules of Acquisition. Quark once conjured up Gint in a dream about resolving his dilemma over keeping his contract with Brunt for his own desiccated remains, or keeping his life by breaking it and suffering the resulting legal Ferengi consequences of poverty, destitution and exile. 

Reference: Body Parts 
Played by Max Grodenchik.
