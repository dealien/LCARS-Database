PERSONNEL: Claire Raymond

Human from 20th-century Earth who was cyrogenically frozen by her husband, Donald. In 2364, the U.S.S. Enterprise-D recovered and revived Raymond and several other frozen humans. While aboard the Enterprise, Raymond searched the databanks for records on her family line. She discovered that a distant grandson, Thomas Raymond, was still alive in Indianapolis, Indiana, Earth. She was transported back to Earth aboard the U.S.S. Charleston.

Reference: The Neutral Zone 
Portrayed by Gracie Harrison.
