PERSONNEL: Aquiel Uhnari
Lieutenant, communications officer, Relay Station 47

Communications technician who served a nine-month term of duty on Relay Station 47 between 2368 and 2369, accompanied by her pet dog Maura. Uhnari had previously been stationed at Deriben V, and homesickness assailed her throughout her postings. While an investigation was carried out into the murder of her colleague Keith Rocha, Uhnari became romantically involved with Geordi La Forge, who was able to converse with her in the language of her homeworld Halii. Following the investigation she was transferred to another posting.
