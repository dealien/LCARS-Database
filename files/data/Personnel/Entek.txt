PERSONNEL: Entek

Cardassian; member of the Obsidian Order who led the effort to expose Legate Ghemor's ties to the dissident Cardassian underground. Although successful, Entek was killed and Ghemor managed to escape. 

Reference: Second Skin
Played by Gregory Sierra.
