PERSONNEL: Solok
Captain, starship commander, U.S.S. T'Kumbra

A haughty Vulcan who repeatedly used Benjamin Sisko to exemplify uncontrolled emotion. Sisko ended Solok's taunts after a baseball game against his Vulcan team in 2375. 

Reference: Take Me Out to the Holosuite 
Portrayed by Gregory Wagrowski.
