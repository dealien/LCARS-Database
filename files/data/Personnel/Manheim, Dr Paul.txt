PERSONNEL: Paul Manheim
PhD, temporal scientist, Vandor IV

Temporal scientist whose theories generated little mainstream support. Manheim established an independent laboratory on the isolated planetoid of Vandor IV to continue his work. In 2364 his experiments generated dangerous waves of time-skips, referred to as the Manheim Effect. 

Reference: We'll Always Have Paris 
Portrayed by Rod Loomis.
