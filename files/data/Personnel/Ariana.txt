PERSONNEL: Ariana

One of the last eight survivors of the Terellian plague and daughter of Terellian leader Wrenn. Ariana had been in telepathic contact with Wyatt Miller for many years, and looked to his medical knowledge as being their hopes for survival. Miller, attracted to the beautiful Ariana, chose to remain with her and the other Tarellian plague victims in the hopes of finding the means for their survival.

Reference: Haven
Played by Danzitza Kingsley.
