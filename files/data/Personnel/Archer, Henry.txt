PERSONNEL: Henry Archer, PhD
Engineering designer, Warp Five Complex

Father of Jonathan Archer and one of the principal designers of the Enterprise NX-01. Henry also collaborated with Zefram Cochrane on the Warp 5 engine project.

Reference: Broken Bow
Played by Mark Moses, that became a regular actor on Desperate Housewives.
