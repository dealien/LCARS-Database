PERSONNEL: Kollos
Ambassador, diplomatic officer
An ambassador from the Medusans. While traveling among humans, Kollos remained in a covered container. Before starting work with Dr. Miranda Jones, Kollos joined with Spock to navigate the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701.
