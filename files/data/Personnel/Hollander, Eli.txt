PERSONNEL: Eli Hollander

A holodeck character, known as the "Buthcer of Boaeman," from the program Ancient West.

Reference: A Fistful of Data
Played by John Pyper-Ferguson. Bozeman, Montana is the hometown of Star Trek writer Brannon Braga.
