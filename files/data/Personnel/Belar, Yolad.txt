PERSONNEL: Yolad Belar

Trill composer, brother to Joran Belar. In 2371, Yolad was questioned by Deep Space Nine personnel about his memories regarding the death of his brother. The investigation revealed that the murderous Joran hosted the Dax symbiont for six months, a fact covered up by the Trill Symbiosis Commission. 

Reference: Equilibrium 
Played by Harvey Vernon.
