PERSONNEL: Patrick Gibson
crewman, U.S.S. Voyager

U.S.S. Voyager crewman. Gibson never missed the weekly rings tournament in the Fair Haven holographic village where he was known as a wharf worker, and was usually accompanied by his cousin Frank, a Voyager crewmate.
