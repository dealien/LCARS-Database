PERSONNEL: Cheney
Ensign, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

Cheney played the cello in an ensemble that performed Chopin's "Trio in G Minor" in a successful concert in Ten-Forward on Stardate 46693 (2369). That ensemble also included Data on violin and Nella Daren on piano.
