PERSONNEL: Douglas Pabst

Editor of the 1950's American science fiction magazine Incredible Tales. Pabst would not publish Benny Russell's story "Deep Space Nine" because it featured a Negro starship captain, and he feared the public reaction. 

Reference: Far Beyond the Stars 
Played by Rene Auberjonois.
