PERSONNEL: Delvok

Vulcan composer. Jadzia Dax thought Delvok didn't seem Vulcan to her, because there was too much emotion in his music. Ensign Melora thought his etudes were pretty. The two women listed to Delvok while on a shuttle mission to the Gamma Quadrant.
