PERSONNEL: T'Pring

Vulcan woman who was telepathically bonded with Spock when they were both seven years old. At the marriage ceremony in 2267, she rejected Spock and selected Captain Kirk as her champion, forcing the two men to fight for her possession. She had chosen Kirk, knowing that Spock would release her for making the challenge and that kirk would not want her, so that she would be free to choose Stonn as her consort.

Reference: Amok Time
Played by Arlene Martell.
