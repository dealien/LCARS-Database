PERSONNEL: Ethan Novakovich

Crewman on the Enterprise NX-01. Novakovich was part of the team that camped out on an unexplored planet, only to suffer from strange and frightening hallucinations. The crew later determined that the hallucinations were caused by a psychotropic-laced pollen. 

Reference: Strange New World
Portrayed by Henri Lubatti.
