PERSONNEL: J`Onn

Bald, humanoid digger of holes, and resident of Nimbus III when Sybok found him in 2287. After being freed of his pain by Sybok, J'Onn joined the Vulcan's search for Sha Ka Ree. 

Reference: The Final Frontier 
Played by Rex Holman, who also played Morgan Earp in "Spectre of the Gun."
