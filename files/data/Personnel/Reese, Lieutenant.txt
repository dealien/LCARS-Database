PERSONNEL: Steven Reese
Lieutenant, security officer, Deep Space Nine

Junior officer at Deep Space 9 who, in 2372, was temporarily in command of the station following Michael Eddington's defection to the Maquis. Reese also placed Kasidy Yates under arrest. 

Reference: For the Cause 
Played by Steven Vincent Leigh. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
