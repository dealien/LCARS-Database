PERSONNEL: Sklar

Male humanoid Nezu colonist and exo-geologist. Sklar was a murderer and traitor who aided an invasion plot by the Etanian Order. Sklar died after his true agenda was discovered. 

Reference: Rise
Portrayed by Kelly Connell.
