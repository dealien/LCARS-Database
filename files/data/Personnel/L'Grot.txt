PERSONNEL: L'Grot
Commander, executive officer, I.K.C. Br'Muk

The former first officer of the Klingon vessel, the I.K.C. Br'Muk before he made a challenge to the honor of the tactical sensor operator on duty, Dargo. The junior officer, feeling threatened and disgraced by his superior officer's bad judgement, challenged him to the death. Dargo won the fight quickly and assumed L'Grot's place among the crew.
