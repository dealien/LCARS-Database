PERSONNEL: Oji 

Female Mintakan and Liko's daughter. She was both in charge of sundial measurements and village record keeper. In 2366, Oji was exposed to Federation technology because of a malfunctioning hologram generator. 

Reference: Who Watches The Watchers? 
Portrayed by Pamala Segall.
