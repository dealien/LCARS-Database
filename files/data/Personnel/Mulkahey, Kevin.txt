PERSONNEL: Kevin Mulkahey
Patrolman, police officer, Harlem, Earth

Plain-clothes police officer in one of Benjamin Sisko's visions 1950's New York City. Mulkahey, who resembled Weyoun, and his partner Burt Ryan harassed Benny Russell. 

Reference: Far Beyond the Stars 
Played by Jeff Combs/ Weyoun, whom also played Krem in "Acquisition," Shran throughout ENT, Tiron in "Meridian," Penk in "Tsunkatse," and Brunt throughout DS9.
