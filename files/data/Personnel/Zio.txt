PERSONNEL: Zio

Prisoner in the Akritirian prison satellite where Harry Kim and Tom Paris were incarcerated in 2373. Zio spent six years writing an expose on the true purpose of the clamp. 

Reference: The Chute
Performed by Don R. McManus.
