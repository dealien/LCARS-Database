PERSONNEL: Errol Flynn
actor, Earth

Human movie actor from the first half of the 20th Century famous for athletic, swashbuckling roles. Vic Fontaine presented Nog with a replica of Flynn's black lion's head cane after the Ferengi lost a leg in battle.
