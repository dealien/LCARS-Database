PERSONNEL: Jo`Bril

An evil Takaran who, in 2369, used deceit and murder to steal metaphasic shield technology from its Ferengi inventor. Dr. Beverly Crusher uncovered Jo'Bril's scheme and killed him in 2369. 

Reference: Suspicions 
Played by James Horan.
