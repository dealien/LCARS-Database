PERSONNEL: Stolzoff
security officer, Deep Space Nine

A female Starfleet security officer who died protecting Starfleet engineers during a scavenging mission to Empok Nor in 2373. Stolzoff was killed by a Cardassian soldier awakened from stasis. 

Reference: Empok Nor 
Portrayed by Marjean Holden.
