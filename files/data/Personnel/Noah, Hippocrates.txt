PERSONNEL: Hippocrates Noah
PhD, mad scientist, Mount Everest, Earth

Dr. Noah was a mad scientist bent on reshaping the Earth by using powerful lasers to cause massive earthquakes and flooding. Dr. Noah had a secret stronghold at the top of Mount Everest, which he expected would remain an island after his plan caused the rest of the planet's surface to be flooded.
(Character in Julian Bashir's Secret Agent holosuite program. A holosuite malfunction in 2372 caused him to look exactly like Captain Benjamin Sisko.)

Reference: Our Man Bashir
Played by Avery Brooks.
