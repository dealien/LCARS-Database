PERSONNEL: Eve McHuron

After a lonely existence on an isolated agricultural planet, McHuron was lured into space by Harry Mudd's promise of a husband on Ophiucus III. Instead she married lithium miner Ben Childress. 

Reference: Mudd's Women 
Played by Karen Steele.
