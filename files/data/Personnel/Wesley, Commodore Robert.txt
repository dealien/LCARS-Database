PERSONNEL: Robert Wesley
Commodore, starship commander, U.S.S. Lexington

Starfleet official and friend of Captain James T. Kirk. Wesley commanded the U.S.S. Lexington NCC-1709 in 2368 during the tragic M-5 tests. Wesley disobeyed Starfleet orders to destroy the U.S.S. Enterprise. 

Reference: The Ultimate Computer 
This character was named for Gene Roddenberry's middle name, Wesley. And the name Robert Wesley was his pen name on his early television scripts in the 1950s while he was still working for the Los Angeles Police Department.
Played by Barry Russo, who also played Lieutenant Commander Giotto in "The Devil in the Dark."
