PERSONNEL: Tyree
State leader, hill people, Neural

Humanoid male on a primitive planet. Tyree's life as the hill people's peace-loving leader and husband to Nona ended when Klingons began supplying weapons to his enemies in 2267. Tyree first met James T. Kirk in 2254 when the young Starfleet officer was on a survey mission from the U.S.S. Farragut.

Reference: A Private Little War 
Played by Michael Whitney.
