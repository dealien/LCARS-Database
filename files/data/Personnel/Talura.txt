PERSONNEL: Talura

Desirable female humanoid escorted by weapons dealer Hagath on Deep Space 9 in 2373. While Hagath tended to business, Talura had a good time on the station spending his latinum. 

Reference: Business as Usual 
Played by Charlie Curtis.
