PERSONNEL: Nydom
PhD, scientist, T'Lani III

He sought Federation help to nullify the harvesters weapon in 2370. Nydom and other T'Lani scientists were killed by Kellerun soldiers to prevent new harvester fabrication. 

Reference: Armageddon Game 
Played by Larry Cedar.
