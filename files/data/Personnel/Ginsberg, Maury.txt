PERSONNEL: Maury Ginsberg

Follow-spot operator at Earth's legendary 1969 Woodstock concert. A member of the Q Continuum (who later gave himself the name "Quinn") gave Ginsberg a lift in a jeep, permitting the event to continue as scheduled, and acquainting Ginsberg with his future wife. Ginsberg later married and had four children, and became an orthodontist in the North American city of Scarsdale.
