PERSONNEL: Odona

The daughter of Prime Minister Hodin of planet Gideon. In 2268, Odona volunteered to sacrifice her life by contracting deadly Vegan choriomeningitis so that her death might serve as an inspiration to others on her polanet in their fight against overpopulation. While carrying out her assignment, Odona was cured of the illness by Dr. Leonard McCoy. She nevertheless chose to remain on her world to help solve its problems, despite the fact that she had fallen in love with Captain James Kirk.

Reference: The Mark of Gideon.
Played by Sharon Acker.
