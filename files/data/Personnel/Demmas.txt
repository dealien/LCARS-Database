PERSONNEL: Demmas

Demmas, the eldest son of the Autarch of Ilari, was next in line to become Autarch in 2373. But the centuries-old consciousness of the deposed tyrant, Tieran, killed the Autarch and staged a coup. Demmas fled the planet and enlisted the aid of the Voyager crew in thwarting Tieran's plans to take over his homeworld. 

Reference: Warlord
Portrayed by Brad Greenquist.
