PERSONNEL: Michael DeSalle
Lieutenant, navigator, U.S.S. Enterprise

Crew member aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise in 2267 who served as navigator and later as an assistant chief engineer. 

Reference: The Squire of Gothos 
Played by Michael Barrier. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name. This character has been seen in yellow and red shirts.
