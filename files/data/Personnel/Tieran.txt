PERSONNEL: Tieran

Strong-willed male who became Autarch of Ilari in 2173. Eventually Tieran lost favor and his office. Before dying in 2373, Tieran transferred his thoughts into Kes and attempted a coup. 

Reference: Warlord
Portrayed by Leigh J McCloskey, and Jennifer Lein/Kes.
