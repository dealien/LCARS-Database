PERSONNEL: Karen Kalandra
Doctor, chief medical officer, Ajilon Prime

Federation physician in charge of a battlefield hospital on Ajilon Prime, during the 2373 Klingon invasion. Her husband was a science officer on the U.S.S. Tecumseh.

Reference: ...Nor the Battle to the Strong 
Played by Karen Austin. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
