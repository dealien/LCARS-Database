PERSONNEL: Gabriel

A 10-year-old boy in the Gaia settlement in the Gamma Quadrant, who was quite impressed by meeting the legendary Worf. Gabriel dreamed of being a Klingon himself someday. Gabriel, like the rest of his people, ceased to exist in 2373 when the timeline was altered by the U.S.S. Defiant.
