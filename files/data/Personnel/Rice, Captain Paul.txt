PERSONNEL: Paul Rice
Captain, starship commander, U.S.S. Drake 

A former colleague of William Riker, was killed along with his crew at the planet Minos by an old automated weapons system. The image of Rice was later used to lure Commander Riker to the planet. 

Reference: The Arsenal of Freedom 
Portrayed by Marco Rodriguez.
