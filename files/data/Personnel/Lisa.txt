PERSONNEL: Lisa

A female child in Gaia, a colony of alternate-timeline progeny of U.S.S. Defiant personnel encountered by Sisko and crew in 2373. Lisa was a descendant of Jadzia Dax, with the spots to prove it. 

Reference: Children of Time 
Portrayed by Davida Williams.
