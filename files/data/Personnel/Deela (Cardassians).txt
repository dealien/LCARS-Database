PERSONNEL: Deela Hensel

Bajoran national who helped care for Cardassian war orphans at the Tozhat Resettlement Center in 2370. Deela was a member of the Bajoran underground during the Cardassian occupation of Bajor.

Reference: Cardassians
Played by Karen Hansel. Deela's name was never mentioned in the aired episode and only appeared in the script. The character's last name is conjectural also, based on the actor's real name.
