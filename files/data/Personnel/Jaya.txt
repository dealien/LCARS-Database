PERSONNEL: Jaya

A fellow inmate of William Riker's in the Tilonus Institute for Mental Disorders on Tilonus IV. She claimed to be Commander Bloom and promised a breakout opportunity via her starship, the Yorktown. 

Reference: Frame of Mind 
Played by Susanna Thompson.
