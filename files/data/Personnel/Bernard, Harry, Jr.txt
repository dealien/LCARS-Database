PERSONNEL: Harry Bernard, Jr.

Ten-year-old son of oceanographer Dr. Harry Bernard, Sr. In 2364 Harry Jr. was abducted by the Aldeans along with several other children living aboard the Enterprise-D in an attempt to perpetuate their dying race. The young Harry was not fond of having to study math, but his father reminded him that a basic understanding of calculus was expected of all children his age. 

Reference: When the Bough Breaks
