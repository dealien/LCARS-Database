PERSONNEL: Theodore Sturgeon
Crewman, U.S.S. Enterprise

Sturgeon lost his life on M-113 at the hands of the last of that planet's race of indigenous sentient lifeforms. 

Reference: The Man Trap 
Named for science-fiction writer Theodore Sturgeon, who wrote "Shore Leave" after this episode.
