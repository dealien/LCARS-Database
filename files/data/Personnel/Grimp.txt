PERSONNEL: Grimp

Ferengi waiter employed at Quark's bar in 2372. Although Grimp shared the Ferengi cultural dislike of labor unions, he joined the Guild of Restaurant and Casino Employees to oppose Quark's exploitation of his employees. 

Reference: Bar Association 
Played by Jason Marsden.
