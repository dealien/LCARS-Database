PERSONNEL: Shahna
Drill Thrall trainer, Triskelion

Female Drill Thrall on Triskelion. In 2268, Shahna acted as James T. Kirk's instructor. 

Reference: The Gamesters of Triskelion 
Played by Angelique Pettyjohn.
