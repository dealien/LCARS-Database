PERSONNEL: Yog

Yridian freighter captain who bought stolen magnesite ore from the Duras sisters. The U.S.S. Enterprise caught Yog in the Ufandi system and used the magnesite in his hold to discover the Duras sisters in a cloaked ship. 

Reference: Firstborn 
Portrayed by Colin Mitchell. The Continuum credits this actor as Joel Swetow, probably by mistake.
