PERSONNEL: Vekma

Female Klingon warrior serving aboard the Klingon battle cruiser I.K.S. Pagh in 2365. Vekma taunted temporary officer Will Riker by wondering if he could endure a Klingon female. 

Reference: A Matter of Honor 
Portrayed by Laura Drake.
