PERSONNEL: Arak'Taral
Second, Bopak III

Jem'Hadar soldier, second in command to Goran'Agar. In 2372 Goran'Agar brought his crew to Bopak III because he believed that the planet could cure his crew of their addiction to ketracel-white. Arak'Taral was against Goran'Agar's idea of curing their addiction if it meant becomng soft like humans. Arak'Taral was killed around stardate 49066 by Goran'Agar when he refused to follow Goran'Agar's orders.

Reference: Hippocratic Oath
Played by Stephen Davis. The character's name was not mentioned in the episode but comes from the script.
