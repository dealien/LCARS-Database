PERSONNEL: Sorad
Vedek, religious officer

Bajoran priest accompanying Minister Rozahn to Deep Space 9 to tell Haneek that the Skrreean colonization petition was not granted. Sorad and Rozahn hoped the personal meeting would soften the blow. 

Reference: Sanctuary 
Portrayed by either Robert Curtis-Brown (as reported by The Continuum) or Michael DUrrell (as stated by the Encyclopedia)
