PERSONNEL: Miko Tamura
Yeoman, U.S.S. Enterprise

Tamura was among the landing party personnel visiting Eminiar VII who were expected to step into a disintegration chamber. Tamura helped guard Mea 3. 

Reference: A Taste of Armageddon 
Played by Miko Mayama. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
