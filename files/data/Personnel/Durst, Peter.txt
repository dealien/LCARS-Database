PERSONNEL: Peter Durst
Lieutenant, U.S.S. Voyager

In 2371, he was captured by the Vidiians, along with Paris and Torres, and killed so that his organs could be harvested for Phage victims. Durst's face was used by the Vidiian doctor Sulan to give himself a more "pleasing" appearance to Torres.
