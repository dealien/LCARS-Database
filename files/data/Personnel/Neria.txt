PERSONNEL: Neria

Chief Thanatologist at the Vhnori infirmary, where Harry Kim was examined after returning from the Vhnori afterlife. The Vhnori afterlife is known as the Next Emanation. 

Reference: Emanations
Portrayed by Jarry Hardin, whom also played Radue in "When the Bough Breaks" and Samuel Clemons in "Times Arrow."
