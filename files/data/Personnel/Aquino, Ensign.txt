PERSONNEL: Aquino
Ensign, engineer, Deep Space 9

Starfleet officer who served aboard Deep Space 9. Aquino was killed in 2369 after investigating a security breach. Neela, who had planned to assassinate Vedek Bareil, was preapring her escape route when Ensign Aquino caught her in the act. Neela killed the ensign, then tried to conceal the crime by placing him into a conduit where energy bursts would reduce the body to ashes.
