PERSONNEL: McKnight
Ensign, helm officer, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

Served aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701-D during the period of 2367 - 2369. She was assigned to the conn station when the ship encountered the Paxans and during a Romulan defection. 

Reference: Clues 
Portrayed by Pamela Winslow.
