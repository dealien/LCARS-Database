PERSONNEL: Jay Ducane

Male Federation officer from the 29th Century. Ducane served on the Relativity, a timeship, under Captain Braxton. Ducane recruited Seven of Nine and Captain Janeway to resolve a massive temporal incursion. 

Reference: Relativity
Portrayed by Jay Karnes. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
