PERSONNEL: Pinar
Deputy, security officer, Deep Space Nine

Bajoran male security officer, working under Constable Odo on DS9 in 2374. Pinar was guarding the 18-year-old feral Molly O'Brien in the brig when Miles O'Brien sedated him and freed his daughter.
