PERSONNEL: Mara
Science officer, I.K.S. K'Tanco

Female Klingon science officer and, in 2268, Kang's wife. She and Kang captured the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701. She was the first to trust humans after being saved from Chekov. She and Kang had a son, which they named Dax after the Federation diplomat of Trill descent. That son was killed by a criminal known as The Albino.

Reference: Day of the Dove
