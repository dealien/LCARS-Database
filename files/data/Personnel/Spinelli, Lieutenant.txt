PERSONNEL: Spinelli
Lieutenant, U.S.S. Enterprise

Spinelli was with the captain when Khan cut the bridge's oxygen. Spinelli thought death imminent, but survived. Kirk noted his bravery. 

Reference: Space Seed 
Played by Blaisdell Makee, who also played Mr. Singh in "The Changeling."
