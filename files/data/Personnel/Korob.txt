PERSONNEL: Korob

One of two tiny explorers from another galaxy stationed on Pyris VII in 2267. Korob rebelled against his corrupt partner, Sylvia. Both died after their power source, the transmuter, was destroyed. 

Reference: Catspaw 
Played by Theo Marcuse.
