PERSONNEL: Trazko

Bajoran male who conspired with Pallra, widow of a Deep Space 9 shopkeeper, to blackmail Bajorans who had collaborated with the Cardassians. Trazko tried to kill Quark twice. 

Reference: Necessary Evil 
Played by Robert MacKenzie.
