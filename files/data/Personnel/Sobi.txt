PERSONNEL: Sobi

Male humanoid native of Brekka who, with Langor, handled sales of felicium to Ornara in 2364. 

Reference: Symbiosis 
Portrayed by Judson Scott, whom also played Joachim in "The Wrath of Khan." Judson played opposite his life partner Merritt Butrick in both episodes.
