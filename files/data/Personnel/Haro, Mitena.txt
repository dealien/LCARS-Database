PERSONNEL: Mitena Haro
Cadet 4th class, engineering officer, Starfleet Academy

A Starfleet Academy cadet from Bolarus IX who began studying in 2366. She was kidnapped by an alien species, name unknown, who duplicated her to understand the concept of authority. 

Reference: Allegiance 
Played by Jocelyn O'Brien.
