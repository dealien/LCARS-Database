PERSONNEL: Meressa
Kai, religeous leader, Bajor

Previous leader of the Bajoran faith. Gul Dukat quoted her sentiments regarding the dead, "What remains after death is but a shell - a sign that the pagh has begun its final journey to the Prophets" to Kira.
