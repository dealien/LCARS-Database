PERSONNEL: Roana

Bajoran female. Roana, a merchant, was mesmerized by an El-Aurian named Martus Mazur. In 2370, she helped Martus establish his own club on Deep Space Nine's Promenade. 

Reference: Rivals 
Portrayed by Barbara Bosson.
