PERSONNEL: Tam Elbrun

Betazoid Starfleet specialist aboard the Enterprise-D in 2366. Elbrun's telepathic abilities led him to be assigned to many significant first contact missions, despite a substantial history of instability. 

Reference: Tin Man, If Only I Had a Brain
Played by Harry Groener.
