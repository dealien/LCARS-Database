PERSONNEL: Bemar
Chief security officer, Prophet's Landing

The Bajoran colony closest to the Cardassian border. Bemar met with Odo, his counterpart from station Deep Space 9, about Stardate 48521 (2371), when Odo and Major Kira visited the colony to review security procedures related to new orbital sensor platforms deployed to warn the colonists against Cardassian attack.
