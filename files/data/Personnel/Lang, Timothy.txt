PERSONNEL: Timothy Lang
Crewman, U.S.S. Voyager

Male assigned to the U.S.S. Voyager NCC-74656 crew. Lang spent time with a malfunctioning alien warhead before the device was brought aboard ship in 2375. Lang was dead by 2377.
