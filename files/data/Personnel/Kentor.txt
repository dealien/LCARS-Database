PERSONNEL: Richard Kentor

One of the influential young colonists on Tau Cygna V in 2366. Kentor was involved in the colony relocation debate. Gosheven, the leader, was against the move. 

Reference: The Ensigns of Command 
Played by Richard Allen. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
