PERSONNEL: Atul

An agent of the Klingon intelligence service. One of three agents sent to Deep Space 9 in 2371 to observe and take appropriate action against a Romulan delegation visiting the station. 

Reference: Visionary
Played by Dennis Madalone, the stunt coordinator for Deep Space Nine and Voyager series. He also played other parts in those shows like Chief Hendrick of "Identity Crisis," the one-eyed Terran in "Crossover," "Through the Looking Glass," and "Shattered Mirror," as well as other roles.
