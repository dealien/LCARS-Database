PERSONNEL: Surat
Jal, Sect Leader, Kazon-Mostral

First Maje of the Kazon-Mostral in the Delta Quadrant. Surat attended the Trabe peace conference in 2372 and nearly died. 

Reference: Maneuvers, Alliances
Portrayed by John Gegenhuber.
