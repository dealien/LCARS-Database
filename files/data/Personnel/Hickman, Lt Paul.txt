PERSONNEL: Paul Hickman
Lieutenant, U.S.S. Victory

Starfleet officer. Former crew member of the U.S.S. Victory who beamed down to planet Tarchannen III in 2362. In 2367, Hickman was compelled to steal a Federation shuttle and return to Tarchannen III. Hickman's shuttle was destroyed attempting to land on the planet. It was later discovered that Hickman's body had been infiltrated by an alien DNA strand in 2362. 

Reference: Identity Crisis 
Played by Amick Byram.
