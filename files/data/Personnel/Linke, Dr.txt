PERSONNEL: Jason Linke, PhD.
Science officer, Minara II

A human male scientist collecting data prior to a nova near Minara II. Linke was murdered by the Vians during their attempt to test the inner qualities of a Minaran empath. 

Reference: The Empath 
Played by Jason Wingreen. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
