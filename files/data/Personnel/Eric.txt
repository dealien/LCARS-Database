PERSONNEL: Eric Collins

Child of a crewman of the U.S.S. Enterprise-D. Eric was the first child encountered by Iyaaran ambassador Loquel aboard the ship in 2370. Eric also participated in a sculpting class abaord the ship along with Commander Data.

Reference: Liaisons, Masks
Played by Rickey D'shon Collins. The character's last name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
