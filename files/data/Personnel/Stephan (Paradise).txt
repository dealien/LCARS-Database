PERSONNEL: Stephan
colonist, Orellius

A colonist journeying to Gemulon V in 2360 when his ship, the S.S. Santa Maria, crashed on Orellius thanks to colony leader Alixus. Stephan remained on Orellius until 2370. He resorted to petty crime against the other colonists and Alixus had to punish him severely to teach him a lesson.

Reference: Paradise 
Played by Erick Weiss.
