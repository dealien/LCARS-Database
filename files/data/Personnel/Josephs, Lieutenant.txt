PERSONNEL: Mitchell Josephs
Lieutenant, security officer, U.S.S. Enterprise

Security officer aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise who discovered the body of the murdered delegate Gav during the journey to the Babel Conference of 2268. 

Reference: Journey to Babel 
Played by James. X. Mitchell.
