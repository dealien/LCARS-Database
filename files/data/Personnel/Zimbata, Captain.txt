PERSONNEL: Zimbata
Captain, starship commander, U.S.S. Victory

Commander of the Constellation-class U.S.S. Victory NCC-9754 and Geordi La Forge's captain prior to Jean-Luc Picard. Zimbata was presented with a model of the sailing ship Victory by La Forge.
