PERSONNEL: Henry the 5th

A fictional King of England created by William Shakespeare. In the tale the King dressed up like a commoner in an effort to walk about his people and learn about how they truly felt about him. Lieutenant Commander Data participated in a holodeck recreation of this character.

Reference: The Defector
Portrayed by Brent Spiner/ Data.
