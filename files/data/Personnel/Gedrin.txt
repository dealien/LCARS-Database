PERSONNEL: Gedrin

Male Vaadwaur who, with his wife, Jisa, was the last to enter bio-stasis. Though Gedrin deactivated 37 other bio-pods to provide energy for his own, he was considered one of the nicer Vaadwaur. 

Reference: Dragon's Teeth
