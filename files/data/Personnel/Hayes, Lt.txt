PERSONNEL: Hayes
Lieutenant, helm officer, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

Crewmember atop the duty roster on Stardate 47653 (2370) when Data needed a shuttle pilot to find an off-course demo torpedo. But because of a hiatus in the ship's schedule, Jean-Luc Picard did the job instead.
