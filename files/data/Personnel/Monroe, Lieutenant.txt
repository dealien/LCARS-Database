PERSONNEL: Jana Monroe
Lieutenant, operations officer, U.S.S. Enterprise-D
 
U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701-D officer in command when the ship was hit by two undetectable quantum filaments in 2368. Monroe died during this event which also nearly destroyed the ship. 

Reference: Disaster 
Portrayed by Jana Marie Hupp.
