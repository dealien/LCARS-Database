PERSONNEL: Maya

Kantare mother of Liana and wife of Ezral. Maya died when the Kantare vessel encountered and ion storm and crashed, but Ezral created a holographic version of her as part of his plan to keep Liana from getting lonely. The holographic Maya chastised Liana for being curious about the Enterprise NX-01 and its crew.
