PERSONNEL: Tina Lawton
Yeoman, U.S.S. Enterprise

Lawton was introduced to Charles Evans by Janice Rand in an attempt to stop the boy's unwanted advances onto her. Lawton agreed to date Charlie but was rebuffed by the lad who later turned her, temporarily, into an iguana. 

Reference: Charlie X 
Played by Patricia McNulty.
