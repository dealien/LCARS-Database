PERSONNEL: Janet Wallace
PhD, science officer, U.S.S. Enterprise

Endocrinologist who ended her romance with James Kirk in 2261 in favor of her career. She later married Dr. Theodore Wallace, whom she admired, after work brought them together on Aldebaran III. He was 26 years older than her and died a few years later. Janet helped McCoy cure a fatal radiation illness that triggered accelerated aging. 

Reference: The Deadly Years 
Played by Sarah Marshall.
