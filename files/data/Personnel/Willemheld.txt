PERSONNEL: Willemheld
playwright, Earth

A playwright of 23rd-century Earth whom Julian Bashir credited with being an original thinker, unlike the current crop of Earth playwrights who seemed obsessed with re-visiting stories from other cultures. 

Reference: The Die Is Cast
