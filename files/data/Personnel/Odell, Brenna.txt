PERSONNEL: Odell, Brenna 
Danilo Odell's beautiful, headstrong daughter. In 2365 Brenna had an encounter with Riker, but, being a sensible girl, opted to help organize a new life for her people on Mariposa. 
Reference: Up The Long Ladder
