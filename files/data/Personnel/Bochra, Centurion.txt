PERSONNEL: Bochra
Centurion

Romulan officer marooned along with Geordi La Forge on planet Galorndon Core in 2366. Bochra at first tried to hold La Forge prisoner, but later cooperated with him to survive the planet's hostile environment. 

Reference: The Enemy
Played By John Snyder.
