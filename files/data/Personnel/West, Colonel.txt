PERSONNEL: Rene West
Colonel, Starfleet HQ

Starfleet officer who tried to stop the Klingon peace talks by assassinating the Federation Council President during the Khitomer conference in 2293. West's attempt was foiled by the intervention of members from the U.S.S. Enterprise and U.S.S. Excelsior crews. 

Reference: The Undiscovered Country 
Played by Rene Auberjonois. He was uncredited during the movie since the scene featuring him was edited out. The scene was returned for the video release. The cahracter's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
