PERSONNEL: Donald Cory
Governor, Elba II

Administrator of the Federation penal colony on Elba II. In 2269, Cory was violently deposed by a group of inmates led by Garth of Izar. 

Reference: Whom Gods Destroy 
Played by Keye Luke.
