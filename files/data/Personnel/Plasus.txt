PERSONNEL: Plasus
State leader, Stratos

In 2269, Plasus ruled Stratos, the cloud city of Ardana. Though Plasus appreciated fine art, he treated Troglyte workers contemptuously and resented Kirk's interference. 
Plasus had a daughter, Droxine. 

Reference: The Cloud Minders 
Played by Jeff Corey.
