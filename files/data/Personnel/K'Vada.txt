PERSONNEL: K'Vada
Captain, starship commander, I.K.S.

Klingon bird-of-prey captain who succeeded in taking Jean-Luc Picard to Romulus in 2368 without being detected by the Romulan sensors. 

Reference: Unification
Played by Stephen Root.
