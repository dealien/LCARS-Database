PERSONNEL: Jeanne Mori
Helm officer, U.S.S. Grissom

This officer was serving aboard the starship when the science vessel began to study the Genesis Planet. She was still on duty when a Klingon bird-of-prey decloaked and attacked the ship. She was killed when the ship was destroyed.

Reference: The Search for Spock
Portrayed y Jeanne Mori. The character's name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
