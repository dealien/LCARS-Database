PERSONNEL: Nellen Tore

Female from Delb II who worked as Admiral Norah Satie's assistant in 2367. Tore was always at Satie's side, silently taking notes or recording Satie's dictation. 

Reference: The Drumhead 
Portrayed by Ann Shea.
