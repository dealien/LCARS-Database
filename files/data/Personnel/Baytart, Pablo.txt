PERSONNEL: Pablo Baytart
Helmsman, U.S.S. Voyager

Starfleet officer stationed aboard the U.S.S. Voyager. Baytart was noted as an exceptional pilot and a talented juggler. His crew quarters were adjacent to Harry Kim's.
