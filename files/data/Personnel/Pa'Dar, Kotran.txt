PERSONNEL: Kotran Pa`Dar
Councillor, Detapa Council, Cardassia Prime

Cardassian leader on Bajor where terrorists killed his family. In 2370 Gul Dukat learned Pa'Dar's son was alive and living with Bajorans and intended to ruin Pa'Dar with this revelation. 

Reference: Cardassians
Played by Robert Mandan.
