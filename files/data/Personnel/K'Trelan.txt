PERSONNEL: K`Trelan

Ancient Klingon general. The Klingon Empire's Second Dynasty ended when K'Trelan assassinated Emperor Reclaw and his entire family, leading to a decade of democracy referred to as 'The Dark Time' by modern Klingon historians.
