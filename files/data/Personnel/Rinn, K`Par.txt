PERSONNEL: K'Par Rinn
Chief administrator, Argrathi prison, Gamma Quadrant

In 2372, Miles O'Brien was sentenced to 15 cycles of correction at this prison. 

Reference: Hard Time 
Played by Margot Rose whom also played Kamin's wife Eline in "The Inner Light." The character was not named in the episode and was in the script.
