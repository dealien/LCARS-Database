PERSONNEL: Torg

Klingon warrior in Commander Kruge's crew. In 2285, Torg killed Kirk's son David Marcus when the young man stepped forward to save Saavik. Torg died soon after while aboard the self-destructing U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701.

Reference: The Search for Spock 
Portrayed by Stephen Liska.
