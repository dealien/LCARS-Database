PERSONNEL: Valjean
petty criminal

Fictional hero of Victor Hugo's novel "Les Miserables," who was guilty of a minor offense, yet tirelessly pursued by the forces of the law. Maquis leader Michael Eddington considered himself a similarly noble and romantic character.
