PERSONNEL: Ahab
Captain, naval commander, Earth

The main character in Earth writer Herman Melville's 1851 classic novel "Moby Dick."
Lily Sloan compared Captain Picard to Ahab because Picard seemed to want revenge on the Borg above all else.

Reference: First Contact
