PERSONNEL: Karen Brackett
Fleet Admiral, Starbase 234

Starfleet officer who met with Captain Picard at Starbase 234 in 2368 to discuss the disappearance of Ambassador Spock. She ordered the Enterprise-D to proceed to planet Vulcan to investigate Spock's whereabouts and motives. 

Reference: Unification
Played by Karen Hensel.
