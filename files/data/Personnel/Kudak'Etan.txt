PERSONNEL: Kudak`Etan
First, Alpha boarding team

A specially-bred Alpha Jem'Hadar warrior. As 'First,' he declared victory in a takeover of the U.S.S. Defiant in 2374. During the Starfleet crew's fight to regain the ship, Worf killed Kudak'Etan with his bare hands.
