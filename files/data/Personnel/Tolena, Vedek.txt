PERSONNEL: Tolena
Vedek, religious member, Bajor

A Bajoran priest being considered for kai, along with Vedek Bareil and Vedek Winn, after Opaka's death in 2370. Tolena had no chance against Bareil or Winn. 

Reference: The Collaborator
