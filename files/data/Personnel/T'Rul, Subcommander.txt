PERSONNEL: T`Rul
Sub-commander, engineering advisor, U.S.S. Defiant

Romulan observer on the U.S.S. Defiant in 2371. When the Romulans loaned cloaking technology to the Federation for use in the Gamma Quadrant, T'Rul was part of the package. 

Reference: The Search
Played by Martha Hackett, who later played Seska in several episodes of Voyager.
