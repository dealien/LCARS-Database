PERSONNEL: Morn

Male Lurian, a frequent, exceedingly composed patron at Quark's who panicked when Quark warned him of a rumored Dominion invasion. A shrewd financier with a shady past, Morn faked his own death in 2374 to keep past cohorts and an ex-wife, Larell, from the illicit fortune of latinum hidden in his second stomach. 

Reference: Emissary, Vortex, Starship Down, Progress, Through the Looking Glass, Looking For par'Mach In All the Wrong Places, The Visitor, Who Mourns for Morn?, What You May Leave Behind
Played by Mark Shepard. He was not named until Vortex. Morn is an anagram for Norm, george Wendt's character in "Cheers."
