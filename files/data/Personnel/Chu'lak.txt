PERSONNEL: Chu`lak
Lieutenant, science officer, Deep Space Nine

Vulcan science officer who turned murderer after loosing 1200 crewmates during the battle of Ricktor Prime in 2375. Chu'lak's use of a TR-116 rifle made catching him very difficult.
