PERSONNEL: James Wainwright
Captain, Roswell, New Mexico, Earth

Overzealous officer in the United States Army Air Corps of 1947. Wainwright was convinced Rom, Quark and Nog were invading Martians and wanted to dissect them to prove it. Wainwright reported to General Denning. 

Reference: Little Green Men 
Played by James G. MacDonald. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
