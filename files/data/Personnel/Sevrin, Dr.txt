PERSONNEL: Sevrin, PhD
Communications officer, Tilonus

Brilliant acoustics and communications scientist, as well as charismatic leader, who started a back-to-nature movement called One. Dr. Sevrin was originally from Tiburon, but he met his fate on a toxic planet that was mistaken for Eden. 

Reference: The Way to Eden 
Played by Skip Homier, who also played Melakon in "Patterns of Force."
