PERSONNEL: Immamura 
Lieutenant, crewman, U.S.S. Enterprise

Member of landing party number two, under Lieutenant Commander Kelowitz, searching for shuttlecraft Galileo on Taurus II in 2267. Immamura was injured by indigenous humanoids, suffering a dislocated shoulder and severe lacerations. 

Reference: The Galileo Seven
