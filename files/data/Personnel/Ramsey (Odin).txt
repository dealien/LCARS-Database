PERSONNEL: Ramsey (Odin) 
Survivor of a Federation freighter crash. Ramsey's ship, the Odin, broke up in space and he, along with four others, drifted to Angel One in escape pods. Upon landing on the planet, Ramsey and the others took up residence. 
Reference: Angel One
