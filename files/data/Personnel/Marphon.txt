PERSONNEL: Marphon

Beta III resident and government official who was actually a key figure in the clandestine resistance to the rule of Landru. Marphon also freed Spock and Kirk from the custody of the Lawgivers. 

Reference: The Return of the Archons 
Played by Torin Thatcher.
