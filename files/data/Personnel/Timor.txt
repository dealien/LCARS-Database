PERSONNEL: Timor
Guardian, Mak'ala, Trill

One of the special Guardians who worked beneath the Trill homeworld in the caves of Mak'ala, where symbionts live before joining. Timor diagnosed the source of Jadzia Dax's illness in 2371. 

Reference: Equilibrium 
Played by Nicholas Cascone.
