PERSONNEL: Brota

Part-Klingon inhabitant of the planet Gaia in an alternate timeline. Brota led a group, the "Sons of Mogh," that chose to follow the ways of his Klingon ancestors. However, Brota and the inhabitants of this alternate reality ceased to exist in 2373.
