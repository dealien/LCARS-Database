PERSONNEL: Butler

Terran canine of the Great Dane breed, pet to James T. Kirk who for a time lived in a mountain cabin on Earth with Kirk and girlfriend Antonia. The dog died in 2286, but seven years later Kirk saw him in the false reality of his Nexus experience.
