PERSONNEL: Arachnia

Alluring queen of the Spider People in 'The Adventures of Captain Proton' holoserial. Arachnia was the object of the evil Doctor Chaotica's affections. Captain Janeway portrayed Arachnia to help defeat Chaotica. 

Reference: Bride of Chaotica!
