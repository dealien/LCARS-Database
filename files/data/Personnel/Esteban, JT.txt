PERSONNEL: J.T. Esteban
Captain, starship commander, U.S.S. Grissom

Commanding officer of the U.S.S. Grissom, in charge of investigating the Genesis Planet. A conservative, by-the-book officer, Esteban was killed along with the rest of the Grissom crew when the ship was destroyed by a Klingon ship during the investigation. 

Reference: The Search for Spock 
Played by Phillip Richard Allen.
