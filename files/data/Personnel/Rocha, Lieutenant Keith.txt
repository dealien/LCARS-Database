PERSONNEL: Kieth Rocha
Lieutenant, Relay Station 47 

Young Starfleet officer with great potential who was murdered in 2369 by a coalescent organism that later assumed his form. The organism then moved on to Relay Station 47 before it was discovered. 
In Rocha's lifetime he had earned two decorations for valor and three outstanding evaluations from his superiors.
