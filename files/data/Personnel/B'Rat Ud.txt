PERSONNEL: B'Rat Ud

Chemist who had a shop at an alien bazaar in the Delphic Expanse. Archer purchased the formula for synthesizing trellium-D from him. B'Rat was payed in spices from Enterprise - black pepper, paprika, mustard seed and cayenne.
