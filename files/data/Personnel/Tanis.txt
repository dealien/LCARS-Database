PERSONNEL: Tanis

Actress in Kelis' theatre company. She played Janeway in two Voyager Eternals, "The Away Mission of B'Elanna Torres" and "The Rescue of B'Elanna Torres." Her performance helped prevent a war. 

Reference: Muse 
Portrayed by Kathleen Garrett. This actress was credit to a different Tanis character on The Continuum website by mistake.
