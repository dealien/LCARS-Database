PERSONNEL: Kalin Trose
Ambassador, diplomatic officer, Peliar Alpha

Male, humanoid official from the Alpha Moon orbiting Peliar Zel. In 2337 and 2367 his people were armed for war with the Beta Moon. He participated in peace negotiations both times with Ambassador Odan as mediator.

Reference: The Host 
Played by William Newman.
