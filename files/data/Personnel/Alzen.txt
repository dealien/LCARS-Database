PERSONNEL: Alzen 

One of several Srivani scientists who surreptitiously boarded the U.S.S. Voyager in 2374 and took part in some dangerous medical tests on the crew. 

Reference: Scientific Method
Played by Rosemary Forsyth. The species was not referred to in the episode, but was in the script.
