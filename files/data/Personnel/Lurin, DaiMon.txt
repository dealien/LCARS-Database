PERSONNEL: Lurin, DaiMon

Independent Ferengi captain who commandeered the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701-D in 2369 using two B'rel-class Klingon ships and the Ferengi Salvage Code. He was defeated by a handful of children.
