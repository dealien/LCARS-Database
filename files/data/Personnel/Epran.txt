PERSONNEL: Epran

One of the countless victims of the virulent Teplan blight. Epran died despite the best efforts of Dr. Julian Bashir, who waged a personal crusade to develop a vaccine for the disease. 

Reference: The Quickening 
Played by Dylan Haggerty.
