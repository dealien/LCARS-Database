PERSONNEL: T'Jona
Rh'iov, starship commander, R.S.E. Vindicator

A female Romulan officer in command of a battle cruiser that captured the U.S.S. Enterprise in 2268. While on a mission to obtain Romulan cloaking technology, Kirk was captured by the Romulans. However, the mission was eventually successful and the Romulan commander was captured and made a Federation prisoner. She was born in ch'Rihan on Romulus.

Reference: The Enterprise Incident 
Played by Joanne Linville. The character's name, birthplace, and other facts are conjectural.
