PERSONNEL: Anton Karidian
actor, Karidian Company of Players

Formerly known as Kodos the Executioner, due to his history of killing over 4,000 colonists while he was Governor of Tarsus IV in 2246. He staged his own "death" and became Karidian, a famous touring Shakespearean actor whose talents and players were much acclaimed. He had a daughter, Lenore, who also joined the acting troupe. Karidian strived diligently to create and support the new life he created and to protect his daughter from ever learning of his past or allowing her to be affected by that history. He died jumping in front of phaser fire meant for Captain James Kirk fired from Lenore when she attempted to assassinate the last surviving member of the Taurus colony that posed a threat to exposing her father's crimes.  

Reference: The Conscience of the King 
Played by Arnold Moss.
