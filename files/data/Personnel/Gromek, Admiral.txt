PERSONNEL: Georgann Gromek
Admiral, Starfleet operations, Earth

Starfleet official who transmitted secret orders to the U.S.S. Enterprise-D to rendezvous with special Federation emissary K'Ehleyr prior to the return of the Klingon ship T'Ong in 2365. 

Reference: The Emissary 
Played by Georgann Johnson. The character's name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
