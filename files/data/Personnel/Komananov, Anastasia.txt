PERSONNEL: Anatasia Komananov
Colonel, Russian KGB, Earth

A holodeck character featured in Julian Bashir's spy/adventure program. The holodeck suite at Quark's bar performed a malfunction and created the character in the image of Kira Nerys. The story's main character, the British secret agent played by Bashir once presented her with a set of explosive earrings as a Christmas gift. Those earrings later helped the two of them escape the assassin known as Falcon.

Reference: Our Man Bashir
Played by Nana Visitor.
