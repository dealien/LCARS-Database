PERSONNEL: Villus Thed

A short-range telepath on Odo's 'most wanted' list enroute to Deep Space 9 in 2370. At the station, Odo's deputies were to follow Thed covertly, keeping at least five meters distant to avoid empathic detection. 

Reference: Second Sight
