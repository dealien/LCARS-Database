PERSONNEL: Albert Macklin

Diffident and absent-minded, mechanical engineer turned sci-fi writer Macklin, who resembled Miles O'Brien, worked at Incredible Tales magazine with Benny Russell in Benjamin Sisko's dream-vision of 1950's New York. 

Reference: Far Beyond the Stars 
Played by Colm Meaney, who also played Chief Miles O'Brien in Next Generation and all of Deep Space 9.
