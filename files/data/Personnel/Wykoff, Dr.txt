PERSONNEL: Casey Wykoff
Medical doctor

Character in Benjamin Sisko's Pah-wraith vision on the planet Tyree. Dr. Wykoff, who resembled Damar in human form, was a mental institution psychiatrist trying to force Benny Russell/Sisko to forget Deep Space 9. 

Reference: Shadows and Symbols 
Played by Casey Biggs. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
