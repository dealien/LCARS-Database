PERSONNEL: Duran`Adar
Sixth, Alpha boarding team

Jem'Hadar soldier under Kudak'Etan. Sixth Duran'Adar helped capture the U.S.S. Defiant in 2374 and would have executed a Defiant crewmember if Sisko had not agreed to repair his ship.
