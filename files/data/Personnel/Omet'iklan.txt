PERSONNEL: Omet`iklan
First, Iconian assault squad

Jem'Hadar soldier. In 2372 his unit joined with a Federation team to destroy an Iconian gateway. Omet'iklan was a vicious disciplinarian and even killed a Vorta Weyoun who questioned his allegiance. 

Reference: To the Death 
Played by Clarence Williams III.
