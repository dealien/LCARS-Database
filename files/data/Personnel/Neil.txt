PERSONNEL: Neil

Engineer and mercenary in the ill-fated attempt to steal trilithium resin from the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701-D under the cover of a baryon sweep. The team leader, Kelsey, killed Neil. 

Reference: Starship Mine
