PERSONNEL: Zor Khan

Man who ruled the planet Sarpeidon with an iron fist. Zor Khan never killed his enemies, but had no regrets about using the atavachron to strand them in his planet's past. That included Zarabeth, who was sent back 5,000 years into a brutal ice age. 

Reference: All Our Yesterdays
