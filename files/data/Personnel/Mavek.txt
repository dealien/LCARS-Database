PERSONNEL: Mavek

Illusional being who tormented William Riker during the latter's cerebrally-induced incarceration in the Tilonius Institute for Mental Disorders, where Mavek served as an orderly. 

Reference: Frame of Mind 
Portrayed by Gary Werntz.
