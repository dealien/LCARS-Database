PERSONNEL: Anthony Haftel
Admiral, cyberneticist, Starfleet Research & Development

Starfleet cyberneticist who ordered Data to relinquish custody of his android 'daughter' Lal in 2366. Picard stood by Data's refusal to comply, and Haftel later assisted Data when Lal suffered an ultimately fatal neural system failure. 

Reference: The Offspring 
Played by Nicolas Coster. The character's first name was never mentioned on air but is included in the script.
