PERSONNEL: Silvestri
Captain, vessel commander, S.S. Shiku Maru

Data quoted Silvestri's description of the Tamarians as "incomprehensible" to Captain Picard. Silvestri's assessment jibed with others who had encountered the Children of Tama.
