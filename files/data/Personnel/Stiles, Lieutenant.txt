PERSONNEL: Andrew Stiles
Lieutenant, navigator, U.S.S. Enterprise

He served during the Romulan incursion. Stiles had lost relatives during an earlier Romulan war and accused Spock of being a Romulan spy. 

Reference: Balance of Terror 
Played by Paul Comi. The character was not issued a first name, but The Continuum gave him the name of Andrew by mistake.
