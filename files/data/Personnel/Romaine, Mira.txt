PERSONNEL: Mira Romaine
Crewman, U.S.S. Enterprise

In 2269, Romaine oversaw the transfer of equipment to Memory Alpha, the Federation's storage station for all information on member planets. 
During the mission, Romaine suffered from an invasive probe of her mind by the non-corporeal Zetarians. She later recovered from the attack, with the help of good friend Montgomery Scott. 

Reference: The Lights of Zetar, Memory Prime
Played by Jan Shutan.
