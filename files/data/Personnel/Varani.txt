PERSONNEL: Varani
musician, Deep Space Nine

Once feted Bajoran musician reduced to playing Quark's bar during the Cardassian occupation of Bajor. He had performed at the prestigious Jalanda Forum. Sanctuary 

Reference: Sanctuary
Played by William Schallert, who also played Nilz Baris in "The Trouble With Tribbles" and "Trouble and Tribble-ations."
