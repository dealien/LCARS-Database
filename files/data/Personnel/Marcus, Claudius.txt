PERSONNEL: Claudius Marcus 
Proconsul, planetary leader, Eight Ninety-Two IV.

Politically powerful man on a world like ancient Rome. Marcus gave R. M. Merrick power and position in 2261, but failed to interest Kirk, Spock, and McCoy with similar trappings in 2267. 

Reference: Bread and Circuses
Played by: Logan Ramsey.
