PERSONNEL: Sakkath

Vulcan male who worked with Ambassador Sarek during the Legaran conference in 2366. Sakkath had to use his mind to help bolster Sarek's during the negotiations as the aging Vulcan ambassador was suffering from the advanced stages of Bendii Syndrome. 

Reference: Sarek 
Portrayed by Rocco Sisto.
