PERSONNEL: Ian Andrew Troi

Human male Starfleet officer who wed Lwaxana Troi and had two daughters, Kestra and Deanna. Troi would read stories to the young Deanna about the Wild West. Ian died when Deanna was seven. 

Reference: The Child 
Portrayed by Amick Byram.
