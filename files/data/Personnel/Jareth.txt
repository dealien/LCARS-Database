PERSONNEL: Jareth

Disciplined officer in the Enaran military, and father of Korenna Mirell. Jareth participated in his government's clandestine efforts to exterminate the Regressives, an operation which Korenna passed on telepathically through B'Elanna Torres. 

Reference: Remember
Portrayed by Bruce Davison.
