PERSONNEL: James Barnaby
Lieutenant, tactical officer, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

Starfleet officer serving aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise-D in 2370 during the encounter with Lore's self-aware Borg. 

Reference: Descent
Played by James Horan who also played Jo'Bril in "Suspicions." The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
