PERSONNEL: Sun Tzu

Chinese author of Earth's first handbook on the strategy, philosophy, and tactics of war. Riker quoted tips from this 4th-century B.C. work to Picard, who was glad it was still being taught at Starfleet Academy. 

Reference: The Last Outpost
