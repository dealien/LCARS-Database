PERSONNEL: Paul Porter

Starfleet Engineer aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701-E in 2373. When the Borg began assimilating the ship, Porter was among those added to the collective. 

Reference: Star Trek: First Contact 
Portrayed by Eric Steinberg.
