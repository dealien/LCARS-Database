PERSONNEL: Sean DePaul
Lieutenant, navigator, U.S.S. Enterprise

Crew member aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise in 2267. DePaul sometimes served as helm officer or navigator. 

Reference: Arena 
Played by Sean Kenney who also played Captain Christopher Pike (in the wheelchair) in "The Menagerie."
