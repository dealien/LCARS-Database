PERSONNEL: Cyrus Redblock

Fictional character from the Dixon Hill detective novels. Redblock, who prided himself on his manners and vocabulary, was nonetheless a ruthless villain. Captain Picard used holodeck re-creations of the Dixon Hill novels as a form of entertainment. 

Reference: The Big Goodbye 
Portrayed by Lawrence Tierney, whom also played the Regent of Palomar in "Business As Usual." The character's name was apparently a play on the name of actor Sydney Greenstreet.
