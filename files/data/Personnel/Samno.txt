PERSONNEL: Samno
Yeoman, U.S.S. Enterprise-A

In 2293, Samno and Yeoman Burke conspired in the murder of Chancellor Gorkon. Samno was later murdered by co-conspirator Valeris. 

Reference: The Undiscovered Country
