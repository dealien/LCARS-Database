PERSONNEL: Luma

An Eymorg, or "giver of pain and delight," of Sigma Draconis VI. Luma, who had the mind of a child, met Kirk's landing party after an elevator took them deep under the planet's surface. 

Reference: Spock's Brain 
Played by Sheila Leighton.
