PERSONNEL: Rivas Jakara

Covert identity established for Commander William Riker in 2367 during a surveillance mission on Malcor III. While in this identity, he was injured during a riot and taken to a hospital, where doctors quickly realized he was not a native of the planet, despite the surgical alterations he had undergone. 

Reference: First Contact 
Played by Jonathan Frakes.
