PERSONNEL: Mark Piper, MD
Chief medical officer, U.S.S. Enterprise

Chief medical officer and head of the life sciences department aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise in 2265. Piper examined Gary Mitchell following the ship's passing through the galactic barrier and was puzzled as to why the crewman would suddenly achieve super powers. 

Reference: Where No Man Has Gone Before 
Played by Paul Fix.
