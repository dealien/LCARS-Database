PERSONNEL: Vargas

Male Starfleet officer assigned to AR-558 during the Dominion War in 2375. Vargas fired on Benjamin Sisko thinking he was a Jem'Hadar. Soon after, a real Jem'Hadar attack took Vargas' life. 

Reference: The Siege of AR-558 
Portrayed by Raymond Cruz.
