PERSONNEL: Will Scarlett

In an alternate-reality adventure suggested by Q, Worf was forced to play the role of swordsman Will Scarlett, one of Robin Hood's band. But not one of his "merry" men, according to Worf. 

Reference: QPid 
Portrayed by Michael Dorn/Worf.
