PERSONNEL: Galt

Master of the drill thralls on planet Triskelion. He was responsible for training newly captured aliens, including crew members from the U.S.S. Enterprise who were captured in 2268. Galt answered only to the rulers of the planet, the Providers.

Reference: The Gamesters of Triskelion
Played by Joseph Ruskin who also played Tumek in "The House of Quark," and "Looking for par'Mach in All the Wrong Places," and the voice of the informant in "Improbable Cause."
