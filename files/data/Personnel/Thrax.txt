PERSONNEL: Thrax
Security Chief, Terok Nor

Cardassian chief of security for Terok Nor's Promenade until 2367. In 2373 Odo, Jadzia Dax, Benjamin Sisko, and Garak experienced a shared telepathic reality in which Thrax played a role. 

Reference: Things Past 
Played by Kurtwood Smith, who also played the Federation President in "The Undiscovered Country," and Annorax in "Year of Hell."
