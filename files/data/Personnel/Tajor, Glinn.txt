PERSONNEL: Tajor
Glinn, executive officer

Cardassian officer serving as Gul Lemec's adjutant in 2369. Tajor accompanied Lemec to the U.S.S. Enterprise-D to meet with Captain Jellico. 

Reference: Chain of Command
Portrayed by Mic Rogers.
