PERSONNEL: Ch'Rega

Female Klingon warrior who spent time aboard the U.S.S. Voyager. At first she pursued Harry Kim, but eventually ended up having a torrid affair with Neelix that resulted in the destruction of Tuvok's quarters. 

Reference: Prophecy
Portrayed by Peggy Jo Jacobs.
