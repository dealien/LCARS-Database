PERSONNEL: Tagana
Nurse, medical officer, Deep Space Nine

Caregiver attending to Jake Sisko in the infirmary after he collapsed in the Replimat. Tagana was attacked by Onaya after she walked through the bulkhead. Tagana's report saved Jake's life. 

Reference: The Muse 
Played by Patricia Tallman, who also played Kiros in "Starship Mine" and appeared as a stunt performer for many other TNG & DS9 episodes and other scifi series.
