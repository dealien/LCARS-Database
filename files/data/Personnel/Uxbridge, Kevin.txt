PERSONNEL: Kevin Uxbridge

Douwd energy being masquerading as a human botanist who was the sole survivor of a colony on Delta Rana IV that was attacked by the Husnock. His revenge was to annihilate the entire Husnock race. Following this, he lived in isolation with an image of his human wife, Rishon, who had died in the Husnock raid. 

Reference: The Survivors 
Portrayed by John Anderson.
