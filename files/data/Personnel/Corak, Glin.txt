PERSONNEL: Corak
Glin, executive assistant

Cardassian officer, aide to Gul Lemec. Corak attended when Lemec held talks aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise-D in 2369 with Captain Jellico. 

Reference: Chain of Command
Layed by Tom Morga who also played a Nausicaan in "Tapestry" and a stunt performer in "The Search for Spock."
