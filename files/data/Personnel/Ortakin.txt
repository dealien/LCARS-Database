PERSONNEL: Ortakin
Warrior, I.K.S. Rotarran

Youthful Klingon warrior serving aboard the Rotarran in 2373. Jadzia Dax used Ortakin's hair to remove him from 'her' mess hall seat, as a way to command respect from the sullen crew. 

Reference: Soldiers of the Empire
