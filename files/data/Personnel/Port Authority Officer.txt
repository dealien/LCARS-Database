PERSONNEL: Port Authority Officer

A Ledosian official, working for Ledos Port Control, the Officer charged Tom Paris with committing Piloting Violation 256 while in his planet's space. The Officer informed Paris that his CO would get word of the transgression. 

Reference: Natural Law
