PERSONNEL: Loquel

A male Iyaaran diplomat who, along with colleagues Voval and Byleth, interacted with U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701-D crewmembers during a first cultural contact mission. Secretly he was studying the concept of pleasure. 

Reference: Liaisons 
Played by Paul Eiding.
