PERSONNEL: Tim Watters
Cadet, Red Squad, acting captain, starship commander, U.S.S. Valiant

Charismatic Starfleet Academy cadet who commanded the U.S.S. Valiant behind enemy lines for eight months. The strain of command, coupled with his youth, eventually killed virtually everyone on board.
