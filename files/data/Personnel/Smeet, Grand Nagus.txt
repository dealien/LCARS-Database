PERSONNEL: Smeet
Grand Nagus, Ferenginar

One of Grand Nagus Zek's predecessors. Smeet's term ended uniquely - he was assassinated after a massive drop in the Ferengi Market Exchange. Smeet and his First Clerk were buried together.
