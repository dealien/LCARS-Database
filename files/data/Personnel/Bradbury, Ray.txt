PERSONNEL: Ray Bradbury

Popular 20th-century Earth-based science fiction writer. Bradbury was versatile and creative, inspiring millions with his fantastical stories. Contemporary writer Herbert Rossoff thought highly of Bradbury's talents. 

Reference: Far Beyond the Stars 
He was an inspiration and friend to Gene Roddenberry, who did not survive to see his associate honored in Star Trek.
