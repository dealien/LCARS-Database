PERSONNEL: Volnoth

Respected gatherer from the Lornak clan on the Alpha Quadrant planet of Acamar III. Volnoth was murdered during peace negotiations on Gamma Hromi in 2366. His murderer, Yuta, was acting in revenge for his clan's massacre of the Tralesta clan 80 years previously. 

Reference: The Vengeance Factor 
Portrayed by Marc Lawrence.
