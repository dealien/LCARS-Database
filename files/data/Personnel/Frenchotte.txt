PERSONNEL: Frenchotte

A self-exiled Romulan composer of soothing, almost ethereal music. Not well-known, but a particular favorite of Jadzia Dax. She once had a runabout computer play a Frenchotte piano piece for her and Arjin. 

Reference: Playing God
