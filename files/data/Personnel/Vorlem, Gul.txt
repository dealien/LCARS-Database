PERSONNEL: Vorlem
Gul, military officer, Cardassia Prime

Cardassian officer who earned the enmity of Enabran Tain, head of Cardassia's feared Obsidian Order. In 2373, Garak dropped hints that he had murdered Vorlem. 

Reference: In Purgatory's Shadow
