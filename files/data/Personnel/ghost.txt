PERSONNEL: ghost

1) Ancient Earth term for a disembodied spirit of a deceased being that walked among the living. Ronin was believed to be the Howard family ghost but it was found that he was a non-corporeal alien.
2) An old Earth slang term for a resident of a Sanctuary District that didn't integrate into the society very well and took out their aggressions by preying on other residents. These people did not want jobs, or help and resented the entire Sanctuary system.

Reference: Sub Rosa, Past Tense
