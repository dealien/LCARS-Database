PERSONNEL: Colti
Admiral

Senior Starfleet officer. In 2373, Admiral Colti attended a ceremony at station Deep Space Nine where Bajor was to be formally admitted to the Federation, until it was stopped by a warning from the Emissary, Captain Sisko.
