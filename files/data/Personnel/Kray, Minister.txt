PERSONNEL: Kray
Minister, scientific leader, Banea

Scientist from a Delta Quadrant world. When crewmembers of the U.S.S. Voyager turned to this high-ranking Banean official, they were promised technical expertise to refurbish their ship's navigational array. 

Reference: Ex Post Facto
