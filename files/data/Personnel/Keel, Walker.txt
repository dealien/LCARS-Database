PERSONNEL: Walker Keel
Commander, starship commander, U.S.S. Horatio

Starfleet commander, and close friend of Jean-Luc Picard and Jack Crusher, who alerted Picard to the possibility that Starfleet had been infiltrated. In 2364, Keel was killed. He was survived by two sisters, Anne and Melissa. 

Reference: Conspiracy 
Played by Jonathan Farwell.
