PERSONNEL: Richardson

Crew member aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise. In 2369, Richardson perished on the surface of Bersallis III in the wake of a deadly firestorm. At the time, Richardson was trying to manually adjust the thermal deflectors against the raging firestorm.
