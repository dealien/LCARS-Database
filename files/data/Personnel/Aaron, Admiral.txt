PERSONNEL: Raymond Aaron 
Admiral, Starfleet Operations

Starfleet officer, stationed at Starfleet Headquarters in San Francisco. In 2364, Admiral Aaron was one of several officers who succumbed to the unknown alien intelligence that infiltrated Starfleet Command. 

Reference: Conspiracy 
Played by Ray Reinhardt in the episode. The first name of the character is conjectural and based on the first name of the actor.
