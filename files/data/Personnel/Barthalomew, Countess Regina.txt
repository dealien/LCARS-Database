PERSONNEL: Regina Barthalomew
Countess, holographic character

Holographic representation of Moriarty's mistress from the Sherlock Holmes stories. In 2369, she and the self-aware hologram Moriarty set off on a trip, albeit holographic, to parts unknown. 

Reference: Ship in a Bottle 
Played by Stephanie Beacham.
