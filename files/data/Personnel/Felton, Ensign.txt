PERSONNEL: Shelia Felton
Ensign, conn officer, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

She was one of the chief relief officers on the bridge.

Reference: A Matter of Time, New Ground, Hero Worship, Masterpiece Society, Imaginary Friend
Played by Shelia Franklin. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
