PERSONNEL: Riska

Resident of Deep Space 9. In 2371, Riska was invited to dinner by her friend Leanne, along with Jake Sisko and Nog. Riska left the meal prematurely after taking offense at Nog's boorish behavior. 

Reference: Life Support 
Played by Eva Loseth.
