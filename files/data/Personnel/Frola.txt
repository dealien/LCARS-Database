PERSONNEL: Frola

A female Voth, the daughter of Professor Forra Gegen. Like most in her society, she had doubts about her father's controversial "Distant Origin Theory" of evolution, and was concerned for his safety. Frola was romantically interested in Tova Veer, her father's assistant.
