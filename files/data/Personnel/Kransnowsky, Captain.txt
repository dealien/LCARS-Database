PERSONNEL: Bart Kransnowsky
Captain, starship commander

Commodore Stone asked Kransnowsky to serve on the four-man panel hearing James T. Kirk's 2267 court-martial. Kransnowsky listened to testimony on both Starbase 11 and the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701. 

Reference: Court Martial 
Played by Bart Conrad. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
