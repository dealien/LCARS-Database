PERSONNEL: Daggin

An Ocampan gardener, Daggin worked at a botanical garden in his people's subterranean complex before the Caretaker's death in 2371. He was a friend of Kes, and he helped her search for Voyager's missing crew members. 

Reference: Caretaker
Portrayed by Eric David Johnson.
