PERSONNEL: Nunk

Ferengi captain of a D'Kora-class ship and instigator of a plot to harvest nanoprobes from Seven. Nunk used girlfriend Leosa to gather information from Reginald Barclay to affect the plan. 

Reference: Inside Man
Portrayed by Michael William Rivkin.
