PERSONNEL: Chandra

A little girl who was part of the Wadi game of Chula. Chandra sang a song and played hopscotch on Wadi symbols built into the floor of the game's maze. A player could only move to the next level by imitating her song and hopscotch steps. 

Reference: Move Along Home 
Played by Clara Bryant.
