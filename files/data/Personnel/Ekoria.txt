PERSONNEL: Ekoria

A pregnant member of the Teplan civilization devastated by a fatal pandemic known as "the blight." Dr. Julian Bashir inoculated her fetus against the disease, although Ekoria died during childbirth. 

Reference: The Quickening 
Played by Ellen Wheeler.
