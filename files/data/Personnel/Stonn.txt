PERSONNEL: Stonn

Spock's rival for T'Pring's hand in marriage. T'Pring selected Kirk as her champion so Stonn could avoid injury during a wedding death match. Stonn later wed T'Pring in 2267. 

Reference: Amok Time 
Played by Lawrence Montaigne, who also played Decius in "Balance of Terror."
