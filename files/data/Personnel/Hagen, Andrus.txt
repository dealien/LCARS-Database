PERSONNEL: Andrus Hagen

Betazoid science advisor and sole surviving crew member aboard the U.S.S. Bratain when the Enterprise-D discovered the ship ensnared in a Tyken's rift. Despite Hagen's cataleptic state, Deanna Troi was able to glean vital information from him. 

Reference: Night Terrors 
Played by John vickery who also Gul Rusot in "The Changing Face of Evil."
