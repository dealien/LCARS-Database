PERSONNEL: Erik Pressman
Vice Admiral, Starfleet Intelligence, Starfleet Headquarters

Associated with Starfleet Intelligence, Pressman was commander of the U.S.S. Pegasus, a controversial ship that was discovered to have been testing a cloaking device in 2358. Pressman's controversial testing of cloaking technology while in command of the Pegasus resulted in a mutinous crew. One crew member at the time, Ensign William Riker, supported his captain. 
Pressman was eventually arrested for his covert actions in 2370 while trying to salvage the wreck of the Pegasus. 

Reference: The Pegasus 
Portrayed by Terry O'Quinn.
