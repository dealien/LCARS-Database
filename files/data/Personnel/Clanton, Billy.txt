PERSONNEL: Billy Clanton

Ancient American outlaw who was killed at the legendary OK Corral showdown of 1881. Montgomery Scott depicted Billy Clanton in the Melkotian re-creation of the gunfight in 2268. 

Reference: Spectre of the Gun 
Played by James Doohan.
