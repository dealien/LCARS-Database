PERSONNEL: Gettor
Minister, Bajor

Member of the Bajoran Chamber of Ministers on Bajor. In 2372, Minister Gettor visited Deep Space 9 to seek the Emissary's support for his land reform program - a meeting which lasted a frustrating three hours for Sisko. 

Reference: Shattered Mirror
