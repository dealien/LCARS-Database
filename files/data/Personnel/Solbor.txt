PERSONNEL: Solbor
Executive assistant, Bajor

Elderly Bajoran man serving as Kai Winn's assistant in 2375. When Solbor learned about Winn's complicity with the Pah-Wraiths, she stabbed him. Solbor's blood then revealed the secrets of the Kosst Amojan. 

Reference: 'Til Death Do Us Part
