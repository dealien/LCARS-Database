PERSONNEL: Kaybock
Commander, starship commander, I.K.S. M'Char

He was assigned to examine ships outbound from Bajor for stowaway shape-shifters. After Benjamin Sisko convinced Kaybok to not search the Xhosa, General Martok executed him for failing in his duty.
