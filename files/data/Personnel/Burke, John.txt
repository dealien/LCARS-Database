PERSONNEL: John Burke
Chief Astronomer, Earth

21st-century Earth astronomer, head of the Royal Academy in England. Burke was the first to map the area of space around Sherman's Planet, a region which 200 years later would be the subject of a territorial dispute between the United Federation of Planets and the Klingon Empire.
