PERSONNEL: Britt

Personal assistant to 21st-century entrepreneur Chris Brynner on Earth in 2024. In an effort to help Jadzia Dax find her "friends" (Sisko and Bashir), Britt checked all the hospitals and trauma wards in San Francisco to see if anyone matching their descriptions had been admitted.
