PERSONNEL: Aluura
Dabo operator, Deep Space Nine

Quark threatened her with losing her job if she didn't perform oo-mox. She sought assistance from Deep Space 9 station commander Captain Benjamin Sisko who reassured her that contracts like the one between her and Quark requiring sexual work were not enforceable within Federation jurisdiction and she would not have to submit.
