PERSONNEL: Lars
Drill thrall trainer, Triskelion

A male drill thrall on Triskelion. Lars was assigned to mate with Uhura and also prepare her for combat. Lars was accidentally killed by an Andorian teammate in 2268. 

Reference: The Gamesters of Triskelion 
Played by Steve Sandor.
