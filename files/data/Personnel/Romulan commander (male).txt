PERSONNEL: Romulan commander
Commander, starship commander

Romulan whose bird-of-prey crossed the Neutral Zone in 2266, touching off a confrontation with the U.S.S. Enterprise. Acting under orders, the Romulan commander's incursion was meant to test the Federation's defenses and a new cloaking device. The commander and his crew were destroyed in the course of their mission. 

Reference: Balance of Terror 
Played by Mark Lenard, who also played Sarek in "Journey to Babel," the movies, and Next Generation, and played the Klingon commander of the I.K.S. Amar in "The Motion Picture." This role was his first appearance on Star Trek.
