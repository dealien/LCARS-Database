PERSONNEL: Fallit Kot

A former business associate of Quark's who aided him in a failed scheme to capture a transport of Romulan ale. Quark gave up Kot to Federation authorities to avoid serving time, leading Kot to attempt to kill Quark in revenge following his prison term. 

Reference: Melora 
Played by Peter Crombie.
