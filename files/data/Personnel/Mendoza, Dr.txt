PERSONNEL: Castulo Mendoza
PhD, diplomatic officer, Starfleet 

Negotiator for the Federation in 2366 who almost died of pyrocyte poisoning, thanks to DaiMon Goss, during the Barzan wormhole trade conference. Riker took Mendoza's spot at the table. 

Reference: The Price 
Portrayed by Castulo Guerra.
