PERSONNEL: Farallon
PhD, scientist, Tyrus VIIA

Brilliant scientist who began her breakthrough work in particle fountain mining technology in 2364 on Tyrus VIIA. In addition, exocomp servomechanisms she designed for the project gain sentient status in 2369. 

Reference: The Quality of Life 
Played by Ellen Bry.
