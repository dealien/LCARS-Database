PERSONNEL: Caithlin Dar
Ambassador, Romulan representative, Nimbus III

Romulan diplomat assigned to the Paradise City settlement on Nimbus III in 2287. A youthful idealist, Dar believed that Paradise City could still serve as a catalyst for galactic peace, despite the failure of the project for two decades. She later left her post to join Sybok in his quest for Sha Ka Ree, under the Vulcan's mental manipulation. 

Reference: The Final Frontier 
Played by Cynthia Gouw.
