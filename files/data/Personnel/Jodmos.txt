PERSONNEL: Jodmos

Klingon identity used by Benjamin Sisko when he led a clandestine assignment to penetrate Klingon military headquarters on Ty'Gokor. Jodmos was a son of Kobor. 

Reference: Apocalypse Rising 
Played by Avery Brooks.
