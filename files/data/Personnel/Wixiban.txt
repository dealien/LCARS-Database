PERSONNEL: Wixiban

Talaxian criminal detained at the Nekrit Expanse Supply Depot for smuggling. Wixiban coerced Neelix's participation in illegal schemes at the Depot. Neelix helped Wixiban earn his freedom by stopping Depot smugglers.
