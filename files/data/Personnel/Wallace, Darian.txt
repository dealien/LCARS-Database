PERSONNEL: Darian Wallace
Lieutenant, engineering and security officer, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

Multifaceted male member of the U.S.S. Enterprise-D crew. Wallace helped build the Enterprise then worked on the Bridge, in Engineering, and with Security. He survived the ship's destruction in 2371. Crewmates considered him a regular guy. 

Reference: The Big Goodbye, Eye of the Beholder, Descent, All Good Things...
Portrayed by Guy Vardaman, who was a regular "stand-in" throught TNG- even in instances for Data's hands as in "Datalore." He also played background Klingons and Romulans.
