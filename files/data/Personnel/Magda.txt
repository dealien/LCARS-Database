PERSONNEL: Magda Kovacs

Plain-looking woman desperate to marry in 2266. She and two others fell under the spell of Harcourt Fenton Mudd and the Venus Drug. Magda wed a miner on Rigel XII. 

Reference: Mudd's Women 
Played by Susan Denberg. The character's last name was not mentioned in the aired version, but a final draft script listed her name as Magda Kovacs.
