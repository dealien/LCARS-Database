PERSONNEL: Keel

Native of the class-M planet Capella IV sought by both Klingon and Federation forces. Keel killed Kras, a Klingon, after being ordered to do so by the Teer, Maab. 

Reference: Friday's Child 
Played by Cal Bolder.
