PERSONNEL: M`Benga
Doctor, U.S.S. Enterprise

A doctor who served aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701 for two years. His background included an internship in a Vulcan ward. M'Benga helped save Spock's life in 2267. Previously he served on Starbase Vanguard as one of the staff physicians, however the CMO Ezekiel Fisher was grooming him to take over. M'Benga made clear intentions that he wanted to serve aboard a starship even though.

Reference: A Private Little War, That Which Survives, Harbinger (Vanguard)
Played by Brooker Bradshaw.
