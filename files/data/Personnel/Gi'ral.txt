PERSONNEL: Gi'ral

Klingon warrior believed killed at a perimeter outpost in the Khitomer massacre of 2346. Gi'ral was held prisoner at the secret Romulan prison camp in the Carraya System. Thre, she married Romulan prison camp commander Tokath, and they had a daughter, Ba'el. Although Gi'ral accepted her lot as a prisoner, she later supported the wishes of some of the children in the camp to go free.

Reference: Birthright
Played by Christine Rose.
