PERSONNEL: Rayna Kapec

Beautiful female android fashioned by Flint on Holberg 917-G. Rayna's feelings were dormant until she met Captain Kirk in 2269. Emotional stress took her life shortly thereafter. 

Reference: Requiem for Methuselah 
Played by Louise Sorel. Rayna was named for Czechoslovakian writer Karel Capek, who first coined the term "robot" in the classic science fiction play entitled "R.U.R."
