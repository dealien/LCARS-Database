PERSONNEL: Porta
Vedek, religious figure, Deep Space Nine

Kindly old monk on DS9. In 2372, Porta enforced Akorem Laan's edict restoring the Bajoran D'jarra system by murdering a vedek who refused to return to the profession of his ancestors. 

Reference: Accession 
Played by Robert Symonds.
