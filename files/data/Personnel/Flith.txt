PERSONNEL: Flith

Humanoid male, native to Farius Prime, who worked as an Orion Syndicate henchman under Liam Bilby. Flith was executed by Bilby's boss, Raimus, for failing to pay his full fare. 

Reference: Honor Among Thieves 
Portrayed by John Chandler.
