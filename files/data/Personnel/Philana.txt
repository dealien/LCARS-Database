PERSONNEL: Philana

Wife of Parmen, leader of the planet Platonius. It was Philana who sent a request for medical aid that drew the U.S.S. Enterprise to the planet in 2268. Philana was 2,300 years old at the time. 

Reference: Plato's Stepchildren 
Played by Barbara Babcock, who also played Mea 3 in "A Taste of Armageddon," the voice of Isis in "Assignment: Earth," the mother to Trelane in "The Squire of Gothos," and Commander Loskene in "The Tholian Web."
