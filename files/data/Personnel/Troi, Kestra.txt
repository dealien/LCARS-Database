PERSONNEL: Kestra Troi

Ian Andrew and Lwaxana Troi's first daughter. Kestra's drowning death at age seven was more than Lwaxana could bear, so she blocked the child from her memory for 30 years. 

Reference: Dark Page 
Portrayed by Andreana Weiner, and Kirsten Dunst.
