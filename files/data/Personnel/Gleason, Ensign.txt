PERSONNEL: Todd Gleason
Ensign, operations officer, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

Enterprise-D officer assigned to battle bridge ops during the rescue of Captain Picard from the Borg ship in early 2367. 

Reference: The Best of Both Worlds
Played by Todd Merrill. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
