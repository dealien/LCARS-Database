PERSONNEL: Tepo
Sect leader, Sigma Iotia II

The third-ranking boss on Sigma Iotia II in 2268. Tepo was transported away from his office to a meeting where James Kirk announced that the Federation was taking over. 

Reference: A Piece of the Action 
Played by John Harmon. The Continuum featured an image of Krako Jojo with this entry by mistake.
