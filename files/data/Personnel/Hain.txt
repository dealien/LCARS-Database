PERSONNEL: Haim

One of five thieves behind the Lissepian Mother's Day Heist. Posing as a member of Lurian Security, Hain wanted Quark to divulge Morn's stash of gold-pressed latinum from the robbery, only to end up arrested with his accomplices, Krit and Nahsk, Larell and Morn. 

Reference: Who Mourns for Morn? 
Portrayed by Gregory Itzin whom also played Ilon Tandro in "Dax" Dysek in "Critical Care" Admiral Black in "In a Mirror Darkly," and Sopek in "Shadows of P'Jem." This actor's name has also been mispelled as Itzen on The Continuum by mistake.
