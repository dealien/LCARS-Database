PERSONNEL: Andrei Novakovich
Astronaut, Ares IV

Ares IV astronaut in 2032. Novakovich was drilling through a Mars lava plain when the orbiting command module disappeared. He, and partner Rose Kumagawa, were rescued several weeks later.
