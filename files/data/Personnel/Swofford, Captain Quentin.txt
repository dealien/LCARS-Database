PERSONNEL: Quentin Swofford
Captain, starship commander, U.S.S. Cortez

Commander of the U.S.S. Cortez who gave his life retaking Deep Space 9 in 2374. Swofford had been a friend of Benjamin Sisko, who introduced Swofford to his future wife.
