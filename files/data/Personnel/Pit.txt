PERSONNEL: Pit

Dominant bully in the Akritirian prison ship where Tom Paris and Harry Kim were being held in 2373. When Kim first arrived, Pit tried to claim Harry, but Paris intervened. 

Reference: The Chute
Portrayed by Ed Trotta.
