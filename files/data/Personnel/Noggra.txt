PERSONNEL: Noggra

Klingon and Mogh's comrade. In 2372, at Worf's request, Noggra added Kurn to his family. Noggra gave Kurn a new name, Rodek, and helped him build a new life. 

Reference: Sons of Mogh 
Portrayed by Robert DoQui. The Continuum website did not use the capital "Q" when presenting the actor's name, apparently by mistake.
