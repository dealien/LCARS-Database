PERSONNEL: Selin Peers
Minister, medical specialist, Trill

Trill host whose symbiont had experienced seven adult lifetimes. The Trillian government sent Peers to Jadzia Dax's extradition hearing on DS9 in 2369. He provided expert testimony about Trills. 

Reference: Dax 
Played by Richard Lineback who also played Romas in "Symbiosis."
