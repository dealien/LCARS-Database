PERSONNEL: Kelly

Crewman serving on the Enterprise NX-01. It was Kelly who first alerted Captain Archer to the strange, symbiotic organism in Cargo Bay Two. Kelly was eventually captured by the alien, along with Archer, Commander Tucker and Crewman Rostov. Luckily, all of the crewmembers were later set free.
