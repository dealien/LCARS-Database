PERSONNEL: Torat

A highly-regarded Delta Quadrant astrophysicist, Torat is responsible for discovering 60 astronomical phenomena. Torat gave Janeway information about an intermittent cyclic vortex in Devore space for mercurium isochromate to power his ship. 

Reference: Counterpoint
