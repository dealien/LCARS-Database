PERSONNEL: Karya

Photometric female, Karya was a Vori child and central character in a Vori war simulation. Vori soldier trainees learned to hate by witnessing Kradin troops slaughter Karya, her grandfather, Penno, and unarmed villagers. 

Reference: Nemesis
Portrayed by Meghan Murphy.
