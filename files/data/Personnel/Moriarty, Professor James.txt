PERSONNEL: James Moriarty
Professor, fictional character

Sherlock Holmes's arch nemesis. He was created as a hologram, in 2365, to best Data, but seized the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701-D. In 2369 the Moriarty hologram was classified sentient and given a computer-generated reality.
