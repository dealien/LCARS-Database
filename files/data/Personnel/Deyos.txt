PERSONNEL: Deyos

Vorta in charge of Dominion Internment Camp 371 in the Gamma Quadrant when Julian Bashir, Worf, Elim Garak, Enabran Tain and General Martok were imprisoned there in 2373. 

Reference: By Inferno's Light 
Played by Ray Buktenica.
