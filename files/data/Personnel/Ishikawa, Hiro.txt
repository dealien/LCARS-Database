PERSONNEL: Hiro Ishikawa

Father of Keiko O'Brien, Miles O'Brien's wife. When pregnant with the couple's first child, Keiko wanted to name the child after her father while Miles himself wanted to name the child Michael. The child turned out to be a girl and was named Molly.
