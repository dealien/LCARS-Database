PERSONNEL: Beauregard

Nickname given by Yeoman Janice Rand to an unusual plant in the botany bay of the U.S.S. Enterprise. Ship's botanist, Lieutenant Hikaru Sulu insisted that the plant's name was Gertrude, but since the plant to a strong liking to Rand, it probably liked her name better. 

Reference: The Man Trap
The puppet for Beauregard was operated by Bob Baker. The plant may have been named for make-up artist Fred Beauregard Phillips.
