PERSONNEL: Alsia

Con artist last reported on Deep Space 9. In 2370, Alsia persuaded Martus Mazur to give her money, 10,000 isiks, to help finance a bogus mining operation on the Vlugta asteroid belt. 

Reference: Rivals 
Played by K. Callan.
