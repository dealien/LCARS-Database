PERSONNEL: Susanna Leijten
Lieutenant Commander, operations officer, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

With Geordi La Forge and three other crewmembers serving on the U.S.S. Victory in 2362, Leijten was exposed to an alien retro-DNA on Tarchannen III. In 2367 the DNA nearly overtook Leijten. 

Reference: Identity Crisis 
Played by Maryann Plunkett.
