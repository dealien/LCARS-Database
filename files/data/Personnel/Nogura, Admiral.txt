PERSONNEL: Nogura
Admiral, operations officer, Starfleet Headquarters

In 2271 this Starfleet Admiral removed Captain Decker from command of the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701 in favor of James Kirk. Nogura gave Kirk his old ship to investigate V'Ger.
