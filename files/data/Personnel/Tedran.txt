PERSONNEL: Tedran

Organizer of a paranoid Kyrian group threatened by U.S.S. Voyager's peaceful meeting with the Vaskan ambassador. Tedran's actions were later thought heroic until the EMH re-wrote Kyrian history. 

Reference: Living Witness
Portrayed by Brian Fitzpatrick.
