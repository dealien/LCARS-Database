PERSONNEL: Lucille Davenport

A holo-character played by Voyager's Captain Janeway. Mrs. Davenport was governess to the children of Lord Burleigh, Henry and Beatrice, in a scenario modelled on the gothic novels of 19th-century England. 

Reference: Cathexis
Portrayed by Kate Mulgrew.
