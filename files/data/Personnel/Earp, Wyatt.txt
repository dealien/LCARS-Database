PERSONNEL: Wyatt Earp

American gambler and lawman, noted for participation in legendary Gunfight at the O.K. Corral in Tombstone, Arizona, 1881. A Melkotian charade in 2268 involved a metamorphosed Earp and brothers attempting to slay Kirk and his senior crew, who were transformed into Earp's foes, the Clantons. 

Reference: Spectre of the Gun 
Played by Ron Soble.
