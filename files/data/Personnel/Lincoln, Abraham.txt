PERSONNEL: Abraham Lincoln
President, state leader, United States, Earth

Human male who was a leader on ancient Earth and a personal hero to James Kirk. In 2269 an Excalbian adopted the form of Abraham Lincoln to represent the concept of good, based on Kirk's thoughts. 

Reference: The Savage Curtain 
Played by Lee Bergere.
