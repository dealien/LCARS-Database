PERSONNEL: Isis

Gary Seven's assistant, a black cat from another planet, who spoke in a language only understood by Seven. If very nervous, Isis would meow. Isis could also appear as a human female. 

Reference: Assignment: Earth 
Played by the voice of Barbara Babcock. This character was intended as a continuing role in the proposed series "Assignment: Earth."
