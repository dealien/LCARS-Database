PERSONNEL: Hester Dealt
Lieutenant Commander, medical trustee, 'audet IX

Medical trustee of the Federation Medical Collection Station on planet 'audet IX. In 2365, Lieutenant Commander Dealt supervised the transport of plasma plague specimens aboard the Enterprise-D to science station Tango Sierra to help combat the plague outbreak in the Rachelis system. 

Reference: The Child 
Played by Seymour Cassel.
