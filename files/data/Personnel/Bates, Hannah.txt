PERSONNEL: Hannah Bates
scientist, Genome, Moab IV

Genetically engineered scientist from the Genome Colony on Moab IV. In 2368, Bates worked with Geordi La Forge in an effort to stop an oncoming stellar core fragment that threatened her homeworld. She later requested, and was granted, asylum aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise-D. 

Reference: The Masterpiece Society 
Played by Dey Young, who also played Arissa in "A Simple Investigation," and Keyla in "Two Days and Two Nights."
