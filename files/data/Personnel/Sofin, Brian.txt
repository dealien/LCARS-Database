PERSONNEL: Brian Sofin
crewman, U.S.S. Equinox

Member of the U.S.S. Equinox crew under Captain Ransom. Sofin was stripped of rank and allowed to join the U.S.S. Voyager after the Equinox was destroyed in 2376.
