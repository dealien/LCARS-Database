PERSONNEL: Drea

Advance agent for the Kelvan Empire of the Andromeda Galaxy. Drea served on a squad that apprehended Enterprise crew members in 2268 as part of Kelvan invasion strategy. 

Reference: By Any Other Name 
Played by Lezlie Dalton.
