PERSONNEL: The Master of Tarquin Hill

Artist who lived 12,000 years ago on the planet Kurl. Exquisite objects discovered on Tarquin Hill were credited to "The Master of Tarquin Hill" because the artist's name was unknown. 

Reference: The Chase
