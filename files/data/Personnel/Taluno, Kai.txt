PERSONNEL: Taluno
Kai, religious leader, Bajor

Bajoran religious figure from the 22nd century whose ship was almost swallowed by the heavens near the Denorios Belt. Taluno's story, plus other sightings, helped locate the Bajoran wormwhole in 2369. 

Reference: Emissary
