PERSONNEL: Telna

Preacher in Deep Space 9's Bajoran temple. Kira Nerys liked Telna's sermon on forgiveness. Benjamin Sisko requested Telna to conduct his wedding to Kasidy Yates, but Kai Winn usurped the job.
