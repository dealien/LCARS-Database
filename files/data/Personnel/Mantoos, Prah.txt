PERSONNEL: Prah Mantoose

Leader of the pilgrims who were on their way to view the stellar phenomenon they call "The Great Plume of Agosoria." The pilgrims spent some time onboard the Enterprise NX-01 in 2151, and were treated to a tour from Trip Tucker. 

Reference: Cold Front
Portrayed by Joseph Hindy.
