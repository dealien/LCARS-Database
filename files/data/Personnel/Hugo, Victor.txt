PERSONNEL: Victor Marie Hugo

A 19th century poet, dramatist, and novelist from the nation of France, Earth. He wrote such famous novels as "The Hunchback of Notre Dame" and "Les Miserables." 
The Maquis leader, Michael Eddington was an admirer of Hugo's works.

TIMELINE:
1802: Born
1831: Wrote "The Hunchback of Notre Dame"
1862: Wrote "Les Miserable"
1885: Died
