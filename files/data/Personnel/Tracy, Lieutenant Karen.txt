PERSONNEL: Karen Tracy
Lieutenant, medical officer, U.S.S. Enterprise

Tracy was taking psychotricorder readings when she was stabbed to death by Redjac in Prefect Jarvis' home on Argelius II. 

Reference: Wolf in the Fold
