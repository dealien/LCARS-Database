PERSONNEL: Eric Sanders
Captain, starship commander, U.S.S. Malinche

Starfleet officer in command of the U.S.S. Malinche. In 2373, Sanders' ship was disabled by a Maquis device. 

Reference: For the Uniform 
Played by Eric Pierpoint, who also played Vovall in "Liaisons." The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
