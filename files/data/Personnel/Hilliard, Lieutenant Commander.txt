PERSONNEL: Hilliard
Lieutenant Commander, Starfleet Medical

Starfleet Medical supervising officer familiar with the Changeling disease who blocked Bashir's attempts to research Odo's medical records. Hilliard's refusal was based on the doctor's lack of Sigma IX clearance. 

Reference: When It Rains...
