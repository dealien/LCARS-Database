PERSONNEL: Weld Ram
PhD, Bajor

Bajoran scientist and associate of Dr. Mora Pel who, in 2370, discovered a silicate life form on the Gamma Quadrant planet L-S VI. During his investigations, Weld was overcome by unstable gas and rendered unconscious for several days. 

Reference: The Alternate 
Played by Matt McKenzie whom also played the port authority officer in "Natural Law." The character's name was not mentioned in the episode but appeared in the script.
