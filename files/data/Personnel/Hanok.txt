PERSONNEL: Hanok

A Gamma Quadrant mercantilist and Minister of the Karemma Commerce Ministry whose guilelessness was exploited shamelessly by Quark. New terms were negotiated after Hanok realized he was being cheated. 

Reference: Starship Down 
Played by James Cromwell, who also played Shrek and Zefram Cochrane in "First Contact."
