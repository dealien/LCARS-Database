PERSONNEL: O`Neill, Tip 
Nickname for 20th Century politician Thomas Phillip O'Neill, Jr. (1912-1994). In 2365 Data took comedy lessons from a holodeck Comic, who insisted, "Tip O'Neill in a dress" is funny. 
Reference: The Outrageous Okona
