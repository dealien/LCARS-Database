PERSONNEL: Altovar

Lethean criminal who was caught stealing bio-mimetic gel from the Deep Space 9 infirmary in 2371. Dr. Julian Bashir was attacked by Altovar during the theft and caused a telepathically induced coma.

Reference: Distant Voices 
Played by Victor Rivers.
