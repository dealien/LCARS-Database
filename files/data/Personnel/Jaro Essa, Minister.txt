PERSONNEL: Jaro Essa
Minister, political leader, Bajor

Conservative Bajoran minister and clandestine leader of the militant Alliance for Global Unity. Jaro used the Cardassian-backed Alliance in his effort to unseat the Bajoran provincial government and install himself as the new leader. 

Reference: The Homecoming 
Played by Frank Langella.
