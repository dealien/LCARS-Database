PERSONNEL: Sabrina Giusti
Ensign, operations officer, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

Starfleet officer stationed at ops aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise-D during the ship's encounter with Arctus Baran early in 2370. She regularly worked at the ops console.

Reference: Gambit
Played by Sabrina LeBeauf. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name. The character's name was not mentioned in the episode but was featured in the end credits.
