PERSONNEL: Byron Komack
Admiral, Starfleet Command

In 2267, this Starfleet officer was one of the people to whom James Kirk reported. He made several reports to the Admiral regarding the spores of Omicron Ceti III. Kirk disobeyed Komack by taking an ailing Spock home instead of reporting to Altair VI. 

Reference: This Side of Paradise, Amok Time
Played by Byron Morrow. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name. The character was named for actor-director James Komack, who later directed "A Piece of the Action."
