PERSONNEL: Fujisaki
Vice Admiral, deputy chief, Starfleet Intelligence (Section 31)

He died in 2374, apparently of food poisoning. Agent Sloan, Fujisaki's protege, believed Fujisaki murdered by the Tal'Shiar. The Tal'Shiar denied involvement.
