PERSONNEL: Norva

Resident of a blight-afflicted city in the Teplan system. In the final or 'quickened' stage of the terminal disease when she was found by Bashir, Norva was taken to Trevean, the city's ritual euthanatizer. 

Reference: The Quickening 
Played by Heidi Margolis.
