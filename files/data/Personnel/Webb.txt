PERSONNEL: Richard Webb

Radar monitor technician at a 20th-century United States Air Force installation. Webb spotted the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701 orbiting Earth in July, 1969, and scrambled Captain John Christopher to investigate. 

Reference: Tomorrow Is Yesterday 
Played by Richard Merrifield. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
