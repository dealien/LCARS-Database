PERSONNEL: Hrothgar
King, fictional character

Character from Earth's Norse mythology. In the holo-novel "Beowulf," Hrothgar, aged father of Freya, and his warriors are besieged by the savage monster Grendel when they are visited by a Dr. Schweitzer.
