PERSONNEL: Grimes
Crewman, conn officer, U.S.S. Voyager

Member of the U.S.S. Voyager crew, qualified to pilot the ship. Chakotay assigned Grimes to take Paris' place at the conn in 2372, when Tom was once again late for his shift.
