PERSONNEL: Gyuir
Captain, fleet commander, Pandrillite colony ship

This captain was the mission commander of a fleet of ships traveling to a new world in a new galaxy with the intent to start a new colony for his people. He was also a patron of the "Captain's Table" bar, and his first trip there he met Julius Ramsey, from Starfleet; Rake d'Aleseo, an Andorian Captain of the Guard, and Matthew Morden, from Starfleet.

Reference: Ramsey's Last Stand, Coming Attractions
