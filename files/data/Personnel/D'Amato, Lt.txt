PERSONNEL: Arthur D`Amato
Lieutenant, senior geologist, U.S.S. Enterprise

Senior geologist aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise who died in 2268 during a mission to the mysterious Kalandan outpost. When D'Amato was on the surface of the planet, a beautiful woman named Losira touched him after calling his name, and killed him by cellular disruption. 

Reference: That Which Survives 
Played by Arthur Batanides. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
