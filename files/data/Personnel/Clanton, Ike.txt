PERSONNEL: Ike Clanton

Leader of the outlaw Clanton gang in 19th-century America, who fought the Earps at the OK Corral. In 2268 the Melkotians forced Captain James T. Kirk to portray Ike Clanton in an elaborate scheme to execute him. 

Reference: Spectre of the Gun 
Played by William Shatner.
