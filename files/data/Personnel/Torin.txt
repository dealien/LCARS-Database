PERSONNEL: Torin

Klingon monk living on Boreth to whom Worf confided seeing Kahless in the caves of No'Mat. Torin, with Koroth, included Worf's vision in the memories instilled in their Kahless clone. 

Reference: Rightful Heir 
Portrayed by Norman Snow.
