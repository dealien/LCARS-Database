PERSONNEL: Rain Robinson

Young woman from a revised 1996 Earth timeline. Robinson, who had received a grant from entrepreneur Henry Starling, spent most of her time searching for extraterrestrials. During this period, Rain met two members of the U.S.S. Voyager crew from the future, Tuvok and Paris. Together, they resisted Starling's quest for technology. 

Reference: Future's End
Portrayed by Sarah Silverman.
