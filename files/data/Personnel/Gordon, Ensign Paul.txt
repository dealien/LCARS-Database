PERSONNEL: Paul Gordon
Ensign, security officer, Starbase 375

Starfleet security officer who served under Captain Sisko in 2374. Gordon was killed while serving on the crew of a commandeered Jem'Hadar warship that crash-landed on a planet in Cardassian space.
