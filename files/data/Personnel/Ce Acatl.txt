PERSONNEL: Ce Acatl

A direct ancestor of Chakotay who was a great leader of his people despite being born of the rape of his mother by a white conqueror. In a vision, Chakotay's father, Kolopak, invoked the story of Ce Acatl in trying to convince Chakotay not to reject an offspring of his genes despite being conceived without his consent.
