PERSONNEL: Ny Terla

Native of a Delta Quadrant planet nearly destroyed by Janeway in 2371. Ny Terla criticized using a planet-wide polaric ion energy grid because it left his world vulnerable to catastrophe. 

Reference: Time and Again
Portrayed by Joel Polis.
