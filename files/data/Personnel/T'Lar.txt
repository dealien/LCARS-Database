PERSONNEL: T`Lar

Highly-respected Vulcan priestess who agreed to attempt the rare and dangerous fal-tor-pan in 2285. T'Lar relieved Leonard McCoy of Spock's katra and, after many hours, successfully rejoined Spock's body and spirit. 

Reference: The Search for Spock 
Played by Dame Judith Anderson.
