PERSONNEL: April Anaya
Ensign, conn officer, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

A relief officer serving aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise-D at the conn station. She was at the helm when the starship encountered the Cytherian probe in 2367.

Reference: The Nth Degree
Played by Page Leong.
