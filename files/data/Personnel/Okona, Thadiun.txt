PERSONNEL: Thadiun Okona
Captain, transport commander, Erstwhile

Charmer, rogue, entrepreneur and captain of small cargo ship. In 2365 Okona became a central figure in a bitter exchange that threatened the Coalition of Madena. Okona was exonerated. 

Reference: The Outrageous Okona 
Portrayed by William O. Campbell, a.k.a. Billy Campbell.
