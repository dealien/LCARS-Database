PERSONNEL: Cassandra Nickson

Resident of a colony that settled on planet Orellius in 2360 and rejected modern technology. When the colony was discovered by Benjamin Sisko and Miles O'Brien, its leader, Alixus, sent Cassandra to seduce Sisko in the hopes that he would want to live there. Failing that, she was offered a chance to leave the planet, but Cassandra opted to remain with the colony, being a believer in Alixus' anti-technology teachings. 

Reference: Paradise 
Played by Julia Nickson who also played Ensign Lian T'su in "The Arsenal of Freedom." The character's last name is conjectural based on the actor's real last name.
