PERSONNEL: Maurice Picard

Father of Jean-Luc and Robert Picard, Maurice was a vinticulturist from Labarre, France on Earth. Maurice abhorred technology and was upset that Jean-Luc chose a Starfleet career over the more domestic life tending the family vineyards. 

Reference: Conundrum, Chain of Command, Family, Tapestry
Portrayed by Clive Church. The character's first name was first seen on a computer readout in "Conundrum," and selected by writer/producer Ron Moore.
