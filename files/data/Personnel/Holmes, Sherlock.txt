PERSONNEL: Sherlock Holmes

London's greatest consulting detective, a fictional character created in 1887 by Earth novelist Sir Arthur Conan Doyle. U.S.S. Enterprise-D operations officer Data was an afficionado of Holmes' adventures, having read and memorized all of Doyle's Holmes stories after Riker suggested Holmes' approach might help solve the murder of an Antican delegate. Data enjoyed Sherlock simulations on the holodeck.

Reference: Elementary, Dear Data, Ship in a Bottle, Lonely Among Us
Played by Brent Spiner.
