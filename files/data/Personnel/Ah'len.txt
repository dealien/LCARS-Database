PERSONNEL: Ah'len
engineer
 
Trip Tucker had a brief flirtation with this Xyrillian engineer while he was fixing her ship's propulsion system. Their encounter led to a surprise interspecies pregnancy, the first documented occurence of a human male becoming pregnant.
