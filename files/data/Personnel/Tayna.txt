PERSONNEL: Tayna

Female engineer assisting Dr. Nel Apgar. After Apgar reported fighting with William Riker, Tayna took Apgar's wife off the orbiting laboratory. During Riker's murder trial, Tayna's heresy testimony was damning. 

Reference: A Matter of Perspective 
Portrayed by Juli Donald.
