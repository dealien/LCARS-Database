PERSONNEL: Skoran

Blacksmith on Barkon IV. Skoran purchased radioactive stones from Data for jewelry. Neither Skoran nor the damaged Data understood radioactivity so when the townspeople became sick, Skoran held Data responsible. 

Reference: Thine Own Self 
Portrayed by Michael G Hagerty.
