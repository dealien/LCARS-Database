PERSONNEL: Abaddon
 
Proprietor of the Repository of Lost Treasures, a Delta Quadrant junkyard. Abaddon sold the ship known as 'Alice' to Tom Paris, after having been tormented by the ship's neurogenic interface to find her a suitable pilot. 

Reference: Alice
Played by John Fleck whom also played Ornithar in "The Search" and Taibak in "The Mind's Eye."
