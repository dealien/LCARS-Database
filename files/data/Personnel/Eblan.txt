PERSONNEL: Eblan

Bajoran man who noticed the Cardassian collaborator Kubus Oak attempting to slip surreptitiously through the Deep Space 9 Promenade in 2370. 

Reference: The Collaborator 
Played by Charles Parks. Eblam's name was not mentione din dialog, but did appear in the script.
