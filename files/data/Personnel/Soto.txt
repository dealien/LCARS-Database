PERSONNEL: Soto

Opportunistic Lethean who used telepathic powers to discover details about the Shroud of the Sword from Kor's mind. Soto and partner Toral trailed Kor, hoping he'd find the Sword of Kahless. 

Reference: The Sword of Kahless 
Played by Tom Morga, whom also played a Nausicaan in "Tapestry."
