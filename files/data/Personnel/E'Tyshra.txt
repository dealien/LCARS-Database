PERSONNEL: E`Tyshra
Ambassador, T'Lani III

T'Lani government envoy. Together with her Kellerun counterpart, E'Tyshra conspired to murder Dr. Bashir and Chief O'Brien, among others, to eliminate all knowledge of Harvester nanogenic weapons technology. 

Reference: Armageddon Game 
Played by Darleen Carr.
