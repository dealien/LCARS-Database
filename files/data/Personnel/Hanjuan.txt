PERSONNEL: Hanjuan

A belligerent Nezu colonist and gallicite miner, Hanjuan took a swing at Tuvok because of his bossy attitude. The Vulcan refused to fight and then saved Hanjuan, and other colonists, from oncoming asteroids. 

Reference: Rise
Portrayed by Geof Prysirr.
