PERSONNEL: Mona Luvsitt

A holosuite character in Julian Bashir's secret agent program. Mona was Bashir's overqualified valet. Mona could speak seven languages, pilot anything in the air, and had advanced degrees in several sciences. 

Reference: Our Man Bashir 
Played by Marci Brickhouse.
