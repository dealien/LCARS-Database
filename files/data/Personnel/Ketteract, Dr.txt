PERSONNEL: Dr. Ketteract
Ph.D., scientist, Lantaru Sector Research Station

Leader of a Starfleet project in 2274 that synthesized an Omega Molecule. The molecule destabilized in an explosion that claimed Ketteract, 126 other scientists, and the Lantaru Sector research station.
