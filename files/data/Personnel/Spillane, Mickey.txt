PERSONNEL: Mickey Spillane

Pseudonym for 20th-century Earth novelist Frank Morrison whose violent detective stories usually featured Mike Hammer. Miles O'Brien, Odo, and Kira Nerys were 24th-century Spillane fans. 
Some of his works included I, the Jury, Survival: Zero!

Reference: Profit and Loss
