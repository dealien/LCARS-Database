PERSONNEL: Dejar

Cardassian female, an operative of the Obsidian Order. In 2371 Dejar posed as a scientific colleague of Ulani and Gilora and attempted to sabotage a joint Cardassian-Bajoran scientific effort to place a subspace relay in the Gamma Quadrant. 

Reference: Destiny 
Played by Jessica Hendra.
