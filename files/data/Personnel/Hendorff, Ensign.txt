PERSONNEL: Mal Hendorff
Ensign, security officer, U.S.S. Enterprise

Security crewman aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise. In 2267, while investigating an odd flower on Gamma Trianguli VI, the plant shot Hendorff mid-chest with a dozen spores. The ensign died instantly. 

Reference: The Apple 
Played by Mal Friedman. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
