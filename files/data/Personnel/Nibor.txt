PERSONNEL: Nibor
 
Ferengi on the D'Kora-class marauder, the Krayton. in 2366. Nibor lost a three-dimensional chess game with William Riker on Betazed. Nibor's desire for a re-match facilitated Riker's escape from the Krayton. 

Reference: Ménage à Troi
