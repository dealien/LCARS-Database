PERSONNEL: Rooney

Starfleet officer, stationed at DS9, who was killed on stardate 50049.3 along with two fellow officers, Hoya and Bertram, in a conflict with the Jem'Hadar above Torga IV. He was a skilled trumpet player and had people dancing in the aisles at Quark's on occassion.

Reference: The Ship 
The Continuum mentioned that there were three fellow officers on the runabout, but that was a mistake.
