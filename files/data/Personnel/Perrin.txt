PERSONNEL: Perrin

Human wife of the Vulcan ambassador Sarek. Perrin was Sarek's last wife and was responsible for nursing him until he finally succumbed to the debilitating Bendii Syndrome. 

Reference: Unification
Portrayed by Joanna Miles.
