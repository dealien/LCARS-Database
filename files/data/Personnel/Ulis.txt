PERSONNEL: Ulis

Leader of the Ferengi thieves who looted Enterprise NX-01 in 2151. Ulis and his gang were thwarted by Captain Archer, who put Ulis' cowardly cousin Krem in command. 

Reference: Acquisition
Portrayed by Ethan Phillips, whom also played Neelix throughout VOY.
