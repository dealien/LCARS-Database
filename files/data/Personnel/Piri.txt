PERSONNEL: Piri

Akritirian teenage girl who, while traveling with her terrorist brother, Vel, announced to the Voyager crew that she knew the location of the prison where Tom Paris and Harry Kim were being incarcerated. 

Reference: The Chute
Portrayed by Rosemary Morgan.
