PERSONNEL: Verin

Leader of a people who claimed that the Friendship 1 probe led to the the large-scale destruction of their people. Verin was suspicious of the Voyager crew sent in search of the probe and was responsible for killing officer Joseph Carey. 

Reference: Friendship One
Portrayed by Ken Land.
