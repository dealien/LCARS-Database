PERSONNEL: Trakor

Bajoran male who authored a series of symbolic prophecies 3,000 years ago based on visions from the Orb of Change. Trakor's Third Prophecy was apparently fulfilled in 2371 during a Cardassian communications relay experiment. Trakor's Fourth Prophecy predicted difficulties for the Emissary.
