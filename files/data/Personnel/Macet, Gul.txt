PERSONNEL: Macet
Gul, starship commander, Trager 

Cardassian commander in 2367. Macet's ship, the Trager, rendezvoused with the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701-D so Macet could monitor the Enterprise's search for the Phoenix. Macet hoped to avoid war. 

Reference: The Wounded 
Portrayed by Marc Alaimo, whom also played Gul Dukat, Badar N'D'D,
