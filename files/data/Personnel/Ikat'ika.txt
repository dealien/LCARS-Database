PERSONNEL: Ikat'ika
First, Interment Camp 371

Jem'Hadar officer at Dominion internment camp 371 who was put to death by his Vorta superior for failing to slay Worf in a mano a mano duel. 

Reference: By Inferno's Light 
Played by James Horan who also played Jo'Bril in "Suspicions," Lieutenant Barnabu in "Descent," and Tosin in "Fair Trade."
