PERSONNEL: Michael Jordan
Ensign, engineering officer, U.S.S. Enterprise

Young, dark-haired male U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701 crewmember working in auxiliary control when Harry Mudd's android, Norman, commandeered the starship in 2267. 

Reference: I, Mudd 
Played by Michael Zaslow. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
