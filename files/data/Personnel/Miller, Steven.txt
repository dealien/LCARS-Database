PERSONNEL: Steven Miller

Human parent to Wyatt Miller, married to Victoria Miller, whom they raised on Betazed. Dear friends of Ian Andrew Troi and Lwaxana Troi, the two couples sealed their friendship by the betrothal of their offspring. In 2364 the Millers arrived on the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701-D to complete the marriage. 

Reference: Haven 
Portrayed by Robert Ellenstein, whom also played the Federation President in "The Voyage Home."
