PERSONNEL: Nicholas Locarno
Cadet First Class, Nova Squad, Starfleet Academy

A brilliant and magnetic Starfleet Academy cadet in 2368 who headed the Nova Squadron. Locarno was expelled after his antics lead to the death of Cadet Joshua Albert. 

Reference: The First Duty 
Played by Robert Duncan McNeill, who also played Tom Paris througout Voyager.
