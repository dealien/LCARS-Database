PERSONNEL: Hylan
Crewman, engineering officer, U.S.S. Sutherland

One of the first persons to be assigned to the U.S.S. Sutherland while it was still in dry-dock. This gentleman continued to serve aboard the craft through the Dominion War and through its assignment to the Starfleet Academy.
