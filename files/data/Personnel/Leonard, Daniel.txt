PERSONNEL: Daniel Leonard
Rear Admiral, Starfleet Headquarters

Leonard was involved with the negotiations with the Vulcans following the discovery of the Klingon warrior Klaang in Broken Bow, Oklahoma. Leonard accidentally referred to the species as "Klingot."
