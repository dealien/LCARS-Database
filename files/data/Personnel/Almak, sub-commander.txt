PERSONNEL: Almak
Sub-Commander, starship commander T’met
 
Officer of the Romulan Warbird who demanded the surrender of the Starfleet vessel Prometheus, whose helm had been retaken by the EMH and EMH-2. 

Reference: Message in a Bottle
The Continuum also refers to this character as Almar.
