PERSONNEL: Latara
Ensign, Deep Space Nine

Bajoran woman living on DS9 in 2372. As a newlywed, she and her husband asked the Emissary, Benjamin Sisko, to bless their marriage. 

Reference: Accession 
Played by Grace Zandarski.
