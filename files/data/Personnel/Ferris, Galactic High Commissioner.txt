PERSONNEL: John Ferris
Galactic High Commissioner

Federation official in charge of the Enterprise's 2267 emergency medical supply mission to planet Makus III. The assignment was delayed by a Spock-led science survey, despite Ferris' objections. 

Reference: The Galileo Seven 
Played by John Crawford. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
