PERSONNEL: Scholar/Artist

One of three members of Riva's Chorus of translators. The Scholar/Artist combined logic, intellect, philosophy, and judgment with the dreamer. Scholar/Artist and the other two members of the Chorus were killed on Solais V in 2365 during a disastrous mediation. 

Reference: Loud as a Whisper 
Portrayed by Thomas Oglesby. It is unsure if this actor is a family member to Randy Oglseby who performed many roles in DS9, VOY, and ENT.
