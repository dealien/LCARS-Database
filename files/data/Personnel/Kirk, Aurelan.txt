PERSONNEL: Aurelan Kirk

James Kirk's sister-in-law. Aurelan, her husband, and son were all infested by the Denevan neural parasites in 2267. Aurelan died on the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701 while debriefing Kirk about the creatures. 

Reference: Operation: Annihilate! 
Played by Joan Swift.
