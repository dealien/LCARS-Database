PERSONNEL: Dexter Remmick
Lieutenant Commander, Inspector General, Starfleet Headquarters

Starfleet officer who worked for the Inspector General's office in 2364. Remmick was assigned to carry out an extensive inspection of the U.S.S. Enterprise following Admiral Quinn's suspicions of a Starfleet-wide conspiracy. Remmick later was killed by phaser fire following an infestation of parasitic aliens of unknown origin. The "mother" alien had infected him and became the focal point of the entire conspiracy.

Reference: Coming of Age, Conspiracy
Portrayed by Robert Schenkkan.
