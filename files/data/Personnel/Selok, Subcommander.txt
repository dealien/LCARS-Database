PERSONNEL: Selok
Subcommander

Romulan spy who went undercover as the Vulcan ambassador T'Pel. She was discovered in 2367 soon after the U.S.S. Enterprise-D beamed her to a Romulan ship. She was initially thought to have died in a transporter accident.
