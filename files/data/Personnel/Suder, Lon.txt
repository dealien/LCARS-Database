PERSONNEL: Lon Suder
Ensign, engineering officer, U.S.S. Voyager

Maquis fighter, engineer and homicidal Betazoid, Suder joined the U.S.S. Voyager in 2371. In 2372, he killed a fellow shipmate. Later in 2372, Suder died saving the Voyager crew. 

Reference: Meld
Portrayed by Brad Dourif.
