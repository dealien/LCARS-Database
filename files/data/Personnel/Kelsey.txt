PERSONNEL: Marie Kelsey

The leader of a band of mercenaries who stole trilithium resin from the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701-D's warp core while dry-docked at Akaria Base. A trilithium explosion took her life. 

Reference: Starship Mine 
Played by Marie Marshall. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
