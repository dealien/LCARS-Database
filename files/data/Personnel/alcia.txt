PERSONNEL: Alcia
First Prelate, Drayan II

Delta Quadrant inhabitant on the planet Drayan II. Alcia was the First Prelate of the planet. She briefly visited the U.S.S. Voyager in 2372, but her society was mainly isolationist. 

Reference: Innocence
Played by Marnie McPhail, who also played Eiger in the movie First Contact.
