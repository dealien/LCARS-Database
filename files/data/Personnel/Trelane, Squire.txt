PERSONNEL: Trelane

A powerful, noncorporeal life form playing the 18th-century gentleman to a captive audience from the U.S.S. Enterprise. Trelane was sent to bed by his parents for mistreating lower lifeforms. 

Reference: The Squire of Gothos 
It is suspected that this entity is part of the Q Continuum.
Played by William Campbell, who also played Koloth in "The Trouble With Tribbles, Trials and Tribble-ations, and Blood Oath.
