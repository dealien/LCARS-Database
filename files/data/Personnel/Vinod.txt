PERSONNEL: Vinod

Son of Alixus, who aided her in maintaining the pretense that the passengers on the Santa Maria had been stranded on the Alpha Quadrant planet of Orellius without access to technology for 10 years. When the deception was revealed he was taken into custody with his mother. 

Reference: Paradise 
Played by Michael Buchman Silver.
