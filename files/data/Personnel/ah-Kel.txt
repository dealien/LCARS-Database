PERSONNEL: Ah-Kel

A Miradorn who had a symbiotic relationship with his twin Ro-Kel. When Ro-Kel was killed in 2369 during a robbery by Gamma Quadrant fugitive Croden, Ah-Kel's bond with his twin was severed, and he swore to kill Croden in retaliation. 

Reference: Vortex 
Played by Randy Oglesby whom also played Silaren Prin in "The Darkness and the Light," Kir in "Counterpoint" Trena'l in "Unexpected," and Degra in various Xindi episodes of Enterprise.
