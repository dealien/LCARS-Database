PERSONNEL: Boyce
crewman, Deep Space Nine

He was killed while serving on the bridge of the U.S.S. Defiant during a Jem'Hadar attack in 2372. 

Reference: Starship Down 
Boyce was mentioned as a tip of the hat to the Enterprise doctor in "The Cage."  The Continuum credited the actor of John Hoyt, however he actually played the TOS character and the website is in error.
