PERSONNEL: Artim

Young Ba'ku boy, son of Sojef. At the time that the Starfleet officers encountered the Ba'ku Artim was 12, but Picard asked him if he were 75 and simply appearing young like the rest of the older residents. Artim befriended Starfleet officer Data and encouraged him to have some fun every day. Artim was fond of his pet, a Ba'ku rhyl. 

Reference: Insurrection
Played by Michael Welch.
