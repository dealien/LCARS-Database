PERSONNEL: Belar

Bajoran man who was a resistance fighter during the Cardassian occupation. In 2367 he was present on station Terok Nor at the time that Ishan Chaye, Jillur Gueta and Timor Landi were wrongly accused of attempting to assassinate Gul Dukat. 

Reference: Things Past 
Played by Victor Bevine, who also played a guard in First Contact.
