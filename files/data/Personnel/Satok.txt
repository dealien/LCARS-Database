PERSONNEL: Satok
Security Minister

First officer of planet security for Vulcan and the V'Shar (the government's security arm). In 2370, Satok helped expose a Vulcan isolationist plot. He denied the existance of T'Paal, an undercover operative discovered by Commander William Riker. Riker passed on the information regardless and the mercenary group under investigation was arrested and charged on Vulcan, then wsa going to be handed over to Federation officials for pending charges on other worlds. 

Reference: Gambit
Portrayed by Martin Goslins.
