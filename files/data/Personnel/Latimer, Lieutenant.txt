PERSONNEL: Reese Latimer
Lieutenant, navigator, U.S.S. Enterprise

Latimer was navigator on the Shuttlecraft Galileo. He survived the shuttlecraft's crash on Taurus II, but not the planet's large humanoid natives. 

Reference: The Galileo Seven 
Played by Reese Vaughn. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
