PERSONNEL: Drofo Awa
Captain, transport commander

Xepolite freighter captain who was caught smuggling weapons to Cardassian colonists in the Demilitarized Zone in 2370, a direct violation of the Federation-Cardassian treaty. 

Reference: The Maquis
Played by Michael Bell who also played Groppler Zorn in "Encounter at Farpoint."
