PERSONNEL: Morica Bilby

Liam Bilby's widow. The Orion Syndicate, via the Tigan Mines, supported Morica after her husband's death in the line of duty. Morica was murdered by Norvo Tigan in 2375. 

Reference: Honor Among Thieves, Prodigal Daughter
