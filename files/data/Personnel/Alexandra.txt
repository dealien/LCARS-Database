PERSONNEL: Alexandra

The young daughter of a pair of crewmen aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise-D. Sher and several other children were abducted from the starship when the vessel investigated the rumors of the planet Aldea. The planet decloaked and transported the children in an attempt to repopulate their planet. Alexandra was supposed to be cared for by a married couple on the planet, but Rashella would not let her go.
