PERSONNEL: Marla Gilmore
Ensign, engineering officer, U.S.S. Equinox

U.S.S. Equinox engineer who used nucleogenic lifeforms to fuel the Equinox after being stranded in the Delta Quadrant by the Caretaker. Gilmore joined the U.S.S. Voyager crew in 2376.
