PERSONNEL: Phyllis Mears
Yeoman, U.S.S. Enterprise

Female crew member aboard the Shuttlecraft Galileo in 2267 when it crashed on Taurus II. Mears was a survivor of the incident, returning to duty on the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701. 

Reference: The Galileo Seven 
Played by Phyllis Douglas. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
