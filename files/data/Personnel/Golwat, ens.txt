PERSONNEL: Golwat
Ensign, U.S.S. Voyager

A female Bolian aboard Voyager, one of the few crewmembers who apparently appreciate Neelix's beverage experiments. Tuvok surmised it was because Bolian tongues have a protective cartilaginous lining.
