PERSONNEL: Bhavani
Premier, planetary leader, Barzan

In 2366 she asked to use the Enterprise-D as a place to hold negotiations for rights to the Barzan wormhole discovered by her people. 

Reference: The Price 
Played by Elizabeth Hoffman.
