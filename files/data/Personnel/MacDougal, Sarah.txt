PERSONNEL: Sarah MacDougal
Lieutenant Commander, chief engineer, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

One of the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701-D's chief engineers. She was assigned to the ship in 2364, but did not stay long at this post.
