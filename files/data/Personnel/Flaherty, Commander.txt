PERSONNEL: Flaherty
Commander, executive officer, U.S.S. Aries

Flaherty spoke over 40 languages, including Klingon, Romulan, Giamon and Stroyerian. Flaherty would have been Riker's first officer if Riker had accepted command of the Aries in 2365. 

Reference: The Icarus Factor
