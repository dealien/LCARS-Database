PERSONNEL: Bill Kellin
Crewman, engineering officer, AR-558

Young, brash engineer with the Starfleet forces pinned down at AR-558. During the decisive Jem'Hadar assault, Kellin was fatally wounded while saving Ezri Dax's life. 

Reference: The Siege of AR-558
