PERSONNEL: Kelly
Ensign, Deep Space Nine

Starfleet officer on Deep Space Nine in 2370 who helped resist the Circle's takeover goals. Kelly's daughter teamed up with a Bajoran girl to create a prize-winning science-fair project. 

Reference: The Siege
