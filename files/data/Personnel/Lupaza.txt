PERSONNEL: Lupaza

Female Bajoran farmer from Dahkur province who became a Shakaar resistance cell fighter during the occupation. She and Furel were murdered by Cardassian Silaran Prin while on DS9 in 2373. 

Reference: Shakaar, The Darkness and the Light
Played by Diane Salinger.
