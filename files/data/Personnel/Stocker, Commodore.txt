PERSONNEL: Charles Stocker
Commodore, station commander, Starbase 10

A Starfleet officer traveling to his new command, Starbase 10, aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise in 2267. Stocker briefly took the captain's chair after radiation sickness incapacitated senior officers. 

Reference: The Deadly Years 
Played by Charles Drake. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
