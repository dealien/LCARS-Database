PERSONNEL: Zim Brott
petty officer, Deep Space 9

Bolian Starfleet petty officer on Deep Space 9 for five years, murdered by Chu'lak in 2375. Brott was friends with Jadzia Dax and left a wife, a co-husband and two children on Bolarus IX.
