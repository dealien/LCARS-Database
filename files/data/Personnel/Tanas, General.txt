PERSONNEL: Tanas
General, starship commander, I.K.S. Vor'nak

High-ranking Klingon commanding the attack cruiser I.K.S. Vor'nak. Tanas responded to General Martok's request for 15 replacement troops by sending him only five - four teenagers and an old man.
