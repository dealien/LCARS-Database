PERSONNEL: Lindstrom
Starbase 11

Human male and temporary adjudicator during the trial of James Kirk for killing Ben Finney in 2267. 

Reference: Court Martial 
The image on The Continuum was supposed to be a different Lindstrom, that was featured in "Return of the Archons" and placed in this character's entry by mistake.
