PERSONNEL: Edith Mulchaey

Holographic citizen of the Fair Haven program who contributed to fears that Kathryn Janeway and others had evil intentions toward their village by recounting a magical rescue affected by Janeway. 

Reference: Spirit Folk
It is possible that the Continuum made a mistake when they spelled this name and intended to put Mulcahey.
