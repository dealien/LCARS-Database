PERSONNEL: Lokai
accused criminal, Cheron

A humanoid male and member of an oppressed race on the planet Cheron. In 2268 his was arrested by Captain Kirk for stealing a shuttlecraft to escape long-time enemy, Bele. 

Reference: Let That Be Your Last Battlefield 
Played by Lou Antonio.
