PERSONNEL: Dessican bartender
merchant, Dessica II

Owner of a bar on planet Dessica II. 
In 2370, he was loath to share information about the kidnapping of Captain Picard when Commander Riker led an investigation into the incident. 

Reference: Gambit
Played by Stephen Lee.
