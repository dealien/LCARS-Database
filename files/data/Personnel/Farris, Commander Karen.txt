PERSONNEL: Karen Farris
Commander, Red Squad, executive officer, U.S.S. Valiant

Member of Starfleet Academy's elite Red Squad. Farris acted as Tim Watters executive officer after a training mission turned tragic. Farris died during a battle with a Dominion ship.
