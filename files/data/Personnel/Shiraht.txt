PERSONNEL: Shiraht

One of the Eskan hunters stalking the indigenous, shape-shifting creatures on the "rogue planet." Shiraht was the first Eskan struck by one of these creatures after they received a chemical masking agent from Captain Archer. 

Reference: Rogue Planet
Portrayed by Eric Pierpoint.
