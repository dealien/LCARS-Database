PERSONNEL: Caretaker
Supervisor, Amusement Park Planet

The overseer of a highly advanced amusement park planet in the Omicron Delta region of space, who ensures the smooth operation of this world's facilities. Visitors to the planet have their minds read, then elaborate virtual creations appear on the surface based on the visitor's thoughts, such as old rivals and fantasy characters. The Caretaker makes sure things don't get out of hand. 

Reference: Shore Leave 
Played by Oliver McGowan.
