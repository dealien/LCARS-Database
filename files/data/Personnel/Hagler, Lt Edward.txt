PERSONNEL: Edward Hagler
Lieutenant, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

One of the sleeping crew members of the U.S.S. Enterprise-D abducted to another spatial domain by solanagen-based lifeforms, for the purposes of medical research. The procedure was fatal to Hagler.
