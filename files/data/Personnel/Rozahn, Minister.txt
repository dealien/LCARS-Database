PERSONNEL: Rozahn
Minister, Bajor

As a member of the Bajoran Chamber of Ministers, Rozahn and Vedek Sorad journeyed to DS9 in 2370 to inform the Skrreean leader Haneek that their request for asylum on Bajor had been turned down. 

Reference: Sanctuary 
Played by Betty McGuire.
