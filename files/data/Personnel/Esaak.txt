PERSONNEL: Esaak

Valakian elder. Esaak was concerned about an epidemic killing his people. Dr. Phlox discovered that it was actually a genetic mutation, but Captain Archer refused to provide the warp technology Esaak wanted, lest it interfere with the natural evolution of the Valakian people. 

Reference: Dear Doctor
Portrayed by David A Kimball.
