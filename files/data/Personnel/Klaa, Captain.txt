PERSONNEL: Klaa
Captain, starship commander

Klingon warrior and bird-of-prey commander. In 2287 Klaa was ordered to Nimbus III to free General Korrd. Five years later, he was a translator during Kirk and McCoy's murder trial. 

Reference: The Final Frontier, The Undiscovered Country
Played by Todd Bryant. He also appeared as a cadet in "The Wrath of Khan."
