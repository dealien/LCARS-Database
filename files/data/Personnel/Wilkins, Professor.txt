PERSONNEL: Wilkins
Professor, Triacus

Starnes Expedition member who committed suicide on Triacus in 2268 by swallowing cyalodin. Wilkins's anxieties and death, along with several other Expedition members, was the work of an entity named Gorgon. 

Reference: And the Children Shall Lead
