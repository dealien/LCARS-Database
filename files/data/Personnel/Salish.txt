PERSONNEL: Salish

Medicine man for a Native American tribe that was transplanted to the Earth-like planet of Miramanee and her people. Salish was engaged to Miramanee, until the appearance of Kirok (James T. Kirk) in 2268. 

Reference: The Paradise Syndrome
Played by Rudy Solari.
