PERSONNEL: Channing
PhD, warp engineer

Federation scientist who specialized in warp physics. He theorized that it might be possible to force dilithium to recrystalize in configurations able to better control the reactions between matter and antimatter.
In a discussion aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise-D, Wesley Crusher cited this theory to his crewmates.

Reference: Lonely Among Us
