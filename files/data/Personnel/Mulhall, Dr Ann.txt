PERSONNEL: Ann Mulhall
PhD, Astrobiologist, U.S.S. Enterprise

She allowed the energy life-form called Thalessa to inhabit her body while constructing a permanent host. Thalessa almost made the loan permanent. 

Reference: Return to Tomorrow 
Played by Diana Muldaur, who also played Dr. Miranda Jones in "Is There In Truth No Beauty," and Dr. Katherine Pulaski during the second season of Next Generation.
