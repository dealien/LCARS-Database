PERSONNEL: Orta

Bajoran male captured by Cardassian troops during the occupation where he was tortured and had his vocal cords cut He was later able to communicate by means of a vocal synthesize embedded in his neck. Orta organized a small terrorist unit after Cardassian interrogations maimed him. In 2368 Cardassians framed Orta and his unit for their strike on a Federation colony. 

Reference: Ensign Ro 
Portrayed by Jeffrey Hayenga.
