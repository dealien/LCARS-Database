PERSONNEL: Jaheel
Captain, transport commander

Commander of a freighter bound for Largo V with a shipment of Tamen Sahsheer that docked at Deep Space 9 in 2369. Jaheel was considerably unhappy at waiting for days for Chief O'Brien to adjust his ship's antimatter flow converter. 

Reference: Babel 
Played by Jack Kehler.
