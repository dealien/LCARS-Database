PERSONNEL: Torres
Lieutenant, helm officer, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

Young human male assigned to the U.S.S. Enterprise-D's conn in 2364. Torres was frozen, literally, while attempting to shoot Q. Torres was later revived. 

Reference: Encounter at Farpoint
Portrayed by Jimmy Ortega.
