PERSONNEL: George Samuel Kirk

James Kirk's elder brother. A research biologist and father of three sons. He died in 2267 after he, son Peter and wife Aurelan were attacked by the Denevan neural parasites. 
He was referred to as "Sam," only by his brother James. He had seen his brother off for his first mission aboard the Enterprise.

Reference: Operation: Annihilate! 
Played by William Shatner. The only scene that George "Sam" Kirk was seen in was as an already dead body laying on the floor in the living area of his home. His head was hidden behind a desk so that the camera would not see his face and recognize the same actor.
