PERSONNEL: Lucy Janeway
Ensign, science officer, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

Crew member who attempted to aide Counselor Deanna Troi with the negative emotions of Ves Alkar. 

Reference: Man of the People 
Played by Lucy Boryer. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
