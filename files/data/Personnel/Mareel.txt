PERSONNEL: Mareel

Attractive female native of Khefka IV. In 2370 she visited DS9 to help abduct Jadzia's symbiont for her boyfriend, Verad. Preferring the old, unjoined Verad, Mareel confessed their crimes. 

Reference: Invasive Procedures 
Played by Megan Gallagher, who also played Nurse Garland in "Little Green Men."
