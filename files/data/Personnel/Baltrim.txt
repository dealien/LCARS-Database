PERSONNEL: Baltrim
farmer, Jerrado

A Bajoran nationalist who was rendered mute by the Cardassians during the occupation of Bajor. 
In 2351 Baltrim fled with his companion, Keena, to Bajor's fifth moon, Jeraddo. He lived there peacefully for 18 years as a farmer with friend Mullibok, until 2369 when the Bajoran provisional government performed a massive energy transfer from the molten core of Jeraddo, forcing evacuation of all the moon's residents.
