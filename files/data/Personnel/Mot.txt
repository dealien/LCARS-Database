PERSONNEL: Mot
civilian, barber, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

The U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701-D's Bolian barber. Mot provides strategic tips to senior officers who come in for a trim. Mot also fashions hairpieces to disguise away team members. 

Reference: Data's Day, Unification, Ensign Ro 
Portrayed by Ken Thorley and Shelly Desai. When Ken played the part the script called him V'Sal but it was not used on air. When Shelly was cast, and referred to by name on air, the name stuck for both. It is considered to be unlikely that the starship had two different Bolian barbers.
