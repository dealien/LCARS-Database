PERSONNEL: Lemik
Captain, starship commander, Ravenous

A K'Vin fleet commander that was in charge of tactical groups of ships during the K'Vin conflicts when the two races made first contact. This officer was invited to attend the retirement party held in honor of leaving Federation ambassador Stephaleh n'Erliarch.

Reference: The K'Vin Wars
