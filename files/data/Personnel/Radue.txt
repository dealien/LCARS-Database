PERSONNEL: Radue

Male leader of the legendary planet of Aldea. In 2364, Radue authorized the abduction of children from the U.S.S. Enterprise in order to help repopulate his planet. 

Reference: When the Bough Breaks 
Portrayed by Jerry Hardin, whom also played Samuel Clemens in "Time's Arrow."
