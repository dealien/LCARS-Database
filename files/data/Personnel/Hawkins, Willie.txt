PERSONNEL: Willie Hawkins

An African-American baseball player in a vision of 1953 Earth where Benjamin Sisko thought he was a writer in Harlem named Benny Russell. Willie looked like a humanized version of Worf. He was an outfielder and wore number 15 for the New York Giants. 

Reference: Far Beyond the Stars 
Played by Michael Dorn, sans make-up that he usually uses to play other characters like Commander Word throughout Next Generation and later seasons of Deep Space 9, and Colonel Worf in "The Undiscovered Country."
