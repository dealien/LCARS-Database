PERSONNEL: waitress

This woman regularly waited on Dr. Leonard McCoy when he visited the bar she worked at. On one occassion she asked for his order but he was under the influence of Spock's katra and ordered ..... She replied, "That isn't your usual poison." Still under the influence of Spock, he replied, "It is not logical to order poison." She recognized he was in a wierd mood and went to fill his drink order as made.

Reference: The Search for Spock
Portrayed by Sharon Thomas.
