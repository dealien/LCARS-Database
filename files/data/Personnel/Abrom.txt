PERSONNEL: Abrom
resistance fighter, Zeon

He fought against the Nazi regime on planet Ekos in 2268 as a Zeon member of the resistance. With his brother Isak, he helped Commander Spock and Captain James Kirk find John Gill, a Federation cultural observer. 

Reference: Patterns of Force 
Played by William Wintersole.
