PERSONNEL: Ruth Bonne

Former love interest of James T. Kirk, Ruth was a stunning blonde female who met the future Starfleet captain during his Academy days in 2252. 
A perfect re-creation of Ruth appeared on the amusement park planet in 2267. 

Reference: Shore Leave 
Played by Shirley Bonne. The character's last name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
