PERSONNEL: Miral

B'Elanna Torres's mother. Miral's strict Klingon beliefs ruined her marriage to B'Elanna's human father, John, and thwarted a harmonious mother-daughter relationship. Miral died in 2376, dishonored by B'Elanna's rejection of Klingon traditions. 

Reference: Barge of the Dead
Portrayed by Karen Austin.
