PERSONNEL: Corrigan

Starfleet officer who graduated from Starfleet Academy in the same class as James T. Kirk. Corrigan and Kirk crossed paths again in 2267 in a bar at Starbase 11, when the Enterprise was docked for repairs. It was a tense encounter because Corrigan considered Kirk to be responsible for the death of Ben Finney.
