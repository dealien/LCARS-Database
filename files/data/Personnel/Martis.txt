PERSONNEL: Martis

Kes's mother. In 2370 she gave birth to Kes, who later relieved the experience and heard her mother hoping she could live above ground. In 2371 Martis's husband, Benaren, died. 

Reference: Before and After
Portrayed by Rachel Harris.
