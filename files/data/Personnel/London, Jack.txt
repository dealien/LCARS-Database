PERSONNEL: Jack London

An eager-to-please young man. Employed as a bellhop at a San Francisco hotel in 1897, London helped Data, tossed back in time by the Devidians. London became an important American author. 

TIMELINE: 
1876: Born in San Francisco, Earth
1897: moved to Klondike region of Earth as an oyster pirate
1916: Died

Reference: Time's Arrow
Played by Michael Aron. This was based on a real person that was born in San Francisco, but his work as a bellhop is conjectural.
