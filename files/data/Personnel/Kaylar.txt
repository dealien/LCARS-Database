PERSONNEL: Kaylar

Powerful, but slow humanoid warrior on Rigel VII. Christopher Pike killed him in a deserted fortress in 2254. Crew casualties numbered three dead, seven wounded. The Talosians later forced Pike to re-live the incident. 

Reference: The Cage
