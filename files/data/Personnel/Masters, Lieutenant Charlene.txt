PERSONNEL: Charlene Masters
Lieutenant, engineering officer, U.S.S. Enterprise

Lazarus started a fire in the dilithium control room where Masters was assigned. She and her assistant escaped without injury. 

Reference: The Alternative Factor 
Played by Janet MacLachlan.
