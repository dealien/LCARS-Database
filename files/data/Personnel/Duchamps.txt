PERSONNEL: Duchamps

Holographic character in Bashir's Secret Agent program. Duchamps ran a casino while in the employ of Dr. Hippocrates Noah. A 2372 sabotage incident temporarily fused Worf and Duchamps. 

Reference: Our Man Bashir 
Played by Michael Dorn.
