PERSONNEL: Mirok

Romulan science officer whose field tests, in 2368, of an interphase generator left him needing help from the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701-D. His tests knocked La Forge and Ensign Ro out of phase. 

Reference: The Next Phase 
Portrayed by Thomas Kopache whom also played Kira Taban in "Wrongs Darker than Death or Night," and Tos in "Broken Bow."
