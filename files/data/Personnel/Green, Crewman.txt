PERSONNEL: Bruce Green
Crewman, security officer, U.S.S. Enterprise

Security member of the Enterprise crew, killed in 2266 on the surface of planet M-113 by the salt-sucking creature who was the last of her race. Green's death was not discovered for several hours because the M-113 creature subsequently assumed Green's identity and transported up to the ship. 

Reference: The Man Trap 
Played by Bruce Watson. The character's name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
