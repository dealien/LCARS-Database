PERSONNEL: Christopher Lindstrom
Sociologist, U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701. 

He was among the landing party to Landru's planet, Beta III, in 2267. He remained on Beta III to help rebuild the culture. 

Reference: The Return of the Archons 
Played by Christopher Held. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name. This character's image was found on The Continuum under a different Lindstrom's entry, one featured in Court Martial. This was done by mistake.
