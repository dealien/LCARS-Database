PERSONNEL: Esoqq

Member of the people known as the Chalnoth. Esoqq was imprisoned with Captain Picard during an alien experiment in 2366. Esoqq had been kidnapped from Chalna by unknown lifeforms, and he was replaced by a near-identical copy of himself, also part of this experiment. 

Reference: Allegiance 
Played by Reiner Schoener.
