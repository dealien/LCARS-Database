PERSONNEL: Steven Mullen
Commander, executive officer, U.S.S. Essex

The U.S.S. Essex's first officer in 2167. Mullen died when the Ux-Mal system criminals tried to use the Essex to escape their prison on a moon orbiting Mab-Bu VI. 

Reference: Power Play
