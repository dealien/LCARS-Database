PERSONNEL: Daneeka

Starfleet officer who was assigned to the U.S.S. Okinawa at the time Benjamin Sisko was the vessel's executive officer. In 2372, Daneeka and several other former Okinawa officers were reassigned to Earth and nearby starships by Admiral Leyton in preparation for Leyton's planned military coup of Earth's government.
