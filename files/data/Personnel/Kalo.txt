PERSONNEL: Kalo

Employed by Bela Oxmyx on Sigma Iotia II. He greeted Captain James Kirk, Commander Spock, and Doctor Leonard McCoy with a tommy gun after the team transported to the planet's surface. While standing guard Kirk taught fizzbin to him and later forced him to give his suit to Spock. 

Reference: A Piece of the Action 
Played by Lee Delano.
