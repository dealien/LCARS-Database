PERSONNEL: Sonak
Science officer, U.S.S. Enterprise

Young Vulcan science officer who died in 2271 while beaming aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise. Sonak had been hand-picked by James Kirk to serve on the V'Ger mission. 

Reference: The Motion Picture 
Played by Jon Rashad Kamal.
