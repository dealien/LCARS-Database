PERSONNEL: Roger Lemli
security officer, U.S.S. Enterprise

Lemli was part of the team that dutifully kept Dr. Janice Lester isolated from the rest of the ship's crew. 

Reference: Turnabout Intruder 
Played by Roger Halloway. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
