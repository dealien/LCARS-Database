PERSONNEL: Jerry Marple
Crewman, security officer, U.S.S. Enterprise

Marple was killed while part of an Enterprise landing party on Gamma Trianguli VI. 

Reference: The Apple 
Played by Jerry Daniels. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
