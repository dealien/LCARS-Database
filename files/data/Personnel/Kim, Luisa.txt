PERSONNEL: Luisa Kim
scientist, Velara III

Scientist assigned to Kurt Mandl's terraforming team on Velara III. Kim's project was terminated when the heretofore unknown native microbrain life form was discovered in 2364. 

Reference: Home Soil
Played by Elizabeth Lindsey. The Continuum is now spelling the character's name as Louisa.
