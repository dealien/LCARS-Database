PERSONNEL: Silva La Forge
Captain, starship commander, U.S.S. Hera

Mother of Geordi La Forge and Ariana La Forge. Silva La Forge, a career Starfleet officer, was lost along with her ship and crew on a routine courier mission in 2370. 

Reference: Interface 
Played by Madge Sinclair, who also played the first female captain in Star Trek in "The Voyage Home."
