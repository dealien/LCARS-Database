PERSONNEL: Nell Chilton
Ensign, helmsman, U.S.S. Pasteur

Starfleet officer killed in the line of duty while manning the helm of the U.S.S. Pasteur in Q's possible future of 2395. The ship, in this timeline, was attacked by Klingon warships. 

Reference: All Good Things... 
Played by Alison Brooks.
