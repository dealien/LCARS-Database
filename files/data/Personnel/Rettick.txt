PERSONNEL: Rettick

Member of the Kazon-Nistrim who was in charge of communications aboard Maj Cullah's ship. Rettick was also in attendance at the Trabe peace summit, along with Cullah. 

Reference: Threshold
Portrayed by Marron E Willis.
