PERSONNEL: Trion Leka
Governor, planetary leader, Peliar Zel

She attended a mediation conference on the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701-D hoping that the strained relationship between inhabitants of her planet's two moons could be repaired. 

Reference: The Host 
Played by Barbara Tarbuck.
