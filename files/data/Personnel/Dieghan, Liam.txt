PERSONNEL: Liam Dieghan

A major philosopher of early 22nd-century Earth, the founder of the Neo-Transcendentalists. During a chaotic period when Earth was still recovering from World War III, Dieghan advocated a return to a simpler life in which one lived in harmony with nature.
