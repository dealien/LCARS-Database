PERSONNEL: Macias

Native of Juhraya, a Federation colony on the wrong side of the line after the Federation-Cardassian treaty. Marcias headed a Maquis cell and recruited Ro Laren. Cardassians killed him around 2370. 

Reference: Preemptive Strike 
Portrayed by John Franklyn-Robbins.
