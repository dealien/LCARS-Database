PERSONNEL: Hodin
Prime minister, planetary leader, Gideon

In 2268 he masterminded a plot to infect his daughter, Odona, with the deadly vegan choriomeningitis virus as the first step to repairing Gideon's overpopulation problem. 

Reference: The Mark of Gideon 
Played by David Hurst.
