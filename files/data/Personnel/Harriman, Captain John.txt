PERSONNEL: John Harriman
Captain, starship commander, U.S.S. Enterprise-B

As captain, hosted a media event aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise-B in 2293 during its maiden voyage. Harriman studied James Kirk in grade school. During an emergency, Harriman froze, then gave command to Kirk. 

Reference: Star Trek Generations 
Played by Alan Ruck.
