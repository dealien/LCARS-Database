PERSONNEL: Kohl Physician

Kohl settlement doctor. The Physician survived his world's mini-ice-age in a hibernation pod for 19 years. During this time he was terrorized by a virtual Clown, created by the pod's computer. The Physician was eventually set free by Captain Janeway in 2372. 

Reference: The Thaw
Portrayed by Miguel Perez. The Continuum featured two persons as the Kohl Physician, but this one cannot be cooberated by other sources.
