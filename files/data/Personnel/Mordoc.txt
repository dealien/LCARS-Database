PERSONNEL: Mordoc

Whip-wielding Ferengi who helped ambush Worf, Riker, Geordi, Data and others during first-contact with humans on Delphi Ardu. Mordoc was particularly disgusted by Tasha Yar's attire. 

Reference: The Last Outpost 
Portrayed by Jake Dengel.
