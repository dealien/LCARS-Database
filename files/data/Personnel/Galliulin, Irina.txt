PERSONNEL: Irina Galliulin

Follower of renegade scientist Dr. Sevrin, who sought the mythical planet Eden in 2269. Galliulin had a romantic relationship with Pavel Chekov when both attended Starfleet Academy, but they broke up because they had differing life philosophies. 

Reference: The Way to Eden 
Played by Mary-Linda Rapelye.
