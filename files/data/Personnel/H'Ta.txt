PERSONNEL: H`Ta

Klingon warrior, the son of Kahmar. H'Ta was granted the highest of honors for a Klingon in 2373, when Gowron bestowed the medallion of the Order of the Bat'leth upon him. 

Reference: Apocalypse Rising
