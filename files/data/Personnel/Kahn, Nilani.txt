PERSONNEL: Nilani Kahn

Host to the Kahn symbiont in 2285, and wife of test pilot Torias Dax when he died in a test she warned him not to perform. Although Nilani was known to panic, almost 100 years later Dax host Jadzia apologized to Lenara Kahn for Torias having ignored her pleas.
