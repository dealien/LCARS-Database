PERSONNEL: William B. Harrison


Merchant vessel S.S. Beagle flight officer stranded in 2261 on planet 892-IV, a 20th-century Roman-style world. Harrison turned gladiator and died during a televised match the evening before the U.S.S. Enterprise arrived in 2267.
