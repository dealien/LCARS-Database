PERSONNEL: Oliana Mirren

Beautiful young contender for the only available slot at Starfleet Academy in 2364. Others on Relva VII vying for the spot included Wesley Crusher and Mordock, who won. 

Reference: Coming of Age 
Portrayed by Estee Chandler.
