PERSONNEL: Drex

Klingon warrior, son of Martok, and had a reputation for being exceedingly belligerent. Drex was part of the massive Klingon invasion force at station Deep Space 9 on stardate 49011. Durnig their visit to the station , Drex and four other Klingons attacked Garak in his tailor shop, breaking seven of his transverse ribs and fracturing his clavicle.

Reference: The Way of the Warrior
Played by Obi Ndefo.
