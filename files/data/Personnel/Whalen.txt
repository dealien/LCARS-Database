PERSONNEL: Whalen
Crewman, historian, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

Crewman assigned to the U.S.S. Enterprise as the literature historian and specialist in the 20th Century. Whalen was shot by Felix Leech in the Dixon Hill holodeck program after the safety protocols were damaged. 

Reference: The Big Goodbye 
Portrayed by David Selburg, even though the Continuum spelled the name as Selsburg by mistake. He also played Dr Syrus in "Frame of Mind."
