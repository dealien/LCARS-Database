PERSONNEL: Aunt Adah

Welcoming human form assumed by the Delta Quadrant Caretaker, and encountered by Voyager crew members in 2371. Aunt Adah proffered lemonade and sugar cookies from the porch of a farm-like setting. 

Reference: Caretaker
Played by Angela Paton.
