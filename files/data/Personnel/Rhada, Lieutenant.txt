PERSONNEL: Naomi Rhada
Lieutenant, helm officer, U.S.S. Enterprise

Relief bridge officer. She controlled the starship while a landing party was stranded on the Kalandan outpost.
 
Reference: That Which Survives 
Played by Naomi Pollock. The character's first name is conjectural based onthe actor's real name.
