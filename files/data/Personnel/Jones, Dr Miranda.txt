PERSONNEL: Miranda Jones
Doctor, psychologist, Medusa

Blind, telepathic, human, psychologist who must face her own problems and save Spock's life before her work can begin to make the first telepathic link with a Medusan ambassador. She studied on Vulcan for years developing her telepathic abilities. She was assigned to the Medusan Ambassador Kollos and traveled aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise to the Medusan homeworld.
As a blind person she wore a sensor net over her garments that gave her the ability to function normally among sighted persons.

Reference: Is There In Truth No Beauty? 
Played by Diana Muldaur, who also played Dr. Katherine Pulaski for the second season of Next Generation and Dr. Ann Mulhall in "Return to Tomorrow." It was rumored that she was romantically involved with Gene Roddenberry for a time.
