PERSONNEL: Hector Ilario
Lieutenant, helm officer, Deep Space Nine

Twenty-two-year old Starfleet pilot shot through the heart by Chu'lak's TR-116 rifle. Ilario was shot in his quarters on DS9 after celebrating victory over six Jem'Hadar attack ships.
