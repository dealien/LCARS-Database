PERSONNEL: Traidy
Agent, Orion Syndicate, Finnea Prime

Thug from Finnea Prime employed by Draim, a blackmail and extortion specialist for the Orion Syndicate. Draim dispatched Traidy and Sorm to kill Arissa and retrieve her datacrystal. 

Reference: A Simple Investigation 
Played by John Durbin.
