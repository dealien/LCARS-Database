PERSONNEL: Nimira

Chief Examiner of the Mari, one of the last constabulary officers in this virtually crime-free society. Nimira was reluctant to accept evidence, uncovered by Tuvok, of a black market in violent imagery among Mari citizens. 

Reference: Random Thoughts
Portrayed by Gwynyth Walsh whom also played B'Etor in many episodes of TNG and DS9.
