PERSONNEL: Nakahn

Smug, swaggering proprietor of an outpost for the itinerant Mikhal Travellers race, where the U.S.S. Voyager stopped in 2373. Nakahn tried to impress Janeway with tales of massive, planetoidal monsters. 

Reference: Darkling
Portrayed by Stephen Davies.
