PERSONNEL: Robert Tomlinson
Lieutenant, phaser control officer, U.S.S. Enterprise

Kirk was joining Tomlinson and Angela Martine in matrimony until a red alert stopped the nuptials. Tomlinson died in the subsequent battle. 

Reference: Balance of Terror 
Played by Stephen Mines.
