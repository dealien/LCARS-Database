PERSONNEL: Dmitri Valtane
Commander, science officer, U.S.S. Excelsior

Human science officer who served on the U.S.S. Excelsior NCC-2000 under Captain Sulu and with then-Ensign Tuvok. Lieutenant Valtane was unaware he carried a memory virus in his brain, which was transferred to Tuvok on his death. This occurred in 2293 in the vicinity of the Azure Nebula during a battle with several Klingon ships. 

Reference: The Undiscovered Country, Flashback
Valtane was seen, along with his crewmates, sending off the U.S.S. Enterprise after their Khitomer rescue. However, Tuvok remembered things differently when he melded with Captain Janeway to expose a memory virus. In that memory he remembered the Valtane was murdered.
Played by Jeremy Roberts.
