PERSONNEL: Gillian Taylor, PhD.
Asst Director, marine biologist, Cetacean Institute

Whale expert working with the two humpbacks, George and Gracie, that James Kirk needed to transport to the 23rd century to answer the call of a mysterious probe. Taylor had no family ties in the 20th century so she accompanied the whales to the future. 

Reference: The Voyage Home 
Played by Catherine Hicks.
