PERSONNEL: Hal Moseley
Meteorologist, Penthara IV

In 2368 he predicted ecological annihilation if a cascading exothermal inversion was allowed to form. The U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701-D crew helped avert this disaster. 

Reference: A Matter of Time 
Portrayed by Stefan Gierasch.
