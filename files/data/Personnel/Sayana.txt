PERSONNEL: Sayana

Young female from Gamma Trianguli VI. In 2267, while watching Enterprise crew members Ensign Chekov and Yeoman Landon kissing, Sayana and Makora learned that there was more to life than feeding the god-like Vaal. 

Reference: The Apple 
Played by Shair Nims.
