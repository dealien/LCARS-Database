PERSONNEL: Newall Rogerson
Commander, U.S.S. Enterprise (aircraft carrier)
Twentieth-century Naval officer in charge of the U.S.S. Enterprise CVN-65, docked in the port of Alameda, California on 1986 Earth. The Enterprise was the first nuclear-powered aircraft carrier. While visiting this era, Chekov and Uhura were ordered to visit the ship and obtain photons from the ship's reactor. 

Reference: The Voyage Home 
Played by Newall Terrant. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
