PERSONNEL: Ben Childress
Miner, Rigel XII

One of three miners on planet Rigel XII. Childress and the other miners were willing to trade lithium crystals for wives from famous con-man Harry Mudd. 

Reference: Mudd's Women 
Played by Gene Dynarski who later played Commander Quinteros in "11001001."
