PERSONNEL: Tolen Ren

Banean scientist who was murdered in a plot hatched by Numiri operatives. The murder plot also involved his wife, Lidell, with Tom Paris of the U.S.S. Voyager was the unwitting fall guy. Tolen was murdered by a Numiri spy who posed as a Banean doctor in an effort to gain valuable weapons information. 

Reference: Ex Post Facto
Portrayed by Ray Reinhardt.
