PERSONNEL: Woban
Tetrarch, state leader, Navot, Bajor

Large, gruff Bajoran leader from the village Navot. Woban used Deep Space 9 facilities to air a border dispute with the Paqu nation and develop a compromise. Woban liked laris pie. 

Reference: The Storyteller
Played by Jordan Lund.
