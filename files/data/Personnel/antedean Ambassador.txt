PERSONNEL: Antedean ambassador

Head of the Antedean delegation to the Pacifica conference of 2365. The ambassador was later found to be an assassin who had conspired to blow up the conference by smuggling large amounts of ultritium explosive to the planet. 

Reference: Manhunt
Played by Mick Fleetwood, the noted rock musician who reportedly is a big fan of Star Trek. (He must have been to put up with all that makeup.)
