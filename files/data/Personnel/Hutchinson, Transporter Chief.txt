PERSONNEL: Venton Hutchinson, 
Chief, transporter operator, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

Crewmember at the transporter controls who brought Data back from the S.S. Vico with the ship's single survivor, Timothy, in 2368. 

Reference: Hero Worship 
Played by Harley Venton. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
