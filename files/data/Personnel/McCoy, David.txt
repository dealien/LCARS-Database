PERSONNEL: David McCoy

Father of Dr. Leonard H. McCoy, who suffered from a debilitating terminal disease. His son decided to end the application of medical technology that kept his father alive but also caused him protracted suffering. 

Reference: The Search for Spock, The Final Frontier 
Played by Bill Quinn.
