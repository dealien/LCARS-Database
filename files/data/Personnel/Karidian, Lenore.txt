PERSONNEL: Lenore Karidian

Beautiful and talented daughter of Anton Karidian. She joined the acting troupe and performed Shakespeare throughout the Federation. During her years she learned her father's secret, that he was Kodos the Executioner. She tried to eradicate anyone who might identify her father including Thomas Leighton, Captain James Kirk, and Lieutenant Kevin Riley. While holding a phaser up to Kirk she fired and hit her father instead. After accidentally killing him and going mad, she was imprisoned. 

Reference: The Conscience of the King 
Played by Barbara Anderson.
