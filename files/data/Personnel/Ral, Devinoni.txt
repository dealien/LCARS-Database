PERSONNEL: Devinoni Ral

In 2366, Ral was present aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise for the bidding to the rights of the Barzan wormhole. At the time, the wormhole was thought to be stable. While aboard the Enterprise, Ral enjoyed a brief romance with Deanna Troi.

Reference: The Price
Portrayed by Matt McCoy.
