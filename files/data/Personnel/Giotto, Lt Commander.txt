PERSONNEL: Barry Giotto
Lieutenant Commander, security officer, U.S.S. Enterprise

A member of the U.S.S. Enterprise security team during the search for the Horta on Janus VI in 2267. 

Reference: The Devil in the Dark 
Played by Barry Russo who also played Commodore Wesley in "The Ultimate Computer." The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
