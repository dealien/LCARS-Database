PERSONNEL: Toran
Gul, starship commander, Sixth Order

Cardassian starship commander serving in the Sixth Order. 
In 2371 Toran pursued the U.S.S. Defiant near Cardassian border outpost 61 in the Almatha sector. Toran realized too late he was chasing a warp signature decoy.
