PERSONNEL: Inad
Telepathic Ullian historian who was part of the delegation that visited the U.S.S. Enterprise-D in 2368. Adept in telepathic memory retrieval, she was an elder member of the delegation. 

Reference: Violations 
Played by Eve Brenner.
