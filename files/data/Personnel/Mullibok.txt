PERSONNEL: Mullibok

Old Bajoran farmer living on the Bajoran moon, Jeraddo. In 2369 Mullibok was informed that Jeraddo would soon be uninhabitable, but he refused to evacuate. Kira had to remove Mullibok. 

Reference: Progress 
Played by Brian Kieth.
