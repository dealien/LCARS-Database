PERSONNEL: Steve Gerber
Commander, U.S.S. Yamato

One of the senior officers on the Galaxy-class starship that investigated the Iconian Gateway and was destroyed when the ship exploded, a result of the computer interferance.

Reference: Contagion
This characters name was seen during an onscreen computer log but was not mentioned in the script or episode.
