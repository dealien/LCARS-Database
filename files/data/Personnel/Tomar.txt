PERSONNEL: Tomar

Member of Rojan's multi-generational Kelvan colonizing expedition in 2268. After becoming human to accommodate the U.S.S. Enterprise's environment, Tomar was unprepared for the debilitating effects of alcohol. 

Reference: By Any Other Name
Played by Robert Fortier.
