PERSONNEL: Keenan
Lieutenant, security officer, U.S.S. Enterprise-E

A young officer that served aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise-D, and U.S.S. Enterprise-E under the department command of Lieutenant Worf. She was included in the roster of people to be selected to attend away missions. On one such mission she made a deal with Mr. Worf in which he had to take her place on a mission to Kirlos.

Reference: Doomsday World, Battlewagons, Front Lines
