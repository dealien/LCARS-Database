PERSONNEL: Wyatt Miller
Doctor, medical officer, Tarellian 

Human physician betrothed to Deanna Troi when a child. In 2364 he came to the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701-D to wed, but realized the woman of his dreams was on a Tarellian plague ship. All along his childhood he kpet having visions of a mysterious woman. He made paintings and sketches of this woman and assumed it was the image of Deanna Troi, implanted there by the telepathic people. He was shocked to learn that Deanna had no resemblance to the image at all. Ariana, of the Tarellian species, also had visions of a mysterious person that turned out to be Wyatt. Her paintings and images of him were displayed on her vessel so that all members of the crew knew of the mysterious stranger. Wyatt collected medical supplies to send to the Tarellian vessel for humanitarian aid, then suddenly jumped into the transporter stream to travel to the quarantined starship. He was not allowed to return after being exposed to the plague.
