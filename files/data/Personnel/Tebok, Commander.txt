PERSONNEL: Tebok
Commander

First Romulan seen by the Federation after the Tomed Incident in 2311. In 2364, Tebok and Jean-Luc Picard each thought the other responsible for devastating attacks that were later determined to be Borg incursions. 

Reference: The Neutral Zone
