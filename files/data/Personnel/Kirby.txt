PERSONNEL: Andrew Kirby
Orderly, Ajilon Prime

A medical helper, in 2373, on Ajilon Prime. As attacking Klingons forced Federation colonists underground, Kirby tended to the casualties. During this attack, Jake Sisko experienced the horrors of war.
