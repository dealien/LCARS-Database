PERSONNEL: Bu'Kah

The lone survivor of the I.K.S. Somraw that got caught by the gravitational pull of a gas giant planet, and whose crew became deathly sick after raiding a colony world. She lasted longer than her shipmates after hiding in the ship's refrigerated section.

Reference: Sleeping Dogs
Portrayed by Michelle C Bonilla.
