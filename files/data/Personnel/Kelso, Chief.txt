PERSONNEL: Jay Kelso
Chief, transporter operator, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

Crew member aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701-D in 2369. Kelso was at the transporter console while the ship orbited Tyrus VIIA to observe their particle fountain mining project. 

Reference: The Quality of Life 
Played by J. Downing. The character's first name is conjectural based onthe actor's real name. This character was named in honor of Lee Kelso from the Original series pilot "Where No Man Has Gone Before."
