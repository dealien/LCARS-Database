PERSONNEL: Leon Toddman
Vice Admiral, senior engineering officer, Starfleet Headquarters

Starfleet officer who, in 2371, vetoed Sisko's search for Odo in the Gamma Quadrant. When Sisko went anyway, Toddman ordered Michael Eddington to sabotage his ship. 

Reference: The Die Is Cast 
Played by Leon Russom, who also played Starfleet's Command in Chief in "The Undiscovered Country." The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
