PERSONNEL: Edouard Vignon

Waiter at the Cafe des Artistes in Paris. Jean-Luc Picard programmed a holodeck replica of the sympathetic Edouard for a re-creation of his abortive 2344 rendezvous with Jenice Manheim. 

Reference: We'll Always Have Paris
Played by Jean-Paul Vignon. The character's last name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
