PERSONNEL: Riann

Riann suspected that Garos' curio shop was the source of an epidemic among her people. She joined forces with Captain Archer to rid her planet of the Malurians, and was the only Akaali to learn that Archer and his crew were actually from Earth. She and Archer also enjoyed a romantic attraction. 

Reference: Civilization
Portrayed by Diane DiLascio.
