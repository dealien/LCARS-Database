PERSONNEL: Jimmy
petty thief, Harlem, Earth

Streetwise human kid in Benjamin Sisko's dream of 1953 Harlem. Jimmy, a petty thief who sold watches and other purloined items, bore an uncanny resemblance to Jake Sisko. 

Reference: Far Beyond the Stars 
Played by Cirroc Lofton.
