PERSONNEL: Tosin

A vicious Kolaati bandit operating near the Nekrit Expanse. Tosin demanded three grams of warp plasma from Neelix and Wixiban to replace revenues lost during a failed transaction. 

Reference: Fair Trade
Portrayed by James Horan, whom also played Jo"Bril in "Suspicions," Lieutenant Barnaby in "Descent," and Ikat'ika in "In Purgatory's Shadow" and "By Inferno's Light."
