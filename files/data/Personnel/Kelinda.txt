PERSONNEL: Kelinda

The name taken by a Kelvan who appeared as a human female in 2268. Kelinda helped seize the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701. Initially attracted to James Kirk, she settled for a fellow Kelvan named Rojan. 

Reference: By Any Other Name 
Played by Barbara Bouchet.
