PERSONNEL: Suna

Humanoid native of Tilonus IV who used a sophisticated mind-controlling apparatus on William Riker after his capture in 2369. Suna was determined to learn Riker's secrets, but did not. 

Reference: Frame of Mind 
Portrayed by Andrew Prine.
