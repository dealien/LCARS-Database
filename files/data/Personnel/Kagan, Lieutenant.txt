PERSONNEL: Kagan
Lieutenant, Section 31

Starfleet officer, who fought against the Dominion in the Tyra System, losing many friends. Kagan accompanied Internal Affair Deputy Director Sloan on an investigation of Julian Bashir, who was suspected of espionage.
