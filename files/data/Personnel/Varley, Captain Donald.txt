PERSONNEL: Donald Varley
Captain, starship commander, U.S.S. Yamato

Captain Varley was in command of the U.S.S. Yamato NCC-71807 when it was destroyed by software malfunction in 2365. Captain Varley had been a friend of Jean-Luc Picard. 

Reference: Contagion 
Portrayed by THalmus Rasulala. This character may have been named for noted s-f writer John Varley.
