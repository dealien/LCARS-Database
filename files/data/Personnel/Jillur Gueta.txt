PERSONNEL: Jillur Gueta

A Bajoran artist from Rakantha falsely charged by Odo, and executed for, terrorist bombings on Terok Nor in 2367 that almost killed Gul Dukat. Garak inhabited Jillur's identity in a guilt-induced morphogenic memory reality of Odo's that also enveloped Sisko and Jadzia.
