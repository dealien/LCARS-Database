PERSONNEL: Redab
Vedek, religious leader, Bajor

Deceased Bajoran holy man of some fame. Quark claimed that Redab blessed the latinum-plated Renewal inscription pens he was selling to commemorate the Third Annual Gratitude Festival in 2371.
