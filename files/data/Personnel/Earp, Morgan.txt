PERSONNEL: Morgan Earp

Brother of ancient Americans Virgil and Wyatt Earp. Deputized prior to 1881 shootout with family enemies the Clantons, Morgan was replicated by the Melkotians in 2268 to eliminate the Clanton-ized Enterprise crew. 

Reference: Spectre of the Gun 
Played by Rex Holman.
