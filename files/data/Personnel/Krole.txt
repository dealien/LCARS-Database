PERSONNEL: Krole

Brooding, nefarious underling of Orion Syndicate operative Bilby, who enjoyed provoking his boss. He was presumably killed with Bilby while attempting to assassinate the Klingon ambassador to Farius. 

Reference: Honor Among Thieves 
Portrayed by Carlos Carrasco.
