PERSONNEL: Simon Tarses
Crewman, medical assistant, U.S.S. Enterprise-D

Young medical technician from the Martian Colonies who became entangled in Admiral Satie's overeager conspiracy investigation. Tarses' only crime was not disclosing his Romulan lineage upon joining Starfleet. 

Reference: The Drumhead
