PERSONNEL: Telle
Glinn, executive officer

Cardassian soldier accompanying Gul Macet on the U.S.S. Enterprise-D in 2367. Telle was caught at a Deck 35 computer terminal searching for military data. Macet then confined Telle to quarters. 

Reference: The Wounded 
Portrayed by Marco Rodriguez, whom also played Captain Paul Rice.
