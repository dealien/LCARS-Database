PERSONNEL: Liator
Mediator, justice officer, Rubicun III

In 2364 Liator sentenced Wesley Crusher to death for crossing into an unrestricted area. Wesley fell into a small greenhouse by accident while playing in the park. The greenhouse was in the middle of the grounds and marked by a small, ankle-high railing that marked the boundaries.

Reference: Justice 
Played by Jay Lauden. The Continuum spelled the actor's name Louden.
