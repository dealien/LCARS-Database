PERSONNEL: Sompek
Emperor, planetary leader, Qo'noS

Legendary Klingon whose 10,000 troops won a lengthy fight that captured the city of Tong Vey. After his victory, Sompek ordered the city burned and its occupants killed. This battle was one of the options stored within the holodeck programming that visiting Klingons liked to recreate and participate in.
