PERSONNEL: Stadi
Lieutenant, chief helm officer, U.S.S. Voyager

Female flight control officer during the U.S.S. Voyager's mission to the Badlands in 2371. Stadi did not survive being hijacked to the Delta Quadrant by the Caretaker. 

Reference: Caretaker
Portrayed by Alicia Coppola.
