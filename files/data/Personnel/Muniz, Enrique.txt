PERSONNEL: Enrique Muniz
Crewman, engineering officer, Deep Space Nine

Known familiarly as 'Quique,' Muniz was also an expert rock climber, a skill he used on the mission to Torga IV, where he was killed by Jem'Hadar soldiers. 

Reference: Hard Time, The Ship, Starship Down 
Played by F.J. Rio.
