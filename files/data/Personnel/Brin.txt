PERSONNEL: Brin

Female from a radiation-ravaged planet, whose inhabitants blamed the Earth-originated Friendship 1 probe for their world's condition. Brin carried two boys and a girl to term, but they were all stillborn. 

Reference: Friendship One
Portrayed by Bari Hochwald, Mark Piatelli
