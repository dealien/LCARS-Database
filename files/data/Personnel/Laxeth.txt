PERSONNEL: Laxeth

Talaxian communications master and Neelix's long-standing comrade. In 2362, Tom Paris pretended to leave the U.S.S. Voyager and join Laxeth's fleet as part of a ruse to catch a spy. 

Reference: Investigations
