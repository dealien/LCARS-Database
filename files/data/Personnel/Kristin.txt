PERSONNEL: Kristin Vassey

Crewmember aboard the U.S.S. Enterpise NCC-1701-D who enjoyed practicing diving on the holodeck — a practice that often sent her to sickbay for injuries sustained. 

Reference: Conundrum 
Played by Liz Vassey. Her last name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
