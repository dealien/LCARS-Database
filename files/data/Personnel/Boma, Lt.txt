PERSONNEL: Donald Boma
Lieutenant, science officer, U.S.S. Enterprise

Crew member from the U.S.S. Enterprise who was aboard the shuttlecraft Galileo when it crash-landed on Taurus II while investigating the Murasaki 312 quasar phenomenon. 

Reference: The Galileo Seven 
Played by Don Marshall. The character's first name is conjectural based on the real name of the actor.
