PERSONNEL: Apella

Leader of the villagers on Tyree's planet. The Klingons gave Apella flintlock weapons to use against Tyree's followers, the hill people, in 2267, thus upsetting the balance of power in that society.

Reference: A Private Little War
Played by Arthur Bearnard.
