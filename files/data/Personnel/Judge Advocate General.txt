PERSONNEL: Judge Advocate General

The chief legal officer of Starfleet. In 2373, the JAG post was held by Rear Admiral Bennett, who adjudicated Richard Bashir's guilty plea to illegal genetic engineering in exchange for son Julian Bashir retaining his commission and medical license. 

Reference: Court Martial, The Menagerie, Measure of a Man, Doctor Bashir, I Presume?
