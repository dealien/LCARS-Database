PERSONNEL: Tos

Vulcan dignitary. Tos was a colleague of Ambassador Soval serving on Earth circa 2151. He provided information on the Klingons to Starfleet, and was against Archer's mission to return Klaang to Kronos. 

Reference: Broken Bow
Portrayed by Thomas Kopache whom also played Kira Taban in "Wrongs Darker Than Death or Night," and Mirok in "The Next Phase."
