PERSONNEL: Grat
Colonel, internment camp commander

A Tandaran officer, Grat was in charge of an internment camp for innocent Suliban. At this camp, crica 2152, Archer and Mayweather were detained by Grat, who was furious when Archer questioned his methods. 

Reference: Detained
Portrayed by Dean Stockwell who played opposite Scott Bakula in a different SciFi TV series, "Quantum Leap."
