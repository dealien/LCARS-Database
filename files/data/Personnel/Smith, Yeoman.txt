PERSONNEL: Andrea Smith
Yeoman, U.S.S. Enterprise

Female crew member on the U.S.S. Enterprise bridge in 2265. Kirk thought her name was Jones. When the ship passed through the galactic barrier, Smith held Gary Mitchell's hand. 

Reference: Where No Man Has Gone Before 
Played by Andrea Dromm. The character's first name is conjectural based on the actor's real name.
