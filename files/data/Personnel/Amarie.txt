PERSONNEL: Amarie
Musician, Qualor II

A popular keyboard artist performing at a lounge on Qualor II.
Her ex-husband was a weapons smuggler that attacked the U.S.S. Enterprise-D.
She was from a race that had four arms.

Reference: Unification
Played by Harriet Leider.
