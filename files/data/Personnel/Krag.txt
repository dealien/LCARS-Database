PERSONNEL: Krag
chief investigator, Tanuga IV

Initially believing William Riker murdered Dr. Nel Apgar in 2366, Krag was swayed by evidence presented aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701-D and exonerated Riker. 

Reference: A Matter of Perspective 
Played by Criag Richard Nelson.
