PERSONNEL: Timicin
Ph.D, scientist, Kaelon II


He formulated a helium fusion enhancement system. When the system's trial failed, Timicin was tempted to cancel his upcoming Resolution, or ritual death, and continue working. He was also briefly planning to marry Lwaxana Troi. 

Reference: Half a Life 
Portrayed by David Ogden Stiers. On one of the computer readouts studie by Timicin in the engineering room bore the numeric code 4077, a salute to Stiers' work in tbe TV show M*A*S*H.
