PERSONNEL: Alenis Grem

Alenis worked for the Bajoran Central Archives and conducted a study on the Elemspur Detention Center. In 2371, Alenis contact Kira Nerys at Deep Space 9 to find out more about her reported detention at Elemspur during the Cardassian occupation. 

Reference: Second Skin
