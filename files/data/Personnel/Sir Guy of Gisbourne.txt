PERSONNEL: Sir Guy

Sir Guy of Gisbourne was believed to be a foe of Robin Hood. In Q's Robin Hood reality, Sir Guy kidnapped Maid Marian and threatened death if she would not marry him. 

Reference: QPid 
Portrayed by Clive Revill.
