PERSONNEL: Larg

A holographic teenage Klingon boy that visited the house that the EMH created for himself to learn about family. After B'Elanna Torres adjusted the program to include variables into the matrix, the EMH's daughter was suddenly being courted by boys that he found surprising. He visited with his friend K'Kath.
