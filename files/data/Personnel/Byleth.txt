PERSONNEL: Byleth

Iyaaran ambassador who was assigned to monitor the emotion of antagonism while undergoing a cultural exchange with members of the U.S.S. Enterprise-D crew in 2370. The ambassador accomplished his task by provoking Lieutenant Worf, with spectacular results.

Reference: Liaisons 
Portrayed by Michael Harris.
