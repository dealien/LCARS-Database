RECREATION: Altonian brain teaser

A holographic puzzle which includes strict mental discipline. The game reacts to thought patterns of the game's user to change ripples of colors of the holographic sphere. The goal of the game is to focus all thought onto a single goal of turning the ball into a single color. The Dax symbiont has been attempting to solve for over 440 years.
