RECREATION: tongo

A gambling game popular among Ferengi dealing with business trade. The game uses cards, money, and a spinning wheel. Players have the ability to acquire, confront, and evade, among other options.
Quark owned a tongo wheel that he used in private games with his friends, and the Grand Nagus. Lieutenant Jadzia Dax was also fond of the game and was included into Quarks inner circle.
