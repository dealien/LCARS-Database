RECREATION: dabo

Game of chance played on a roulette-like wheel located in Quark's bar on the Promenade of station Deep Space Nine. "Double down" and "triple over" were among the wagers that could be made in dabo. "Pass 5" was a possible outcome.

Reference: Emissary, Starship Down
