RECREATION: fencing

Ancient art and sport of swordplay with foils. A hobby of Lieutenant Hikaru Sulu, and Captain Jean-Luc Picard. Picard even used the sport to entice Guinan and others to spar with him in the starship's gymnasium.

Reference: The Naked Time, We'll Always Have Paris
