RECREATION: Night of the Killer Androids

This horror film was shown at the Enterprise NX-01's movie night in 2151. Hoshi Sato missed it, but Travis Mayweather told her that she should be glad that she did. Malcolm Reed added that he wished he could have back the two hours of his life he spent watching the movie.
