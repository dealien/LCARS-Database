RECREATION: Roladan Wild Draw

A gambling card game. Chief Miles O'Brien, impressed by the performance of Major Kira Nerys against the Cardassian aggressors asked her to remind him NOT to play this game, expecting that her "bluffing" skills would be too formidable.

Reference: Emissary
