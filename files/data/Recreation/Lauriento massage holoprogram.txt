RECREATION: Lauriento massage holoprogram #101-A

Holosuite program available at Quark's bar on space station Deep Space Nine. This program provides the customer with a body massage by a humanoid woman with webbed hands. On stardate 46421.5 the clone of a humanoid named Ibudan was killed while running this simulation, #101-A.
