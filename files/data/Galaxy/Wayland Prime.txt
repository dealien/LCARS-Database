PLANET: Wayland Prime
SYSTEM:
SECTOR:
QUADRANT:

This was the site of one of Starfleet's weapons research facilities. Every weapons innovation of the 2320s and the beginning of the 2330s came from there. Lieutenant Vigo visited Wayland Prime for a demonstration of the new type nine phaser emitter.
