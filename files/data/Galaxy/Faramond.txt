PLANET: Faramond
SYSTEM: 
SECTOR:
QUADRANT:

Name of purple-gray planet and archaeological site on it, ruins from a very advanced otherwise unknown society. Site where Bill of Rights is attacked.  Robert April at the groundbreaking. Site has five domes, each measuring 10 miles by 3 miles.
