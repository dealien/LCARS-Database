STATION: Starbase 4 

The U.S.S. Enterprise put in for repairs after the Argelius matter. Federation facility the Enterprise took a layover at for several weeks after encountering a plasma storm. Commanded by Commodore Wolfe in Quadrant Zero. Orbits a purple gas giant. Five days from Memory Prime at Warp 4. Its insignia is the Orion constellation. The Enterprise picked up nominees for the Nobel and Z. Magnees Prizes there in 2269.
