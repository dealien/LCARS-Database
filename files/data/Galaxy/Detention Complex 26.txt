STATION: Detention Complex 26

Facility for detaining Suliban living in the Tandaran Sector. Located on Tandar Prime. Captain Archer and Ensign Mayweather were imprisoned here in January 2152.  With the help of Enterprise NX 01, they managed to escape the complex and free the eighty-nine Suliban imprisoned there. 

Reference: Detained, Shockwave
