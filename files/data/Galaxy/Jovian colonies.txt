LOCATION: Jovian colonies
PLANET: Jupiter
SYSTEM: Sol
SECTOR: 001 Sol
QUADRANT: Alpha

Colonies in the Sol System whose unification was studied by Academician Sradek. The Jovian colonies are a collection of colonies spread out amongst the moons and stations around Jupiter; including Io, Europa, Ganymede, and others.
