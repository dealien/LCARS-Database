LOCATION: Quark’s holosuites

At Dr. Bashir's request, Odo put monitoring devices in Quark's holosuites.  This was in response to the erotic program that Quark had in 2369 which used images of Jadzia Dax and Kira, though Bashir kept that information from Odo.
