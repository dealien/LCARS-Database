STATION: Kelp Agrostation
STATE: Pacific Ocean
PLANET: Earth
SYSTEM: Sol
SECTOR: 001 Sol
QUADRANT: Alpha

Agricultural Station for the production of industrial grade kelp which was reprocessed into food.  Kelp was a major food source on Earth in the mid to late 21st century. The agrostation that Yoshi Nakamura and Tatya Bilash worked on was located in Earth’s Pacific Ocean, North by Northwest of Easter Island. 

Reference: Strangers from the Sky
