PLANET: Uni-life 
SYSTEM:
SECTOR
QUADRANT:

Fourth planet in G-class star system containing four minor planets and two gas giants. Possesses an ecosystem where all lifeforms, including plant, animal and humanoid, function in tandem as a single planet-wide organism, living and responding as a unit. Lorelei negotiated with the organism to obtain shielding for the Enterprise NCC-1701 warp engines. Ambassador Zarv, Donald Lorritson and Mek Jokkor were all killed on the surface after disrupting the natural processes of the environment.  Kirk, Spock and McCoy were able to escape in the Galileo Seven shuttlecraft. 

Reference: Mutiny on the Enterprise
