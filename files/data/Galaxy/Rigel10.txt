PLANET: Rigel X
SYSTEM: Rigel
SECTOR:
QUADRANT:

Planet orbiting the blue giant Rigel, home to a trade complex 36 levels tall.  About three days from Earth at warp four-point-five.  Klaang visited Rigel X to meet Sarin before fleeing toward Earth to escape agents of the Suliban Cabal.  Enterprise NX01 tracked him there, and Jonathan Archer met with Sarin and the other Suliban dissidents.
