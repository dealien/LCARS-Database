SYSTEM: Uri Tang
SECTOR:
QUADRANT: Beta

Part of space in which the Furies’ ship Rath appeared in 2267, causing a sudden drop in mass that destroyed one Klingon ship and the inner planets of the system as well as the Uri Tang sun.
