MOON: Sardalia
PLANET:
SYSTEM:
SECTOR:
QUADRANT: Delta

Class M moon where Harry Kim found dilithium mineral deposits with a pre-warp civilization. Sea foam on this planet is red.  Inhabitants are tall, thin, dress in silver tunics, leggings, and tapered boots. Males wear their hair in braids; females hair is loose.  Hair is purple. Limbs have extra joints, legs can bend in reverse compared to other humanoids. Golden eyes. Need special treatment as they age. 

Reference: Bless the Beasts
