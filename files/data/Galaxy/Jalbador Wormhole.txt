ANOMALY: Jalbador Wormhole
SYSTEM: B’hava’el
SECTOR: Bajoran
QUADRANT: Alpha

The second wormhole in the Bajoran System.  Opened in Quark’s Bar in 2374 when the Red Orbs of Jalbador came into alignment.  At that time, the Defiant hit the wormhole’s event horizon in a slingshot effect and was catapulted to the year 2400. 

Reference: Fall of Terok Nor
