ANOMALY: Orassin Distortion Fields

Field of anomalies near the planet of Blue and the Xindi homeworld, within the Delphic Expanse.  An ancient Xindi saying said that the Orassin fields "had great teeth that longed to chew on ships before it spit them out."    

Reference: The Expanse
