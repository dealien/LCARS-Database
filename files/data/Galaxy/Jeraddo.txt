MOON: Jeraddo
PLANET: Bajor
SYSTEM: B’hava’el
SECTOR: Bajoran
QUADRANT: Alpha

Closest moon to Bajor. The moon was Class-M until a core energy tap in 2369 rendered it Class-Y. The tap was expected to provide Bajor with energy for a thousand years. The second of the Red Orbs of Jalbador was found here in 2374.
