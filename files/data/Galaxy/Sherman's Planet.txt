PLANET: Sherman's Planet
SYSTEM:
SECTOR:
QUADRANT: Beta

Farming planet held by the Federation. The U.S.S. Enterprise delivered grain there in 2269 to alleviate a famine. Unfortunately, the grain did not take hold as quickly as projected due to interference by Pathfinder Twelve at Memory Gamma. 

Reference: The Trouble With Tribbles, More Tribbles, More Trouble, Memory Prime, Trials and Tribble-ations
