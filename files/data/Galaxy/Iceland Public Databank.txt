STATION: Iceland Public Databank
CITY: Reyjavik
STATE: Iceland
PLANET: Earth
SYSTEM: Sol
SECTOR: 001 Sol
QUADRANT: Alpha

Computer facility on Earth during the 2000s. Legendary for its role in the development of synthetic consciousnesses.  In the 2150s a conscious probeshipmind backed itself up into the databank and then thrust itself into the Sun. An examination of the backup showed that it, while possessing all the information of original, was not conscious.
