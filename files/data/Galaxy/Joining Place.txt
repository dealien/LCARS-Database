LOCATION: The Joining Place
PLANET: Vulcan
SYSTEM: 40 Eridani
SECTOR: Vulcan
QUADRANT: Beta

A special room held for generations by the family of Voltak of Vulcan, reserved for the formal Joining ceremony of mates and the consummation of the pon-farr ritual.
