PLANET: Fazi
SYSTEM:
SECTOR:
QUADRANT:

Class-M planet, a reddish-tinted green and blue in color. A third of the planet was covered in oceans.  Inhabited by two species: the Fazi, who cover most of the planet, and the Hipon, who inhabit the southern continent. Discovered by the Enterprise NX01 in June 2151.
