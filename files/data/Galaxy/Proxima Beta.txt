PLANET: Proxima Beta
SYSTEM:
SECTOR:
QUADRANT:

Homeworld of Piper.  Planet newly colonized by humans.  Just four generations before Piper.  Surnames discarded, all still one extended family.  Landscape includes Club Moss, Lepidodendron Tree.  Gravity slightly less than Earth-normal.
