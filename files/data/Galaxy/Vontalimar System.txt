SYSTEM: Vontalimar
SECTOR:
QUADRANT: Beta

Star system inside Gorn space. A colony world inhabited by a few million people, its fourth planet was the destination of the U.S.S. Stargazer after the Gorn initiated contact with the Federation and invited the ship to enter their space - to declare war upon the Federation, a situation which Captain Jean-Luc Picard managed to resolve peacefully.    

Reference: Requiem
