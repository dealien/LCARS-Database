PLANET: Setlik III
SYSTEM: Setlik
SECTOR: 
QUADRANT: Beta

A Federation colony. In 2333, its administrator was called Haywood.  The head engineer, Moraal Chiidasi, contacted Lieutenant Phigus Simenon when the colony's main power source ceased working reliably.
