LOCATION: Byrd Research Complex
PLANET: Earth
SYSTEM: Sol
SECTOR: Sol 001
QUADRANT: Alpha
Polar research center located on the inland edge of the Ross Ice Shelf on the Earth Southern continent of Antarctica.  The Complex had been abandoned in the 1990s. 

Reference: Strangers from the Sky
