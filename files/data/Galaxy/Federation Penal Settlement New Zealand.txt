LOCATION: Federation Penal Settlement

A prison camp system created by the United Federation of Planets for those persons being rehabilitated to return to productive society. One such facility was in New Zealand, located in Auckland. It had energy security barriers to ensure the separation of the inmates from the general population. Thomas Eugene Paris was deterred here after he was charged with aiding the Maquis, and before Captain Kathryn Janeway secured his release in exchange for his help locating the Maquis.
