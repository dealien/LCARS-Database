PLANET: Narenda III
SYSTEM: Narenda
SECTOR:
QUADRANT: Beta

Location of a Klingon outpost.  In 2152, Jonathan Archer was brought before a Klingon tribunal on this planet for rendering assistance to the Raatooras rebels.
