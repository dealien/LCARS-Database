SECTOR: Epsilon Z-3
QUADRANT: 

On stardate 1305.4 the U.S.S. Enterprise under the command of James Kirk was on a mission to scan, map and catalog individual planets within the previously uncharted star systems of this sector.  By this stardate, the current mission had detected 13 star systems containing 17 planets and 4 planetoids all class D or lower within the sector. 

Reference: Strangers from the Sky
