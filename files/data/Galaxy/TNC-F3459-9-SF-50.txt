PLANET: TNC F3459-9-SF-50
SYSTEM:
SECTOR:
QUADRANT:

Unclaimed desert planet. Sun began to swell in approximately 1769 A.D., destroying all indigenous forms of life. Studied by the Andorians, Vulcans, humans, and Klingons. Rejected for use by the governments of all those races.  Known as TNC 50 for short, the planet's sole settlement is Town. It is used usually for illegal purposes. 

Reference: Memory Prime
