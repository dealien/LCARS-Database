PLANET: Kappa Xi II
SYSTEM: Kappa Xi
SECTOR:
QUADRANT:

Minshara-class planet discovered by Enterprise NX01 in late 2152. The planet was slightly larger than Earth and most of its land surface consisted of islands, not continents. It was tropical in climate. Enterprise found it after receiving a distress call from the planet's inhabitants, the Oani.
