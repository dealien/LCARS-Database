CITY: Lacroya
PLANET: Bajor
SYSTEM: B’hava’el
SECTOR: Bajoran
QUADRANT: Alpha

An old Bajoran farming village in the Kaladrys Valley. The village was practically destroyed during the Cardassian withdrawal and the villagers were forced to relocate to a refugee camp. Gis's refugee camp was located near the village of Lacroya.
