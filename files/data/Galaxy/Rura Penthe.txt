PLANET: Rura Penthe
SYSTEM:
SECTOR:
QUADRANT:

Klingon ice world home to a mining prison.  Unspeakably brutal.  Jonathan Archer and Kolos were sentenced to labour here in 2152.  Archer managed to escape, but Kolos elected to remain behind.

Reference:    	The Expanse
