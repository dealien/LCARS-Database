PLANET: Ferenginar
SYSTEM:
SECTOR:
QUADRANT: Alpha

Rumors in 2364 indicated the Ferengi were cannibals and were still active a decade later, despite the fact they were untrue. According to Quark, Ferengi have been in the galactic community since the 20th century. 

Reference: Fall of Terok Nor, Fallen Heroes
This would seem to be incorrect, since in "Little Green Men", Quark said the Ferengi didn't have warp capability in the 20th Century.
