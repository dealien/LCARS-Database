PLANET: Ammdon
SYSTEM:
SECTOR:
QUADRANT: Alpha

Planet in the Orion Arm, rich in mining and manufacturing resources, under continuing threat of Romulan invasion. Not a Federation member world, but under a mutual defense agreement with Starfleet. Despite sharing a common language with the planet Jurnamoria in an adjacent system, relations were strained until the Speaker of Hyla negotiated a treaty aboard the Enterprise NCC-1701 on stardate 5012.5.   

Reference: Mutiny on the Enterprise
