PLANET: Dalarte Prime
SYSTEM:
SECTOR:
QUADRANT:

Planet on which there is a place called Nova Scotia. There are no salmon on Dalarte Prime, the closest thing to it is a so-called second-sunset fish, considered by most people too salty for their taste.
