PLANET: Elekiwi Prime
SYSTEM:
SECTOR:
QUADRANT:

In 2333, it was a dying world, with volcanic eruptions and the resulting clouds of carbon dioxide threatening its plant life. As a result, it was increasingly difficult to extract anything from the planet, and samples were very valuable to the scientists at Starfleet Medical. The pirate known as the White Wolf captured a cargo vessel transporting flora from Elekiwi Prime, which turned him into a high priority for Starfleet.
