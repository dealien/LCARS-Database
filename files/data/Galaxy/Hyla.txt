PLANET: Hyla
SYSTEM:
SECTOR:
QUADRANT:

Planet populated by empathic and extremely pacifistic humanoids, unknown prior to the rescue of Lorelei, the Speaker of Hyla, from the disabled vessel Sklora by the U.S.S. Enterprise.
