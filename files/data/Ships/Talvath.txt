SHIPS: Talvath, R.S.E.
TYPE: science vessel

A Romulan science ship captained in 2351 by Dr. Telek R'Mor, who picked up the U.S.S. Voyager's communication through a micro-wormhole but first lies to say his ship is a cargo hauler to protect his cover.
