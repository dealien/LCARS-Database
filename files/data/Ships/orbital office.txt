STATIONS: orbital offices

Many cultures use a variety of different kinds of stations in orbit of their planets from which to provide office space to conduct assorted kinds of business. An orbital office complex typically houses several modules for various tasks and operations. 
Around 2270, one such complex provided offices for Admiral Kirk and his crew during the Enterprise refit.
