SHUTTLECRAFT: 
TYPE: 10
MODEL: MKX-X
MISSION: Heavy Medium-Range Warp Shuttle

DISPLACEMENT
Light: 19.37 MT
Standard: 19.73 MT
Full: 20.01 MT

DIMENSIONS
Length: 9.64 m
Beam: 5.82 m
Draught: 3.35 m

POWER PLANT
Warp Engines: 2 2,500 Millicochrane Warp Nacelles
Impulse Engines: 1 WLS Fusion Reaction Impulse Unit

Warp 1.2 for 120 hours; Warp 2 for 72 hours

Passengers: 4 (Standard); 1 (Diplomatic); 8 (Emergency)
