SHIPS: Gift of Flight

Xawe ship with a crew complement of 14.  The Gift of Flight was destroyed in 2370.  When it was destroyed, the Gift of Flight was carrying letters of credit, 3,000 bars of gold pressed latinum and handicrafts from Anabasi.
