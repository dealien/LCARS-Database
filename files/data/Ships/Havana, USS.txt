SHIPS: Havana, U.S.S.
CLASS: Istanbul
REGISTRY: NCC-3403

This starship was scheduled to meet the Enterprise-D after that ship studied the deadly firestorms over Bersallis III in 2369. 

Reference: Lessons 
The Continuum lists the registry as 3404, the encyclopedia lists it as NCC-34043.
