CLASS: Lanas
TYPE: interceptor
Also known as the Bajoran assault vessel or Bajoran interceptor

An impulse driven starship often used to ferry passengers and troops between Bajor and its colonies and Deep Space Nine. Two ships of this unspecified class sympathetic to Jaro's coup were en route to Deep Space Nine when Commander Sisko updated Admiral Chekote of the situation. 
The mirror universe Terrans used this small space vessel in their rebellion against the Alliance.
