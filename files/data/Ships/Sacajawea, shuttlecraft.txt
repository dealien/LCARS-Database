SHUTTLECRAFT: Sacajawea
TYPE: 6
REGISTRY: NCC-74656-

In 2372, this U.S.S. Voyager shuttlecraft was caught in a magnetic storm and crashed. Captain Kathryn Janeway was nearly killed in the accident. 

Reference: Coda 
The shuttle was named for the Shoshone woman who guided the Lewis & Clark expedition to the Pacific Northwest.
