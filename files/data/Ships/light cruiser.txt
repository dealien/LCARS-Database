SHIPS: Light cruisers

A multipurpose craft designed for follow-up missions after heavy and medium class cruisers have made initial contact in an area. Again, these ships are smaller than the other cruisers and have crews under 200 persons. Some species call these craft Birds-of-prey.

Intrepid-class
K’Vort-class
