SHIPS: Urusei Yatsura, S.S.
CLASS: DY-430

Under the command of Captain Dave Glick. This deep space explorer made a nebula survey on an expedition to the Ficus Sector in 2146.

Reference: Up the Long Ladder
The ship was read from a list taken off the Enterprise-D's monitor. The captain's name is actually that of a Star Trek team member included as one of the shows many on set jokes.
