SHUTTLECRAFT: Onizuka
TYPE: 15
REGISTRY: NCC-1701-D-7

Given name of the Enterprise's Shuttlepod 7. Data piloted the craft to planet Tau Cygna V when it as necessary to evacuate the colony from that planet. Geordi La Forge flew this same ship to Risa for an artificial-intelligence seminar in 2367. It was later revealed that the shuttlepod and pilot were abducted in midflight, then returned to the Enterprise-D with La Forge under Romulan mental control.
 
Reference: The Ensigns of Command, The Mind's Eye
Initially mis-numbered as 05 during its first appearance, the El-Baz's number. Named for Terran American astronaut Ellison Onizuka, an astronaut who perished with seven others in an early space tragedy aboard the orbital shuttle Challenger in 1986. A tiny replica of the Shuttlepod Onizuka is on display at the Ellison Onizuka Space Center museum in Kona, Hawaii, Ellison's hometown.
