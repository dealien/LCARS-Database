SHIPS: Terix, R.S.E.
CLASS: D'deridex

Romulan Warbird led by Commander Sirol, who dogged the U.S.S. Enterprise-E's search for the recently discovered U.S.S. Pegasus embedded in an asteroid after its phasing cloak test failed. 
In an alternate reality this starship was under the command of Tomalak and led a fleet of 30 other vessels deployed on the Romulan side of the neutral zone.

Reference: The Pegasus, All Good Things
