SHIPS: Livingston, U.S.S.
CLASS: Excelsior
REGISTRY: NCC-34099

Curzon Dax and Ben Sisko served together on the Livingston for several months during their 20-year friendship. On stardate 47182.1, they recalled one incident involving the ship's science officer, Kustanovich. 

Reference: Invasive Procedures 
The Livingston may have been named for American Statesman Robert R. Livingston, a signer of the Declaration of Independence, but most likely was an homage to Star Trek producer-director David Livingston.
