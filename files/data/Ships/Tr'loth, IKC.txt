SHIPS: I.K.C. Tr'loth
CLASS: D7
TYPE: heavy cruiser

Klingon Starship. Commanded by Dorak. Battled the U.S.S. Enterprise near the Badlands in 2268 while attempting to recapture Commander Teral and data on the Romulan plasma torpedo. 

Reference: Badlands
