SHIPS: Yaro

An experimental, one-manned Cardassian cruiser.  The Yaro, commanded by Gul Nerwat was one of three cruisers that went into the Gamma Quadrant in 2370 to steal the Antimatter that was stolen from starbase Deep Space Nine.  While attacking the tanker the Yaro was destroyed by an antimatter bomb.
