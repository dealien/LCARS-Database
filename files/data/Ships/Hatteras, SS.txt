SHIPS: Hatteras, S.S.
CLASS: DY-245

Under the command of Captain Gary Loes. This deep space explorer made an expedition to the Ficus Sector in 2183.

Reference: Up the Long Ladder
The ship was read from a list taken off the Enterprise-D's monitor.
