SHIPS: Arcos, U.S.S.
TYPE: Freighter

A small Federation freighter transporting supplies through space. It contained one escape pod that was large enough to house the ship's entire crew.

On stardate 44215.2, the crew was taken captive by one of the factions on Turkana IV known as the Alliance due to a warp containment breach which forced them onto the surface. The crew was rescued by the U.S.S. Enterprise-D with assistance from the opposing faction.
