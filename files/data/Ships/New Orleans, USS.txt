SHIPS: U.S.S. New Orleans
CLASS:
TYPE:
REGISTRY:

Federation starship. It had once been a state-of-the-art prototype, but by 2333 it was far from that. It was commanded by Captain DeMontreville. Errigo Shalay was the ship's first officer.
