SHIPS: Sarajevo, U.S.S.
CLASS: Istanbul
REGISTRY: NCC-38529. 

The Sarajevo was lost in the Gamma Quadrant at some point before 2371. As with the Promixa and the Maryland, it is assumed that the Dominion is responsible. 

Reference: In Purgatory's Shadow 
Named for the capital city of Boznia and Herzegovina.
