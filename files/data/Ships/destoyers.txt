TYPE: Destroyers

A heavily armed starship used for escorting, picket duty, coast guard work, and other synonyms. These vessels are smaller than cruisers but large enough to be used for offensive maneuvers when necessary. Destroyers often a similar amount of weapons as cruisers despite their small size. 

Ships of this type:
Defiant-class
Corvette-class
