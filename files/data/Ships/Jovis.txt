SHIPS: Jovis
TYPE: transport

A ship of Zibalian design and privately owned by the unscrupulous trader and collector Kivas Fajo. It is capable of Warp 3 and equipped with at least two cargo bays-at least one of them with force fields and escape pods.
 
Reference: The Most Toys
