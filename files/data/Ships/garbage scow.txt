SHIPS: garbage scow
TYPE: tug

While recreating on board Deep Space Station K7, Krax and his Klingon shipmates teased the crew of the U.S.S. Enterprise by first saying that the Enterprise was a bad starship and should be reassigned to hauling garbage. Krax then amended his comments to say that the starship should be hauled away AS garbage.
An unmanned sublight freighter of unknown origin, pulled into the orbit of Gamelan V, whose leaky toxic cargo raises the atmospheric radioactivity levels there by 3,000% before being towed into Gamelan by the NCC-1701-D.
