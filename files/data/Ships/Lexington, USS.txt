SHIPS: Lexington, U.S.S. 
CLASS: Constitution
REGISTRY: NCC-1709

Constitution-class Federation starship, registry no. NCC-1709, commanded by Commodore Robert Wesley. 
Lt. Whatley transferred from the Lexington to the U.S.S. Enterprise a week before the tribble incident at K-7. A few months later it served as the flagship of four vessels involved in the disastrous M-5 wargames, during which 53 crew members were killed.
