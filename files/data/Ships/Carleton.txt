SHIPS: Carleton
TYPE: Naval

A schooner in the British Royal Navy's fleet at the battle with the United States Continental Navy at Valcour Island in October, 1776.  Among the U.S. vessels she engaged in combat were the Sloop-of-war Enterprise and the Gunboat Philadelphia.  The Carleton took heavy damage and casualties, and had to be towed back out of the harbor in retreat by a British gunboat.   

Reference: Veil at Valcour
