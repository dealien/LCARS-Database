SHIPS: S.S. Hokule'a
CLASS: DY-500

Under the command of Melinda Snodgrass, this deep space exploration craft made a trip to the Ficus sector in 2102.

Reference: Up the Long Ladder
The ship was read from a list taken off the Enterprise-D's monitor. The captain actually is one of the Star Trek staff members and her name was included as one of the many jokes around the set; the list was full of them.
