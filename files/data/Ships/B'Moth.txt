SHIPS: B`Moth, I.K.S.
CLASS: D-7

Klingon battle cruiser that was disabled during the Dominion War by the Jem'Hadar and then used as a trap for unsuspecting samaritans. The I.K.S. Rotarran, under the command of General Martok, braved the trap and succeeded, destroying the waiting Jem'Hadar and saving 30 B'Moth crew members.
