CLASS: Pyramid
TYPE: transport

A Borg design for a colonizing ship for transporting drones and supplies to newly acquired worlds. This ship was outfitted with a new weapon that attack from orbit and wipe out a population and other life forms, making more suitable for colonization of already assimilated Borg drones.
