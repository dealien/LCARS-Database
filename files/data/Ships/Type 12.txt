SHUTTLECRAFT: Cochrane-class
TYPE: 12
MODEL: MKX-IX
MISSION: Medium/ Long-Range Warp Shuttle

The design style of the sleek-look shuttles also carried about Voyager, for one. Its hull, but not nacelles, according to standard Starfleet practice, is made of a tritanium alloy. Its sensor log memory can hold at least 5 billion gigaquads.
