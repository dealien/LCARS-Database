SHIPS: Toh`Kaht, I.K.S.
CLASS: Vor'cha

A Klingon ship (pronounced "toe-CAT") that had filed a flight plan and returned a month early from the Gamma Quadrant, imploding seconds after exiting the wormhole from a bomb left by first officer Hon'Tihl - having fallen victim to the telepathic energy spheres which originated from the Saltah'na race. 

CREW MANIFEST:
Captain: Tel'Peh
First Officer: Hon'Tihl
Medical Officer: Kee'Bhor
