SHIPS: Drovna, I.K.S.
CLASS: Vor'cha

Klingon ship which regretfully accepted the Defiant's offer of a tow back to Deep Space 9 for repairs after a mysterious blast, later confirmed to be exploded cloaked mines laid illegally by the ship around the Bajor sector in 2372. Its computer core was tapped by Worf and Kurn to provide codes and location so all Klingon mines could be remotely detonated.
