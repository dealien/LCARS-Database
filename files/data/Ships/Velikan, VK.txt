SHIPS: Velikan, V.K.
CLASS: DY-1200

Under the command of Captain Gene Roddenberry. This deep space explorer made an expedition to the Ficus Sector in 2160.

Reference: Up the Long Ladder
The ship was read from a list taken off the Enterprise-D's monitor. The captain's name is actually that of the executive producer (like you didn't know that) and included as one of the shows many on set jokes.
