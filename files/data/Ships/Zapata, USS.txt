SHIPS: Zapata, U.S.S.
CLASS: Surak
REGISTRY: NCC-33814

A starship that was assigned to rendezvous with the U.S.S. Enterprise-D at Xanthras III, following the Betazed trade conference in 2366. 

Reference: Ménage à Troi
The encyclopedia states that the ships were to meet after the Enterprise mission to the Gamma Erandi Nebula; but the Continuum is supposed to be the official source for Trek news.
