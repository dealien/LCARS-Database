CLASS: Declaration
TYPE: transport

This original Enterprise starship was the first spaceliner built specifically for the major space lanes. the travel demand that blossomed in the 22nd century resulted in 957 of these Declaration-class ships being commissioned. The Enterprise’s name lives on through the subsequent flagships of the United Federation of Planet’s Starfleet. This vessel was the first ship to feature subspace radio communication.
