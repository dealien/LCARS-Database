SHIPS: Woden, S.S.
CLASS: DY-100

A simple old-style automated ore freighter, carrying no crew. In 2268, the Woden was accidentally destroyed by the M-5 multitronic computer due to its serious malfunctioning.
 
Reference: The Ultimate Computer 
The Woden was a re-use of the S.S. Botany Bay model from Space Seed.
