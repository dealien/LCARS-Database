SHIPS: Farragut, U.S.S.
CLASS: Nebula
REGISTRY: NCC-60591

The second ship to bear the name.

Timeline:
2371: It was one of three starships used to transport the crew of the U.S.S. Enterprise-D from Veridian III, after that ship's saucer crash landed there.
2373: It was destroyed by Klingon forces near the Lembatta cluster after attempting to evacuate colonists on Ajilon Prime.
