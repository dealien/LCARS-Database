FLEET: Serosian vessels

Space-going ships that use holographic beings to perform unsafe tasks. The crew of a Serosian ship was killed by Dejaren, a murderous holographic technician aboard the ship. Dejaran was subsequently destroyed by B'Elanna Torres and The Emergency Medical Hologram from the U.S.S. Voyager.
