SHIPS: Okinawa, U.S.S.
CLASS: Excelsior
REGISTRY: NCC-

One-time command of then-Captain Leyton when he promoted Sisko to lieutenant commander and his first officer. Involved in Tzenkethi wars, the ship once chased three raiders into an asteroid belt. Other officers included Daneeka, McWatt, Snowden, Orr, and Moodus. 

Reference: Homefront, Paradise
Named for the islands in the South China Sea that were the scene of heavy fighting near the end of Earth's second world war.
