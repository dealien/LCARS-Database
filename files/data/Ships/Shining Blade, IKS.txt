SHIPS: I.K.S. Shining Blade

Klingon vessel that docked at Deep Space Nine in early 2370.  Before going through the Wormhole to a vital rendezvous in the Gamma Quadrant, the Shining Blade dropped off medical supplies to Gis's refugee camp in the Kaladrys Valley. The Shining Blade was also willing to transport down a male nurse.
