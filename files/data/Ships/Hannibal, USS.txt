SHIPS: U.S.S. Hannibal
CLASS: Ambassador
TYPE: heavy cruiser
REGISTRY:

The U.S.S. Hannibal was constructed on Bajor in 2370 and was the first starship constructed at the Okana Shipyard since the Cardassian Occupation of Bajor began.
