SHIPS: S.S. Rosenberg
CLASS: Seidmann
TYPE: Transport
REGISTRY:

Colonizer ship, also called a long-distance mover.  Caught in Ion storm while transporting 51 people (including 27 under the age of 15) to a destination to be colonized.  Captained by Anita Zagaroli.

Reference:    Final Frontier
