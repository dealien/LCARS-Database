SHIPS: Dorian
TYPE: Transport

This vessel was attacked near planet Rekag-Seronia in 2369 while attempting to deliver Ambassadro Ves Alkar to that planet in hopes of mediating peace there.

Reference: Man of the People
The Dorian was a re-dress of the Straleb transport ship originally built for "The Outrageous Okona."
