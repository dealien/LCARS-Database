SHIPS: Roosevelt, U.S.S.
CLASS: Excelsior
REGISTRY: NCC-2573

One of the 39 starships that served at the battle of Wolf 359 and was destroyed. Dr. Riley Frasier was serving as science officer when the Borg assimilated that ship. She was taken back to the Delta Quadrant. (The Roosevelt is the ship on the left side of the image, the U.S.S. Saratoga is the other)

Reference: Unity 
Named for the American President Theodore Roosevelt.
