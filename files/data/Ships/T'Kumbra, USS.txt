SHIPS: T`Kumbra, U.S.S.
CLASS: Nebula
REGISTRY: NCC-

This vessel, commanded by Captain Solok in 2375, boasted an all-Vulcan crew. When the T'Kumbra checked into Deep Space Nine for repairs after fighting at the Dominion/Cardassian front, Solok challenged longtime rival Sisko to a game of baseball.
