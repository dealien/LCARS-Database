STATIONS: McKinley
TYPE: drydock

A prep and repair station designed to assist starships in Earth orbit. Before being dispatched on its maiden voyage, the U.S.S. Enterprise-D was docked here while it awaited the transfer of its commanding officer, Captain Jean-Luc Picard. On Stardate 44012.3, the Enterprise-D docked here again to undergo a major overhaul and refit following the previous year's incident with the Borg.
