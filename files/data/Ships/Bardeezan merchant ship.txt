SHIPS: Bardeezan merchant ship

A ship of this registry, otherwise undescribed, headed through the wormhole when it left Deep Space Nine the night Dukat was kidnapped by the Maquis. As one of only two to do so, suspicion for the abduction quickly shifted to the other ship, a Galador freighter.
