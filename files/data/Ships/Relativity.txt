SHIPS: U.T.S. Relativity
CLASS:
TYPE: Timeship
REGISTRY:

Federation Timeship commanded by Captain Braxton of the Department for Temporal Investigations. Braxton recruited Seven of Nine to help him figure out who planted a deadly weapon on the U.S.S. Voyager. It was eventually revealed that Braxton himself planted the weapon and he was relieved of command by his first officer. 
The U.T.S. Aeon is believed to have been a shuttlecraft of this larger vessel.
