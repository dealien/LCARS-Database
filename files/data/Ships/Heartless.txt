SHIPS: Heartless

Cardassian ship commanded by Gul Dijmas.  The Heartless was one of the ships sent out in 2370 to locate and destroy the Helios.  Though the Heartless was able to locate the Helios, they were unable to keep it from escaping. 

Reference: Proud Helios
