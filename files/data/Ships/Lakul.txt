SHIPS: S.S. Lakul
CLASS: Whorfin
TYPE: Transport

A Federation ship that was destroyed in the Nexus while transporting El-Aurian refugees to Earth from the Delta Quadrant. The U.S.S. Enterprise-B was able to delay the destruction of the ship long enough to rescue some of the passengers before it eventually succumbed to the gravimetric pressure and electrical charges.

Reference: Well of Souls
