SHIPS: Revere, U.S.S.
TYPE: scout
NCC-595

Commodore Probert ordered this scout vessel to rendezvous with the U.S.S. Columbia in 2271. This was one of the last orders overheard at the Epsilon IX Monitoring Station before it was destroyed by V'Ger. 

Reference: The Motion Picture 
The Revere was not seen, but was mentioned in a Starfleet communique overheard at the station, ordered to rendezvous with the U.S.S. Columbia by Probert, a gag reference to production illustrator Andrew Probert.
