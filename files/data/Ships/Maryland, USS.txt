SHIPS: U.S.S. Maryland 
CLASS: Renaissance
TYPE:
REGISTRY: NCC-45109

Federation starship. Patricia Paris served on the Maryland at the time of the Battle of Ankaata, where she lost an arm saving two of her fellow officers. The Dominion was believed to be responsible for this ship's disappearance in the Gamma Quadrant. 

Reference: In Purgatory's Shadow, Progenitor
