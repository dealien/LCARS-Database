SHIPS: Adelphi, U.S.S.
CLASS: Ambassador
TYPE: heavy cruiser
REGISTRY: NCC-26849 

The ship was involved with telepath Tam Elbrun at the Ghorusda disaster, in which Captain Darson and 46 of his crewmates were killed.
