SHIPS: Subytt freighter

Merchant vessel of an unspecified class with a crew of at least three, with two officers. Quark turned in would-be Subytt associates that were smuggling defective isolinear rods to Bajor just to throw off Constable Odo, who arrested the first officer and two crew members.
