SHIPS: Devoras, R.S.E.
CLASS: D'deridex
TYPE: heavy cruiser

A Romulan Warbird which met the Galaxy-class U.S.S. Enterprise inside the Neutral Zone on stardate 44390.1, ostensibly for the purpose of taking on Federation Ambassador T'Pel for Romulan/Federation treaty negotiations. However, the real purpose was to deliver the covert Romulan agent Subcommander Selok - posing as Ambassador T'Pel - back to the Romulan empire.
