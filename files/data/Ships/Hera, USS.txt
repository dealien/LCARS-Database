SHIPS: Hera, U.S.S.
CLASS: Nebula
REGISTRY: NCC-62006

Commanded by Captain Silva La Forge, mother to Enterprise crewman Geordi La Forge. It was crewed by over 300 persons, mostly Vulcans.

Timeline:
2370: The ship and crew disappeared without a trace, and has not been seen again. 

Reference: Interface 
This vessel was named for the Greek goddess, the wife of Zeus and Queen of the gods.
