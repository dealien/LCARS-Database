SHIPS: Tolstoy, U.S.S.
CLASS: Rigel
REGISTRY: NCC-62095

A starship visually recognized by Shelby among the 39 destroyed by the Borg at Wolf 359. 

Reference: The Best of Both Worlds
The Tolstoy was named for the Russian author Leo Tolstoy, who wrote “War and Peace.”
