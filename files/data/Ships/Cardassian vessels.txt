FLEET: Cardassian vessels

A fleet of vessels serving the Cardassian Union.

Classifications of ships built by the Cardassians include:
Aspen-class medium cruisers
Galor-class heavy cruisers
Goby-class transports
Gul Vystan-class
Hideki-class interceptors
Jolanga-class light cruisers
Keldan-class heavy cruisers
Landroni-class interceptors
Nor-class stations
Trabek-class military transports
-class freighters
