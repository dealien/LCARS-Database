SHUTTLECRAFT: Voltaire
TYPE: 15
REGISTRY: NCC-1701-D-3

The destroyed Enterprise Shuttlepod 3 that Picard took out ahead of the starship to detect and dodge pockets of dark matter in the Mar Oscura Nebula. 

Reference: In Theory 
Its namesake is the 18th century Terran French writer and philosopher.
