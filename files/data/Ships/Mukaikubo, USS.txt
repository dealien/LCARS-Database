SHIPS: U.S.S. Mukaikubo
CLASS: Galaxy
TYPE: Heavy cruiser
REGISTRY:

Federation Starship. In 2371, the Mukaikubo was directed to Starbase 1 after another Defiant was found frozen in an asteroid.
