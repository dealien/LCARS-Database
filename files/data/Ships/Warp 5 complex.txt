STATIONS: Warp Five Complex

A shipyard built in Earth orbit by Starfleet to develop the faster starships of the Terran fleet. Dr. Henry Archer and Dr. Zefram Cochrane dedicated the station in a ceremony on Earth before they began their research and implementation. Starfleet's main center for constructing this innovative propulsion system for deep space exploration. The complex was dedicated by Zefram Cochrane in 2119. Henry Archer did much of his important research and development here.
