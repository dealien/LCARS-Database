SHIP CLASS: Crawlabouts
TYPE: Workbee

Small craft stationed at Deep Space Nine for maintenance of the station's exterior.  Nog and Jake Sisko tried to operate the crawlabouts in order to salvage all the debris drifting outside the station and sell it.  However they were caught by security and forced to help Chief O’Brien with the salvage operation. 

Reference: Betrayal
