SHIPS: Saratoga, U.S.S.
CLASS: Miranda
REGISTRY: NCC-1937

This starship was patrolling the Neutral Zone when it was disabled by the Cetacean probe. 

Reference: The Voyage Home 
The Saratoga was a re-use of the Reliant model originally built for "The Wrath of Khan." The captain of the Saratoga was played by Madge Sinclair, who was the first woman on Star Trek to portray a starship commander. The Saratoga's science officer was played by Mike Brislane, and the helm officer was played by Nick Ramus.
