SHUTTLECRAFT: Galileo
TYPE: 2
REGISTRY: NCC-1701-7

One of the shuttlecraft on board the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701. It picked up Klingon survivors after the fight between the Furies and the combined forces of Klingon Border Patrol and the Enterprise. The craft was lost in 2267 near Taurus II during its investigation of the Murasaki Effect and replaced with a same-numbered craft dubbed the "Galileo II." 

Reference: The Galileo Seven, First Strike
The shuttlecraft bears the name of the great Earth mathematician and astronomer who first used a telescope, Galileo Galilei.
