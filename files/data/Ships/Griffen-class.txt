CLASS: Griffen
TYPE: light cruiser

A Romulan warship designed to compliment the Romulan fleet by confusing and disabling the enemy. Its reinforced shielding and ample crew allow it to be a formidable opponent in combat. All Griffens are armed with powerful disruptors, as well as cloaking devices.
Griffens can also be outfitted with a sensor jammer; a weapon that unleashes a subspace wave disabling all enemy ship’s sensors in its wake.
