SHIPS: Hegh`ta, I.K.S.
CLASS: K'Vort

Kurn's bird-of-prey ship that comes to the rescue of a beleaguered Gowron during the Klingon Civil War of 2367-68. Worf briefly served as tactical officer during that conflict.
In Klingonese, Hegh'ta literally translates to "death accomplished." The bridge design of the ship places the helmsman to the rear starboard, and its tactical stations to port.
