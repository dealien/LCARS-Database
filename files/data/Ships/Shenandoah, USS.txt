SHIPS: Shenandoah, U.S.S.
CLASS: Danube
REGISTRY: NCC-72024

The Shenandoah was assigned to Deep Space Nine. In 2374, the craft kept Jake Sisko and Nog from the Jem'Hadar until they could be beamed away. It also carried Jadzia Dax and Worf to Soukara.
