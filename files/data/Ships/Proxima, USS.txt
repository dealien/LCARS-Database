SHIPS: Proxima, U.S.S.
CLASS: Nebula
REGISTRY: NCC-61952

The Proxima disappeared in the Gamma Quadrant at some point before 2371. It is presumed The Dominion had something to do with its disappearance. 

Reference: In Purgatory's Shadow 
Named for Proxima Centauri, a star 4.3 light years from Earth.
