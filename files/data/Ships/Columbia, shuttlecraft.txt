SHUTTLECRAFT: Columbia
CLASS: Type 2
REGISTRY: NCC-1701-3

Enterprise shuttlecraft that picked up Klingon survivors after the fight between the Furies and the combined forces of Klingon Border Patrol and the Enterprise NCC-1701. 

Reference: First Strike
