SHIPS: Republic, U.S.S.
CLASS: Constitution
REGISTRY: NCC-1371

While serving aboard this vessel, Ensign James Kirk reported a careless mistake made by Ensign Ben Finney. Finney later claimed that this kept him from receiving a promotion for three years. The Republic eventually became a training ship. 

Reference: Court Martial
