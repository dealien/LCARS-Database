SHIPS: Drayan ship

Large, flat ship of the Delta Quadrant's Drayan II culture which oddly enough resembles a Talarian or T'Lani cruiser from the Alpha Quadrant. It carries several flat, angular shuttles of the same type. 

Reference: Innocence
