SHIPS: U.S.S. Excelsior,
CLASS: Excelsior
TYPE: heavy cruiser
REGISTRY: NX-2000, NCC-2000

Federation starship. Commander Michael Paris served on the ship for some time before transferring onto the U.S.S. Agamemnon. In 2333, Rachel Garrett was the ship's second officer.  
The ship was created as a testbed for the Federation’s attempt to find a workable transwarp engine. It was originally commanded by Captain Styles. He was replaced by Captain Hikaru Sulu and assigned to Beta Quadrant mapping.

Reference: Serpents Among the Ruins, Progenitor
