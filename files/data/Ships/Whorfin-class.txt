CLASS: Whorfin
TYPE: transport

A Federation craft used for a variety of duties. A pair of these ships were sent to rescue El-Aurian refugees after the Borg attacked their world and they had escaped to the far edge of Federation space. The S.S. Lakul and its companion were destroyed by the energy ribbon Nexus, but some of the refugees were rescued; including Dr. Tolian Soren and Guinan by the U.S.S. Enterprise-B.
