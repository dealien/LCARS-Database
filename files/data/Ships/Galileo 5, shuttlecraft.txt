SHUTTLECRAFT: Galileo
TYPE: 3
REGISTRY: NCC-1701-A-5

This U.S.S. Enterprise-A shuttle was commandeered by Sybok at Nimbus III, eventually crashing in the Enterprise-A hangar deck. Uhura also used it to pick up Kirk and other crew members from Yosemite National Park. 

Reference: The Final Frontier 
The ship was designed by Nilo Rodis and Andy Neskoromny. The miniature was built by Greg Jein. Two full scale exterior mockups were built for the film, one of which was later modified and used as a shuttle on the Next Generation.
