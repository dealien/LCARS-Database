SHIPS: Sitak, U.S.S.
CLASS: Miranda
REGISTRY: NCC-

The Sitak fought alongside the U.S.S. Majestic and the U.S.S. Defiant to retake Deep Space Nine. The vessel was destroyed by the Dominion blockade before Lieutenant Commander Worf arrived with Klingon reinforcements.
