SHIPS: Vorn, I.K.S.
CLASS: K'Vort

Duras's ship used to rendezvous with the U.S.S. Enterprise-D for the succession ceremony atfer K'mpec died. Duras was killed aboard this ship by Lieutenant Worf after the Starfleet officer learned that Duras had killed his wife, K'Ehleyr.
