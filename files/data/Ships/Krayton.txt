SHIPS: Krayton
CLASS: D'Kora

Under the command of Daimon Tog. The Krayton attended the Trade Agreements Conference on Betazed in 2366. At the conference Tog abducted Deanna Troi, Ambassador Lwaxana Troi, and Commander William Riker in an attempt to convince the Ambassador to work for him and use her telepathic abilities to his advantage.

Reference: Ménage à Troi
