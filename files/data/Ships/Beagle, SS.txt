SHIPS: Beagle, S.S.
TYPE: Scout

A small, non-combat survey ship with a class-IV stardrive commanded by Captain R.M. Merrick. 
In 2261, the crew of the vessel was taken prisoner by the inhabitants of Planet 892-IV.
