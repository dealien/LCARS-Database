SHIPS: Benthan patrol ship

A guardian of Benthan space within the Delta Quadrant. The U.S.S. Voyager encountered some of these craft, and its fighters while en route back to Earth. In the one image two cruisers and a fighter are seen engaging Daelen's vessel.
