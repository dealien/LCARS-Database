SHIPS: Biko, U.S.S.
CLASS: Olympic
REGISTRY: NCC-50331

Federation supply vessel was set to meet up with the U.S.S. Enterprise-D at the planet Deinonychus VII. 

Reference: A Fistful of Datas 
Named for Steven Biko, South African civil rights activist, martyred in 1977. The image on the Continuum website pictured an Obert-class ship incorrectly.
