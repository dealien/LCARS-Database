CLASS: Theta
TYPE: cruiser

A type of vessel in use by the Miradorn people often refered to as a Miradorn raider. One such vessel was used by the twins Ah-Kel and Ro-Kel when they docked at Deep Space Nine. Ah-Kel attempted to avenge the murder of his brother and chased a Federation runabout into a nebula trying to destroy the ship.
