TYPE: all terrain vehicle

A ground-based vessel designed to travel over various terrain types, rough ground, swamp and marshy conditions, ice, shallow water (perspective), grassy, and others. Most recent vessels are equipped with anti-grav generators to allow them to be amphibious and to transit large canyons, cliffs, steep inclines, and other hazards.
