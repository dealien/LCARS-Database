SHIPS: Fesarius

The First Federation flagship; first contact with this ship for the UFP was made by the original U.S.S. Enterprise in 2266. Lieutenant Bailey remained with that ship as a cultural envoy, until formal arrangements were made.

Reference: The Corbomite Maneuver 
The miniature for this ship was designed by Matt Jefferies.
