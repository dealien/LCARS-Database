SHIPS: U.S.S. Agamemnon
CLASS: Odysseus
TYPE:
REGISTRY:

Federation Starship in 2311 that was rumored to be decommissioned when it was destroyed during what became known in later years as the Tomed Incident. Commander Michael Paris was the Agamemnon's first officer.     

Reference: Serpents Among the Ruins
