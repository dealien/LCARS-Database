SHIPS: Cochrane, U.S.S.
CLASS: Oberth

The ship that escorted Admiral Satie to the U.S.S. Enterprise for a security breach investigation on stardate 44769.2. The ship also transported Dr. Julian Bashir to Deep Space Nine. Named for the discoverer of warp drive, Zefram Cochrane.
