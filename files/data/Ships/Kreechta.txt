SHIPS: Kreechta
CLASS: D'Kora

Commanded by Daimon Bractor.
In 2365, this D'Kora-class Ferengi Marauder found its way into a Starfleet battle simulation. Bractor tried to rescue the U.S.S. Hathaway from the U.S.S. Enterprise-D, both were participating in the battle simulation, because Bractor thought that he would be able to exploit some secrets that the Starfleet was trying to destroy.
