SHIPS: Malinche, U.S.S.
CLASS: Excelsior
TYPE: heavy cruiser
REGISTRY: NCC-38997

Captain Sanders commanded this vessel in 2373, when it was attacked in the Demilitarized Zone near the Gamma 7 outpost by Michael Eddington and the Maquis. 

Reference: For the Uniform 
The ship was named after the 16th century Mexican princess who was originally a slave given as a peace offering to the Spanish conquistadors by the Tabascan Indians. Images of the starship were not available on The Continuum due to internet problems.
