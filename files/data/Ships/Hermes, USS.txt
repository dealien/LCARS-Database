SHIPS: Hermes, U.S.S.
CLASS: Antares
REGISTRY: NCC-10376

A new starship, built in the wake of the massacre at Wolf 424, that was hauled out of spacedock before it was been completely finished in order to join the blockading fleet along the Klingon- Romulan border a year later.
