SHIPS: T'Marisu

Vulcan ship. In 2368 The T'Marisu used a computer subroutine that made it possible to detect a nearby cloaked Klingon vessel. Miles O’Brien modified this program for Deep Space Nine to track the cloaked ship, Helios. 

Reference: Proud Helios
