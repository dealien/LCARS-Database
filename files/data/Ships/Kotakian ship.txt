SHIPS: Kotakian ship
TYPE: transport

A passenger ship registered with its own world, otherwise unspecified, whose run along the Cardassian-Bajoran-Federation frontier includes a stop at Deep Space Nine. Dukat got aboard as an unregistered passenger on his unauthorized visit to Commander Sisko after the Bok'Nor incident.
