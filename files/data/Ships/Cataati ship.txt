SHIPS: Cataati ship

A variety transport vessels carrying refugees from a Borg ravaged world in the Delta Quadrant. The U.S.S. Voyager encountered some of these ships while en route back to Earth. One of these vessels claimed salvage rights over the Voyager’s ejected warp core, and destroyed the shuttlecraft Cochrane for it.
