SHIPS: Soyuz XIX
TYPE: orbiter

In 1975 this craft launched from the Soviet Union, and the Apollo XVIII, launched from the United States, linked up in space. This culminated two years of cooperation between the two rivaling governments and began the forward thinking of universal equipment for space exploration.
