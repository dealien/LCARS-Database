SHIPS: Amar, I.K.C.
CLASS: K't'inga

On stardate 7412.6 this Klingon battle-cruiser was destroyed during its investigation of the immense space-faring machine life-form known as "V'Ger." 

Reference: The Motion Picture 
The new Klingon battle cruiser designed by Matt Jefferies fr the Original Star Trek television series. This new version, built at Magicam and at Future General, incorporated much more elaborate surface detailing for a more realiztic look. The commander of the Amar was played by Mark Lenard, who also played Sarek- Spock's father and Federation Ambassador, and had been the Romulan commander in "Balance of Terror."
