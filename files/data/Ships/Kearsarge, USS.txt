SHIPS: Kearsarge, U.S.S.
CLASS: Challenger
REGISTRY: NCC-57566 

This vessel had a rendezvous scheduled with the U.S.S. Enterprise-D in 2370, but the meeting was postponed for four days so that Worf and Alexander Rozhenko could attend the Kot'baval festival. 

Reference: Firstborn 
Named for the American aircraft carrier that served as the recovery vessel for Alan Shepard's Freedom Seven Mercury spacecraft.
