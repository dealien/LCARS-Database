SHIPS: Cortez, U.S.S.
CLASS: Galaxy

This Federation vessel was one of the ships in the Seventh Fleet that helped Sisko retake Deep Space Nine from the Dominion in 2374. Unfortunately, the ship went missing and was most likely destroyed. 

Reference: Favor the Bold
(It is featured as the right center ship in the image orbiting Starbase 375 with the fleet.)
