SHIPS: Kazon raider

A Trabe vessel and the Kazon’s principle spaceship. These ships are roughly the size of a Federation Intrepid-class, slightly smaller. Brown in color, oblong with a large fin descending from the underside of the ship in the same design as the smaller fighters and shuttles they can carry.
