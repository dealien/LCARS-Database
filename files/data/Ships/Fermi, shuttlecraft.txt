SHUTTLECRAFT: Fermi
TYPE: 6
REGISTRY: NCC-1701-D-09

Shuttle carrying Picard, Guinan, Ro and Keiko O'Brien when they were reverted to children in 2369 amid a molecular reversion field. 

Reference: Rascals 
It was named for Enrico Fermi of 20th Century Earth, who developed the first nuclear fission reactor.
