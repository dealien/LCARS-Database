CLASS: Huey 204
TYPE: helicopter

This rotor-winged aircraft or "helicopter" was used on Earth in the late 20th century. 
In 1986, Sulu used one such vehicle to deliver Plexiglas panels to the time-traveling Klingon Bird-of-Prey. The panels were used to build a massive whale tank onboard the ship. 

Reference: The Voyage Home 
The pilot who "loaned" Sulu his ship was played by Tony Edwards.
