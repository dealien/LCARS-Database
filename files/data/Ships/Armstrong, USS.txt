SHIPS: Armstrong, U.S.S.
CLASS: Challenger
REGISTRY: NCC-57537

Starship which took heavy casualties with the Drake following a Klingon ambush during the Archanis tensions of 2372-73. It docked with station Deep Space Nine following that encounter to receive medical assistance.

Reference: Apocalypse Rising 
Named for Neil Armstrong, commander of the Apollo 11 mission and the first Terran to set foot on a foreign world when he and Edwin "Buzz" Aldrin landed on Luna in AD 1969.
