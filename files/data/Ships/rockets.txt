TYPE: rockets

Combustion engine used to give incredible amounts of thrust used by pre-warp civilizations. These powerful fuselages carried payloads of various kinds including satellites, space station parts, and warheads.

CLASSES OF THIS TYPE:
Saturn-class
Titan-class
