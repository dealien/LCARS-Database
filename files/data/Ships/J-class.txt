CLASS: J
TYPE: transport

A common type of older transport vessel with warp 1.8 engines owned by families and friends to ferry ores to Earth. Ensign Travis Mayweather's family served aboard the Horizon on runs between Dralax and Vega.
Harry Mudd used one of these small, outdated ships when he was swept up in an asteroid belt. He was rescued by the Enterprise. 

Reference: Mudd's Women, Fortunate Son
