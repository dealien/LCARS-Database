SHIPS: Belak, R.S.E.
CLASS: D'deridex

A warbird assigned to the Tal Shiar.
In 2371 it was one of two Romulan Warbirds destroyed in the early seconds of the Jem'Hadar surprise of the Cardassian-Romulan attack on the Founders' Homeworld.
