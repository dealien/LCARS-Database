SHIPS: Kobayashi Maru, S.S.
TYPE: 3rd-class neutronic fuel freighter
REGISTRY: (Amber, Tau Ceti IV)

Federation fuel carrier that served as part of the Kobayashi Maru scenario, a simulated Starfleet Academy training exercise wherein cadets had to decide what to do after receiving a distress call from the freighter. The scenario was considered unwinnable. As a cadet, James Kirk found a way around the test by reprogramming the simulation, making it possible for him to beat it.
