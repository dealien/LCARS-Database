SHIPS: Think Tank

A vessel carrying a collection of “brainy” individuals that offer their intelligence and problem solving abilities to various customers in exchange for their supplies. This vessel contained a variety of atmospheres since some of the aliens were aquatic and robotic as well as humanoid.
