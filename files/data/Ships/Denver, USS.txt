SHIPS: Denver, U.S.S.
CLASS: Yorkshire
REGISTRY: NCC-54927

A transport ship with a standard crew of 23, capable of crash-landing on a planet as other Starfleet ships are designed to do. While transporting 517 colonists to the Beloti sector in 2368, it struck a leftover gravitic mine from the Cardassian War and required evacuation. The U.S.S. Enterprise-D assisted the ship from its location in the Mericor System.
