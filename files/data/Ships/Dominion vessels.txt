FLEET: Dominion vessels

A variety of armed military ships crewed by the Jem’Hadar and commanded by the Vorta. Officially Dominion ship do include any race allied with the alliance, but the Jem’Hadar versions are the only ones created for military use. 
The Karemma and Cardassian ships are allied members.
