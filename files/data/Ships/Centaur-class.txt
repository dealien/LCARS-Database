SHIPS: Centaur, U.S.S.
CLASS: Centaur

Captain Charles Reynolds commanded this starship in 2374. The Centaur engaged a Jem'Hadar attack ship, not realizing that the vessel was undertaking a covert mission in Cardassian space. The Jem'Hadar attack ship was actually commanded by Captain Sisko.
