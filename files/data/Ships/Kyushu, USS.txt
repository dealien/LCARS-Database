SHIPS: Kyushu, U.S.S.
CLASS: New Orleans
REGISTRY: NCC-65491

The Kyushu was destroyed by the Borg at Wolf 359 in early 2367. Commander Shelby recognized the remaining pieces of its hull when the U.S.S. Enterprise-D entered the system. It was among the three ships to go in during the second wave; along with the U.S.S. Saratoga and the U.S.S. Gage. (It is featured below the Enterprise in the image, what remains of it)
