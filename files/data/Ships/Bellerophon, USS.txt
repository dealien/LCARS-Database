SHIPS: Bellerophon, U.S.S.
CLASS: Nebula
TYPE: medium cruiser
REGISTRY: NCC-62048

A Federation starship among those destroyed in the Battle of Wolf 359 with the Borg, as Commander Sisko recalled the incident on stardate 46379.1. 

Reference: Emissary
The ship is named for the mythical Greek hero who slew the Chimera with the aid of Pegasus. It is also named after the ship from the movie "Forbidden Planet."
