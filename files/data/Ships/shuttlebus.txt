SHUTTLECRAFT: shuttlebus

A Rutian transportation vehicle commonly used on Rutia IV. On stardate 43510.7, such a vessel was destroyed by Ansata terrorists shortly after Alexana Devos assumed the position of Rutian security chief, killing 60 school children.
