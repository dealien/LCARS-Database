SHIPS: Monitor, U.S.S.
CLASS: Nebula
REGISTRY: NCC-61826

One of the two starships diverted to the Nelvana area during the tensions of stardate 43463.7, in response to defecting Romulan Admiral Jarok's story of a planned secret invasion.
