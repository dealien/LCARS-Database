SHIPS: Kallisko
TYPE: transport

A warp-capable transport ship that is consumed by the Crystalline Entity near the Brechtian Cluster in 2368. It is of unknown registry, although its home port or owners apparently are from Boreal III. 

Reference: Silicon Avatar 
The ship appears to be a re-use of the Jovis model, which may have also been used for the Kaelon warships and many others.
