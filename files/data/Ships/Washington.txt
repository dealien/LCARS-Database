SHIPS: Washington

A galley in the United States Continental Navy in October, 1776.  The fleet was under the command of General Benedict Arnold, and consisted of three schooners, four galleys, eight fifty-four foot gondalas, and a sloop-of-war (the Enterprise), sixteen ships in all.  The Washington was one of the sixteen Continental Navy ships to engage fifty-three ships of the British Royal Navy at Valcour Island on October 11.  The Washington took heavy damage, and was taking on water later that evening after the shooting stopped.
