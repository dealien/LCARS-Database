SHIPS: Valiant, U.S.S.
CLASS: Daedalus
REGISTRY: NCC-1223

"WE SET SAIL ON THIS NEW SEA BECAUSE THERE IS NEW KNOWLEDGE TO BE GAINED AND NEW RIGHTS TO BE WON," ~ John F. Kennedy
The second starship to bear the name. This Federation starship was a casualty of the computer-run, 500-year-old war between planets Eminiar VII and Vendikar in 2217.
