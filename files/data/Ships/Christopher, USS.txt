SHIPS: U.S.S. Christopher
CLASS:
TYPE:
REGSITRY:

Federation Starship. During the hunt for the White Wolf, it encountered  energy vortices after it passed the debris barrier surrounding Beta Barritus and lost a warp nacelle, forcing the ship to retreat.   

Reference: Gauntlet
Probably named after Shaun Geoffrey Christopher and a class of starships used in the early days of the Federation.
