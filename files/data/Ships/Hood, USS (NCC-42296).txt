SHIPS: Hood, U.S.S.
CLASS: Excelsior
TYPE: heavy cruiser
REGISTRY: NCC-42296

The second starship to bear the name Hood. Assigned to Captain Robert DeSoto.

Timeline:
2363: Commander Riker's previous assignment, the Hood dropped him off at Deneb IV for transfer to the U.S.S. Enterprise-D and was the destination of Admiral Leonard H. McCoy after a pre-departure medical inspection of the new ship. 
The Starship Hood and the U.S.S. Monitor were diverted to the Nelvana area during the Romulan tensions of stardate 43463.7 in response to defecting Admiral Jarok's story of a secret invasion. 
Circa stardate 43741, the U.S.S. Hood was due the U.S.S. Enterprise's assistance for a terraforming project on Browder IV, until Captain Picard's kidnapping delayed the rendezvous. 
It rendezvoused with the Starship Enterprise-D to drop off Tam Elbrun for the "Tin Man" mission. 
2368: It was among Picard's blockade fleet on the Klingon-Romulan border. 

Reference: Encounter at Farpoint, Tin Man, The Defector, Allegiance
The Hood was a re-use of te Excelsior model built for The Search for Spock. The footage of the Hood flying alongside the Enterprise-D created for Encounter at Farpoint was re-used numerous times to represent other Excelsior-class ships.
