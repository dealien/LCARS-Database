CLASS: Dauntless
TYPE: 

Arturis' fraud Federation starship. In 2374, Arturis tried to deceive the crew of the U.S.S. Voyager into thinking that the Dauntless was a rescue ship. Captain Janeway figured out the truth, and the ship was assimilated by the Borg.
