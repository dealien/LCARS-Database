SHIPS: Ravinok
TYPE: transport

Cardassian prison ship of 18 crew and 32 Bajoran prisoners lost in 2366 while under Dukat's general command and later found to have been captured for Breen mining labor on Dozaria. The survivors were liberated by Dukat and Kira in 2372; the passengers had included two more unlisted civilians, of which was Dukat's Bajoran mistress, Tora Naprem, and their illegitimate child, Tora Ziyal.
