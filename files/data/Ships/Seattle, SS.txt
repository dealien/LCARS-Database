SHIPS: Seattle, S.S.
REGISTRY: NAR-18834

Under the command of Captain Wendy Neuss, This colony transport made an expedition to the Ficus Sector in 2120.

Reference: Up the Long Ladder
The ship was read from a list taken off the Enterprise-D's monitor. The captain actually is one of the Star Trek ADR loop editors and her name was included as one of the many jokes around the set; the list was full of them.
