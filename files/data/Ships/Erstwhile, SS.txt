SHIPS: Erstwhile, S.S.
CLASS: 9
TYPE: transport

Interplanetary ship making runs between the trojan Madena planets. Its cargo holds were empty and its guidance system burned out when discovered by the Enterprise in 2365. 

Reference: The Outrageous Okona 
The Erstwhile was a re-use of the Merchantman miniature originally built for The Search For Spock. The Continuum featured images of a different type of vessel.
