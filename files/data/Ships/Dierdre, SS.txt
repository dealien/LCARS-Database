SHIPS: Dierdre, S.S.
TYPE: Freighter

This ship supposedly sent a distress call to the Enterprise in 2267. In actuality, the fake transmission was part of a Klingon scheme to keep the Enterprise from returning to planet Capella IV. 

Reference: Friday's Child
