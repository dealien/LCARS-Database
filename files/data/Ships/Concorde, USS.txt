SHIPS: Concorde, U.S.S.
CLASS: Freedom
REGISTRY: NCC-68711

During the Q-induced anti-time incident of 2370, the Concorde and 15 other ships were deployed to the Romulan Neutral Zone to investigate increased Romulan military activity by Admiral Nakamura.
