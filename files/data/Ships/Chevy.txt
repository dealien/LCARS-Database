VEHICLE: Chevy

Slang term for Chevrolet, a brand of internal-combustion powered wheeled land vehicle popular on 20th-century Earth.
Voyager officer Tom Paris' idea of a perfect date was a visit to the hills overlooking Utopia Planitia on Mars in a 1957 model Chevy.
