FLEET: K'Vin vessels

A fleet of ships designed and operated by the K'Vin Hegemony. 

Classifications of ships built by the K'Vin include: 
Galo-class transport
Ravenous-class heavy cruiser

Reference: Doomsday World, The K'Vin Wars
