MEDICAL: direct reticular stimulation

Medical procedure which applies electrical energy directly to a humanoid nervous system in an effort to revive neural activity, using a neural stimulator device. When Natasha Yar was critically wounded by Armus in 2364, direct reticular stimulation was attempted but unsuccessful in her revival. 

Reference: Skin of Evil
