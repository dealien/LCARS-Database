MEDICAL: cardiostimulator

Medical instrument which revives victims in cardiac arrest, available both as a hand-held unit and built into the cryo-surgivcal support frame. In 2267, McCoy used it on Ambassador Sarek aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701, allowing him to continue heart surgery on the ambassador.
