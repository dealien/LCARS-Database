MEDICAL: Mimetic Simbiot

A clone that is created when a Lyssarrian Desert Larvae is implanted with DNA from another species. The Larvae is able to replicate that species' life cycle at a rapidly accelerated rate. According to Phlox, they are a closely guarded secret and only a few people know of their existence. Trip's clone Sim was a simbiot.
