MEDICAL: mutagenic virus

Type of pathogen that alters those who are infected. The crew of the Enterprise NX-01 encountered one such virus on the Loque'eque homeworld - it was created by the Loque'eque in order to preserve their people and transformed infected humanoids into Loque'eque.
