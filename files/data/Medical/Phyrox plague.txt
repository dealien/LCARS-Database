MEDICAL: Phyrox plague

An outbreak on Cor Caroli V is eradicated with the U.S.S. Enterprise NCC-1701-D's help in a classified mission, just before Captain Picard is kidnapped by aliens for a lab test on authority. He finally realizes that a fellow hostage's knowledge of the mission isn't possible and unmasks her as a captor.
