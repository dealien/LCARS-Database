MEDICAL: vegan choriomengitis

A very rare and harmful disease, often deadly.
Captain James Kirk caught the disease at a young age but fortunately survived. Gideon citizen Odona was deliberately infected with the disease from blood samples extracted from Kirk in hopes of spreading the plague to the population in an effort to stop over-population.

Reference: The Mark of Gideon
