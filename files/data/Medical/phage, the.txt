MEDICAL: phage, the

A rapidly advancing, adaptive and highly resistant disease which has decimated the Vidiian population by consuming bodies, destroying genetic codes and cellular structures faster than the race's medical science can keep up. Thousands die each day, and the only cure is to replace organs - usually by harvesting from living victims.
