MEDICAL: Orkett's Disease

A childhood virus that swept through the Bajoran work camps during the Cardassian Occupation, killing thousands. To cover for a time her true Cardassian heritage, Seska claims to have had the disease but received a bone marrow transplant from a sympathetic Cardassian woman named Kattell.
