MEDICAL: Interspecies Medical Exchange

Vulcan-initiated program that facilitates the exchange of knowledge among physicians of many different species. Dr. Phlox served in the program prior to joining the crew of Enterprise. Phlox also made a human friend through the program, Dr. Jeremy Lucas.
