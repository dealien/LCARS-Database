MEDICAL: tribnel root extract

The derivative of the Tribnel root, possibly medicinal, is one of the ingredients in an anesthetic Quark tries to pawn off on Doctor Bashir as an energy boost from the well-wishing Bajoran Monks before his Racquetball charity match with Chief O'Brien.
