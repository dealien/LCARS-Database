MEDICAL: Triptacederine

An anesthetic (pronounced "trip-tuh-SEED-er-in") used by Garak to kill the pain of his failing implant from the Obsidian Order. 30 CCs would anesthetize an Algorian Mammoth, but it hardly dents the stout Cardassian physiology.
