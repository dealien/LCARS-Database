MEDICAL: antiproton radiation treatment

A treatment used by the Holographic Doctor on board the U.S.S. Voyager NCC-74656 for Captain Janeway and Lieutenant Paris after they underwent a transwarp evolutionary syndrome brought on by traveling above Warp Factor 10. The treatment, a complete success, was used to destroy mutated DNA which would force original cells to create new, normal DNA.
