MEDICAL: Vulcan neuro-pressure

An intimate Vulcan technique that relaxes the neural nodes of the body. Dr. Phlox suggested that T'Pol guide Trip Tucker through the treatment in order to help the commander sleep. Though Trip was initially resistant, he found the treatment to be surprisingly effective.
