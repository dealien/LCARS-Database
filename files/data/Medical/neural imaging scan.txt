MEDICAL: neural imaging scan

Medical diagnostic scan employed to test the acuity of a patient's visual cortex. In 2369, on station Deep Space Nine, Chief Medical Officer Doctor Julian Bashir performed a neural imaging scan on Chief Miles O'Brien as part of an effort to determine the cause of his unexplained aphasia.
