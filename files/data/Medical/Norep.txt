MEDICAL: Norep

A drug injected into Yar in the neck during the last-ditch but futile effort to save her deteriorating synaptic system after being hit by Armus. Doctor Crusher ordered 2 cc of the drug for anthropologist Doctor Mary Warren in a futile last-ditch attempt to avoid her death on stardate 43173.5.
