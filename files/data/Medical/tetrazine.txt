MEDICAL: tetrazine

Volatile gaseous element. Tetrazine was found in the atmospher of the Paraagan mining colony planet. Enterprise's shuttle plasma exhaust appeared to ignite the tetrazine in the atmosphere, destroying the colony and colonists. Further investigation revealed that the Suliban were actually responsible.
