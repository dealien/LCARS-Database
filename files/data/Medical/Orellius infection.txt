MEDICAL: Orellius infection

An affliction, otherwise unspecified, which is transmitted by an insect native to the Class M world colonized by Alixus and her unintended followers, who have not yet found a cure for it after 10 years as they have 17 others. Symptoms include swelling and high fever.
