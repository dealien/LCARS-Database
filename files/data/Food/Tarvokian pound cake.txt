FOOD: Tarvokian pound cake

Baked torte that is dark brown and rich in flavor. 
Lieutenant Worf prepared Cadet Crusher a homemade version of this baked torte for his first visit back aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise-D from Starfleet Academy in 2369.
