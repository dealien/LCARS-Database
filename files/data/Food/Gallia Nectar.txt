FOOD: Gallia Nectar

Reference:
Although Gallia is actually a drink made from a blossom that grows only in the Delta Quadrant on the planet Paxau, its beautiful pale yellow color can be duplicated by mixing lemon yellow Kool-Aid with Welch's white grape juice and serving it over ice.
