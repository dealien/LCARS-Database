FOOD: martini

An Old Earth mixed drink of gin or vodka and dry vermouth that the U.S.S. Enterprise-D food replicators know well enough, despite the 24th Century trend away from alcoholic beverages. The crygenically thawed and cured Sonny Clemonds ordered one with two olives in 2364.
