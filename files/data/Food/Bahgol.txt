FOOD: Bahgol

A Klingon beverage (pronounced "BACK-goal") that Curzon Dax shared with Kang at their first meeting at the Korvat colon; Jadzia Dax later offered it to her three Klingon comrades before their "blood oath" mission. 

Reference: Blood Oath
