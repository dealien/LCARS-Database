FOOD: tulaberry wine

The Gamma Quadrant product was the object of a Ferengi trade agreement for the Nagus arranged by Quark, thanks to the Dosi, with the Karemma of the Dominion in 2370. Eight months later Sisko asked Quark to go back with him when trying to contact the Dominion's Founders, based on his prior contacts.
