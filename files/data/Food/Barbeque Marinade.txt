FOOD: Barbeque Marinade

2 pounds chicken pieces, pork, spareribs, beef short ribs, or baby back ribs
1 lemon
virgin olive oil
soy sauce or light soy sauce
pepper

In a deep dish or covered pot, cover meat with oil Drizzle the mixture with soy sauce. Squeeze the juice of the lemon into the mixture. Add pepper. Flip the meat and add more pepper. Cover and let sit for at least 3 hours at room temperature or overnight in your refrigerator. Barbeque your favorite way. Serves four to six.

Reference:
Here's a recipe Alfre Woodard shared with [Jonathan Frakes]- actually he stole it- for a tangy BBQ marinade that's great for chicken or ribs. It's the perfect dish before any first contact.
