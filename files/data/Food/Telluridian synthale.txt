FOOD: Telluridian synthale

Dark-colored, non-alcoholic beverage. 
The last silver-metallic crate of the Alliance's supply on Turkana IV was stolen in a raid by factional enemies, the Coalition. One of the stoppered bottles of this drink was sent back to Captain Jean-Luc Picard as a gift.
