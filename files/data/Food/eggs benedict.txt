FOOD: eggs benedict with asparagus

Earth breakfast dish consisting of two halves of an English muffin topped with ham, poached eggs and hollandaise sauce. Other ingredients, like asparagus, may also be included.  
The dish was part of a dream breakfast of Captain Kathryn Janeway when she settled for ration pack #5 early in U.S.S. Voyager's mission.
