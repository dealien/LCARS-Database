FOOD: Thalian chocolate mousse

A delicacy made with a cocoa plant native to Thalos VII where the beans are aged 400 years. It was programmed aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise-D replicators. Acting Ensign Wesley Crusher and crewman Guinan both offered some to the Dauphin Salia.
