FOOD: stew, Seyetik`s

Dish perfected by Professor Gideon Seyetik over several years and prepared by his wife Nidell for the Deep Space Nine staff on the eve of the Epsilon 119 reignition project; Chief Miles O'Brien asked for a recipe until it got too complex to remember.
