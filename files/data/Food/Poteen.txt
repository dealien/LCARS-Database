FOOD: Poteen

An illicit home-brewed Irish whiskey of Old Earth still enjoyed by the Bringloidis. O'Dell and his men tried to brew it in their own still aboard the U.S.S. Enterprise-D until Lieutenant Worf let them know they could get beverages more potent than synthehol from the food replicators.
