FOOD: Vulcan port

A dark blue-colored Vulcan variety of - if the Terran namesake is any guide - a hearty, fortified dark wine, bottled for at least 300 years. Quark had a bottle vintered in 2070 that he almost served to the Maquis member Sakonna.
