FOOD: Karvino juice

Beverage included among Bashir's prescription for Kira's stress ond overwork. 

Reference: Defiant 
This is a bright green drink; actually it's a green Gatorade called Whitewater Splash. It's funny that green Gatorade is Whitewater, while the opaque Gatorade is called Alpine Snow. Alpine Snow, by the way, looks just like ketracel-white, which is what the Jem'Hadar live on.
