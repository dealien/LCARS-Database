FOOD: dessert

An after-meal entree of sweets, usually following the dinner or supper meal.
After taking "control" of the U.S.S. Enterprise while affected by the Tsiolkovsky infection, young Wes Crusher decreed that a dessert course shall both precede and follow all meals, including breakfast. 

popular desserts have included:
banana split and ice cream dishes
cakes and tortes
chocolate and fudge in various forms
flan
Jell-O and other gelatins
jumja stick
Kai Winn souffle
pastries
pies and cobblers 
puddings, custards, and mousse
