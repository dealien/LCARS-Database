FOOD: zilm'kach

A Klingon dish (pronounced "ZIHLM-kak") served with or without sauce - including such spots as the Klingon restaurant on Deep Space Nine. Doctor Julian Bashir is famliliar with it and got a side order when he and Melora Pazlar ate there.
