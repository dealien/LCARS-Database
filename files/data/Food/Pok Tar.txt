FOOD: Pok Tar

A type of food served to Ti'Mur captain Vanik during his visit to the Enterprise NX-01. The Vulcan did not partake of the meal, however. Chef did know that Vulcans are vegetarians and chose this entree with that in mind.

Reference: Breaking the Ice
