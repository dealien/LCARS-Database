FOOD: zabu stew

A dish, named for the Cardassian animal meat it uses, that is still part of the Deep Space Nine private replicator menu programming over a year after the Cardassian withdrawal from DS9. The well-intentioned Keiko O'Brien served it to war orphan Rugal.
