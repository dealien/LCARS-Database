FOOD: Arcturian fizz

A drink that Lwaxana Troi claims has "certain pleasure-enhancing qualities": she offers to make one for her captor, DaiMon Tog, as a ruse in order to learn his security access code, but the suspicious Farek interrupted them during the 2366 incident.
