XENOLOGY: Tzenkethi
LOCATION: Tzenketh, Alpha Quadrant
GOVERNMENT: Tzenkethi Coalition

A people who engaged in border wars with the United Federation of Planets during Benjamin Sisko's posting as first officer on the U.S.S. Okinawa under Captain Leyton. In 2371 its dictatorial Autarch leader was overthrown by a coup. A Founder impersonated a Federation ambassador and tricked Captain Sisko and the crew of the U.S.S. Defiant into heading into Tzenkethi space and nearly starting a war. At Starfleet Academy, some training simulations involved Tzenkethi ships. In 2329, Corin Zweller became attracted to a Tzenkethi saboteur while on shore leave.
At least one world is M'kemas III. 

Reference: The Adversary, Starcharts, Rogue
