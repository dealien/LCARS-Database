XENOLOGY: Bandi
LOCATION: Deneb IV
GOVERNMENT: unaligned

A humanoid culture who applied for entry into the United Federation of Planets on stardate 41153.7. However, the Bandi were refused Federation membership due to the discovery that they had trapped and enslaved another life-form in order to gain entry into the Federation. The population did agree to assist the Federation in the creation of a new Farpoint Station for use by the Starfleet.

Reference: Encounter at Farpoint, Fall of Terok Nor
